The yeast Saccharomyces cerevisiae is, arguably, the best understood eukaryotic 
model organism, yet comparatively little is known about its membrane proteome. 
Here, we report the cloning and expression of 617 S. cerevisiae membrane 
proteins as fusions to a C-terminal topology reporter and present experimentally 
constrained topology models for 546 proteins. By homology, the experimental 
topology information can be extended to approximately 15,000 membrane proteins 
from 38 fully sequenced eukaryotic genomes.
