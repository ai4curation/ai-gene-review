## Abstract

1. Gene. 1996 Sep 16;173(2):289-90. doi: 10.1016/0378-1119(96)00234-x.

Cloning and sequencing analysis of a human synapsin IIb-encoding brain cDNA.

Xie Y(1).

Author information:
(1)Biology Department, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, 
Clearwater Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong.

Cloning and sequencing of the gene encoding human synapsin IIb, an abundant 
peripheral membrane protein of synaptic vesicles, is described. The human and 
rat coding sequences showed 89% nucleotide and 94% amino acid sequence homology.
