## Abstract

1. Genomics. 1996 Jun 15;34(3):418-21. doi: 10.1006/geno.1996.0306.

The NEUROD gene maps to human chromosome 2q32 and mouse chromosome 2.

Tamimi R(1), Steingrimsson E, Copeland NG, Dyer-Montgomery K, Lee JE, Hernandez 
R, Jenkins NA, Tapscott SJ.

Author information:
(1)Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, 1124 Columbia Street, Seattle, 
Washington, 98104, USA.

The Neurod gene is a basic-helix-loop-helix gene that regulates neurogenesis and 
is identical to the hamster beta2 gene that was cloned as a regulator of insulin 
transcription. Here we report the cloning of human NEUROD and mapping of the 
gene to human chromosome 2q32 and to mouse chromosome 2.
