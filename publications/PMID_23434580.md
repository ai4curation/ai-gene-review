# Akt kinase targets the association of CBP with histone H3 to regulate the acetylation of lysine K18.
**Authors:** Liu Y, Xing ZB, Zhang JH, Fang Y

CREB binding protein (CBP) is an acetyltransferase that plays an important role
in many biological processes. Here, we show that Akt phosphorylates CBP at
threonine 1871 and suppresses its acetyltransferase activity by impeding the
binding of CBP to histone H3, which results in a decrease in lysine K18
acetylation and dysregulation of target genes. Our results demonstrate that Akt
regulates acetyltransferase activity through CBP phosphorylation, which may
contribute to tumorigenesis.
