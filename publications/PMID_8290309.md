# [Values and limits of adenoviral vectors for gene transfer in vivo].
**Authors:** Briand P, Kahn A
**Journal:** Pathol Biol (Paris) (1993)

## Abstract

1. Pathol Biol (Paris). 1993 Oct;41(8):663-71.

[Values and limits of adenoviral vectors for gene transfer in vivo].

[Article in French]

Briand P(1), Kahn A.

Author information:
(1)Institut Cochin de Génétique Moléculaire, Paris, France.

Review of the therapeutic use of DNA transfer to treat a certain number of 
hereditary diseases (such as adenosine deaminase deficiency) or acquired 
diseases. The strategies (ex vivo manipulation or direct in vivo transfer of the 
corrective gene), vectors (retrovirus, adenovirus, nonviral vectors), and 
diseases which can benefit from gene therapy are considered and discussed 
together with an evaluation of the risk of gene therapy.
