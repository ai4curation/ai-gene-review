Erratum in
    FEBS Lett. 2009 Oct 20;583(20):3375.

The plant hormone abscisic acid (ABA) triggers production of reactive oxygen 
species (ROS) in guard cells via the AtrbohD and AtrbohF NADPH oxidases, leading 
to stomatal closure. The ABA-activated SnRK2 protein kinase open stomata 1 
(OST1) (SRK2E/SnRK2.6) acts upstream of ROS in guard cell ABA signaling. Here, 
we report that OST1 phosphorylates Ser13 and Ser174 on AtrbohF. In addition, 
substitution of Ser174 to Ala results in a approximately 40% reduction in the 
phosphorylation of AtrbohF by OST1. We also show that OST1 physically interacts 
with AtrbohF. These results provide biochemical evidence suggesting that OST1 
regulates AtrbohF activity.
