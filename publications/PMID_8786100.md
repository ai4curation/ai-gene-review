# Molecular cloning and mapping of a human cDNA for cytosolic malate dehydrogenase (MDH1).
**Authors:** Tanaka T, Inazawa J, Nakamura Y
**Journal:** Genomics (1996)
**DOI:** [10.1006/geno.1996.0087](https://doi.org/10.1006/geno.1996.0087)

## Abstract

1. Genomics. 1996 Feb 15;32(1):128-30. doi: 10.1006/geno.1996.0087.

Molecular cloning and mapping of a human cDNA for cytosolic malate dehydrogenase 
(MDH1).

Tanaka T(1), Inazawa J, Nakamura Y.

Author information:
(1)Laboratory of Molecular Medicine, Institute of Medical Science, University of 
Tokyo, Japan.

We have isolated a human cDNA encoding a protein of 334 amino acids that shows 
96% identity in amino acid sequence to murine cytosolic malate dehydrogenase. 
Heart and skeletal muscle expressed this gene most highly among the adult human 
tissues examined by Northern blot analysis. By fluorescence in situ 
hybridization, we determined its chromosomal location to be 2p15.
