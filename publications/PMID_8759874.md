## Abstract

1. J Bacteriol. 1996 Aug;178(16):5039-41. doi: 10.1128/jb.178.16.5039-5041.1996.

Identification of additional genes under the control of the transcription factor 
sigma F of Bacillus subtilis.

Decatur A(1), Losick R.

Author information:
(1)Department of Molecular and Cellular Biology, Harvard University, Cambridge, 
Massachusetts 02138, USA.

We describe the identification of five transcriptional units under the control 
of the sporulation transcription factor sigma F in Bacillus subtilis. These are 
csfA, csfB, csfC, csfD, and csfF, located at approximately 230 degrees, 2 
degrees, 316 degrees, 205 degrees, and approximately 290 degrees, respectively, 
on the genetic map. Null mutations in csfA, csfB, csfC, or csfD, either alone or 
together, do not cause a noticeable defect in sporulation or germination.
