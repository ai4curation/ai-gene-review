# Pex19p dampens the p19ARF-p53-p21WAF1 tumor suppressor pathway.
**Authors:** Sugihara T, Kaul SC, Kato J, Reddel RR, Nomura H, Wadhwa R
**Journal:** J Biol Chem (2001)
**DOI:** [10.1074/jbc.C100011200](https://doi.org/10.1074/jbc.C100011200)

## Abstract

1. J Biol Chem. 2001 Jun 1;276(22):18649-52. doi: 10.1074/jbc.C100011200. Epub
2001  Mar 19.

Pex19p dampens the p19ARF-p53-p21WAF1 tumor suppressor pathway.

Sugihara T(1), Kaul SC, Kato J, Reddel RR, Nomura H, Wadhwa R.

Author information:
(1)Chugai Research Institute for Molecular Medicine, 153-2 Nagai, Niihari-Mura, 
Ibaraki 300-41, Japan.

We isolated a 33-kDa protein, Pex19p/HK33/HsPXF, as a p19ARF-binding protein in 
a yeast two-hybrid screen. We demonstrate here that Pex19p interacts with p19ARF 
in the cell cytoplasm and excludes p19ARF from the nucleus, leading to a 
concurrent inactivation of p53 function. Down-regulation of Pex19p by its 
antisense expression resulted in increased levels of p19ARF, increased p53 
function, and a p53/p21WAF1-mediated senescence-like cell cycle arrest. The data 
demonstrated a novel mechanism of down-regulation of the p19ARF-p53 pathway.
