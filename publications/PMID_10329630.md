# The interferon-inducible nucleolar p204 protein binds the ribosomal RNA-specific UBF1 transcription factor and inhibits ribosomal RNA transcription.
**Authors:** Liu CJ, Wang H, Lengyel P
**Journal:** EMBO J (1999)
**DOI:** [10.1093/emboj/18.10.2845](https://doi.org/10.1093/emboj/18.10.2845)
**PMC:** [PMC1171365](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1171365/)

## Abstract

1. EMBO J. 1999 May 17;18(10):2845-54. doi: 10.1093/emboj/18.10.2845.

The interferon-inducible nucleolar p204 protein binds the ribosomal RNA-specific 
UBF1 transcription factor and inhibits ribosomal RNA transcription.

Liu CJ(1), Wang H, Lengyel P.

Author information:
(1)Department of Molecular Biophysics and Biochemistry, Yale University, PO Box 
208024, 333 Cedar Street, New Haven, CT 06520-8024, USA.

p204, a member of the interferon-inducible p200 family of murine proteins, is 
primarily nucleolar. We generated cell lines in which p204 was inducible by 
muristerone. This induction resulted in retardation of cell proliferation and 
inhibition of rRNA transcription in vivo. Interferon treatment, resulting in 
p204 induction and retardation of proliferation, also caused inhibition of rRNA 
transcription in vivo. p204 also inhibited rRNA transcription in vitro. This 
inhibition was overcome by addition of UBF1, the rRNA-specific transcription 
factor. A direct interaction between p204 and UBF1 was revealed in vitro in 
pull-down assays, and in vivo by co-immunoprecipitation from cell extracts. UBF1 
bound strongly to at least two regions of p204: the N-terminal segment linked to 
the conserved 200 amino acid a segment, and the conserved 200 amino acid b 
segment. Cleavage of the a or b segments into two segments (encoded by single 
exons) resulted in a strong decrease or loss of binding. The inhibition of rRNA 
transcription by p204 may be due to the inhibition by p204 of the specific DNA 
binding of UBF1. This was revealed in electrophoretic mobility shift, magnetic 
bead and footprinting assays. Thus, p204 serves as a mediator of the inhibition 
of rRNA transcription by interferon.
