## Abstract

1. Nat Methods. 2011 Oct 30;8(12):1053-5. doi: 10.1038/nmeth.1750.

Enhanced Y1H assays for Arabidopsis.

Gaudinier A(1), Zhang L, Reece-Hoyes JS, Taylor-Teeples M, Pu L, Liu Z, Breton 
G, Pruneda-Paz JL, Kim D, Kay SA, Walhout AJ, Ware D, Brady SM.

Author information:
(1)Department of Plant Biology and Genome Center, University of California 
Davis, Davis, California, USA.

We present an Arabidopsis thaliana full-length transcription factor resource of 
92% of root stele-expressed transcription factors and 74.5% of root-expressed 
transcription factors. We demonstrate its use with enhanced yeast one-hybrid 
(eY1H) screening for rapid, systematic mapping of plant transcription 
factor-promoter interactions. We identified 158 interactions with 13 
stele-expressed promoters, many of which occur physically or are regulatory in 
planta.
