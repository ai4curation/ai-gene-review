# Histidine decarboxylase-mediated histamine synthesis in glomeruli from rat kidneys.
**Authors:** Heald JI, Hollis TM
**Journal:** Am J Physiol (1976)
**DOI:** [10.1152/ajplegacy.1976.230.5.1349](https://doi.org/10.1152/ajplegacy.1976.230.5.1349)

## Abstract

1. Am J Physiol. 1976 May;230(5):1349-53. doi: 10.1152/ajplegacy.1976.230.5.1349.

Histidine decarboxylase-mediated histamine synthesis in glomeruli from rat 
kidneys.

Heald JI, Hollis TM.

Enzymatic histamine synthesis by renal glomeruli of the rat has been examined. 
Assays of the partially purified enzyme demonstrated a pH optimum of 6.2, a 
Michaelis-Menten constant of 2.4 X 10(-4) histidine, and lack of potentiation by 
benzene. These data thus indicate that renal glomeruli contain the 
histidine-specific histidine decarboxylase.
