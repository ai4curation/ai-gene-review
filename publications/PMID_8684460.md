## Abstract

Crystal structure of the p27Kip1 cyclin-dependent-kinase inhibitor bound to the

Comment in
    Nature. 1996 Jul 25;382(6589):295-6. doi: 10.1038/382295a0.

The crystal structure of the human p27Kip1 kinase inhibitory domain bound to the 
phosphorylated cyclin A-cyclin-dependent kinase 2 (Cdk2) complex has been 
determined at 2.3 angstrom. p27Kip1 binds the complex as an extended structure 
interacting with both cyclin A and Cdk2. On cyclin A, it binds in a groove 
formed by conserved cyclin box residues. On Cdk2, it binds and rearranges the 
amino-terminal lobe and also inserts into the catalytic cleft, mimicking ATP.
