## Abstract

1. J Clin Microbiol. 1987 Jan;25(1):150-1. doi: 10.1128/jcm.25.1.150-151.1987.

Cytotoxic and enterotoxic activities of Campylobacter jejuni are not specified 
by tetracycline resistance plasmids pMAK175 and pUA466.

Taylor DE, Johnson WM, Lior H.

The 45-kilobase tetracycline resistance plasmids pMAK175 and pUA466 from 
Campylobacter jejuni were examined using curing and mating experiments. However, 
these plasmids encoded neither cytotoxin production, as determined in Vero 
cells, nor enterotoxin activity, as determined in Chinese hamster ovary cells.
