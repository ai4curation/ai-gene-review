Comment in
    Nature. 1993 May 6;363(6424):15-6. doi: 10.1038/363015a0.

The proteins Grb2-Sem-5, Shc and Sos have been implicated in the signalling 
pathway from tyrosine kinase receptors to Ras. Grb2-Sem-5 binds directly to 
murine Sos1, a Ras exchange factor, through two SH3 domains. Sos is also 
associated with ligand-activated tyrosine kinase receptors which bind 
Grb2-Sem-5, and with the Grb2-Sem-5 binding protein, Shc. Ectopic expression of 
Drosophila Sos stimulates morphological transformation of rodent fibroblasts. 
These data define a pathway by which tyrosine kinases act through Ras to control 
cell growth and differentiation.
