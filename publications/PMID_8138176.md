transcriptionally silent loci (mat2 and mat3). The region between these sites (K 
region) is inert for meiotic crossing over. The mating-type genes (M or P) are

shown that the positional regulation of P genes is based on repression at the 
silent site, caused by elements in the flanking DNA sequences. In this study we
