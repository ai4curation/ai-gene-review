# Neural hyperactivation in carriers of the Alzheimer's risk variant on the clusterin gene.
**Authors:** Lancaster TM, Baird A, Wolf C, Jackson MC, Johnston SJ, Donev R, Thome J, Linden DE
**Journal:** Eur Neuropsychopharmacol (2011)
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.euroneuro.2011.02.001](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.euroneuro.2011.02.001)

## Abstract

1. Eur Neuropsychopharmacol. 2011 Dec;21(12):880-4. doi: 
10.1016/j.euroneuro.2011.02.001. Epub 2011 Mar 11.

Neural hyperactivation in carriers of the Alzheimer's risk variant on the 
clusterin gene.

Lancaster TM(1), Baird A, Wolf C, Jackson MC, Johnston SJ, Donev R, Thome J, 
Linden DE.

Author information:
(1)School of Medical Sciences, Bangor University, UK.

Recent GWAS identified a risk variant for Alzheimer's disease (AD) at a locus 
(rs11136000) of the clusterin gene (CLU). Here we use functional magnetic 
resonance imaging (fMRI) during working memory to probe the effect of the risk 
variant on brain activation in healthy individuals. Participants with the CLU 
risk genotype had higher activity than participants with the protective allele 
in frontal and posterior cingulate cortex and the hippocampus, particularly 
during high memory demand. These results inform pathophysiological models of the 
preclinical progression of AD.
