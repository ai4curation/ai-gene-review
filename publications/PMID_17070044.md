Adamantane sulfone and sulfonamide 11-beta-HSD1 Inhibitors.

Sorensen B(1), Winn M, Rohde J, Shuai Q, Wang J, Fung S, Monzon K, Chiou W, 
Stolarik D, Imade H, Pan L, Deng X, Chovan L, Longenecker K, Judge R, Qin W, 
Brune M, Camp H, Frevert EU, Jacobson P, Link JT.

Author information:
(1)Abbott, Dept. 47F, Bldg. AP-10, Rm. 301, 100 Abbott Park Road, Abbott Park, 
IL 60064-6098, USA.

Potent and selective adamantane sulfone and sulfonamide inhibitors of 
11-beta-HSD-1 have been discovered. Selected compounds from these series have 
robust pharmacokinetic profiles and strongly inhibit liver, fat, and brain HSD1 
for extended periods after oral dosing.
