# Cloning of cDNA for rat eosinophil major basic protein.

## Abstract

1. Biochim Biophys Acta. 1995 Dec 27;1264(3):261-4. doi:

Cloning of cDNA for rat eosinophil major basic protein.

Nittoh T(1), Watanabe M, Okayama H, Misawa S, Isobe Y, Hayashi H, Mue S, Ohuchi 
K.

Author information:
(1)Department of Pathophysiological Biochemistry, Faculty of Pharmaceutical 
Sciences, Tohoku University, Miyagi, Japan.

We have determined the complete nucleotide sequence for the cDNA encoding rat 
eosinophil major basic protein (MBP) using the rapid amplification of cDNA ends 
(RACE) procedure. The deduced amino acid sequence revealed that the rat 
prepro-MBP has three functional domains, namely the signal peptide, the acidic 
peptide that contains numerous acidic amino acids, and the mature MBP, as in 
human and guinea pig MBP.
