# Ca<sup>2+</sup> entry through mechanotransduction channels localizes BAIAP2L2 to stereocilia tips
**Authors:** Julia Halford, Michael Bateschell, Peter G. Barr-Gillespie
**Journal:** Molecular Biology of the Cell (2022)
**DOI:** [10.1091/mbc.e21-10-0491](https://doi.org/10.1091/mbc.e21-10-0491)

## Content

Many proteins are directed to specific locations within the cell. Brain-specific angiogenesis inhibitor 1-associated protein 2-like protein 2, a membrane-binding protein required for maintenance of mechanotransduction in hair cells, is selectively targeted to tips of transducing stereocilia through the combined activity of a MYO15A-EPS motor complex and by Ca2+ entering through open mechanotransduction channels.
