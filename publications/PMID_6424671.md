# Characterization of the precursor form of the exocellular levansucrase from Bacillus subtilis.
**Authors:** Fouet A, Arnaud M, Klier A, Rapoport G
**Journal:** Biochem Biophys Res Commun (1984)
**DOI:** [10.1016/s0006-291x(84)80320-4](https://doi.org/10.1016/s0006-291x(84)80320-4)

## Abstract

1. Biochem Biophys Res Commun. 1984 Mar 15;119(2):795-800. doi: 
10.1016/s0006-291x(84)80320-4.

Characterization of the precursor form of the exocellular levansucrase from 
Bacillus subtilis.

Fouet A, Arnaud M, Klier A, Rapoport G.

Expression of the cloned levansucrase gene (sacB) was demonstrated in E. coli 
minicells by assay of the enzyme in crude extracts, SDS-polyacrylamide gel 
electrophoresis and immunoblotting. The existence of a precursor form of the 
enzyme of MW 53000 was also demonstrated and confirmed by the DNA sequence 
corresponding to the NH2 terminal region of the protein.
