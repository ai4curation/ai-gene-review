Comment in
    Science. 2006 Aug 11;313(5788):773-4. doi: 10.1126/science.1131833.

The Arabidopsis CLAVATA3 (CLV3) gene encodes a stem cell-specific protein 
presumed to be a precursor of a secreted peptide hormone. Matrix-assisted laser 
desorption/ionization-time-of-flight mass spectrometry (MALDI-TOF MS) applied to 
in situ Arabidopsis tissues determined the structure of a modified 12-amino acid 
peptide (MCLV3), which was derived from a conserved motif in the CLV3 sequence. 
Synthetic MCLV3 induced shoot and root meristem consumption as cells 
differentiated into other organs, displaying the typical phenotype of transgenic 
plants overexpressing CLV3. These results suggest that the functional peptide of 
CLV3 is MCLV3.
