Victivallis vadensis ATCC BAA-548 represents the first cultured representative 
from the novel phylum Lentisphaerae, a deep-branching bacterial lineage. Few 
cultured bacteria from this phylum are known, and V. vadensis therefore 
represents an important organism for evolutionary studies. V. vadensis is a 
strictly anaerobic sugar-fermenting isolate from the human gastrointestinal 
tract.
