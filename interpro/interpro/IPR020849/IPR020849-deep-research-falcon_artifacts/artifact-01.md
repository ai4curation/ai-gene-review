| GO:0007165 signal transduction | MODIFY | Ras-type proteins are widely described as molecular switches in signaling pathways, but the family is functionally diversified into multiple subfamilies (Ras, Ral, Rheb, Rap, Rad, Rit, DIRAS) with distinct downstream outputs and pathway contexts.
