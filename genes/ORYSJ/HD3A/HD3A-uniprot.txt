CC   -!- FUNCTION: Probable mobile flower-promoting signal (florigen) that moves
CC       from the leaf to the shoot apical meristem (SAM) and induces flowering.
CC       Promotes the transition from vegetative growth to flowering downstream
CC       of HD1 and EHD1 under short day (SD) conditions. Acts upstream of
CC       MADS14 and MADS15. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12407188,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17446351, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18223202,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19762423}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000305}. Nucleus {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in the inner region of the SAM, stem and
CC       leaf blade vascular tissues (at protein level).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17446351}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Expressed with a circadian rhythm showing a peak at dawn and
CC       then decreasing to reach the lowest levels early in the night in SD
CC       conditions. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17446351}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the phosphatidylethanolamine-binding protein
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
