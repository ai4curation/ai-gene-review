CC   -!- FUNCTION: Probable transcription factor involved in the development of
CC       floral organs. Acts as C-class protein in association with MADS58.
CC       Involved in the control of lodicule number (whorl 2), stamen
CC       specification (whorl 3) and floral meristem determinacy (whorl 4), but
CC       not in the regulation of carpel morphogenesis. Plays a more predominant
CC       role in controlling lodicule development and in specifying stamen
CC       identity than MADS58. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11828031,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16326928, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9869408}.
