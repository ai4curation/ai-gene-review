CC   -!- FUNCTION: Dioxygenase that cleaves the interphenyl C-alpha-C-beta
CC       double bond of resveratrol to yield 3,5-dihydroxybenzaldehyde and 4-
CC       hydroxybenzaldehyde (PubMed:21073977). Also cleaves piceatannol, a
CC       compound that differs from resveratrol only in the occurrence of an
CC       additional hydroxyl group, which leads to the production of 3,4-
CC       dihydroxybenzaldehyde and 3,5-hydroxybenzaldehyde (PubMed:21073977).
CC       Exhibits a high degree of substrate specificity, as suggested by the
CC       lack of activity on carotenoids and the other resveratrol- related
CC       compounds (PubMed:21073977). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21073977}.
