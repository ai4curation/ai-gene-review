CC   -!- FUNCTION: Structural component of the bacteriophage tail which consists
CC       of a contractile sheath, a tube and a baseplate. The central
CC       cylindrical segment of the tail consists of a rigid tube, composed of
CC       multiple copies of gp19, surrounded by the outer contractile sheath
CC       assembled from gp18 subunits. A total of 138 copies of gp18 arranged
CC       into 23 hexameric rings constitutes the sheath. During infection,
CC       contraction of the sheath drives the central tube through the host
CC       outer membrane, creating a channel for DNA ejection from the capsid
CC       into the host cell. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14625682}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Hexamer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Virion {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15315755}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the myoviridae tail sheath protein family.
