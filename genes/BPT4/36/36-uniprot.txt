CC   -!- FUNCTION: Structural component of the distal-half of the long-tail
CC       fiber. The long-tail fiber of T4 is about 1600 Angstroms long with a
CC       kink in the middle that divides the fiber into proximal and distal
CC       halves. The thin tip of the distal half-fiber interacts with the
CC       bacterial lipopolysaccharide receptor and specifies the host range of
CC       the phage. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8709154}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The long-tail fibers are trimeric, with a stoichiometry of
CC       gp34/gp37/gp36/gp35 of 3:3:3:1. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:8709154}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Virion {ECO:0000305}.
