CC   -!- FUNCTION: DNA helicase that stimulates viral DNA replication and
CC       recombination. Plays a role in T4 DNA replication initiation by
CC       selecting and activating DNA origins. Acts by dissociating and
CC       reassociating with the DNA molecule being unwound. Unwinds DNA as a
CC       monomer in a 5'-3' direction at a rate of 250 bp/s and can efficiently
CC       displace proteins from the DNA (PubMed:17823128, PubMed:22504228).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12411580, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17823128,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18314134, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22504228}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=Couples ATP hydrolysis with the unwinding of duplex DNA at the
CC         replication fork by translocating in the 5'-3' direction.;
CC         EC=5.6.2.3; Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:12411580,
CC         ECO:0000269|PubMed:17823128};
