CC   -!- FUNCTION: Modulates blood feeding of female mosquitoes on vertebrate
CC       species by binding and sequestering different mediators involved in the
CC       host response (By similarity). Binds serotonin, noradrenaline,
CC       histamine and tryptamine (PubMed:16301315, PubMed:17928288). Inhibits
CC       histamine-, serotonin- and partially noradrenaline-induced smooth
CC       muscle contraction (PubMed:16301315). Exhibits vasodilating activity
CC       (PubMed:16301315). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P18153,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16301315, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17928288}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17913537}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Female saliva (at protein level) (PubMed:17913537).
CC       Female salivary gland (PubMed:12062411). Not detected in female carcass
CC       without salivary glands (PubMed:12062411). Not detected in male tissues
CC       (PubMed:11841502, PubMed:12062411). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11841502,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12062411, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17913537}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed in late pupal stage (PubMed:11841502,
CC       PubMed:12062411). Not detected in embryo, larval and early pupal stages
CC       (PubMed:11841502, PubMed:12062411). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11841502,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12062411}.
