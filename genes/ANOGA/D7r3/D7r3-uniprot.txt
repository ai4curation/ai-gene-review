CC   -!- FUNCTION: Modulates blood feeding of female mosquitoes on vertebrate
CC       species by binding and sequestering different mediators involved in the
CC       host response (By similarity). Binds serotonin, noradrenaline,
CC       histamine and adrenaline (PubMed:16301315). Inhibits histamine-,
CC       serotonin- and noradrenaline-induced smooth muscle contraction
CC       (PubMed:16301315). Exhibits vasodilating activity (PubMed:16301315).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P18153, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16301315}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17913537}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Female saliva (at protein level) (PubMed:17913537).
CC       Female salivary gland (PubMed:9990055). Low-level expression in female
CC       carcass without salivary glands (PubMed:9990055). Not detected in male
CC       tissues (PubMed:11841502, PubMed:9990055).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11841502, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17913537,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9990055}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Not detected in embryo, larval and pupal stages.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11841502}.
