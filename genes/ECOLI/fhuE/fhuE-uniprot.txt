CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the active transport across the outer membrane of
CC       iron complexed with linear hydroxamate siderophores coprogen,
CC       rhodotorulic acid and ferrioxamine B. Binds Fe-coprogen with high
CC       affinity, rhodotorulic acid to a lesser extent, and weakly to
CC       ferrioxamine B. Selective for planar siderophores. Does not use cyclic
CC       siderophores ferrichrome nor ferrioxamine E as substrates.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31098021}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell outer membrane {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-
CC       ProRule:PRU01360, ECO:0000305|PubMed:31098021}; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU01360}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: For induction, the TonB and the ExbB proteins have to be
CC       active. Induced by hydroxyurea (PubMed:20005847).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20005847}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Simultaneous deletion of TonB-depedent
CC       transporter (TBDT) receptor genes fhuE, fhuA, fecA, cirA, fepA and fiu
CC       results in loss of growth on solid agar using Fe-coprogen, Fe-
CC       rhodotorulic acid or ferrioxamine B as the sole iron source.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31098021}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the TonB-dependent receptor family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
