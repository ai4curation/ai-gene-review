================================================================================
KEY CURATION DECISIONS
================================================================================

1. REMOVE ALL PROTEIN BINDING ANNOTATIONS (8 total)
   Rationale: Generic, uninformative term violates GO best practices. More 
   specific alternatives exist (complex membership for BRE5, process terms 
   for other interactions). Catalytic activity (GO:0004843) already captures
   primary function.

2. ACCEPT ALL RIBOPHAGY ANNOTATIONS (3 total)
   Rationale: Major biological function with strong evidence. Catalytic 
   activity mechanistically required. Functionally important for nutrient 
   starvation response and cell survival.

3. MARK MRNA BINDING AS OVER-ANNOTATED (2 annotations)
   Rationale: Identified from proteome-wide surveys detecting co-localization
   in stress granules. Actual functional role is deubiquitination (catalytic 
   activity requirement), not mRNA recognition.

4. ACCEPT ALL TRANSPORT-RELATED ANNOTATIONS
   Rationale: SEC23/COPII substrate specificity well-characterized through
   multiple independent approaches. Mechanism clearly documented.

5. ACCEPT ALL STRESS-RESPONSE ANNOTATIONS
   Rationale: Osmotic stress response through Hog1 MAPK regulation documented.
   Stress granule assembly requires Ubp3 catalytic activity uniquely among
   UBP family members.
