IMPORTANT CORRECTION TO TASK DESCRIPTION:

The task states: "H3K9-specific histone acetyltransferase activity"

THIS IS INCORRECT.

ACTUAL SAS2 SUBSTRATES:
- H4K16 (histone H4 lysine 16) - PRIMARY substrate
- H3K14 (histone H3 lysine 14) - SECONDARY substrate
- NOT H3K9 (acetylated by other HATs, e.g., Gcn5)

This distinction is critical for accurate functional annotation.
