CC   -!- FUNCTION: Nuclear import adapter that specifically recruits the two
CC       functionally and topologically linked ribosomal proteins RPL5 and RPL11
CC       (encoded by RPL11A and RPL11B). Guarantees that this cargo pair remains
CC       bound together from the time of synthesis in the cytoplasm until
CC       delivery to the nascent 5S rRNA in the nucleus.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23118189}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Forms a heterotrimeric complex with RPL5 and RPL11A or RPL11B;
CC       interaction of this complex with KAP104 allows the nuclear import of
CC       the heterotrimer (PubMed:23118189). Component of a hexameric 5S RNP
CC       precursor complex, composed of 5S RNA, RRS1, RPF2, RPL5, RPL11A/RPL11B
CC       and SYO1; this complex acts as a precursor for ribosome assembly
CC       (PubMed:37291423). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23118189,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:37291423}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm. Nucleus. Note=The SYO1/RPL11/RPL5
CC       complex is transported into the nucleus by KAP104.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Present with 2600 molecules/cell in log phase SD medium.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14562106}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the nuclear import and ribosome assembly adapter
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
