CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalytic component of the NuA3 histone acetyltransferase
CC       complex, that acetylates H3K14 (PubMed:10600516, PubMed:10817755,
CC       PubMed:11731478, PubMed:16581777, PubMed:17157260, PubMed:25104842).
CC       The NuA3 HAT complex has 2 functionally distinct forms. NuA3a binds
CC       H3K4me3, through the PHD finger of YNG1, and acetylates H3K14 at the
CC       promoter region of actively transcribed genes to promote transcription
CC       initiation. NuA3b binds H3K36me3 at the coding regions of actively
CC       transcribed genes, through the PWWP domain of PDP3, and coordinates
CC       transcription elongation (PubMed:17157260, PubMed:25104842). In vitro,
CC       SAS3 acetylates free histones H3 and H4 (PubMed:10600516). It is
CC       involved in silencing the HMR locus (PubMed:16581777, PubMed:8782818).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10600516, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10817755,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11731478, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16581777,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17157260, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25104842,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8782818}.

CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the NuA3 histone acetyltransferase (HAT) complex
CC       (PubMed:17157260). The NuA3 HAT complex has 2 functionally distinct
CC       forms that participate in transcription (PubMed:25104842). The NuA3a
CC       HAT complex is composed of at least NTO1, SAS3, TAF14, YNG1 and EAF6
CC       (PubMed:17157260). The NuA3b HAT complex contains an additional
CC       subunit, PDP3 (PubMed:25104842). SAS3 interacts with CDC68/SPT16
CC       (PubMed:10817755). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10817755,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17157260, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25104842}.
