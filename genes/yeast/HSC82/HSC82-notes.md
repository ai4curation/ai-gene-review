# HSC82 notes

## 2026-05-13 Falcon incorporation

Falcon supports HSC82 as the constitutive cytosolic Hsp90 isoform, distinct from the more heat-inducible HSP82 paralog [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "HSC82 encodes the constitutive cytosolic Hsp90 isoform (Hsc82), whereas HSP82 encodes a strongly heat‑inducible isoform (Hsp82); both are ~97% identical but differ measurably in ATPase/conformational properties and stress robustness."]. Core function should therefore emphasize ATP-dependent Hsp90 chaperone cycling and client maturation, not only generic protein binding or generic complex membership [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "Hsp90/Hsc82 functions as an ATP-dependent folding chaperone for late intermediates/clients, and specific Hsp90 mutants that disrupt client loading/closing/reopening cause client-specific maturation defects and temperature-sensitive growth."].

For MF curation, ATP binding and ATP hydrolysis are central and should be preferred over generic nucleotide binding [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "N-terminal ATP-binding domain; ATP binding drives lid closure and N-terminal association; Hsc82 shows higher ATPase activity than Hsp82 at 30–37°C"] [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "True but much less informative than ATP binding/ATPase activity; GO should prefer the specific ATP-related terms"]. Likewise, `protein-containing complex` is too broad because Hsc82 forms many specific Hsp90/co-chaperone/client assemblies [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "Hsc82 forms many complexes, but specific complexes (homodimer; Sti1/Cpr6/Sba1/Cdc37/Sgt1 complexes) are more biologically useful than a generic complex term"].

Falcon supports cytosol as the core localization and treats mitochondrial, plasma membrane, and perinuclear localizations cautiously unless backed by direct HSC82-specific localization evidence [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "Hsc82 is described as the constitutive cytosolic Hsp90 isoform; many experimental Hsc82 interactome/purification studies use cytosolic lysates, consistent with CC:cytosol annotations for both isoforms, with expression-level differences for HSC82 vs HSP82."] [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "No direct Hsc82 localization evidence in the retrieved sources"].

Client-dependent processes such as calcineurin regulation, box C/D snoRNP/R2TP, proteasome assembly, telomere maintenance, and SGT1/kinetochore biology should be kept separate from HSC82's core Hsp90 foldase activity unless the annotation is directly tied to the relevant client evidence [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "Direct Hsc82 binding to CNB1 and effects on CNA2/CNB1 complex state/activity support calcineurin as an Hsp90 client/regulatory target"] [file:yeast/HSC82/HSC82-deep-research-falcon.md "Hsp90 network reviews mention Tah1/Pih1 (R2TP-like) functions, but direct Hsc82-specific experimental support in the retrieved set is weak"].
