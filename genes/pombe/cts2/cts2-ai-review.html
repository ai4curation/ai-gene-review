Gene Description

SPAPB1E7.04c is a chitinase-like protein belonging to the glycosyl hydrolase 18 family, specifically the chitinase class III subfamily. It is a secreted protein with a signal peptide and contains a GH18 domain. However, it lacks the conserved Glu residue at position 166 that is essential for chitinase activity, making its enzymatic function uncertain. The protein localizes to extracellular regions and fungal-type cell wall, and may be involved in carbohydrate metabolism despite its questionable chitinase activity.
