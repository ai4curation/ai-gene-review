Curated from the ALLERGENS backlog (alias Feld7; UniProt unreviewed/TrEMBL).

  "Belongs to the calycin superfamily. Lipocalin family."]; minor cat allergen
  (Allergome 8431), homolog of dog Can f 1 / von Ebner's gland protein.
- No curated UniProt FUNCTION (TrEMBL). GOA: extracellular region (IEA) + generic
  small molecule binding (IEA).
- Curation: ACCEPT extracellular region (secreted); ACCEPT small molecule binding —
  kept (not refined) because the lipocalin calyx binds small hydrophobic ligands but
  no specific ligand is established for Fel d 7.
- Core function: lipocalin small-molecule (lipophilic ligand) carrier, extracellular;
  **knowledge gap**: specific ligand/role unknown → a function-gap gene (contrast with
  the well-characterized Fel d 2/3/4).
