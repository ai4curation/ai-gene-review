Gene Description

Hsp90 co-chaperone Cdc37 in Candida albicans. Functions as a kinase-specific co-chaperone that binds to numerous kinases and promotes their interaction with the Hsp90 complex, resulting in stabilization and promotion of their activity. Interacts with the Crk1 kinase domain and is required for Crk1 protein production (PMID:15013782). Belongs to the CDC37 family. Unlike classical holdase chaperones, CDC37 does not broadly bind unfolded proteins; rather, it specifically recognizes protein kinase domains and recruits them to the Hsp90 system.
