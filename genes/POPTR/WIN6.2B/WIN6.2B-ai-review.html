Gene Description

Wound-inducible secreted acidic endochitinase of glycoside hydrolase family 19 (class I chitinase). It contains an N-terminal chitin-binding type-1 (hevein-like) domain and a C-terminal catalytic chitinase domain, and randomly hydrolyzes the beta-1,4 linkages of chitin and chitodextrins. The enzyme contributes to plant defense against chitin-containing fungal pathogens and is induced by wounding.
