# NRAMP6.1 notes

- Reviewed NRAMP6.1 as a probable NRAMP-family divalent metal transporter. UniProt supports a broad multi-pass membrane metal transporter role, but not direct cation-specific assays for this paralog [file:POPTR/NRAMP6.1/NRAMP6.1-uniprot.txt].
- Kept iron and manganese homeostasis annotations as non-core homology-based inferences. PMID:20623158 identifies Populus NRAMPs in the metal transporter repertoire, and PMID:35700212 provides Populus NRAMP family context [PMID:20623158, PMID:35700212].
