## Curation notes

- UniProt identifies ACCC1 as chloroplast biotin carboxylase 1, a biotin carboxylase subunit of the acetyl-CoA carboxylase complex [file:POPTR/ACCC1/ACCC1-uniprot.txt].
- Biotin carboxylase activity was accepted as the direct subunit activity; CoA carboxylase activity was modified to contributes_to acetyl-CoA carboxylase activity at the complex level [file:POPTR/ACCC1/ACCC1-uniprot.txt; file:POPTR/ACCC1/ACCC1-goa.tsv].
