Gene Description

Golgi membrane xyloglucan O-acetyltransferase that acetylates fucosylated galactose residues on xyloglucan side chains.

Proposed New Ontology Terms

xyloglucan O-acetyltransferase activity

Definition: Catalysis of the transfer of an acetyl group to xyloglucan, including acetylation of galactosyl side-chain residues of fucosylated xyloglucan.

Justification: Populus XGOAT proteins experimentally acetylate fucosylated galactose residues on xyloglucan, but the existing GO xylan O-acetyltransferase term names the wrong polysaccharide substrate; this is a concrete candidate for a GO editorial term request.

Parent term: O-acetyltransferase activity

Supporting Evidence:

  • file:POPTR/XGOAT2/XGOAT2-uniprot.txt : Xyloglucan acetyltransferase that catalyzes the acetylation of fucosylated Gal residues on xyloglucan side chains
  • PMID:30083810 : PtrXGOATs are O-acetyltransferases acetylating fucosylated galactose residues on xyloglucan
