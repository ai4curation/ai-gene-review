Gene Description

Trans-Golgi network NRAMP-family divalent metal transporter that can transport Mn and Fe in heterologous systems and has an in-planta role in cell-to-cell manganese distribution.

Core Functions

Transports Mn(2+) at the trans-Golgi network membrane, with a core role in intracellular manganese homeostasis and cell-to-cell manganese distribution.

Molecular Function:
manganese ion transmembrane transporter activity
Directly Involved In:
manganese ion transmembrane transport intracellular manganese ion homeostasis
Cellular Locations:
trans-Golgi network membrane

NRAMP3.1 notes

  • Reviewed existing GOA annotations against NRAMP3.1-uniprot.txt and GOA provenance.
  • Core interpretation: Trans-Golgi network NRAMP-family divalent metal transporter that transports manganese and iron and helps control cell-to-cell manganese movement in poplar.

  • Direct NRAMP paralog evidence: PMID:35700212.

  • Fe-homeostasis caveat: PMID:35700212 supports Fe transport in yeast but also notes its inability to complement the nramp3nramp4 double mutant phenotypes, so GO:0006879 is treated as over-annotated for NRAMP3.1 in planta.
