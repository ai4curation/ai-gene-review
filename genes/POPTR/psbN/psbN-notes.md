# psbN notes

- UniProt identifies psbN as Protein PsbN [file:POPTR/psbN/psbN-uniprot.txt "RecName: Full=Protein PsbN"].
- UniProt states PsbN may play a role in photosystem I and II biogenesis and places it in the chloroplast thylakoid membrane [file:POPTR/psbN/psbN-uniprot.txt "May play a role in photosystem I and II biogenesis"; file:POPTR/psbN/psbN-uniprot.txt "SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Plastid, chloroplast thylakoid membrane"].
- Because UniProt explicitly cautions that PsbN is probably not a PSII component, the review avoids adding PSII membership and treats broad photosynthesis as non-core pathway context.
