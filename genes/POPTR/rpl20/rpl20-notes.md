# rpl20 notes

- UniProt identifies rpl20 as chloroplast large ribosomal subunit protein bL20c / 50S ribosomal protein L20 [file:POPTR/rpl20/rpl20-uniprot.txt "RecName: Full=Large ribosomal subunit protein bL20c"; file:POPTR/rpl20/rpl20-uniprot.txt "AltName: Full=50S ribosomal protein L20, chloroplastic"].
- UniProt states that L20 binds 23S rRNA and is necessary for in vitro 50S subunit assembly [file:POPTR/rpl20/rpl20-uniprot.txt "Binds directly to 23S ribosomal RNA and is necessary for the in vitro assembly process of the 50S ribosomal subunit."].
- Because UniProt also notes that L20 is not involved in the protein-synthesizing functions of that subunit, translation was retained as non-core while ribosomal large subunit assembly and rRNA binding were made core [file:POPTR/rpl20/rpl20-uniprot.txt "It is not involved in the protein synthesizing functions of that subunit."].
- The rpl20 function is homology-inferred rather than directly assayed in Populus, so future Populus-specific data could refine the translation-versus-assembly distinction.
