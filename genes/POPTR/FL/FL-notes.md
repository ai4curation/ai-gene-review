# FL notes

- Reviewed UniProt, GOA, and PMID:10849341 on 2026-05-10.
- UniProt identifies FL/PTLF as a Floricaula/LEAFY homolog and probable nuclear transcription factor [file:POPTR/FL/FL-uniprot.txt "RecName: Full=Floricaula/leafy homolog"; file:POPTR/FL/FL-uniprot.txt "Probable transcription factor."].
- The Populus PTLF paper supports a floral-development role: PTLF is the Populus homolog of LEAFY/FLORICAULA and was strongest in developing inflorescences [PMID:10849341 "PTLF, the Populus trichocarpa homolog of LEAFY (LFY) and FLORICAULA, was cloned to assess its function in a dioecious tree species."; PMID:10849341 "In situ hybridization studies showed that the gene was expressed most strongly in developing inflorescences."].
- Accepted DNA binding, transcription regulation, nucleus, and regulation of flower development.
