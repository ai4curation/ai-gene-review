Gene Description

Type II (1-Cys, glutaredoxin/thioredoxin-dependent) peroxiredoxin of the Prx5 subfamily. It is a thiol-based peroxidase that uses a conserved redox-active peroxidatic cysteine to reduce hydrogen peroxide and organic, fatty acid, and phospholipid hydroperoxides to water and the corresponding alcohols, receiving reducing equivalents preferentially from glutaredoxin or from thioredoxin. Through peroxide detoxification it contributes to cell protection against oxidative stress and to cellular redox homeostasis.
