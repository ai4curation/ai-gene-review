CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for jasmonate-regulated plant fertility and defense
CC       processes, and for coronatine and/or other elicitors
CC       perceptions/responses. Does not seem to be required for meiosis.
CC       Required for the regulation of some genes induced by wounding, but not
CC       for all. Component of SCF(COI1) E3 ubiquitin ligase complexes, which
CC       may mediate the ubiquitination and subsequent proteasomal degradation
CC       of target proteins (probably including the ribulose bisphosphate
CC       carboxylase small chain 1B RBCS-1B and the histone deacetylase HDA6).
CC       These SCF complexes play crucial roles in regulating response to
CC       jasmonate, and their interactions with the COP9 signalosome (CSN)
CC       appear to be important for their activity. Interacts with TIFY10A and
CC       inositol pentakisphosphate to form a high-affinity jasmonates
CC       coreceptor. Involved in the regulation of plant gene expression during
CC       plant-pathogen interactions with Pseudomonas syringae and Alternaria
CC       brassicicola. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10810145,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12172031, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12172836,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12244256, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12445118,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12724535, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12805591,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14756769, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9582125}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Protein modification; protein ubiquitination.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of SCF(COI1) E3 ubiquitin ligase complexes at least
CC       composed of ASK1 or ASK2, CUL1, RBX1A or RBX1B and COI1. Interacts with
CC       ASK1 and ASK2, but separately. Also binds to ASK11 and ASK12. Interacts
CC       with RBCS-1B and HDA6. SCF complexes interact with the COP9 signalosome
CC       (CSN). Interacts with TIFY10A. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12172031,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12445118, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12724535,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14749489, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20927106}.
