CC   -!- FUNCTION: Prevents gene silencing by suppressing CHG methylation as
CC       well as histone H3 lysine 9 dimethylation (H3K9me2) status at target
CC       loci (PubMed:28808009, PubMed:30407670). Collaboratively with ASI1 and
CC       EDM2, the AAE complex regulates alternative RNA processing (e.g.
CC       alternative splicing) and epigenetic silencing (e.g. H3K9me2) of
CC       intronic heterochromatin-containing genes as well as genic
CC       heterochromatin-containing genes by promoting distal 3'
CC       polyadenylation, thus being required for the accumulation of their
CC       full-length transcripts (PubMed:28808009). May also modulate
CC       transposable elements (TE) expression (PubMed:28808009). Mediates RPP7-
CC       dependent race-specific disease resistance by promoting histone H3
CC       lysine 9 dimethylation (H3K9me2) at the proximal RPP7 polyadenylation
CC       site, thus controlling alternative polyadenylation of RPP7 immune
CC       receptor transcripts and facilitating 2-phosphoserine RNAPII occupancy
CC       (PubMed:30407670). In cv. Columbia, required for RPP7-dependent disease
CC       resistance against the Hyaloperonospora arabidopsidis isolate Hiks1
CC       (PubMed:30407670). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28808009,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:30407670}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the ASI1-AIPP1-EDM2 (AAE) RNA regulatory complex
CC       composed of at least AIPP1/EDM3, ASI1 and EDM2 and may contain CPL2,
CC       AIPP2 and AIPP3/BDT1 (PubMed:28808009). Binds directly to ASI1 and EDM2
CC       and may function as a bridge protein between them (PubMed:28808009).
CC       Co-associates with EDM2 to histone H3 lysine 9 dimethylation (H3K9me2)-
CC       marked chromatin and transcripts at a critical proximal polyadenylation
CC       site of RPP7 to hamper proximal transcript polyadeylation/termination
CC       (PubMed:30407670). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28808009,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:30407670}.

CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Hyper-dimethylation of histone H3 lysine 9
CC       (H3K9me2) in several genes (PubMed:30407670). Impeded use of distal
CC       polyadenylation sites at intronic heterochromatin (HC)-containing genes
CC       (e.g. histone demethylase gene IBM1) resulting in a lack of functional
CC       full-length transcripts (PubMed:28808009). Silencing of transgenes such
CC       as 35S-SUC2 and genome-wide CHG hypermethylation at gene body regions,
CC       leading to reduced transcription of several genes (e.g. IBM1)
CC       (PubMed:28808009). Abolished expression of At4g16870, a transposable
CC       element (TE) of Copia-like retrotransposon origin, associated with
CC       methylated status (PubMed:28808009). Compromised RPP7-dependent
CC       immunity associated with reduced histone H3 lysine 9 dimethylation
CC       (H3K9me2) at the COPIA-R7 retrotransposon 5'LTR present in the first
CC       RPP7 intron, and leading to an increased sporulation of the
CC       incompatible Hyaloperonospora arabidopsidis (downy mildew) Hpa-Hiks1
CC       isolate, but normal resistance to Hpa-Cala2 and Hpa-Cand5 isolates in
CC       cv. Col-5 background; full susceptibility to these isolates is observed
CC       in cv. Wassilewskija background (PubMed:30407670).
