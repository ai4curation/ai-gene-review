CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription activator, when associated with
CC       BHLH2/EGL3/MYC146 or BHLH12/MYC1. Involved in epidermal cell fate
CC       specification in leaves. Together with TTG1 and GL3, promotes trichome
CC       formation and endoreplication. Regulates the production of a signal
CC       that induces hair (trichome) precursor cells on leaf primordia to
CC       differentiate. Binds to the WER-binding sites (WBS) promoter regions
CC       and activates the transcription of target genes (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11063707, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12356720,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15361138, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15728674,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9625690}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer and heterodimer with MYB82 (PubMed:24803498).
CC       Interacts directly with GL3 and BHLH2. Part of a complex made of GL1,
CC       GL3 or BHLH2, and TTG1. Also interacts with BHLH2/EGL3/MYC146 and
CC       BHLH12/MYC1. Interacts with MYB82 (PubMed:24803498).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11063707, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12917293,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14561633, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15361138,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15728674, ECO:0000269|PubMed:24803498}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       P27900; Q9FN69: GL3; NbExp=5; IntAct=EBI-1543742, EBI-533348;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00625,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14561633}. Note=Detected in trichome nucleus.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in leaves, stems and flowers
CC       (PubMed:11437443). Expressed in trichome cells and in leaf primordia
CC       (PubMed:9625690). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11437443,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9625690}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Ubiquitous in young leaves primordia. Becomes more
CC       prominent in developing trichome cells but disappears progressively
CC       when trichomes begin to initiate branches.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15728674}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Up-regulated by gibberellins (PubMed:9625690). May be
CC       regulated by GEBP and GEBP-like proteins (PubMed:12535344).
