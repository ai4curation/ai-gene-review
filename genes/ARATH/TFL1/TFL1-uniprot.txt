CC   -!- FUNCTION: Controls inflorescence meristem identity and is required for
CC       maintenance of an indeterminate inflorescence. Prevents the expression
CC       of 'APETALA1' and 'LEAFY'. Also plays a role in the regulation of the
CC       time of flowering in the long-day flowering pathway. May form complexes
CC       with phosphorylated ligands by interfering with kinases and their
CC       effectors (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed below the apical dome of inflorescence
CC       and coflorescence meristems, and in inflorescence stem.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Weakly expressed in vegetative phase from day 2 or
CC       day 3. Increased expression after commitment to flowering from day 7
CC       on.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Mutagenesis of His-88 to Tyr converts the repressor of
CC       flowering 'TERMINAL FLOWER 1' into a 'FLOWERING LOCUS T'-like activator
CC       of flowering.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the phosphatidylethanolamine-binding protein
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
