CC   -!- INDUCTION: Levels follow a circadian cycle with higher levels during

CC       mannitol and sorbitol (PubMed:17145720). Triggered by gibberellic acid
CC       (GA) and salt stresses (e.g. NaCl and KCl) (PubMed:17145720).

CC       ions (PubMed:17145720). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17145720}.
