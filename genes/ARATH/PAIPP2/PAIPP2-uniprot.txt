CC   -!- FUNCTION: Together with AIPP2 and AIPP3/BDT1, cooperates to form a BAH-
CC       PHD bivalent histone reader complex able to read histone H3 lysine 27
CC       trimethylation (H3K27me3) and low-methylated H3K4 histone marks in
CC       order to regulate transcription, especially to prevent early flowering;
CC       promotes AIPP3/BDT1 binding to H3K27me3 (PubMed:33277495,
CC       PubMed:33433058). CPL2 is subsequently recruited to form a BAH-PHD-CPL2
CC       complex (BPC) in order to silence several H3K27me3 and low-methylated
CC       H3K4 enriched loci, including AGO5, via the phosphorylation state-
CC       dependent inhibition of Pol II release from the transcriptional start
CC       site (e.g. Ser5P-Pol II dephosphorylation) (PubMed:33277495). The BPC
CC       complex represses flowering by inhibiting the expression of several
CC       genes, including AGL6, FT, FUL and SOC1 (PubMed:33277495).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:33277495, ECO:0000269|PubMed:33433058}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the BAH-PHD bivalent histone reader complex that
CC       contains AIPP2, PAIPP2 and AIPP3/BDT1; the BAH-PHD module associates
CC       with CPL2 to form the BAH-PHD-CPL2 complex (BPC) for transcriptional
CC       repression (PubMed:33277495). Binds directly to AIPP3/BDT1 and CPL2,
CC       but not to AIPP2 (PubMed:33277495, PubMed:33433058).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:33277495, ECO:0000269|PubMed:33433058}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed ubiquitously.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:33277495}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The PHD domain (283-334) recognizes specifically unmodified
CC       histone H3 lysine 4 (H3K4me0), binding affinity being inversely
CC       proportional to H3K4 methylation level. {ECO:0000303|PubMed:33277495}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: No obvious developmental defects
CC       (PubMed:33433058). The double mutant aipp2-1 paipp2-1 exhibits multiple
CC       developmental defects, such as a dwarfed size, early flowering, small
CC       leaves and poor fertility (PubMed:33277495). Abnormally up-regulated
CC       expression of many genes localized at H3K27me3 and low-methylated H3K4
CC       enriched loci associated with a higher accumulation of activated RNA
CC       polymerase II phosphorylated at 'Ser-5' (Ser5P-Pol II) in the double
CC       mutant aipp2-1 paipp2-1 (PubMed:33277495). Plants missing all PHD
CC       finger-containing proteins (e.g. PHD1, PAIPP2/PHD2, AIPP2/PHD3, PHD4,
CC       PHD5 and PHD6) exhibit an increased expression of flowering genes
CC       leading to an early flowering phenotype under long-day conditions as
CC       well as growth retardation (PubMed:33433058).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:33277495, ECO:0000269|PubMed:33433058}.
