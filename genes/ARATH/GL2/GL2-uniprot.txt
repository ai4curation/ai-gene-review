CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor involved in the determination of
CC       epidermal cell identity (Probable). Required for correct morphological
CC       development and maturation of trichomes (PubMed:7926739). Regulates the
CC       frequency of trichome initiation and determines trichome spacing
CC       (PubMed:11844112). Acts as a negative factor for root hair development
CC       (PubMed:26486447, PubMed:8620852, PubMed:8811855). Required for ectopic
CC       repression of root hair development in a subset of epidermal cells
CC       (PubMed:8811855). May suppress hair formation in root epidermis by
CC       promoting differentiation into hairless epidermal cells
CC       (PubMed:8620852). Directly suppresses the bHLH transcription factor
CC       genes, RHD6, RSL1, RSL2, LRL1, and LRL2, which have diverse functions
CC       in root hair development (PubMed:26486447). Required for normal
CC       development of seed coat mucilage (PubMed:21883555, PubMed:7926739).
CC       Involved in the control of seed oil accumulation (PubMed:16514561,
CC       PubMed:21883555). Acts as a negative regulator of anthocyanin
CC       biosynthesis (PubMed:26017690). May directly repress the expression of
CC       some component genes from the MYB-bHLH-WD40 (MBW) transcriptional
CC       activator complex (PubMed:26017690). The MBW complex activates the
CC       transcription of late biosynthesis genes in the flavonoid pathway,
CC       leading to the production of anthocyanins (Probable).
