CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in regulation of circadian rhythm and photoperiodic
CC       flowering. May play a role in maintenance of circadian amplitude and
CC       period length. Is involved in phytochrome B signaling. Stabilizes ADO3
CC       and the circadian photoreceptor ADO1/ZTL. Regulates 'CONSTANS' (CO) in
CC       the long-day flowering pathway by modulating the ADO3-dependent protein
CC       stability of CDF1 and CDF2, but is not essential to activate CO
CC       transcription. Regulates, via the microRNA miR172, a CO-independent
CC       pathway that promotes photoperiodic flowering by inducing 'FLOWERING
CC       LOCUS T'. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10920210, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17890372,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19619493}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with SPY (PubMed:15155885). Interacts with ADO1 (via

CC       terminus) with CDF1 (PubMed:17872410). Interacts (via N-terminus) with
