# HSFA1E (AT3G02990) Curation Notes

## Current Review Position

HSFA1E is reviewed conservatively as a class A heat shock transcription factor with
supported DNA-binding transcription factor activity, nuclear/cytoplasmic localization,
and a broad heat-response annotation retained as non-core.

## Supported Conclusions

- HSFA1E has the conserved HSF DNA-binding domain and UniProt describes HSE binding.
- Existing `GO:0003700 DNA-binding transcription factor activity`, DNA-binding, and
  regulatory-region binding annotations are retained.
- Nuclear and cytoplasmic localization annotations are retained; PMID:21931939 is
  treated as group-level HsfA1 nuclear-accumulation support rather than uniquely
  strong HSFA1E-specific localization evidence.
- `GO:0034605 cellular response to heat` is kept as non-core because HSFA1A/B/D are
  better supported as the primary thermotolerance regulators.

## Non-Retained Draft Claims

Earlier notes and generated research text suggested HSFA1E-specialized osmotic,
salt, oxidative, and cold stress functions. The final YAML does not propose those
as new GO annotations because the local evidence does not support HSFA1E-specific
curation actions at that strength.

## Review Scope

No new GO annotations are proposed for HSFA1E in this batch.
