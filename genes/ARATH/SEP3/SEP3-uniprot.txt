CC   -!- FUNCTION: Probable transcription factor active in inflorescence
CC       development and floral organogenesis. Functions with SEPALLATA1/AGL2
CC       and SEPALLATA2/AGL4 to ensure proper development of petals, stamens and
CC       carpels and to prevent the indeterminate growth of the flower meristem.
CC       Interacts with APETALA1, AGAMOUS or APETALA3/PISTILLATA to form
CC       complexes, that could be involved in genes regulation during floral
CC       meristem development (PubMed:10821278, PubMed:11206550). Binds
CC       specifically to the CArG box DNA sequence 5'-CC (A/T)6 GG-3'
CC       (PubMed:16080001). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10821278,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11206550, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16080001}.

CC       TT16/AGL32 (PubMed:16080001). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15805477,
