# HSFA1D (AT1G32330) Curation Notes

## Gene Summary
**Co-master regulator** of heat stress response with HSFA1A/B - unique light/circadian/hormone integration [deep-research:perplexity]

## Core Function (Molecular Activity)
- **DNA-binding transcription factor** - binds heat shock elements (HSE: nGAAn repeats) [deep-research]
- Forms **homotrimers** for high-affinity DNA binding [deep-research]
- Contains **AHA activation domain** (aromatic, hydrophobic, acidic motifs) [deep-research]
- **TDR domain** with conserved QIVKYQP motif for temperature-dependent repression [deep-research]

## Relationship to Other Class A1 HSFs

### Position in HSFA1 Family:
- **HSFA1A + HSFA1B**: Strongest heat stress response
- **HSFA1D**: **Intermediate activity** for heat, contributes to salt/osmotic stress
- **HSFA1E**: No thermotolerance alone, strong salt/osmotic response
- Subfunctionalization provides stress-type specificity [deep-research]

### Triple/Quadruple KO Evidence:
- **Triple KO (hsfa1a/b/d)**: Dramatic thermotolerance defects [deep-research]
- **Quadruple KO (hsfa1a/b/d/e)**: Complete loss of acquired thermotolerance
- HSFA1D is essential as part of master regulator network

## Key Biological Processes

### Heat Stress Response (PRIMARY/CORE)
- Co-master regulator with HSFA1A/B [deep-research]
- Activates HSP genes: HSP17, HSP70, HSP90, HSP101 [deep-research]
- Essential for thermotolerance (as part of triple) [deep-research]
- Activates HSFA2 and transcriptional cascade [deep-research]

### Light-Dependent Thermomorphogenesis (DISTINCT/CORE)
- **CRY1 photoreceptor integration** - blue light enhances HSFA1D nuclear localization [deep-research]
- **Light-induced thermotolerance (LIT)** - prepares for elevated temps on sunny days [deep-research]
- CRY1 interacts with HSFA1D, promotes IMPα1 binding [deep-research]
- cry1 mutants have reduced thermotolerance [deep-research]

### Circadian Regulation (DISTINCT/CORE)
- **Time-of-day-dependent** heat stress responses [deep-research]
- **70% of heat-responsive genes** show circadian gating [deep-research]
- Integrated with CCA1/LHY clock components [deep-research]
- Allows appropriately timed stress responses [deep-research]

### Brassinosteroid Signaling Integration (DISTINCT)
- **BIN2 phosphorylates HSFA1D** → reduces nuclear localization and DNA-binding [deep-research]
- **COP1 inhibits BIN2** → restores HSFA1D activity [deep-research]
- BR signaling promotes HSFA1D function [deep-research]
- Integrates growth (BR) with stress responses [deep-research]

### Hypocotyl Elongation
- Regulates hypocotyl growth under temperature stress [deep-research]
- Integration with developmental programs [deep-research]

### Chilling Tolerance (SECONDARY)
- Contributes to cold stress responses [deep-research]
- Broader stress integration than just heat [deep-research]

### Salt/Osmotic Stress (SECONDARY)
- Intermediate activity (stronger than HSFA1A/B, weaker than HSFA1E) [deep-research]
- Functional specialization within class A1 family [deep-research]

## Regulation of HSFA1D

### By Molecular Chaperones (Like HSFA1A/B)
- **HSP70/HSP90 bind to TDR domain** → inactive cytoplasmic state [deep-research]
- Heat stress → dissociation → trimerization → nuclear translocation [deep-research]
- TDR domain contains **QIVKYQP motif** (conserved plant HSFA1 feature) [deep-research]
- **Y271 residue** critical for repression (Y271D mimics phosphorylation) [deep-research]

### By Light Signaling (UNIQUE to HSFA1D emphasis)
- **CRY1 photoreceptor** physically interacts with HSFA1D [deep-research]
- Blue light → CRY1-HSFA1D interaction → enhanced nuclear import via IMPα1 [deep-research]
- **Light-induced thermotolerance**: primes heat response before stress [deep-research]
- cry1 mutants: reduced thermotolerance after light pretreatment [deep-research]

### By Brassinosteroid/COP1 Pathway (UNIQUE)
- **BIN2 kinase phosphorylates HSFA1D** → inhibits nuclear localization [deep-research]
- **COP1 antagonizes BIN2** → promotes HSFA1D nuclear accumulation [deep-research]
- BR signaling inhibits BIN2 → activates HSFA1D [deep-research]
- Integration of light + hormone + stress signals [deep-research]

### By Circadian Clock (UNIQUE emphasis)
- **CCA1/LHY** clock proteins modulate heat responses [deep-research]
- Time-of-day-dependent transcription (70% of heat genes) [deep-research]
- Allows anticipatory stress responses [deep-research]

### Subcellular Dynamics
- **Cytoplasm** (basal state, HSP-bound) [deep-research]
- **Nucleus** (heat stress, light-enhanced, circadian-gated) [deep-research]
- **Nuclear import**: IMPα1-mediated, CRY1-enhanced [deep-research]
- **Nuclear export signal (NES)** maintains cytoplasmic retention [deep-research]

## Unique Features of HSFA1D vs HSFA1A/B

### Regulatory Integration (HSFA1D-specific emphasis):
1. **Light signaling**: CRY1 photoreceptor integration
2. **Circadian clock**: Time-of-day gating (70% of genes)
3. **BR signaling**: BIN2/COP1 hormone integration
4. **Chilling tolerance**: Broader temp range

### Functional Activity:
- **Intermediate** thermotolerance activity (between HSFA1A/B and HSFA1E)
- Contributes to **salt/osmotic stress** (more than HSFA1A/B)
- **Hypocotyl elongation** role

### Mechanistic Features:
- Strong emphasis on **light-dependent thermotolerance**
- **Anticipatory** stress responses (light/circadian priming)
- Integration of **growth (BR) + stress** signals
