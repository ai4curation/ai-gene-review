CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor, which may be involved in the
CC       specification and maintenance of the stem cells (QC cells) in the root
CC       apical meristem (RAM). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25631790}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00108}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Specifically expressed in the central cells of a
CC       quiescent center (QC) of the root. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14711878}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Specifically expressed in the hypophysis of the
CC       majority of early globular embryos, approximately one round of cell
CC       division after the 16-cell stage, but never at the 16-cell stage
CC       itself. After the division of the hypophysis, it is detected in the
CC       upper lens-shaped cell that gives rise to the QC, but not in the lower
CC       daughter cell that gives rise to the central root cap. Subsequently, in
CC       heart stage and bent cotyledon stage embryos, it is detected in the
CC       four cells of the QC, which are the direct descendants of the lens-
CC       shaped cell. Also expressed in patches of cells that appeared
CC       associated with the vascular primordium of the cotyledons. This
CC       expression is strongest in late heart stage embryos and then gradually
CC       decreases. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14711878}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Abnormal quiescent center (QC) in the root apical
CC       meristem (RAM) and defects in cell differentiation.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25631790}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the WUS homeobox family. {ECO:0000305}.
