CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor acting negatively in the phytochrome B
CC       signaling pathway. May regulate the expression of a subset of genes
CC       involved in cell expansion by binding to the G-box motif (By
CC       similarity). Activated by CRY1 and CRY2 in response to low blue light
CC       (LBL) by direct binding at chromatin on E-box variant 5'-CA[CT]GTG-3'
CC       to stimulate specific gene expression to adapt global physiology (e.g.
CC       hypocotyl elongation in low blue light) (PubMed:26724867). Element of a
CC       PIF4/HMR/MED14-dependent thermoresponsive process; collaboratively with
CC       its transcriptional coactivator PTAC12/HMR/PAP5, involved in the
CC       regulation of thermoresponsive growth-relevant genes (e.g. mainly
CC       involved in biosynthesis and signaling of the phytohormone auxin)
CC       leading to daytime warm temperature elicitation of MED14-dependent
CC       thermomorphogenesis (e.g. hypocotyl elongation) (PubMed:30635559,
CC       PubMed:33824329, PubMed:36063057). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q84LH8,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:26724867, ECO:0000269|PubMed:30635559,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:33824329, ECO:0000269|PubMed:36063057}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts preferentially with the Pfr form of phytochrome B
CC       (phyB). Binds DNA as a homodimer, but once bound to DNA, loses its
CC       capacity to interact with phyB. Interacts with APRR1/TOC1 and PIF3.
CC       Binds to RGL2 and RGA. Forms non-functional heterodimer with HFR1.
CC       Interacts with PHYB, CRY1 and CRY2 in the nucleus in response to low
CC       blue light (LBL) (PubMed:12826627, PubMed:12897250, PubMed:20093430,
CC       PubMed:23224238, PubMed:26724867). Interacts with FYPP1 and FYPP3
CC       (PubMed:31527236). Associates to PTAC12/HMR/PAP5, which acts as a
CC       transcriptional coactivator to trigger the thermoresponsive growth-
CC       relevant genes and promote warm-temperature-dependent PIF4 accumulation
CC       (PubMed:25944101, PubMed:30635559). Interacts with MED14
CC       (PubMed:36063057). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12826627,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12897250, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20093430,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23224238, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25944101,
