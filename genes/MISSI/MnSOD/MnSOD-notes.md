## Curation notes

- GOA contains 8 IEA annotations for MnSOD; all were reviewed rather than treating them as new annotations.
- Superoxide dismutase activity, removal of superoxide radicals, mitochondrial matrix localization, and manganese ion binding are accepted as core-supported annotations.
- Broader parent/context terms (`mitochondrion`, `superoxide metabolic process`, `response to stress`, `metal ion binding`) are retained as non-core because more specific annotations capture the core biology.
- Added the UniProt-cited transcript-level PMID:26334392 reference after PR review suggested making the Miscanthus-specific cloning evidence explicit.
