# rpl16 notes

- The GOA file is header-only, so there are no existing annotations to review.
- UniProt identifies rpl16 as a plastid 50S/uL16 ribosomal protein with `GO:0019843` rRNA binding and `GO:0003735` structural constituent of ribosome.
- The TreeGrafter mitochondrial large ribosomal subunit and mitochondrial translation calls were not propagated because the UniProt entry is for a plastid-encoded chloroplast ribosomal protein.
