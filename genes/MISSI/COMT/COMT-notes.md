## Curation notes

- GOA contains 4 IEA annotations for COMT; all were reviewed explicitly.
- Generic methyltransferase and O-methyltransferase annotations are modified to the specific caffeate O-methyltransferase activity supported by the UniProt EC/Rhea reaction.
- Protein dimerization is retained as non-core because UniProt describes COMT as a homodimer, but the core function is the caffeate methyltransferase reaction in phenylpropanoid/lignin biosynthesis.
