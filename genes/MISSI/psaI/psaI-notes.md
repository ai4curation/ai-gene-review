# psaI notes

- The GOA file is header-only, so there are no existing annotations to review.
- UniProt describes PsaI as a photosystem I thylakoid membrane subunit that may help organize PsaL.
- I used the broad supported process `GO:0015979` photosynthesis rather than photosystem I assembly, matching the approach used for other constitutive PSI subunits.
- I did not switch to an assembly term because PsaI is a constitutive PSI subunit, not a dedicated assembly factor; `GO:0010207` is photosystem II assembly and is not applicable to PsaI.
