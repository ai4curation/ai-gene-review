CC   -!- FUNCTION: Histone H3-like nucleosomal protein that is specifically
CC       found in centromeric nucleosomes. Replaces conventional H3 in the
CC       nucleosome core of centromeric chromatin that serves as an assembly
CC       site for the inner kinetochore. Required for recruitment and assembly
CC       of kinetochore proteins, mitotic progression and chromosome
CC       segregation. May serve as an epigenetic mark that propagates centromere
CC       identity through replication and cell division.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10864871}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of centromeric nucleosomes, where DNA is wrapped
CC       around a histone octamer core. The octamer contains two molecules each
CC       of H2A, H2B, cnp1/CENPA and H4 assembled in one cnp1-H4 heterotetramer
CC       and two H2A-H2B heterodimers. Interacts with the inner kinetochore
CC       (PubMed:10864871). Component of centromeric nucleosomes
CC       (PubMed:10864871). Interacts with mis6 (PubMed:10864871). Interacts
CC       with sim4 (PubMed:10864871). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10864871}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10864871}.
CC       Chromosome, centromere {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10864871}. Note=Localizes to
CC       the nonrepetitive inner centromere. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10864871}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated. Is degraded through ubiquitin-mediated proteolysis
CC       when not protected by its association to the kinetochore.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P36012}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the histone H3 family. {ECO:0000305}.
