CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the checkpoint control that prevents mitosis from
CC       occurring until S phase is completed. Plays a key role in coupling S
CC       phase to start and mitosis. Acts at the initiation of DNA replication
CC       and plays a major role in controlling the onset of S-phase. Together
CC       with orc1, involved in the maintenance of replication forks and
CC       activation of cds1-dependent S-phase checkpoint. Together with orc2,
CC       plays a role in preventing DNA rereplication and resulting genetic
CC       instability (PubMed:11486016). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10766248,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11486016, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11988741,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7916658, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8521469,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9203581}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with the origin recognition complex (ORC) and is
CC       recruited to the ars1 origin of replication. Interacts with cdt1, pop1
CC       and pop2. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10209119, ECO:0000269|PubMed:10766248,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11850415, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9203581,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9653157}.

CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated by pop1 and pop2 and targeted to the 26S proteasome
