CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as a part of the autophagy-specific VPS34 PI3-
CC       kinase complex I that plays a role in autophagosome assembly
CC       (PubMed:31941401). This complex is essential to recruit the atg8-
CC       phosphatidylinositol conjugate and the atg12-atg5 conjugate to the pre-
CC       autophagosomal structure (PubMed:31941401). By binding to atg8 at the
CC       phagophore assembly site, atg38 helps establish a positive feedback
CC       loop for recruitment of phagophore assembly proteins, including atg8
CC       (PubMed:31941401). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31941401}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the autophagy-specific vps34 PI3-kinase complex I
CC       composed of vps15, atg6, pik3/vps34, atg14 and atg38 (PubMed:31941401).
CC       Interacts (via AIM motif) with atg8; the interaction is direct and
CC       leads to recruitment of the autophagy-specific vps34 PI3-kinase complex
CC       I to the phagophore assembly site (PubMed:31941401).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31941401}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Preautophagosomal structure membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31941401}. Cytoplasm, cytosol
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31941401}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Defective in nitrogen starvation-induced
CC       autophagy (PubMed:31941401). Cells are not temperature sensitive and
CC       have normal sized vacuoles (PubMed:31941401).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31941401}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the ATG38 family. {ECO:0000305}.
