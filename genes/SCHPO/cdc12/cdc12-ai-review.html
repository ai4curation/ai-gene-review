Gene Description

Cdc12 is the single essential cytokinesis formin of fission yeast, a member of the formin homology (FH) family (BNI1/diaphanous subfamily). Through its C-terminal FH1 and FH2 domains it nucleates and processively elongates the unbranched actin filaments that form the cytokinetic actomyosin contractile ring (CAR). The proline-rich FH1 domain binds the profilin Cdc3, delivering profilin-actin to the growing filament, while the FH2 domain binds and caps the barbed end; in the absence of profilin Cdc12 caps barbed ends and nucleates filaments that grow only from pointed ends, whereas with profilin it nucleates fewer filaments that elongate rapidly from their barbed ends (profilin-gated capping). Cdc12 is recruited to the medial cell-division site early in mitosis, first to anillin (Mid1)-organized cortical nodes and then to the condensing ring, in part through direct binding of an N-terminal motif to the F-BAR domain of the scaffold Cdc15. Its activity and localization are cell-cycle regulated by phosphorylation: Cdk1 phosphorylation near the Cdc15-binding motif antagonizes recruitment, and SIN kinase Sid2 phosphorylation modulates a multimerization/F-actin-bundling domain. Cdc12 is one of three S. pombe formins, distinct from For3 (interphase actin cables/cell polarity) and Fus1 (mating), and is essential for actin ring assembly and septum formation during cell division.

cdc12 (S. pombe) review notes

UniProt: Q10059 (CDC12_SCHPO). ORF SPAC1F5.04c. 1841 aa. Formin homology family, BNI1 subfamily.
Domains: GBD/FH3 (232-620), FH1 (740-972, proline-rich, profilin-binding), FH2 (980-1391, actin nucleation/capping). PDB 8RTT/8RTY cover FH2 region (~972-1390).

Core function

Cdc12 is the single essential cytokinetic formin of fission yeast. It nucleates and processively elongates unbranched actin filaments for the cytokinetic actomyosin contractile ring (CR/CAR).

  • Required for actin ring assembly and septum formation; essential gene. cdc12-112 ts mutant has specific defect in actin ring formation and cell division PMID:9105045.
  • Member of formin family (diaphanous, BNI1, fus1) PMID:9105045.
  • Localizes to the cell division ring, not other actin structures; medial spot in interphase when overexpressed PMID:9105045.
  • FH1 binds profilin (Cdc3); proline-rich domain binds directly to profilin cdc3p in vitro PMID:9105045.

Biochemistry (Kovar/Pollard)

  • FH1 binds profilin, FH2 binds actin PMID:12796476.
  • Caps barbed ends; profilin-gated barbed-end capping protein PMID:12796476.
  • With profilin: nucleates filaments that grow rapidly from barbed ends PMID:12796476.
  • Conclusion: "formins are profilin-gated barbed end capping proteins with the ability to initiate actin filaments from actin monomers bound to profilin" PMID:12796476.

Mechanism of elongation (cryo-EM)

  • Formins processively elongate F-actin at barbed ends; common asymmetric formin conformation imposed by the filament PMID:38603491. (PDB 8RTT/8RTY are Cdc12 FH2 on F-actin barbed end.)

Localization / CC

  • Localized to cell division site; cdc12p spot moves to future division site at mitotic onset PMID:10469572.
  • Joins cytokinetic nodes (Mid1 band) with myosin-II, Rng2, Cdc15 PMID:16864655.
  • Recruited to cytokinetic precursor nodes by both modules PMID:21422229.
  • In Δmid1, Cdc12 in scattered cytokinetic nodes producing actin filaments PMID:22918943.
  • Spatiotemporal ring assembly: formin Cdc12p joins broad band independent of actin filaments PMID:14602073.
  • Nanoscale architecture: formin in membrane-proximal layer (0-80 nm) of the CR PMID:28914606.
  • Global localization screen (HDA), division site / cytoplasm [PMID:16823372 ORFeome localization study].
  • Blt1p roles in cytokinesis — Cdc12 in ring PMID:24790095.

Interactions / recruitment / regulation

  • Cdc15 (F-BAR) binds directly to Cdc12; mutual dependence for CR localization PMID:12939254.
  • Cdc12 N-terminal motif binds directly to F-BAR domain of Cdc15 PMID:25688133. -> F-bar domain binding (GO:1990808).
  • Cdk1 phosphorylates Cdc12 to inhibit the Cdc12-Cdc15 (formin-F-BAR) interaction; opposes CR formation PMID:29343550. -> F-bar domain binding (GO:1990808).
  • SIN kinase Sid2 phosphorylates Cdc12; phosphoregulation of formin multimerization / F-actin bundling activity PMID:24115772. -> supports actin filament bundle assembly (GO:0051017) and CR localization.
  • Cdc12 and For3 cooperate during CR assembly; For3 essential when Cdc12 N-truncated PMID:24127216.
  • Formins dictate which tropomyosin associates with filament; Cdc12 vs For3 PMID:24954052.
  • Sop2p modulates profilin function; interacts with Arp3 — IPI link to Cdc12 via shared assay (cdc12 binds sop2/Arp-complex pathway) [PMID:8978670 abstract only; full text assays not in cache]. Defer (PomBase curated IPI).
  • Oligomerization of Cdc15 F-BAR underlies cytokinesis function [PMID:26702831 abstract only].
  • cdc12-112 genetic screen for ring assembly mutants PMID:8834798.
