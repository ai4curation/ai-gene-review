CC   -!- FUNCTION: Co-chaperone involved in the folding of nascent eukaryotic
CC       elongation factor eEF2, an essential component of the translation
CC       machinery. Recognizes a non-native conformation in the central domain
CC       of eEF2 and recruits the chaperonin-containing T-complex (TRiC) to the
CC       C-terminal region of the nascent polypeptide, preventing unproductive
CC       interactions and promoting efficient folding of the N-terminal GTPase
CC       domain. May also assist in eEF2 maturation by recruiting the molecular
CC       chaperone Hsp90. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P48362}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Forms a ternary complex wiht eEF2 and the chaperonin-
CC       containing T-complex (TRiC); mediates the interaction of eEF2 with
CC       TRiC. Interacts with CNS1; both could collaborate to recruit the
CC       CCT/TRiC and the Hsp90 chaperone systems for eEF2 folding.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P48362}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P48362}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the HGH1 family. {ECO:0000305}.
