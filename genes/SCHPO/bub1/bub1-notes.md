# bub1 (S. pombe) review notes

UniProt O94751, PomBase SPCC1322.12c. Spindle assembly checkpoint Ser/Thr-protein kinase, BUB1 subfamily. 1044 aa. Domains: BUB1 N-terminal TPR (36-204), protein kinase (718-1044). EC 2.7.11.1 (by similarity to human BUB1 P41695).

## Core function summary
Conserved Ser/Thr kinase of the spindle assembly checkpoint (SAC). Recruited early to unattached/tensionless kinetochores via Mph1(Mps1)-phosphorylated Spc7/KNL1 MELT motifs (as Bub1-Bub3 complex). Acts as a scaffold for downstream SAC components (Mad1, Mad3, CPC) and its kinase activity phosphorylates histone H2A-S121 to recruit shugoshin (Sgo1/Sgo2) to the pericentromere/inner centromere, supporting centromeric cohesion protection and chromosome biorientation/error correction.

## Evidence
- [PMID:9864354 "We find that bub1(+) is essential for the fission yeast spindle checkpoint response to spindle damage and to defects in centromere function."] — SAC role, founding paper. Also: ["Bub1 is recruited to kinetochores during the early stages of mitosis"] and ["a pool of Bub1 remains centromere-associated at metaphase and even until telophase"]. Localizes to centromere/kinetochore; loss causes lagging chromosomes, chromosome loss, ploidy changes.
- [PMID:12606573 "the spindle checkpoint kinase Bub1p becomes phosphorylated by Cdc2p during spindle damage in mitotic cells"]; Bub1 kinase activity and Cdc2-dependent phosphorylation studied in mitotic and meiotic checkpoint; Bub1 at centromeric region; meiosis I SAC.
- [PMID:15509783] Kinetochore targeting of Mad/Bub proteins; interaction with Bub3 and Mad3; nucleus localization. Abstract-only in cache (full_text_available: false) but PomBase EXP/IDA from full text.
- [PMID:15525673] A78V in Mad3-like domain perturbs nuclear accumulation and kinetochore targeting of Bub1, Bub3, Mad3 and SAC. Nucleus localization.
- [PMID:22521786 "phosphorylation of the conserved MELT motifs in Spc7 by Mph1 (Mps1) recruits Bub1 and Bub3 to the kinetochore and that this is required to maintain the SAC signal."] Bub1-Bub3 complex; binds Spc7; chromosome segregation defects on disruption.
- [PMID:22660415 "fission yeast Mph1 (MPS1 homologue) phosphorylates the kinetochore protein Spc7 (KNL1/Blinkin homologue) at the MELT repeat sequences. This phosphorylation promotes the in vitro binding to the Bub1-Bub3 complex, which is required for kinetochore-based SAC activation (Mad1-Mad2-Mad3 localization) and chromosome alignment."] Bub1 at kinetochore (IDA). Bub1-Bub3 complex.
- [PMID:19965387 "Bub1 phosphorylates the conserved serine 121 of histone H2A in fission yeast Schizosaccharomyces pombe."] and ["Bub1 kinase creates a mark for shugoshin localization and the correct partitioning of chromosomes."] H2A-S121 kinase (IDA), centromeric core domain localization, meiotic cohesion protection. h2a-SA phenocopies bub1-KD losing centromeric shugoshin.
- [PMID:20929775 "Bub1-mediated histone 2A-serine 121 (H2A-S121) phosphorylation in targeting the CPC to the inner centromere in fission yeast and human cells"] and ["phosphorylated H2A-S121 facilitates the binding of shugoshin, the centromeric CPC adaptor"]; ["Bub1 localizes at kinetochores"]. Mitotic biorientation (IMP).
- [PMID:19680287 "Bub3, together with Bub1, has a specific function in promoting the conversion from chromosome mono-orientation to bi-orientation."] Also ["Bub3, unlike its binding partner Bub1, is not essential for the SAC in fission yeast."] Biorientation (IMP).
- [PMID:28497540 "Shugoshin, which is localized to centromeres depending on the phosphorylation of histone H2A by Bub1 kinase, plays a central role in protecting meiotic cohesin Rec8 from separase cleavage."] and ["meiotic Bub1 persists at kinetochores until anaphase I"]; Moa1(meikin)-Plo1 specifies Bub1 distribution. Kinetochore localization (IDA), meiotic cohesion protection (IMP).
- [PMID:29769606 "the chromosomal localization of shugoshin proteins, which depends on Bub1-catalyzed phosphorylation of H2A at serine 121 (H2A-S121), was significantly reduced in the H2A-K119D and the H2A-K119E mutants"] and ["H2A-K119 malonylation inhibited the interaction between Bub1 and H2A, leading to a decrease in Bub1-dependent H2A-S121 phosphorylation."] Ser/Thr kinase activity (IDA).
- [PMID:31257143 "the Bub1 TPR domain is both necessary and sufficient to bind and recruit Mad3"] — direct Bub1-Mad3 (SPBC3D6.04c = mad3) interaction; also Bub3 (SPCC1795.01c). Protein binding (IPI).
- [PMID:22825872 "a three-layered hierarchy with Ark1 and Mph1 on top, Bub1 and Bub3 in the middle, and Mad3 as well as the Mad1-Mad2 complex at the lower end of the hierarchy"]; SAC signaling (IMP).
- [PMID:24477934 "mutations in Bub1 and within the Mad1 C-terminal domain impair the kinetochore localization of Mad1:Mad2 and abrogate checkpoint activity"]; SAC signaling (IMP). Bub1 needed to recruit Mad1-Mad2.
- [PMID:16179942 "Deletion of Mal3 results in early mitotic delay, which is dependent on the Bub1, but not the Mad2, spindle checkpoint"]; Bub1 at kinetochore (IDA) and old/mitotic SPB (IDA); SAC (IMP). Bub1/Bub3/Mad3/Mph1-dependent checkpoint prevents monopolar attachment.
- [PMID:16394105 "atb2-983 ... is synthetically lethal with deletions in a subset of spindle checkpoint genes including bub1, bub3, and mph1 ... Bub1, but not Mad2, remains for a prolonged period in the kinetochores"]; Bub1 at mitotic SPB (IDA).
- [PMID:12426374 "In klp5 mutants, spindle checkpoint proteins Mad2 and Bub1 are recruited to mitotic kinetochores for a prolonged duration"]; ["there are kinetochores to which only Bub1, but not Mad2, localizes. These kinetochores are likely to have been captured, yet lack tension."] Bub1 at kinetochore (IDA).
- [PMID:11882285 "Bub1, a checkpoint kinase, localizes briefly at early mitotic kinetochores in wild-type ... Bub1 is bound to the central centromere region of mitotic cells."] Kinetochore (IDA).
- [PMID:11331883 "Fission yeast Bub1 is essential in setting up the meiotic pattern of chromosome segregation."] Meiosis I SAC and meiotic centromeric cohesion protection (IMP). Abstract-only in cache.
- [PMID:36537249 "Mhf1-Mhf2 ... plays crucial roles in promoting the SAC"]; used as evidence for Bub1 kinetochore localization (EXP).

## Annotation notes / judgments
- GO:0006338 chromatin remodeling (IEA, GO_REF:0000108, inter-ontology link from GO:0072371 histone H2AS121 kinase activity). This is an over-broad inferred BP. H2A-S121 phosphorylation is a signaling mark for shugoshin recruitment, not chromatin remodeling per se. MARK_AS_OVER_ANNOTATED / REMOVE candidate (logical-link artifact).
- GO:0005515 protein binding (IPI) — bare protein binding, uninformative; partners are mad3 and bub3. MODIFY toward informative or KEEP_AS_NON_CORE; per guidelines avoid bare protein binding.
- GO:0042802 identical protein binding (self-IPI) — Bub1 self-interaction listed in IntAct; minor, KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- GO:0044732 mitotic spindle pole body / GO:0071957 old mitotic spindle pole body — Bub1 detected at SPB in some studies; non-core relative to kinetochore. KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- GO:0051754 meiotic sister chromatid cohesion, centromeric (IBA) — consistent with meiotic cohesion protection role; KEEP_AS_NON_CORE / ACCEPT (non-core for a mitotic checkpoint kinase but real).
