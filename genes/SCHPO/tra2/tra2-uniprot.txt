CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the NuA4 histone H4/H2A acetyltransferase
CC       involved in transcription and DNA repair. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19040720,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21642955}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the NuA4 acetyltransferase complex. Tra1 is the
CC       scaffold subunit for binding to a variety of transcription activators
CC       or transcription factors to recruit NuA4 for targeted gene activation
CC       (PubMed:19040720, PubMed:21642955). Requires Hsp90 and its co-
CC       chaperone, the Triple-T complex (TTT), for its incorporation into NuA4
CC       (PubMed:31748520). Interacts with tel2 (PubMed:18076573).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18076573, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19040720,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21642955, ECO:0000269|PubMed:31748520}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Although strongly related to the PI3/PI4-kinase family,
CC       it lacks the typical motifs that constitute the catalytic site of
CC       PI3/PI4-kinase proteins, suggesting that it may lack such activity.
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:21642955}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the PI3/PI4-kinase family. TRA1 subfamily.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
