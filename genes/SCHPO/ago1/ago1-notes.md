# ago1 (Argonaute) — S. pombe — review notes

UniProt: O74957 (AGO1_SCHPO), PomBase SPCC736.11. 834 aa. Synonym csp9.
Domains: PAZ (210-326), Piwi/PIWI (500-799, RNase H-like). Catalytic DDH triad:
Asp580, Asp651, His788 (mutagenesis defined). Single Argonaute of fission yeast.

## Core biology

Ago1 is the sole Argonaute and the catalytic core ("slicer") of the S. pombe RNAi
machinery. It binds single-stranded siRNAs (~22 nt) generated by Dicer (Dcr1) / Rdp1,
and uses them as guides to base-pair with nascent centromeric (and other) transcripts.

RITS complex = Ago1 + Chp1 (chromodomain) + Tas3 + centromeric siRNAs. RITS is the
RNA-induced transcriptional silencing effector that targets heterochromatin.
[PMID:14704433 "The RITS complex contains Ago1 (the fission yeast Argonaute homolog), Chp1 (a heterochromatin-associated chromodomain protein), and Tas3 (a novel protein). In addition, the complex contains small RNAs that require the Dicer ribonuclease for their production."]

ARC complex = Ago1 + Arb1 + Arb2; loads siRNA, contains mostly dsRNA.
[PMID:17310250 "we identify a second fission yeast Argonaute complex (Argonaute siRNA chaperone, ARC), which contains, in addition to Ago1, two previously uncharacterized proteins, Arb1 and Arb2, both of which are required for histone H3 Lys9 (H3-K9) methylation, heterochromatin assembly and siRNA generation. Furthermore, whereas siRNAs in the RITS complex are mostly single-stranded, siRNAs associated with ARC are mostly double-stranded"]

## Molecular function: slicer / endoribonuclease

[PMID:16931764 "Argonaute requires the conserved aspartate-aspartate-histidine motif for heterochromatic silencing and for ribonuclease H-like cleavage (slicing) of target messages complementary to siRNA"] — D580A, D651A, H788A mutants up-regulate dg/dh centromeric transcripts (UniProt FT MUTAGEN).

[PMID:17310250 "purified Arb1 inhibits the slicer activity of Ago1 in vitro, and purified catalytically inactive Ago1 contains only double-stranded siRNA. Finally, we show that slicer activity is required for the siRNA-dependent association of Ago1 with chromatin and for the spreading of histone H3-K9 methylation."]

Slicing converts double-stranded siRNA to single-stranded (passenger strand cleavage). GO:0070551 (endoribonuclease activity, cleaving siRNA-paired mRNA), GO:0016891.

## siRNA / ssRNA binding

GO:0035197 siRNA binding, GO:0003727 ssRNA binding — IDA from multiple papers (24095277, 17512405, 28541282). Ago1 binds 22-nt siRNAs; F276A reduces siRNA association.
[PMID:17310250 (FT) F276 "Decreased association with siRNAs."]

## priRNA / siRNA 3'-end processing & adaptor activity (Triman)

[PMID:24095277 "Argonaute, loaded with longer RNA precursors, recruits Triman to generate mature priRNAs and siRNAs. We show that priRNA and siRNA trimming is required for de novo assembly of heterochromatin at centromeric repeats and the mat locus"] — basis for GO:1990431 priRNA 3'-end processing, GO:1990432 siRNA 3'-end processing, and GO:0030674 protein-macromolecule adaptor activity (Ago1 recruits Triman to its bound RNA). Also GO:0016891 / ssRNA binding annotations.

## BP: RNAi-directed heterochromatin formation

[PMID:12193640 "we deleted the argonaute, dicer, and RNA-dependent RNA polymerase gene homologs... Deletion results in the aberrant accumulation of complementary transcripts from centromeric heterochromatic repeats... loss of histone H3 lysine-9 methylation, and impairment of centromere function."] — GO:0140727 siRNA-mediated pericentric heterochromatin formation.

[PMID:15475954 "RITS components, including Argonaute protein, bind to all known heterochromatic loci... RNA interference machinery operates in cis as a stable component of heterochromatic domains with RITS tethered to silenced loci by methylation of histone H3 at Lys9."] — IDA for is_active_in pericentric (GO:0005721), mating-type region (GO:0031934), subtelomeric (GO:0140720) heterochromatin and silent mating-type cassette heterochromatin formation (GO:0030466).

[PMID:15615848 "factors involved in the RNA interference (RNAi) pathway including Argonaute, Dicer, and RNA-dependent RNA polymerase are required for heterochromatin assembly at centromeric repeats and the silent mating-type region... self-enforcing RNAi loop"] — GO:0140727.

[PMID:22895252 "In fission yeast, siRNA is generated from pericentromeric noncoding RNA by the RNAi machinery. siRNA synthesis and heterochromatin formation are interdependent, forming a self-reinforcing loop on chromatin."] — Dsh1 amplifies siRNA; supports GO:0140727 (ago1 IMP).

[PMID:17531816 "The establishment and maintenance of centromeric heterochromatin in fission yeast require the RITS complex. Comprised of centromeric siRNAs, the chromodomain protein Chp1, Argonaute (Ago1), and Tas3, RITS couples the cellular RNAi pathway with assembly of constitutive heterochromatin."] — RITS complex (GO:0030958) EXP.

## Co-transcriptional gene silencing (CTGS)

[PMID:22431512 "RNAi is crucial to establish and maintain centromeric heterochromatin and functions to repress genome activity by a chromatin silencing mechanism referred to as cotranscriptional gene silencing (CTGS)... RNAi-mediated CTGS represses stress-inducible genes by degrading mRNAs under noninduced conditions."] — GO:0033562. Also a euchromatic/Atf1-gene role at nuclear pores.

[PMID:17512405 "transgene transcripts at centromeric repeats are processed into siRNAs and are therefore direct targets of RNAi"] — GO:0033562 (IMP) and siRNA binding (IDA).

## Mating-type and subtelomeric heterochromatin

[PMID:15218150 "At the silent mating-type interval of fission yeast, the RNA interference (RNAi) machinery cooperates with cenH... to initiate heterochromatin formation. However, in RNAi mutants, heterochromatin assembly can still occur at low efficiency."] — GO:0030466 (mating-type cassette) IMP. Note: RNAi is one of two redundant pathways at mat (ATF/CREB Atf1/Pcr1 parallel) — supports KEEP rather than sole.

[PMID:28404620 "heterochromatin is lost at transcribed regions in the absence of RNA degradation... heterochromatic RNAs are retained on chromatin, form DNA:RNA hybrids, and need to be degraded by the Ccr4-Not complex or RNAi to maintain heterochromatic silencing."] — subtelomeric heterochromatin formation GO:0031509 EXP.

## Heterochromatin islands (facultative)

[PMID:22144463 "we describe facultative heterochromatin islands in fission yeast and show that their formation at meiotic genes requires factors that eliminate meiotic messenger RNAs (mRNAs) during vegetative growth."] — is_active_in heterochromatin island GO:1990342 IDA.

## Recruitment of CLRC (Clr4) — Stc1 link

[PMID:20211136 "siRNAs that are incorporated into the Argonaute-containing RITS complex and direct it to nascent homologous transcripts. This leads to recruitment of the CLRC complex, including the histone methyltransferase Clr4... Stc1, that is required for centromeric heterochromatin integrity... interacts both with the RNAi effector Ago1, and with the chromatin-modifying CLRC complex."] — supports adaptor role and Ago1-Stc1 protein interaction.

## RITS assembly checkpoint & GW proteins

[PMID:25730778 "loading of small RNAs onto Argonaute is a checkpoint for Argonaute's association with conserved GW-protein components of silencing complexes... the Argonaute small interfering RNA chaperone (ARC) complex mediates loading of small RNAs onto Ago1... deletion of its subunits, or mutations in Ago1 that prevent small-RNA loading, abolish the assembly of the GW protein-containing RITS complex."] — Ago1-Tas3 interaction; ARC loads siRNA before RITS assembly.

[PMID:17891150 "a repetitive motif within Tas3, termed the 'Argonaute hook'... binds Ago proteins through their PIWI domains in vitro and in vivo... a PIWI domain pocket that binds the 5' end of the short interfering RNA guide strand is required for direct binding of the Ago hook."] — Ago1-Tas3 interaction via PIWI domain.

[PMID:26443059 "The RNA-induced transcriptional silencing complex (RITS), composed of Ago1, Tas3 and Chp1... Sgf73 is physically associated with Ago1 and Chp1. The interactions among the subunits of the RITS, including those between Tas3 and Chp1, between Chp1 and Ago1, between Ago1 and Tas3, were all impaired by the deletion of sgf73(+)."] — Ago1-Sgf73 (SPCC126.04c) interaction; RITS assembly.

## Cell-cycle / quiescence roles

[PMID:27738016 "RNAi mutants lose viability at G0 entry and are unable to maintain long-term quiescence... ago1Δ cells had comparable, though less severe viability defects... RNAi both promotes heterochromatin formation at centromeres allowing proper chromosome segregation during G0-entry, and prevents heterochromatin formation at the rDNA locus during quiescence maintenance"] — GO:0070317 negative regulation of G0 to G1 transition (IMP). This is a downstream/indirect consequence of the RNAi heterochromatin role; KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.

UniProt FUNCTION: "Required for G1 arrest and mating in response to nitrogen starvation. Ago1 regulation of cytokinesis and cell cycle checkpoints occurs downstream of dcr1." Cell-cycle effects are indirect/pleiotropic.

## Localization

Nucleus (heterochromatin: centromere, mat, telomere) + cytoplasm/cytosol. Cytosol HDA (PMID:16823372). Slicing & siRNA loading partly cytoplasmic; chromatin-targeting nuclear.

## Tailing / turnover of Ago1-bound sRNAs

[PMID:28541282 "Cid14... and the uridyl-transferase Cid16 add non-templated nucleotides to Argonaute-bound small RNAs in fission yeast. The tailing of Argonaute-bound small RNAs recruits the 3'-5' exonuclease Rrp6 to degrade small RNAs."] — siRNA binding IDA; surveillance of Ago1-bound sRNAs.

## Annotation decisions summary

- siRNA binding, ssRNA binding, slicer/endoribonuclease (GO:0070551, GO:0016891): CORE MF — ACCEPT.
- nucleic acid binding (GO:0003676), RNA binding (GO:0003723): IEA general; MARK_AS_OVER_ANNOTATED / parent-of-specific (keep but non-core; redundant with siRNA binding). Use KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- protein binding (GO:0005515) IPI: uninformative bare term — flag. Several capture real RITS/ARC subunit interactions (Tas3, Arb1, Arb2, Sgf73). MARK_AS_OVER_ANNOTATED (replace with complex CC / adaptor MF).
- RITS complex (GO:0030958), ARC complex (GO:0033167): CORE CC — ACCEPT.
- heterochromatin (GO:0000792) NAS: too general vs pericentric/mat/subtelomeric — KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- siRNA-mediated pericentric heterochromatin formation (GO:0140727): CORE BP — ACCEPT.
- regulatory ncRNA-mediated heterochromatin formation (GO:0031048): parent — KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- co-transcriptional gene silencing (GO:0033562): CORE BP — ACCEPT.
- silent mating-type cassette (GO:0030466), subtelomeric (GO:0031509): real but redundant pathway; KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- priRNA/siRNA 3'-end processing (GO:1990431/1990432), adaptor (GO:0030674): ACCEPT (Triman recruitment).
- negative regulation of G0 to G1 transition (GO:0070317): downstream/indirect — KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- heterochromatin island (GO:1990342): facultative; KEEP_AS_NON_CORE.
- CC centromeric region/telomeric region (IEA SubCell), nucleus, cytoplasm, cytosol: ACCEPT/KEEP_AS_NON_CORE (localization accurate; nucleus is where chromatin function happens).
