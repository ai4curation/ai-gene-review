CC   -!- FUNCTION: Serine/threonine-protein kinase that performs several
CC       important functions throughout M phase of the cell cycle
CC       (PubMed:12615979, PubMed:12815070, PubMed:22375062, PubMed:25533956,
CC       PubMed:7744248). During prometaphase and metaphase, phosphorylates the
CC       kinetochore component dam1 of unaligned or unattached chromosomes to
CC       promote mitotic sister chromatid biorientation (PubMed:22375062).
CC       During meiosis I and together with moa1, required both for mono-
CC       orientation of kinetochores on sister chromosomes and protection of
CC       centromeric cohesin from separase-mediated cleavage (PubMed:25533956).
CC       Behaves as a 'septum-promoting factor', and could also be involved in
CC       inducing other late events of cell division (PubMed:12615979,
CC       PubMed:12815070, PubMed:7744248). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12615979,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12815070, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22375062,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:25533956, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7744248}.
