CC       complex that acts as a key regulator of chromosome segregation and
CC       cytokinesis (PubMed:11950927, PubMed:12676091). Has a role in error-
CC       correction of aberrent kinetochore-microtubule attachments to ensure
CC       that sister kinetochores become bioriented and connect to opposite
CC       poles by promoting spindle assembly checkpoint signaling
CC       (PubMed:11792803, PubMed:11950927, PubMed:12676091). Ark1 is also
CC       required for phosphorylation of histone H3 that accompanies chromosome
CC       condensation and condensin recruitment to mitotic chromatin
CC       (PubMed:11792803, PubMed:12676091). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11792803,
