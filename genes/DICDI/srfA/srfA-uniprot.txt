CC   -!- INDUCTION: Induced by the protein kinase A (PKA) which is activated

CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Cells have an abnormal spore morphology and show

CC       formation of mature actin rods. The spore outer layer gets wavier as
CC       the spore ages and suffers a progressive degradation.
