Gene Description

DorR (also known as Regulatory protein VirG) is a transcriptional regulator that controls photosynthetic gene expression in response to oxygen and light conditions. It acts as a redox-sensitive repressor of photosynthetic genes under aerobic conditions and integrates signals through interactions with the anti-repressor AppA. DorR contains response regulator and DNA-binding domains typical of two-component regulatory systems.
