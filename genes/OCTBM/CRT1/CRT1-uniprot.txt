CC   -!- FUNCTION: Chemotactile receptors that mediate contact-dependent
CC       chemosensation in cephalopods, allowing them to respond to stimuli in
CC       the marine environment (PubMed:33125889, PubMed:37045917,
CC       PubMed:37045920, PubMed:40532695). Enriched in the suction cups on the
CC       arms, they elicit robust autonomous movement and modify predatory and
CC       maternal behavior upon activation (PubMed:37045920, PubMed:40532695).
CC       Permeable to cations including sodium, potassium and calcium ions
CC       (PubMed:33125889, PubMed:37045920, PubMed:40532695). Binds a wide range
CC       of hydrophobic agonists, but the extent of receptor activation is
CC       modulated by the electronegativity of the ligands (PubMed:37045920,
CC       PubMed:40532695). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:33125889,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:37045917, ECO:0000269|PubMed:37045920,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:40532695}.
