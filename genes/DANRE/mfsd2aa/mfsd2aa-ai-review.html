Gene Description

mfsd2aa encodes a sodium-dependent lysophospholipid/lysophosphatidylcholine symporter at endothelial plasma membrane and ER membrane, supporting lipid transport across the blood-brain barrier. Free fatty-acid and carbohydrate transport annotations are treated cautiously because the supported substrate is lysophospholipid/LPC, not free fatty acid or carbohydrate.
