# Notes for DANRE adss1

- Core function is cytoplasmic adenylosuccinate synthase activity in AMP biosynthesis from IMP [file:DANRE/adss1/adss1-uniprot.txt "Catalyzes the first committed step in the biosynthesis of AMP from IMP."].
- Muscle-development/homeostasis terms are kept as non-core phenotype annotations because zebrafish knockdown disrupts muscle fibers and human ADSSL1 mutations cause myopathy [PMID:26506222 "wild-type ADSSL1 protein reversed the muscle defect"].
