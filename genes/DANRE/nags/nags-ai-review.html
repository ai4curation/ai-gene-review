Gene Description

nags encodes mitochondrial N-acetylglutamate synthase, which produces N-acetylglutamate from L-glutamate and acetyl-CoA. In vertebrates, N-acetylglutamate is the essential allosteric activator of carbamoyl phosphate synthetase I, linking NAGS activity to urea-cycle/ureagenesis regulation; the enzyme is allosterically regulated by L-arginine.
