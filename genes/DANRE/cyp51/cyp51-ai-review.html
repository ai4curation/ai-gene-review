Gene Description

cyp51 encodes sterol 14-alpha-demethylase, an endoplasmic-reticulum membrane cytochrome P450 monooxygenase in cholesterol/zymosterol biosynthesis. Broad monooxygenase/oxidoreductase and heme/iron-binding annotations are less informative than the core sterol 14-demethylase activity, while response-to-yeast evidence is treated as a non-core infection-response observation.

Core Functions

cyp51 enables sterol 14-demethylase activity at the endoplasmic reticulum membrane, catalyzing oxidative demethylation steps in cholesterol biosynthesis via 24,25-dihydrolanosterol/lanosterol-derived sterols.
