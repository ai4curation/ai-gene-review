Gene Description

tdp2 encodes tyrosyl-DNA phosphodiesterase 2, a metal-dependent DNA repair enzyme that hydrolyzes 5-prime phosphotyrosyl-DNA adducts, including topoisomerase II-linked DNA breaks. The core function is nuclear/PML-body tyrosyl-DNA phosphodiesterase activity in double-strand break repair; Smad/Nodal, gastrulation, and left-right phenotypes are retained as non-core developmental signaling roles.
