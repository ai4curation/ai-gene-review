CC   -!- FUNCTION: Choline uniporter that specifically mediates choline uptake
CC       at the blood-brain-barrier (PubMed:38302740). Responsible for the
CC       majority of choline uptake across the blood-brain-barrier from the
CC       circulation into the brain (By similarity). Choline, a nutrient
CC       critical for brain development, is a precursor of phosphatidylcholine,
CC       as well as betaine (By similarity). Also mediates transport of
CC       ethanolamine (By similarity). Choline and ethanolamine transport is not
CC       coupled with proton transport and is exclusively driven by the choline
CC       gradient across the plasma membrane (By similarity). Also acts as a
CC       heme b transporter (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q91X85,
