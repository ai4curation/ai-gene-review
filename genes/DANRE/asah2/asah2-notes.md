# asah2 (Danio rerio) review notes


## Batch 05 curation notes
- Core interpretation: asah2 encodes neutral ceramidase, a membrane-associated zinc-dependent N-acylsphingosine amidohydrolase that hydrolyzes ceramides to sphingosine and fatty acids at neutral pH. The core function is ceramide catabolism/sphingosine production at plasma/apical and related membrane compartments.
- Key UniProt support: hydrolyzes sphingolipid ceramides into sphingosine and free fatty acids at neutral pH.
- Existing GOA annotations were reviewed against this core role; broad, inferred, or downstream phenotype annotations were kept as non-core unless they overstate the direct function.
