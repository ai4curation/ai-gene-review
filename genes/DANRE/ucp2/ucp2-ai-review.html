Gene Description

ucp2 encodes a mitochondrial inner-membrane carrier related to uncoupling proteins. The zebrafish protein is best reviewed as a mitochondrial transmembrane transporter/proton leak protein, with cold response, thermogenesis, and fatty-acid transport annotations treated as supported but peripheral or overly broad relative to the core mitochondrial carrier role.

Core Functions

ucp2 enables oxidative phosphorylation uncoupler activity at the mitochondrial inner membrane, creating proton leak/regulated proton entry while also functioning as a mitochondrial metabolite carrier.
