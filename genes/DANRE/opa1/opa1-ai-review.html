Gene Description

opa1 encodes a mitochondrial dynamin-like GTPase that mediates mitochondrial inner-membrane fusion, cristae morphology, and mitochondrial organization. The core function is GTPase-dependent membrane remodeling/fusion at the mitochondrial inner membrane and intermembrane space; cardiac and embryonic phenotypes are downstream developmental consequences.
