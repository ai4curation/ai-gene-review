| Mechanism / key residues | Structural and mutational evidence supports a cofactor-free decarboxylation mechanism. His67 is proposed to donate the proton that determines stereoselective product formation; Glu87 likely destabilizes the substrate carboxylate and promotes CO2 release; Arg161 is also required for activity. Mutants H67N, E87Q, and R161Q failed to accelerate OHCU decay. (pqac-00000002, pqac-00000003, pqac-00000004) | Key residues: His67, Glu87, Arg161; no metal ions or cofactors required. | Cendron et al., 2007, *Journal of Biological Chemistry* — https://doi.org/10.1074/jbc.m701297200 |
