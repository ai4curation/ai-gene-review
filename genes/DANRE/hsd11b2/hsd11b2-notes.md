# Notes for DANRE hsd11b2

- Core function is NAD(+)-dependent cortisol/11-beta-hydroxysteroid oxidation to inactive 11-keto forms, not a generic steroid dehydrogenase assignment [PMID:23042946 "Stressor exposure increased the conversion of (3)H-cortisol to (3)H-cortisone"].
- Stress response annotations are retained as downstream physiology because hsd11b2 controls cortisol catabolism during stress-axis recovery [PMID:23349977 "11β-HSD type 2 and 20β-HSD type 2 are involved in cortisol catabolism and stress response"].
- NADP(+)-dependent 11-beta-HSD and 7-beta-HSD assignments are not supported for zebrafish Hsd11b2; UniProt and experimental papers support NAD(+)-dependent cortisol to cortisone conversion.
