Gene Description

Sesquiterpene synthase that catalyzes the conversion of farnesyl diphosphate to multiple sesquiterpene products, primarily corvol ether B and corvol ether A. This bacterial-origin terpene synthase was acquired through horizontal gene transfer and produces sesquiterpenes important for fungi-environment interactions. The enzyme requires Mg2+ as a cofactor and contains a conserved DDXXXD motif essential for catalytic activity.
