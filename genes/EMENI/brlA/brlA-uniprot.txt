CC   -!- FUNCTION: BrlA, abaA and wetA are pivotal regulators of conidiophore
CC       development and conidium maturation (PubMed:2196567, PubMed:2655931,
CC       PubMed:2823119, PubMed:5366214, PubMed:7704830, PubMed:8508769). They
CC       act individually and together to regulate their own expression and that
CC       of numerous other sporulation-specific genes (PubMed:2108321,
CC       PubMed:2655931, PubMed:2823119). Binds promoters of target genes at
CC       brlA response elements (BREs) containing the conserved sequence 5'-
CC       (C/A)(A/G)AGGG(G/A)-3' (PubMed:8417986). Controls the expression of the
CC       conidiophore-specific phenol oxidase ivoB (PubMed:1901560). Controls
CC       the expression of the hydrophobin rodA (PubMed:2065971). Mediates the
CC       developmental switch from the indeterminate, apical growth pattern of
CC       vegetative cells to the budding growth pattern of conidiophores
CC       (PubMed:3293800). Expression of brlA leads to activation of abaA, wetA
CC       and stuA, cessation of vegetative growth, cellular vacuolization and
CC       spore formation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1901560,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2065971, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2108321,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2196567, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2655931,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2823119, ECO:0000269|PubMed:3293800,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:5366214, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7704830,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8417986, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8508769}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Expression is induced during conidiation, after conidiophore
CC       stalks had formed and had begun to vesiculate (PubMed:2823119).
CC       Positively regulated by abaA (PubMed:2655931). Negatively regulated by
CC       velC (PubMed:24587098). Expression is also controlled by acoD
CC       (PubMed:1508186). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1508186,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24587098, ECO:0000269|PubMed:2655931,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2823119}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Both zinc fingers are required to induce development
CC       (PubMed:2108321). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:2108321}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Prevents the formation of normal conidiophores
CC       (PubMed:2823119). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:2823119}.
