# yA (Aspergillus nidulans) — curation notes
UniProt P17489 (LAC1_EMENI / yA). Conidial laccase (p-diphenol oxidase); structural-output tier.
- Copper laccase converting the wA-derived precursor to the mature green pigment; yellow (yA) mutants lack laccase. [PMID:4624671 "Absence of laccase from yellow-spored mutants of Aspergillus nidulans"].
- Core MF GO:0052716 hydroquinone:oxygen oxidoreductase activity (IDA). Acts downstream of the wA PKS.
