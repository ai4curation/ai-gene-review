# sfgA (Aspergillus nidulans) — curation notes

UniProt Q3I5F3 (SfgA). Zn(II)2Cys6 (zinc-cluster) transcription factor; negative
regulator of conidiation. Repressive input of the upstream-activation tier.

- Zn(II)2Cys6 DNA-binding domain (UniProt: "Zn(2)-C6 fungal-type DNA-binding domain").
- Suppressor of fluG: acts downstream of FluG and upstream of the Flb activators to
  repress conidiation until FluG signalling relieves the repression (literature role;
  NOT yet captured in GOA — all current GOA annotations are electronic/generic TF terms).
- Core MF GO:0003700 (from the zinc-cluster domain). GO:0006351 DNA-templated
  transcription kept non-core (TF regulates rather than catalyzes transcription).
