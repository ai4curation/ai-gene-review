# wA (Aspergillus nidulans) — curation notes
UniProt Q03149 (PKSP_EMENI / wA). Conidial-pigment polyketide synthase; structural-output tier.
- PKS/FAS-homologous; required for green conidial pigment; wA mutants are white. [PMID:1465094 "The developmentally regulated Aspergillus nidulans wA gene encodes a polypeptide homologous to polyketide and fatty acid synthases"; "wA mutants produce colorless (white) conidia"].
- Core MF GO:0016218 polyketide synthase activity (proposed; not in GOA).
- **Cross-attribution → REMOVE:** GO:0052716 hydroquinone:oxygen oxidoreductase (laccase) activity on wA cites PMID:7050088. That paper's own abstract refutes the link — laccase activity is "absent in a strain carrying a mutation in the structural gene for laccase" (yA), and "wA strains lack the precursor of the wild-type green pigment, i.e., the laccase substrate" (wA supplies the substrate, upstream of the laccase). Combined with wA being a PKS (PMID:1465094), this is a genuinely-contradicted molecular function → **REMOVE** (not merely over-annotated); reference_review WRONG_IDENTIFIER. Also GO:0006633 fatty acid biosynthesis over-annotated (FAS-homology propagation).
