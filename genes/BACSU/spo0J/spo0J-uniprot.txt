CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required for the initiation of sporulation and for normal
CC       chromosome segregation (PubMed:8071208, PubMed:18854156). Antagonizes
CC       sporulation inhibition by Soj (PubMed:8071208, PubMed:18854156). It
CC       probably interacts with a specific DNA site and other proteins involved
CC       in partitioning and cell division, and antagonizes Soj in response to
CC       cell cycle events related to chromosome partitioning.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18854156, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8071208}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm, nucleoid
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18854156}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Increased number of chromosome origins, probably
CC       due to early DNA replication initiation (PubMed:18854156). In a double
CC       soj-spo0J deletion DnaA transiently arrests for longer at its
CC       chromosomal binding sites, not just at oriC (PubMed:28166228).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18854156, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28166228}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the ParB family. {ECO:0000305}.
