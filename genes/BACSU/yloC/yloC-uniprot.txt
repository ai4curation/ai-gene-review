CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endonucleolytically cleaves ssRNA generating a 3'-OH and
CC       (presumably) a 5'-phosphate group. Cleaves preferentially at U
CC       residues, has no activity on dsRNA or dsDNA. Upon expression in E.coli
CC       contributes to the degradation of small RNA (sRNA) RhyB.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:34815358}.

CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Oligomerizes, perhaps as a hexamer (PubMed:34815358). Might

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:34815358, ECO:0000305|PubMed:34815358}.
