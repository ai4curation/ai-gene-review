CC   -!- FUNCTION: Specifier protein that contributes to constitutive and
CC       herbivore-induced simple nitrile formation (By similarity). Catalyzes
CC       allylthiocyanate and corresponding epithionitrile formation from
CC       allylglucosinolate in the presence of myrosinase (PubMed:21783213,
CC       PubMed:23999604, PubMed:26260516, PubMed:30900313). Also converts
CC       aliphatic glucosinolates, such as indol-3-ylmethylglucosinolate, 4-
CC       methylsulfinylbutylglucosinolate, 4-methylthiobutyl- and
CC       benzylisothiocyanate, to simple nitriles (PubMed:21783213).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q93XW5, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21783213,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23999604, ECO:0000269|PubMed:26260516,
