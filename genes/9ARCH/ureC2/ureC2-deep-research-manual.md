# Manual Deep Research: ureC2

Automated Falcon and OpenScientist runs did not complete for ureC2, so this is a manual synthesis from the fetched UniProt record and the completed PTHR43440 family research.

## Evidence synthesis

ureC2 is annotated as urease with EC 3.5.1.5 [file:9ARCH/ureC2/ureC2-uniprot.txt "RecName: Full=Urease; EC=3.5.1.5"]. UniProt gives the urease reaction as urea plus water yielding hydrogencarbonate and ammonium, and maps the protein to urea degradation, step 1 of 1 [file:9ARCH/ureC2/ureC2-uniprot.txt "Reaction=urea + 2 H2O + H(+) = hydrogencarbonate + 2 NH4(+)"; "PATHWAY: Nitrogen metabolism; urea degradation; CO(2) and NH(3) from urea (urease route): step 1/1"].

The PTHR43440 report supports urease alpha/large subunits as direct catalytic subunits for urea hydrolysis, but also documents exceptional iron ureases. Therefore the nickel annotation is acceptable for ureC2 because the UniProt record itself states two nickel ions per subunit [file:9ARCH/ureC2/ureC2-uniprot.txt "Binds 2 nickel ions per subunit"], not merely because the protein is in the urease family.

## Curation consequence

Accept urease activity, nickel binding, urea metabolic process, and UniPathway urea catabolic process for ureC2. Keep broad hydrolase parents as non-core.
