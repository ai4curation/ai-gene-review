CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-independent anion exchanger mediating bicarbonate,
CC       chloride, sulfate and oxalate transport. Exhibits sodium-independent
CC       sulfate anion transporter activity that may cooperate with SLC26A2 to
CC       mediate DIDS-sensitive sulfate uptake into high endothelial venules
CC       endothelial cells (HEVEC). In the kidney, mediates chloride-bicarbonate
CC       exchange, facilitating V-ATPase-mediated acid secretion. May function
CC       as a chloride channel, playing an important role in moderating chloride
CC       homeostasis and neuronal activity in the cerebellum.
