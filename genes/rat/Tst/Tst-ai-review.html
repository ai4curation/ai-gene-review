Gene Description

Tst encodes thiosulfate sulfurtransferase/rhodanese. It catalyzes sulfur transfer to cyanide, contributes to sulfur/iron-sulfur metabolism, and together with MRPL18 supports mitochondrial import of cytosolic 5S rRNA. The review accepts sulfurtransferase, cyanide detoxification, 5S rRNA binding/import, rRNA transport, and iron-sulfur cluster assembly annotations, while keeping mitochondrial localization as context and marking epithelial differentiation as over-annotation.
