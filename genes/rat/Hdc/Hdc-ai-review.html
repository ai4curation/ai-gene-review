Gene Description

Hdc encodes histidine decarboxylase, the enzyme that converts L-histidine to histamine. The review accepts histidine decarboxylase activity, histamine biosynthesis, and L-histidine catabolism as direct functions; generic lyase/amino-acid metabolism terms are modified to the specific decarboxylase role, while cofactor binding and neuronal/localization terms are non-core context.
