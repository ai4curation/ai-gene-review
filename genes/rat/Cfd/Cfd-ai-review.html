Gene Description

Cfd encodes complement factor D/adipsin, a secreted serine protease that cleaves factor B in the C3b-factor B complex to initiate the alternative complement pathway. The review accepts serine endopeptidase and alternative complement pathway annotations, keeps extracellular/maturation context as non-core, and removes developmental or Notch-response annotations that reflect expression changes rather than Cfd function.
