| Core function | Pathway role: bile-acid synthesis | AMACR contributes to side-chain processing of C27 bile-acid intermediates; depletion/deficiency raises bile-acid precursors and lowers mature bile acids, consistent with a role in bile-acid biosynthesis and peroxisomal chain shortening. | Kong et al., 2020, https://doi.org/10.3389/fmolb.2020.00153 | (pqac-00000003, pqac-00000020, pqac-00000027) |
