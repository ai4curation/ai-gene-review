Gene Description

Amacr encodes alpha-methylacyl-CoA racemase, a peroxisomal and mitochondrial enzyme that interconverts stereoisomers of alpha-methyl-branched acyl-CoA esters, including pristanoyl-CoA and bile-acid intermediates. The review accepts the racemase activity and bile-acid/fatty-acid metabolic roles, keeps organelle locations as non-core, and removes generic or unsupported receptor-binding annotations.
