Gene Description

Aprt encodes adenine phosphoribosyltransferase, the purine salvage enzyme that converts adenine and PRPP to AMP. The review accepts the specific enzyme activity and adenine/AMP salvage annotations, keeps substrate/product binding and cytosolic localization as context, modifies broad binding/salvage parents, and flags lactation, grooming, insulin-response, IMP, and GMP annotations as over-extended relative to APRT specificity.
