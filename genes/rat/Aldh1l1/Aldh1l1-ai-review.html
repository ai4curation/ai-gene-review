Gene Description

Aldh1l1 encodes cytosolic 10-formyltetrahydrofolate dehydrogenase. It converts 10-formyltetrahydrofolate to tetrahydrofolate and carbon dioxide with NADPH generation, and has secondary NADP-dependent aldehyde dehydrogenase activity. The review accepts formyltetrahydrofolate dehydrogenase and folate/one-carbon process terms, modifies generic oxidoreductase/catalytic and NAD(P) aldehyde-dehydrogenase parents where appropriate, keeps complex/binding context, and removes a mitochondrial localization annotation inconsistent with the cytosolic protein.
