Gene Description

Pnlip encodes secreted pancreatic triglyceride lipase, the colipase-dependent digestive lipase that hydrolyzes dietary triacylglycerols and can also hydrolyze retinyl esters. The review accepts the specific lipase and retinyl ester hydrolase activities, keeps secretion and lipid/sterol absorption contexts as non-core, and removes or modifies terms that confuse Pnlip with related pancreatic lipases or downstream expression responses.
