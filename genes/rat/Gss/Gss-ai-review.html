Gene Description

Gss encodes glutathione synthetase, the ATP-dependent ligase that catalyzes the final step of glutathione biosynthesis from gamma-glutamylcysteine and glycine. The review accepts glutathione synthase activity and glutathione biosynthesis, keeps substrate/cofactor binding and homodimerization as context, modifies generic ligase activity, and marks stress/nutrient expression-response annotations as non-core over-annotations.
