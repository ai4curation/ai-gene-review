Gene Description

Tpi1 encodes cytosolic triose-phosphate isomerase, which interconverts dihydroxyacetone phosphate and glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate in glycolysis and gluconeogenesis. The review accepts the specific isomerase activity and central carbohydrate pathway annotations, keeps cytosolic localization, homodimerization, and methylglyoxal side activity as non-core, modifies broad isomerase/glucose terms, and removes uninformative protein-binding annotation.
