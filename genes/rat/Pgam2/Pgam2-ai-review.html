Gene Description

Pgam2 encodes rat muscle phosphoglycerate mutase, a glycolytic mutase that interconverts 3-phosphoglycerate and 2-phosphoglycerate and can also act on 2,3-bisphosphoglycerate. The review accepts phosphoglycerate/bisphosphoglycerate mutase activities and glycolysis/gluconeogenesis terms as core/direct metabolism, while localization, Notch, spermatogenesis, and stress-response annotations are non-core or over-annotated.
