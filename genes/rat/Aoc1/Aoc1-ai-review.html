Organism: Rattus norvegicus

Gene Description

Aoc1 encodes copper-containing diamine oxidase/histaminase, an extracellular or membrane-associated amine oxidase that oxidatively deaminates histamine, putrescine, and related primary amines. The review accepts diamine/putrescine/histamine oxidase activity and putrescine metabolism, keeps cofactor, dimerization, and localization annotations as non-core, and removes the tight-junction and ND process annotations unsupported by the cited evidence.
