Gene Description

Sqle encodes squalene monooxygenase, a FAD-dependent endoplasmic-reticulum-associated enzyme that oxidizes squalene to (S)-2,3-epoxysqualene in sterol and cholesterol biosynthesis. The review accepts the monooxygenase activity and general sterol/cholesterol biosynthetic roles, keeps ER/FAD/lipid-droplet context as non-core, and modifies or marks route-specific and response/proliferation terms that overstate the direct evidence.
