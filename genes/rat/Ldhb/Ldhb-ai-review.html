Gene Description

Ldhb encodes the lactate dehydrogenase B chain, which interconverts L-lactate and pyruvate with coupled NAD+/NADH conversion. The review accepts L-lactate dehydrogenase activity and lactate/pyruvate metabolism as direct functions, modifies generic catalytic/oxidoreductase terms, and keeps NAD binding, protein interactions, complexes, and localizations as context.
