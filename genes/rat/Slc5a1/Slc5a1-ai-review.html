Gene Description

Sodium/glucose cotransporter 1 (SGLT1) is a high-affinity Na+-coupled symporter that actively transports D-glucose and D-galactose across the plasma membrane with 2:1 Na+:glucose stoichiometry. SGLT1 is the primary transporter for dietary hexose absorption across the apical brush-border membrane of enterocytes in the small intestine. In kidney, it functions in the S3 segment of proximal tubules for glucose reabsorption, handling approximately 3% of filtered glucose. Additionally serves as a water channel enabling passive water transport. Located at the blood-brain barrier in endothelial cells.
