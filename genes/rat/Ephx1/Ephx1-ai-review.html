Gene Description

Ephx1 encodes rat microsomal epoxide hydrolase, an endoplasmic-reticulum membrane biotransformation enzyme that hydrolyzes arene and aliphatic epoxides to dihydrodiols. The review accepts epoxide hydrolase activity and direct epoxide/lipid metabolism as core or direct functions, and keeps membrane localization, oxysterol/enzyme binding, and hormone-response annotations as non-core context.

Curation decisions

  • Core function: microsomal epoxide hydrolase 1 (epoxide hydrolase activity, GO:0004301).
  • Specific catalytic activities and direct metabolic processes were accepted.
  • Broad parent, localization, binding, and stimulus-response annotations were modified, kept non-core, or marked over-annotated according to support.
