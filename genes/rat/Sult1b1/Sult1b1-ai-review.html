Gene Description

Sult1b1 encodes a cytosolic aryl sulfotransferase that uses PAPS to sulfate dopamine, small phenols, and thyroid hormones. The review accepts aryl sulfotransferase activity, sulfation, and thyroid/phenolic substrate metabolism, keeps cytoplasmic and xenobiotic/flavonoid context as non-core, and marks developmental or ethanol-catabolic inferences as over-annotations.

Core Functions

Sult1b1 catalyzes PAPS-dependent sulfation of aryl/phenolic substrates including dopamine, small phenols, and iodothyronines.

Molecular Function:
aryl sulfotransferase activity
Directly Involved In:
