Gene Description

Cyb5r3 encodes NADH-cytochrome b5 reductase 3, a flavoprotein that transfers electrons from NADH to cytochrome b5. The review accepts the cytochrome-b5 reductase activities as direct functions, modifies generic oxidoreductase activity, keeps cofactor/nucleotide binding and membrane/localization terms as context, and removes the uninformative ND biological_process annotation.
