Gene Description

EngL is a glycoside hydrolase family 9 (GH9) endoglucanase from the Clostridium cellulovorans cellulosome. It contains a GH9 catalytic domain and a C-terminal type I dockerin domain for cellulosome assembly. Unlike non-cellulosomal cellulases (e.g., EngD), EngL lacks an intrinsic carbohydrate-binding module and relies on dockerin-mediated incorporation into the CbpA scaffoldin for cellulose targeting. EngL functions as an endo-Œ≤-1,4-glucanase that hydrolyzes internal Œ≤-1,4 linkages in cellulose.
