Gene Description

PP_5259 encodes a predicted LysR-family DNA-binding transcriptional activator in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The product annotation (HTH-type transcriptional regulator TrpI (TrpBA operon transcriptional activator)) and conserved LysR HTH and substrate-binding domains support transcriptional activation, but the direct promoters, effector ligand, and pathway-specific regulon require gene-specific validation.
