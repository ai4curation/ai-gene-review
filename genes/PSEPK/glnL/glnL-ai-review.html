Gene Description

glnL encodes NtrB, the sensory histidine kinase/phosphatase of the NtrB/NtrC two-component system in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. It autophosphorylates under nitrogen limitation, transfers phosphoryl groups to NtrC/GlnG, and can dephosphorylate NtrC when nitrogen is available.
