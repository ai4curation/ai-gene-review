Gene Description

moeB encodes the cytosolic molybdopterin-synthase sulfur-carrier adenylyltransferase. MoeB uses ATP to adenylate the C-terminal glycine of MoaD, activating the carrier for thiocarboxylate formation and subsequent sulfur donation during molybdopterin synthesis.
