Gene Description

thiO encodes a monomeric FAD-dependent glycine oxidase that oxidizes glycine to glyoxyl imine, hydrogen peroxide, and ammonia. The glyoxyl imine product feeds thiazole-ring formation in thiamine diphosphate biosynthesis.
