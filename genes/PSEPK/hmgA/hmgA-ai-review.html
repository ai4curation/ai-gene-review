Gene Description

hmgA encodes homogentisate 1,2-dioxygenase, a nonheme iron enzyme of the homogentisate pathway. It catalyzes oxidative ring cleavage of homogentisate to maleylacetoacetate during L-phenylalanine and L-tyrosine catabolism in P. putida. DNA-binding and transcription-repressor GO rows currently attached to hmgA appear to be misassigned from the neighboring hmgR regulator and should be removed.
