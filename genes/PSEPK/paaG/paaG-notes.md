# paaG curation notes

- UniProt accession Q88HS0 names the protein
  `1,2-epoxyphenylacetyl-CoA isomerase` and assigns EC 5.3.3.18
  [file:PSEPK/paaG/paaG-uniprot.txt,
  "SubName: Full=1,2-epoxyphenylacetyl-CoA isomerase"].
- The existing `catalytic activity` annotation is retained in substance but
  replaced by the available isomerase molecular-function term. The exact
  substrate-specific activity does not currently have a GO term in the local
  record.
- The phenylacetate catabolic-process annotation is accepted because the
  assigned reaction is the pathway step immediately following ring
  epoxidation.
