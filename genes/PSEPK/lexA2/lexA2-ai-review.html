Gene Description

lexA2 encodes one of two P. putida LexA repressors. LexA2 is a DNA-binding transcriptional repressor with LexA-family autocleavage activity; literature indicates it mainly regulates its own imuA-imuB-dnaE2 transcriptional unit and a resident prophage gene rather than the conventional LexA1 SOS regulon.
