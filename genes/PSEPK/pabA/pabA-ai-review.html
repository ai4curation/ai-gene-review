Gene Description

PP_0420 is a soluble class-I glutamine amidotransferase domain protein in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. Although the current UniProt gene name is pabA and the submitted product name emphasizes aminodeoxychorismate/para-aminobenzoate synthase, domain and family evidence support assignment as a TrpG-like anthranilate synthase amidotransferase. KT2440 aromatic-pathway literature describes tryptophan-biosynthesis genes in unlinked regions including a trpGDE operon. In the inferred TrpG-like role, PP_0420 would supply ammonia from L-glutamine to the TrpE synthase component for conversion of chorismate to anthranilate, the first committed step of L-tryptophan biosynthesis. A physiological folate/pABA role for PP_0420 in KT2440 remains possible but is not established by the available evidence.
