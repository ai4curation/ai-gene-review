Gene Description

PvdS (PP_4244) is a 176 aa extracytoplasmic-function sigma factor of the sigma-70 family in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. Sequence architecture and database assignment strongly support a role as an RNA polymerase promoter-specificity factor rather than an enzyme. In KT2440, the most defensible strain-specific synthesis is that PvdS mediates an iron-limitation transcriptional program centered on pyoverdine/siderophore production, but this remains an inference from gene identity, conserved sigma-factor architecture, and KT2440 pyoverdine biology rather than a direct PP_4244 knockout or promoter-binding study.
