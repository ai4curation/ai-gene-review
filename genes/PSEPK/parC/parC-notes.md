# parC notes

2026-07-10: First-pass DNA topology/topoisomerase sub-batch curation.
parC (PP_4912) encodes DNA topoisomerase IV subunit A in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. Together with ParE it forms a type II topoisomerase heterotetramer that relaxes supercoiled DNA and performs the decatenation events required for segregation of circular chromosomes. ParC provides the GyrA/ParC-family DNA cleavage/rejoining subunit of the Topo IV complex; ATP binding is retained only as complex-level context because ParE is the ATPase subunit.
