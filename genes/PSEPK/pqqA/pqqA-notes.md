## 2026-07-20 OpenScientist assessment

- OpenScientist correctly identifies Q88QV4 as the 23-residue PQQ precursor
  peptide and places Glu15 and Tyr19 at the conserved reactive positions.
- The generated conservation artifact contains executable plotting code and
  embedded sequences, but it omits source accessions for the claimed 25
  reviewed orthologs. Its exact percentages and inferred leader boundary are
  therefore not treated as verified evidence.

  target UniProt sequence and established PqqA/PqqD/PqqE ortholog biochemistry.
