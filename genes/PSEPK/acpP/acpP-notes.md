# acpP curation notes

- Reviewed UniProt accession Q88LL5 describes AcpP as the carrier of the
  growing fatty-acid chain
  [file:PSEPK/acpP/acpP-uniprot.txt,
  "Carrier of the growing fatty acid chain in fatty acid"].
- Acyl carrier activity and fatty-acid biosynthesis are the core assignments;
  acyl binding is retained as a less-informative ancillary property.
- Cytoplasm is retained and the redundant cytosol annotation is marked
  over-annotated.
- Lipid A biosynthesis is kept as a non-core downstream use of acyl-ACP
  intermediates rather than conflated with the central FAS-II role.
