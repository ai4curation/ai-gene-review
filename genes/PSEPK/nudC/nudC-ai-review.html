Gene Description

nudC encodes a Nudix-family NAD-capped RNA hydrolase that removes NAD caps from RNA and can hydrolyze free NAD+ and NADH pyrophosphate bonds. It is an NAD-catabolism/RNA-decapping side node rather than a NAD biosynthetic enzyme.
