Gene Description

catR encodes the LysR-family transcriptional activator CatR, which binds the divergent catRBC promoter region and activates catBCA/catBC expression in response to catechol-pathway inducer context such as cis,cis-muconate. Its core role is pathway-specific transcription activation of catechol branch genes rather than aromatic catabolism catalysis.
