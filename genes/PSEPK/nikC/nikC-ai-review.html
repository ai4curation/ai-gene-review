Gene Description

nikC (PP_3344) encodes the second predicted multi-pass inner-membrane permease subunit of the NikABCDE nickel ABC importer. Its NikC-family domain and local nikABCDE context support nickel import, while peptide/dipeptide annotations reflect broader permease-family transfer.
