# murG curation notes

Q88N76/PP_1337 forms lipid II by transferring GlcNAc to lipid I on the
cytoplasmic face of the inner membrane
[UniProtKB:Q88N76, "FUNCTION: Cell wall formation. Catalyzes the transfer of a
GlcNAc"; "SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane"].

The DAP-specific Rhea term is used for the KT2440 gene-level core function.
Generic glycosyltransferase terms and broad carbohydrate-process terms are
modified to the exact reaction or peptidoglycan biosynthesis. The reusable
module retains the shorter MurG activity term so it is not restricted to one
stem-peptide chemistry.
