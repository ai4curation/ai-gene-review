# retS notes

- KEGG identifies KT2440 locus `PP_4824` as the RetS-family ortholog: `K20972  two-component system, sensor histidine kinase RetS` [KEGG:ppu:PP_4824, "K20972  two-component system, sensor histidine kinase RetS"].
- UniProt entry `Q88DK1` describes a 923 aa membrane hybrid sensor kinase with `Pfam; PF07695; 7TMR-DISM_7TM; 1.`, `Pfam; PF07696; 7TMR-DISMED2; 1.`, `Pfam; PF00512; HisKA; 1.`, and `Pfam; PF00072; Response_reg; 2.`, consistent with a RetS-like histidine kinase carrying two receiver domains [UniProt:Q88DK1, "Pfam; PF07695; 7TMR-DISM_7TM; 1.", "Pfam; PF07696; 7TMR-DISMED2; 1.", "Pfam; PF00512; HisKA; 1.", "Pfam; PF00072; Response_reg; 2."].
- The direct KT2440 evidence currently located is the K1-T6SS regulatory study. In that work, `the three genes were significantly upregulated in the retS mutant` during exponential growth, and the authors concluded that `repression of the K1-T6SS by RetS occurs mainly during exponential growth` [PMID:36748579 Transcriptional organization and regulation of the Pseudomonas putida K1 type VI secretion system gene cluster., "the three genes were significantly upregulated in the retS mutant", "repression of the K1-T6SS by RetS occurs mainly during exponential growth"].
- The same study showed that `activity of both P_S and P_V in the gacS response regulator mutant was considerably reduced` and concluded that `GacS is a positive regulator of K1-T6SS expression`, consistent with RetS acting upstream in the Gac signaling circuit rather than as a secretion structural component [PMID:36748579 Transcriptional organization and regulation of the Pseudomonas putida K1 type VI secretion system gene cluster., "activity of both P_S and P_V in the gacS response regulator mutant was considerably reduced", "GacS is a positive regulator of K1-T6SS expression"].
- No direct KT2440 evidence for osmotic-stress signaling by PP_4824 surfaced in the literature reviewed here. The current GO hyperosmotic-response annotation appears to be a family-transfer inference rather than a KT2440-specific experimental result [GO_REF:0000118 TreeGrafter-generated GO annotations, "TreeGrafter-generated GO annotations"].
- KEGG maps `PP_4824` into the `Biofilm formation - Pseudomonas aeruginosa` orthology pathway, but this is orthology-level transfer, not direct KT2440 experimental evidence for a specific biofilm phenotype [KEGG:ppu:PP_4824, "PATHWAY     ppu02025  Biofilm formation - Pseudomonas aeruginosa"].
