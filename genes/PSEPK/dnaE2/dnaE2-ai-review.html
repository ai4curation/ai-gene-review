Gene Description

dnaE2 encodes an error-prone DnaE2-family DNA polymerase in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. UniProt/HAMAP describes a cytoplasmic DNA polymerase involved in damage-induced mutagenesis and translesion synthesis, not the major replicative DNA polymerase. It is part of the lexA2-imuA-imuB-dnaE2 damage-inducible cassette.
