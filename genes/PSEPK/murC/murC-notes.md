# murC curation notes

Q88N75/PP_1338 is the ATP-dependent UDP-N-acetylmuramate-L-alanine ligase in
peptidoglycan stem-peptide assembly
[UniProtKB:Q88N75, "Reaction=UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-muramate + L-alanine + ATP =
UDP-N-"; "PATHWAY: Cell wall biogenesis; peptidoglycan biosynthesis."].

ATP binding is retained as ancillary, while generic acid-amino-acid ligase
activity is replaced by the substrate-specific MurC molecular function.
