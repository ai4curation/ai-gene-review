# prpF curation notes

- UniProt accession Q88KF3 names the target `Aconitate isomerase`
  [file:PSEPK/prpF/prpF-uniprot.txt,
  "SubName: Full=Aconitate isomerase"].
- Pathway-level family and locus analysis identifies it as PrpF, acting after
  AcnD to convert a methyl-trans-aconitate intermediate to
  2-methyl-cis-aconitate.
- The existing generic isomerase and propionate-catabolic-process annotations
  are accepted. A specific `2-methylaconitate isomerase activity` term is
  proposed because the existing unmethylated aconitate delta-isomerase term
  does not describe the PrpF substrate.
