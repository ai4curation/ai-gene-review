Gene Description

PP_2483 encodes a predicted MocA-type molybdenum-cofactor cytidylyltransferase. Orthology and PANTHER subfamily assignments support transfer of CMP from CTP to Mo-molybdopterin to form the MCD cofactor variant; this target-specific activity has not been assayed directly in KT2440.
