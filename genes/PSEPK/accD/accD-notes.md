# accD curation notes

- Reviewed UniProt accession Q88LD9 identifies AccD as the zinc-binding beta
  carboxyltransferase subunit of acetyl-CoA carboxylase
  [file:PSEPK/accD/accD-uniprot.txt,
  "Component of the acetyl coenzyme A carboxylase (ACC) complex."].
- Whole-complex activity and the carboxyltransferase class annotation use
  contribution semantics because AccD does not act independently of AccA and
  the other ACC subunits.
- The TreeGrafter `acetate CoA-transferase complex` annotation is removed; it
  conflicts with the reviewed ACC complex assignment.
- Zinc binding is retained as an ancillary property.
