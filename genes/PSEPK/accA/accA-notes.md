# accA curation notes

- Reviewed UniProt accession Q88MG4 identifies AccA as the alpha
  carboxyltransferase subunit of heteromeric acetyl-CoA carboxylase
  [file:PSEPK/accA/accA-uniprot.txt,
  "Component of the acetyl coenzyme A carboxylase (ACC) complex."].
- Whole-complex acetyl-CoA carboxylase activity and the carboxyltransferase
  reaction are modeled with contribution semantics because AccA does not act
  independently of AccD and the other ACC subunits.
- Generic ligase activity is removed: AccA's half-reaction is EC 2.1.3.15,
  while ATP-dependent ligase classification belongs to the complete ACC
  reaction.
