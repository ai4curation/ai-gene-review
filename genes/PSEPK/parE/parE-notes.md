# parE notes

2026-07-10: First-pass DNA topology/topoisomerase sub-batch curation.
parE (PP_4915) encodes DNA topoisomerase IV subunit B in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. Together with ParC it forms the type II Topo IV heterotetramer that uses ATP-dependent double-strand passage to relax supercoiled DNA and decatenate replicated circular chromosomes for segregation. ParE supplies the topoisomerase B-family ATPase/TOPRIM subunit of this complex.
