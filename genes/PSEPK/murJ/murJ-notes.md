# murJ curation notes

Q88Q94/PP_0601 is the multispanning inner-membrane flippase for lipid-linked
peptidoglycan precursors
[UniProtKB:Q88Q94, "linked peptidoglycan precursors from the inner to the outer
leaflet of"; "SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane"].

The exact transporter activity and lipid-linked peptidoglycan transport process
are core. Generic lipid translocation is modified to the substrate-specific
process; cell-shape regulation is marked as an indirect phenotype-level
over-annotation.
