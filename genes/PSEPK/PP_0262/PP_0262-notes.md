# PP_0262 notes

PP_0262 is MadB, the standalone hot-dog-fold malonyl-ACP decarboxylase that
forms acetyl-ACP as an alternative fatty-acid initiation primer
[PMID:36796578 "The third route utilizes a malonyl-ACP decarboxylase enzyme,
MadB."].

There is currently no substrate-specific GO molecular-function term for
malonyl-ACP decarboxylation. GO:0016831 (carboxy-lyase activity) is the correct
available parent; a specific malonyl-[acyl-carrier-protein] decarboxylase term
is proposed in the review.
