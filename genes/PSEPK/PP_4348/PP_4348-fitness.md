## Data Source

Data from the Fitness Browser (fit.genomics.lbl.gov), based on RB-TnSeq experiments from the Deutschbauer lab. See: Wetmore et al. (2015) "Rapid quantification of mutant fitness in diverse bacteria by sequencing randomly bar-coded transposons" mBio 6:e00306-15.

### How to interpret fitness values

- **Fitness = 0**: No effect of gene disruption
- **Fitness < -1** (t < -4): Gene is important for growth in this condition
- **Fitness > 1** (t > 4): Gene disruption improves growth (possible regulatory role)
- **Cofitness > 0.8**: Strong evidence for shared pathway/complex
- **Cofitness 0.5-0.8**: Moderate evidence for functional relationship
