# murD curation notes

Q88N78/PP_1335 adds D-glutamate to the MurC product during peptidoglycan
stem-peptide assembly
[UniProtKB:Q88N78, "Reaction=UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-muramoyl-L-alanine +
D-glutamate + ATP ="; "PATHWAY: Cell wall biogenesis; peptidoglycan
biosynthesis."].

ATP binding is ancillary and generic ligase activity is replaced by the exact
MurD function. Cell-division and cell-shape terms are marked over-annotated:
they describe downstream consequences of impaired peptidoglycan synthesis, not
direct MurD regulatory roles.
