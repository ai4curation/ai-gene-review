Gene Description

livK (PP_1141) encodes a signal-peptide-bearing periplasmic substrate-binding component of the PP_1137-PP_1141 LivFGMHK branched-chain amino-acid ABC uptake system.     > However, genome annotation is not standardised, and each service provider uses their own custom-built annotation pipelines.
