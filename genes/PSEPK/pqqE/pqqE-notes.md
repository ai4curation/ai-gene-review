# pqqE curation notes

## 2026-07-20 OpenScientist assessment

- OpenScientist retrieved the correct radical-SAM PqqA cross-linking reaction,
  PqqD dependence, and KT2440 `pqqFABCDEG` context.
- The direct enzymology and auxiliary-cluster structures cited in the report
  were obtained from non-KT2440 PqqE orthologs. They support the conserved
  family function but are not direct assays of Q88QV8.
- The report also states exact conservation across 25 PqqA sequences without
  retaining the alignment or sequence accessions. Those quantitative claims
  are not used in the review.
- Q88QV8's core function remains grounded in its UniProt reaction and cofactor
  annotation, the GOA record, and the accessible abstract for PMID:26961875.
