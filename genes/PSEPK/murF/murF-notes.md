# murF curation notes

Q88N80/PP_1333 completes the soluble UDP-MurNAc pentapeptide precursor by
adding D-Ala-D-Ala to a meso-diaminopimelate-containing tripeptide
[UniProtKB:Q88N80, "FUNCTION: Involved in cell wall formation. Catalyzes the
final step in"; "gamma-D-glutamyl-meso-2,6-diaminopimelate + ATP =
UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-"].

The DAP-specific IBA term is retained as the core gene-level activity.
The broader MurF activity and generic acid-amino-acid ligase terms are modified
to that precise term. The reusable module keeps the broader MurF term so it can
cover pathways with alternative third stem residues.
