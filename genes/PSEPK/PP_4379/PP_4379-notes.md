# PP_4379 curation notes

- UniProt accession Q88ES4 has a conflicting submitted name
  (`Beta-ketoacyl-acyl-carrier-protein synthase I`) but KAS-III N- and
  C-terminal InterPro domains, EC-derived KAS-III GO, and PANTHER subfamily
  PTHR34069:SF2.
- Direct KT2440 work identifies PP_4379 as FabH1, the
  short-chain-acyl-CoA-selective KAS-III initiation route
  [PMID:36796578 "The first two routes employ conventional
  β-ketoacyl-ACP synthase III enzymes, FabH1 and FabH2, that accept short- and
  medium-chain-length acyl-CoAs, respectively."].
- The direct result agrees with the convergent family evidence
  [file:PSEPK/PP_4379/PP_4379-uniprot.txt,
  "PANTHER; PTHR34069:SF2; BETA-KETOACYL-[ACYL-CARRIER-PROTEIN] SYNTHASE III;"].
- The secondary-metabolite biosynthesis annotation is removed because no
  specific secondary-metabolite pathway is supported and the target-specific
  study establishes core fatty-acid initiation.
