Gene Description

ku (PP_3255) encodes the prokaryotic Ku DNA-end binding factor in the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 non-homologous end joining system. Ku forms a homodimer, binds linear double-stranded DNA ends with 5-prime or 3-prime overhangs, and interacts with LigD to recruit and stimulate the LigD repair enzyme at double-strand breaks. It is therefore best treated as a DNA-end recognition and recruitment factor for bacterial NHEJ rather than as a standalone DNA-repair catalyst.
