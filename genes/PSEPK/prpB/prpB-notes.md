# prpB curation notes

- UniProt accession Q88KF6 assigns `2-methylisocitrate lyase`, EC 4.1.3.30,
  and a magnesium cofactor
  [file:PSEPK/prpB/prpB-uniprot.txt, "Full=2-methylisocitrate lyase"].
- The precise methylisocitrate-lyase annotation and propionate-catabolic
  process are accepted; magnesium binding is ancillary.
- `isocitrate lyase activity` is removed. PrpB belongs to the
  methylisocitrate-lyase family and cleaves the methyl-substituted cycle
  intermediate, while AceA is the separate glyoxylate-shunt isocitrate lyase.
