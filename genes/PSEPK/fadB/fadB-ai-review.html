Gene Description

fadB encodes the alpha subunit of the FadBA fatty acid beta-oxidation multienzyme complex. It provides hydroxyacyl-CoA dehydrogenase, enoyl-CoA hydratase, enoyl-CoA isomerase, and 3-hydroxybutyryl-CoA epimerase activities used in long-chain fatty acid beta-oxidation.
