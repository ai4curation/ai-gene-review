CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulatory component of the Usp12-46 deubiquitylase complex
CC       (PubMed:37798281). This complex deubiquitylates the wg/wingless-
CC       signaling receptor arr/arrow, which stabilizes the receptor and
CC       increases its concentration at the cell surface; this enhances the
CC       sensitivity of cells to wg/wingless-signal stimulation
CC       (PubMed:37798281). This increases the amplitude and spatial range of
CC       the signaling response to the wg/wingless morphogen gradient,
CC       facilitating the precise concentration-dependent regulation of its
CC       target genes (PubMed:37798281). Required for wg/wingless-mediated
CC       signaling in the wing imaginal disc and for wg/wingless-dependent
CC       regulation of intestinal stem cell proliferation (PubMed:37798281).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:37798281}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the Usp12-46 deubiquitylase complex consisting of
CC       Usp12-46, Wdr20 and Uaf1; regulatory subunit that, together with Uaf1,
CC       stabilizes Usp12-46 (PubMed:37798281). The Usp12-46 deubiquitylase
CC       complex associates with arr/arrow; the interaction leads to
CC       deubiquitination and stabilization of arr/arrow (PubMed:37798281).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:37798281}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed in the larval wing imaginal disc.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:37798281}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Viable but sterile with reduced lifespan
CC       (PubMed:37798281). Severe defects in adult intestinal epithelium;
CC       increased number of intestinal stem and progenitor cells in the adult
CC       midgut (PubMed:37798281). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:37798281}.
