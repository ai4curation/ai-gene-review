CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the target of rapamycin complex 2 (TORC2), which
CC       transduces signals from growth factors to pathways involved in
CC       proliferation, cytoskeletal organization and anabolic output
CC       (PubMed:16919458, PubMed:17043309). In response to growth factors,
CC       TORC2 phosphorylates and activates AGC protein kinase family members,
CC       such as Akt1 (PubMed:16919458, PubMed:17043309). Within the TORC2
CC       complex, Sin1 acts as a substrate adapter which recognizes and binds
CC       AGC protein kinase family members for phosphorylation by mTor (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9BPZ7,
