Gene Description

Serum albumin of the horse, the allergen Equ c 3. The most abundant plasma protein and the archetypal multi-ligand transport protein of the ALB/AFP/VDB family: it binds and carries fatty acids, metal ions, hormones, bilirubin and drugs, and its principal physiological role is regulation of the colloidal osmotic pressure of blood; it also sequesters the bacterial siderophore enterobactin (nutritional immunity). As an allergen, Equ c 3 is a minor, cross-reactive component (mammalian serum-albumin cross-reactivity).
