CC   -!- FUNCTION: Oxidoreductase that catalyzes the last step in proline
CC       biosynthesis, which corresponds to the reduction of pyrroline-5-
CC       carboxylate to L-proline using NAD(P)H (PubMed:23024808,
CC       PubMed:2722838, PubMed:6894153). At physiologic concentrations, has
CC       higher specific activity in the presence of NADH (PubMed:23024808,
CC       PubMed:2722838, PubMed:6894153). Involved in cellular response to
CC       oxidative stress (PubMed:25865492). In some cell types, such as
CC       erythrocytes, its primary function may be the generation of NADP(+)
CC       (PubMed:2722838, PubMed:6894153). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23024808,
