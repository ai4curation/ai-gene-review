Gene Description

Cryptochrome-2 (CRY2) is a FAD-binding transcriptional repressor in the mammalian circadian clock that partners with PER proteins to inhibit CLOCK:BMAL1-driven transcription and regulate rhythmic gene expression; CRY2 lacks photolyase activity and can bind DNA weakly.
