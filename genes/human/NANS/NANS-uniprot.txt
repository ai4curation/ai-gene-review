CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the condensation of phosphoenolpyruvate (PEP) and
CC       N-acetylmannosamine 6-phosphate (ManNAc-6-P) to synthesize N-
CC       acetylneuraminate-9-phosphate (Neu5Ac-9-P) (PubMed:10749855). Also
CC       catalyzes the condensation of PEP and D-mannose 6-phosphate (Man-6-P)
CC       to produce 3-deoxy-D-glycero-beta-D-galacto-non-2-ulopyranosonate 9-
CC       phosphate (KDN-9-P) (PubMed:10749855). Neu5Ac-9-P and KDN-9-P are the
CC       phosphorylated forms of sialic acids N-acetylneuraminic acid (Neu5Ac)
CC       and deaminoneuraminic acid (KDN), respectively (PubMed:10749855).
CC       Required for brain and skeletal development (PubMed:27213289).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10749855, ECO:0000269|PubMed:27213289}.
