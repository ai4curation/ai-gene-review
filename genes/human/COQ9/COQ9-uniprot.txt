CC       COQ7:COQ9 come together on the same side of the lipid pseudo-bilayer
CC       and form a curved tetramer with a hydrophobic surface suitable for
CC       membrane interaction (PubMed:36306796). These two tetramers assemble
CC       into a soluble octamer with a pseudo-bilayer of lipids captured within
CC       (PubMed:36306796). Interacts with COQ7; this interaction allows
CC       ubiquinone (CoQ) isoprene intermediates presentation to COQ7 and
