Gene Description

RPS3 is human 40S ribosomal protein uS3, a structural component of the cytosolic small ribosomal subunit that supports cytoplasmic translation. It also has reported extra-ribosomal roles in damaged-DNA binding/base-excision repair contexts, NF-kB-associated transcriptional regulation, and apoptosis, but these are secondary and context-dependent relative to its core ribosomal function.

Core Functions

RPS3/uS3 is a structural component of the human cytosolic small ribosomal subunit and supports cytoplasmic translation. This ribosomal role is the physiological primary function; DNA repair, NF-kB/transcription, and apoptosis annotations should be treated as context-dependent extra-ribosomal functions.
