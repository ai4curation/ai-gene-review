Gene Description

SLC25A5 (ADP/ATP translocase 2; ANT2; AAC2) is an ADP/ATP translocase (adenine nucleotide translocator) of the SLC25 mitochondrial carrier family. It is a polytopic integral protein of the mitochondrial inner membrane, built from three homologous Solcar repeats forming a six-transmembrane-helix carrier fold, and functions as an antiporter that exchanges ATP synthesized in the mitochondrial matrix for cytosolic ADP by an alternating-access (cytoplasmic-open/matrix-open) mechanism. This ATP:ADP exchange supplies the ATP-synthase/electron-transport chain with ADP and delivers ATP to the cytosol, making the carrier a central node of cellular energy homeostasis and oxidative phosphorylation. ANT2 is the isoform characteristically expressed in proliferative and glycolytic tissues (fibroblasts, growth-activated and undifferentiated cells, and many tumors). Beyond its antiporter activity, ANT can act as a fatty-acid-dependent proton transporter that uncouples oxidative phosphorylation and contributes to thermogenesis in tissues lacking UCP1, and it participates in mitochondrial permeability transition pore (mPTP) activity linked to apoptosis; it has also been reported as a component of the mitotic spindle-associated MMXD (MMS19-MIP18-XPD) cytosolic iron-sulfur assembly complex and as a regulator of PINK1-dependent mitophagy.

SLC25A5 (ADP/ATP translocase 2, ANT2) — review notes

UniProt: P05141 (ADT2_HUMAN). Gene: SLC25A5 (HGNC:10991), X-linked (Xq24-25).
Synonyms: ANT2, AAC2, ADP/ATP carrier protein 2, "fibroblast isoform". 298 aa.

Core identity and function

SLC25A5/ANT2 is the ADP/ATP translocase (adenine nucleotide translocator, ANT)
isoform characteristically expressed in proliferative and glycolytic tissues
(fibroblasts, undifferentiated/growth-activated cells, tumors). It is a member of
the SLC25 mitochondrial carrier family (TC 2.A.29) and is an integral polytopic
protein of the mitochondrial inner membrane with the classic 6-transmembrane,
three-repeat (Solcar) carrier fold.

Primary molecular function is an ATP:ADP antiporter (GO:0005471): it exchanges
ATP synthesized in the matrix for cytosolic ADP across the inner membrane by an
alternating-access (c-state ⇄ m-state) mechanism with a single substrate-binding
site. This supplies the electron-transport chain / ATP synthase with ADP and
exports ATP to fuel the cell.
