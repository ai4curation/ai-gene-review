CC   -!- FUNCTION: mRNA-binding protein that binds to the SECIS (selenocysteine
CC       insertion sequence) element present in the 3'-UTR of mRNAs encoding
CC       selenoproteins and facilitates the incorporation of the rare amino acid
CC       selenocysteine (PubMed:35709277). Insertion of selenocysteine at UGA
CC       codons is mediated by SECISBP2 and EEFSEC: SECISBP2 (1) specifically
CC       binds the SECIS sequence once the 80S ribosome encounters an in-frame
CC       UGA codon and (2) contacts the RPS27A/eS31 of the 40S ribosome before
CC       ribosome stalling (PubMed:35709277). (3) GTP-bound EEFSEC then delivers
CC       selenocysteinyl-tRNA(Sec) to the 80S ribosome and adopts a
CC       preaccommodated state conformation (PubMed:35709277). (4) After GTP
CC       hydrolysis, EEFSEC dissociates from the assembly, selenocysteinyl-
CC       tRNA(Sec) accommodates, and peptide bond synthesis and selenoprotein
CC       elongation occur (PubMed:35709277). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:35709277}.
