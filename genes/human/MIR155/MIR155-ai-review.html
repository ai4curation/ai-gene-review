Gene Description

MicroRNA involved in immune regulation and oncogenesis. Known as an oncomiR that regulates gene expression including KRAS targeting.

Proposed New Ontology Terms

Justification: MIR155 is a well-established oncomiR, but there is no specific GO term for oncomiR activity

Parent term: RNA binding
