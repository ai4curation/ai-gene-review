Gene Description

CHMP2A is a core ESCRT-III subunit that functions in membrane scission events with reverse topology. Key roles include MVB biogenesis, cytokinetic abscission, nuclear envelope reformation, viral budding, autophagosome closure, and plasma membrane repair.
