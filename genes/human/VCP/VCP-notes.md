# VCP notes

## 2026-04-20 PN review

- `just fetch-gene human VCP` hung on the UniProt text request in this worktree, so I created the scaffold manually with `curl` for UniProt, paged QuickGO for GOA, and then seeded `VCP-ai-review.yaml` locally.
- Core call: VCP is best treated as an ATP-driven segregase/unfoldase centered on ERAD and retrotranslocation rather than as a generic "all proteostasis" gene. [PMID:15215856 A membrane protein complex mediates retro-translocation from the ER lumen into the cytosol., "This process requires recognition of a substrate in the ER lumen and its subsequent movement through the membrane by the cytosolic p97 ATPase."] [PMID:16186510 Recruitment of the p97 ATPase and ubiquitin ligases to the site of retrotranslocation at the endoplasmic reticulum membrane., "The translocation of polypeptides across the ER membrane requires their polyubiquitination and subsequent extraction from the membrane by the p97 ATPase"] [PMID:25660456 Identification of ERAD components essential for dislocation of the null Hong Kong variant of α-1-antitrypsin (NHK)., "RNAi revealed that 12 out of 21 ERAD components examined are required for efficient dislocation of NHK among which Hrd1, Sel1L, GRP94 and p97/VCP are critically required."] [PMID:33058883 Protein Phosphatase-1 Complex Disassembly by p97 is Initiated through Multivalent Recognition of Catalytic and Regulatory Subunits by the p97 SEP-domain Adapters., "binds and engages the direct target I3 in the central pore of p97 for unfolding"]
- Contextual autophagy branch: autophagosome maturation/autophagy is real, but it looks like one proteostasis deployment of VCP rather than the core conserved role. [PMID:20104022 VCP/p97 is essential for maturation of ubiquitin-containing autophagosomes and this function is impaired by mutations that cause IBMPFD, "VCP is essential for autophagosome maturation."] 
- Class III PI3K/autophagy initiation: literature supports a regulatory link to BECN1/PtdIns3K assembly, but this looks like a contextual regulatory input rather than evidence that VCP is itself a class III PI3K complex component. [PMID:33510452 VCP/p97 regulates Beclin-1-dependent autophagy initiation, "A decreased level of VCP, or inhibition of its ATPase activity, impairs starvation-induced production of PI(3)P and limits downstream recruitment of WIPI2, ATG16L and LC3, thereby decreasing autophagosome formation"]
- Translation/RQC: GOA contains review-derived RQC terms, but I did not elevate translation/RQC into core because the accessible support here is weaker and branch-specific.
- Cargo adaptor activity: I did not find a good basis to treat VCP itself as an autophagy cargo adaptor. The accessible literature supports segregase/extraction and regulatory roles instead.
- `PMID:27753622` remained unresolved locally after PubMed rate limiting, so the generic `macroautophagy` annotation stays `UNDECIDED`.
