CC       serine/threonine phosphatase involved in the regulation of a wide
CC       variety of enzymes, signal transduction pathways, and cellular events
CC       (PubMed:10801873, PubMed:12473674, PubMed:17245430, PubMed:22613722,
CC       PubMed:33243860, PubMed:34004147, PubMed:9920888). PP2A is the major
CC       phosphatase for microtubule-associated proteins (MAPs)
CC       (PubMed:22613722). PP2A can modulate the activity of phosphorylase B
CC       kinase casein kinase 2, mitogen-stimulated S6 kinase, and MAP-2 kinase
CC       (PubMed:22613722). Cooperates with SGO2 to protect centromeric cohesin
CC       from separase-mediated cleavage in oocytes specifically during meiosis
CC       I (By similarity). Can dephosphorylate various proteins, such as SV40
CC       large T antigen, AXIN1, p53/TP53, PIM3, WEE1 (PubMed:10801873,
CC       PubMed:12473674, PubMed:17245430, PubMed:9920888). Activates RAF1 by
CC       dephosphorylating it at 'Ser-259' (PubMed:10801873). Mediates
CC       dephosphorylation of WEE1, preventing its ubiquitin-mediated
CC       proteolysis, increasing WEE1 protein levels, and promoting the G2/M
CC       checkpoint (PubMed:33108758). Mediates dephosphorylation of MYC;
CC       promoting its ubiquitin-mediated proteolysis: interaction with AMBRA1
CC       enhances interaction between PPP2CA and MYC (PubMed:25438055). Mediates
CC       dephosphorylation of FOXO3; promoting its stabilization: interaction
CC       with AMBRA1 enhances interaction between PPP2CA and FOXO3
CC       (PubMed:30513302). Catalyzes dephosphorylation of the pyrin domain of
CC       NLRP3, promoting assembly of the NLRP3 inflammasome (By similarity).
CC       Together with RACK1 adapter, mediates dephosphorylation of AKT1 at
CC       'Ser-473', preventing AKT1 activation and AKT-mTOR signaling pathway
CC       (By similarity). Dephosphorylation of AKT1 is essential for regulatory
CC       T-cells (Treg) homeostasis and stability (By similarity). Catalyzes
CC       dephosphorylation of PIM3, promoting PIM3 ubiquitination and
CC       proteasomal degradation (PubMed:12473674). Part of the striatin-
CC       interacting phosphatase and kinase (STRIPAK) complexes
CC       (PubMed:33633399). STRIPAK complexes have critical roles in protein
CC       (de)phosphorylation and are regulators of multiple signaling pathways
CC       including Hippo, MAPK, nuclear receptor and cytoskeleton remodeling
CC       (PubMed:33633399). Different types of STRIPAK complexes are involved in
CC       a variety of biological processes such as cell growth, differentiation,
CC       apoptosis, metabolism and immune regulation (PubMed:33633399). Key
CC       mediator of a quality checkpoint during transcription elongation as
CC       part of the Integrator-PP2A (INTAC) complex (PubMed:33243860,
CC       PubMed:34004147, PubMed:37080207). The INTAC complex drives premature
CC       transcription termination of transcripts that are unfavorably
CC       configured for transcriptional elongation: within the INTAC complex,
CC       PPP2CA catalyzes dephosphorylation of the C-terminal domain (CTD) of
CC       Pol II subunit POLR2A/RPB1 and SUPT5H/SPT5, thereby preventing
CC       transcriptional elongation (PubMed:33243860, PubMed:34004147,
CC       PubMed:37080207). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P63330,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10801873, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12473674,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17245430, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22613722,
