CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protease involved in the processing of a variety of
CC       prenylated proteins containing the C-terminal CAAX motif, where C is a
CC       cysteine modified with an isoprenoid lipid, A is an aliphatic amino
CC       acid and X is any C-terminal amino acid. Proteolytically removes the C-
CC       terminal three residues of farnesylated and geranylated proteins,
CC       leaving the prenylated cysteine as the new C-terminus. Is able to
CC       process K-Ras, N-Ras, H-Ras, RAP1B and G-gamma-1 (PubMed:10085068).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10085068, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11038283,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19188362}.
