CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mitochondrial transporter that imports/exports pyrimidine
CC       nucleotides into and from mitochondria. Selectively transports
CC       cytosine, guanosine, inosine and uridine (deoxy)nucleoside mono-, di-,
CC       and triphosphates by antiport mechanism. Catalyzes uniport at much
CC       lower rate (PubMed:25320081). May import (deoxy)nucleoside
CC       triphosphates in exchange for intramitochondrial (deoxy)nucleoside
CC       mono- and diphosphates, thus providing precursors necessary for de novo
CC       synthesis of mitochondrial DNA and RNA while exporting products of
CC       their catabolism (PubMed:25320081). Participates in mitochondrial
CC       genome maintenance, regulation of mitochondrial membrane potential and
CC       mitochondrial respiration. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25320081}.
