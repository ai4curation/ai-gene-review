CC       NADH through the respiratory chain, using ubiquinone as an electron
CC       acceptor (PubMed:28844695). Part of the peripheral arm of the enzyme,
CC       where the electrons from NADH are accepted by flavin mononucleotide
CC       (FMN) and then passed along a chain of iron-sulfur clusters by electron
CC       tunnelling to the final acceptor ubiquinone (PubMed:28844695). Contains
CC       FMN, which is the initial electron acceptor as well as one iron-sulfur
CC       cluster (PubMed:28844695). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28844695}.
