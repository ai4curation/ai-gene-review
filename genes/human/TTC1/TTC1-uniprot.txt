CC   -!- FUNCTION: Adapter protein that plays a role downstream of GNA15 to
CC       stimulate Ras activation and subsequent phosphorylation of ERK1/2
CC       independently of phospholipase Cbeta signaling (PubMed:12748287,
CC       PubMed:21486497). Mechanistically, interacts with GNA15 and SOS2 to
CC       form a macromolecular signaling complex which has a high affinity for
CC       HRAS (PubMed:20639119). Additionally, participates in signaling via
CC       other G-alpha proteins like GNA14, activating NF-kappa-B through Ras
CC       and ERK pathways (PubMed:22711498). Beyond signaling, supports protein
CC       homeostasis by cooperating with HSP40 in the HSP70 chaperone cycle,
CC       promoting protein folding through the regulated dissociation of the
CC       HSP70 inhibitor HSPBP1 (PubMed:14503850). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12748287,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14503850, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20639119,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21486497, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22711498}.
