CC   -!- FUNCTION: Scaffold protein which mediates protein-protein interactions
CC       during a range of cellular events including focal adhesion assembly,
CC       cell adhesion and cell migration (PubMed:17420447, PubMed:20005845,
CC       PubMed:30367047, PubMed:32528174). Regulates integrin-mediated signal
CC       transduction by contributing to inside-out integrin activation (By
CC       similarity). Recruits PARVA and LIMS1/PITCH to form the heterotrimeric
CC       IPP (ILK-PINCH-PARVIN) complex which binds to F-actin via the C-
CC       terminal tail of LIMS1 and the N-terminal region of PARVA, promoting F-
CC       actin filament bundling, a process required to generate force for actin
CC       cytoskeleton reorganization and subsequent dynamic cell adhesion events
CC       such as cell spreading and migration (PubMed:30367047). Binding to
CC       PARVA promotes effective assembly of ILK into focal adhesions while
CC       PARVA-bound ILK can simultaneously engage integrin-beta cytoplasmic
CC       tails to mediate cell adhesion (PubMed:20005845). Plays a role with
CC       PARVG in promoting the cell adhesion and spreading of leukocytes
CC       (PubMed:16517730). Acts as an upstream effector of both AKT1/PKB and
CC       GSK3 (PubMed:9736715). Mediates trafficking of caveolae to the cell
CC       surface in an ITGB1-dependent manner by promoting the recruitment of
CC       IQGAP1 to the cell cortex which cooperates with its effector DIAPH1 to
CC       locally stabilize microtubules and allow stable insertion of caveolae
CC       into the plasma membrane (By similarity). Required for the maintenance
CC       of mitotic spindle integrity by promoting phosphorylation of TACC3 by
CC       AURKA (PubMed:18283114). Associates with chromatin and may act as a
CC       negative regulator of transcription when located in the nucleus
CC       (PubMed:17420447). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O55222,
CC       ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q99J82, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16517730,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17420447, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18283114,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:20005845, ECO:0000269|PubMed:30367047,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:32528174, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9736715}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the heterotrimeric IPP (ILK-PINCH-PARVIN) complex
CC       composed of ILK, LIMS1/PINCH and PARVA; the complex binds to F-actin
