CC   -!- FUNCTION: Substrate-recognition component of the SCF (SKP1-CUL1-F-box
CC       protein)-type E3 ubiquitin ligase complex. Mediates the ubiquitination
CC       and degradation of BCL2, an antiapoptotic protein, thereby playing a
CC       role in apoptosis by controlling the stability of BCL2. Targets also
CC       the receptor for advanced glycation end products RAGE for
CC       ubiquitination and subsequent lysosomal degradation (PubMed:28515150).
CC       Directly controls HGAL/GCSAM ubiquitination and degradation and thereby
CC       decreases BCR signaling (PubMed:31570756).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23431138, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28515150,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31570756}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Protein modification; protein ubiquitination.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the SCF(FBXO10) complex consisting of CUL1, SKP1
CC       and FBXO10 (PubMed:23431138). Interacts with BCL2 (PubMed:23431138).
CC       Interacts with PRDM1 (PubMed:24613396). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23431138,
