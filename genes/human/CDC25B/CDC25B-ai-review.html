Summary: CRITICAL MISANNOTATION: CDC25B is a PHOSPHATASE (EC 3.1.3.48 "protein tyrosine phosphatase"), NOT a kinase. The UniProt function states: "Directly dephosphorylates CDK1 and stimulates its kinase activity." PMID:17332740 explicitly describes CDC25B as "the phosphatase required for activation of mitotic cyclin/Cdk1 complexes" - it dephosphorylates CDK1 to activate it. Annotating a phosphatase to "protein phosphorylation" (the opposite reaction) is fundamentally incorrect.
