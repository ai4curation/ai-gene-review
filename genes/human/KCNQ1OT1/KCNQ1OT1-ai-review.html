Gene Description

KCNQ1OT1 is a 91kb paternally-expressed imprinted long non-coding RNA that regulates genomic imprinting at the KCNQ1 locus on chromosome 11p15.5. It silences genes in the KCNQ1 imprinted domain by recruiting Polycomb Repressive Complex 2 (PRC2) and establishing heterochromatin. Loss of imprinting leads to Beckwith-Wiedemann syndrome.
