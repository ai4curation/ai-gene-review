CC   -!- FUNCTION: Key component of the cytosolic iron-sulfur protein assembly
CC       (CIA) complex, a multiprotein complex that mediates the incorporation
CC       of iron-sulfur cluster into extramitochondrial Fe/S proteins
CC       (PubMed:17937914, PubMed:23891004, PubMed:38950322). As a CIA complex
CC       component, interacts specifically with CIAO2A or CIAO2B and MMS19 to
CC       assist different branches of iron-sulfur protein assembly, depending of
CC       its interactors. The complex CIAO1:CIAO2B:MMS19 binds to and
CC       facilitates the assembly of most cytosolic-nuclear Fe/S proteins.
CC       CIAO1:CIAO2A specifically matures ACO1 and stabilizes IREB2
CC       (PubMed:23891004). Seems to specifically modulate the transactivation
CC       activity of WT1 (PubMed:9556563). As part of the mitotic spindle-
CC       associated MMXD complex it may play a role in chromosome segregation
CC       (PubMed:20797633). {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_03037,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17937914, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20797633,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23891004, ECO:0000269|PubMed:38950322,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9556563}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the CIA complex (PubMed:22678361,
CC       PubMed:22678362, PubMed:23585563, PubMed:38950322). Interacts with
CC       CIAO2A and forms a complex with CIAO2B and MMS19; the interactions with
CC       CIAO2A and CIAO2B are mutually exclusive (PubMed:22683786,
CC       PubMed:23585563, PubMed:23891004, PubMed:38950322). Interacts with
CC       CHD1L, ERCC2, IREB2 and POLD1 (PubMed:23891004, PubMed:38950322).
CC       Component of the MMXD complex, which includes CIAO1, ERCC2, CIAO2B,
CC       MMS19 and SLC25A5 (PubMed:20797633). Interacts with WT1
CC       (PubMed:9556563). Interacts with CIAO3 (PubMed:23585563). Interacts
CC       (via LYR motif) with HSC20 (PubMed:29309586, PubMed:38950322).
CC       {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_03037, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20797633,
