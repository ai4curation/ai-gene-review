CC       morphogen that activates the smoothened signaling pathway, and which is
CC       essential for a variety of patterning events during development
CC       (PubMed:29954986, PubMed:29995851, PubMed:30139912, PubMed:31127104,

CC       morphogen, while ShhC is degraded in the endoplasmic reticulum (By

CC       sonic hedgehog protein N-product (ShhN) is a morphogen that activates
