CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalytic subunit of the glycosylphosphatidylinositol-
CC       mannosyltransferase I complex which catalyzes the transfer of the first
CC       mannose, via an alpha-1,4 bond from a dolichol-phosphate-mannose (Dol-
CC       P-Man) to a 2-acyl-6-alpha-D-glucosaminyl-1-(1-radyl,2-acyl-sn-glycero-
CC       3-phospho)-1D-myo-inositol (glucosaminyl acyl phosphatidylinositol,
CC       GlcN-(acyl)PI) intermediate to generate 2-acyl-6-(alpha-D-mannosyl-
CC       (1->4)-alpha-D-glucosaminyl)-1-(1-radyl,2-acyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho)-
CC       1D-myo-inositol (also termed H2) and participates in the sixth step of
CC       the glycosylphosphatidylinositol-anchor biosynthesis.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11226175}.
