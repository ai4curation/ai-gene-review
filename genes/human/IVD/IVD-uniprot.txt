CC   -!- FUNCTION: A mitochondrial matrix enzyme that catalyzes the third step
CC       in leucine catabolism, where isovaleryl-CoA (3-methylbutanoyl-CoA) is
CC       metabolized to 3-methylbut-2-enoyl-CoA (PubMed:7640268). To a lesser
CC       extent, it also participates in the first step in fatty acid beta-
CC       oxidation, in which it catalyzes the proR-proR stereospecific
CC       alpha,beta-dehydrogenation of other saturated short-chain acyl-CoA
CC       thioesters such as pentanoyl-CoA, hexanoyl-CoA and butanoyl-CoA, using
CC       the electron transfer flavoprotein (ETF) as their physiologic electron
CC       acceptor(PubMed:7640268). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:7640268}.
