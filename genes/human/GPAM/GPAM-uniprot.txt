CC       phosphatidic acids and lysophosphatidic acids (PubMed:18238778,

CC       coenzyme A (acyl-CoA) to the sn-1 position of glycerol-3-phosphate, to
CC       produce lysophosphatidic acid (PubMed:18238778). Has a narrow
CC       hydrophobic binding cleft that selects for a linear acyl chain
CC       (PubMed:36522428). Catalytic activity is higher for substrates with a
CC       16-carbon acyl chain (PubMed:36522428). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18238778,
