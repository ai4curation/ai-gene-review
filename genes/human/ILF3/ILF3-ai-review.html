PMID:21123651
Phosphorylation of the NFAR proteins by the dsRNA-dependent ... 		REMOVE
Summary: MISANNOTATION: ILF3 (also known as NFAR) is NOT a kinase. PMID:21123651 title explicitly states "Phosphorylation of the NFAR proteins BY the dsRNA-dependent protein kinase PKR" - ILF3/NFAR is the SUBSTRATE being phosphorylated by PKR at Thr-188 and Thr-315. UniProt also notes ILF3 is a "nuclear phosphoprotein" (M-phase phosphoprotein 4) and describes it being "phosphorylated by the interferon-induced protein kinase, PKR". ILF3 is a dsRNA-binding protein, not an enzyme that performs phosphorylation.
