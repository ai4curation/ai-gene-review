Regulatory role for a novel human thioredoxin peroxidase in ... 		MODIFY
Summary: OVER-ANNOTATION: PRDX4 is a peroxiredoxin (EC 1.11.1.24) - a thiol-specific peroxidase that reduces hydrogen peroxide, NOT a kinase. PMID:9388242 describes PRDX4 as having a "Regulatory role... in NF-kappaB activation" and "regulates NF-kappaB activity via a modulation of IkappaB-alpha phosphorylation". PRDX4 affects IkappaB phosphorylation through redox regulation (antioxidant activity), not by performing phosphorylation itself. The IKK complex kinases perform the actual phosphorylation.
Proposed replacements: regulation of I-kappaB phosphorylation
