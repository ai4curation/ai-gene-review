CC   -!- FUNCTION: Calmodulin acts as part of a calcium signal transduction
CC       pathway by mediating the control of a large number of enzymes, ion
CC       channels, aquaporins and other proteins through calcium-binding
CC       (PubMed:16760425, PubMed:31454269). Calcium-binding is required for the
CC       activation of calmodulin (PubMed:16760425, PubMed:31454269,

CC       calcium complex are a number of protein kinases, such as myosin light-
CC       chain kinases and calmodulin-dependent protein kinase type II (CaMK2),

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16760425, ECO:0000269|PubMed:31454269,
