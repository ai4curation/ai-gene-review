CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in peroxisomal beta-oxidation of branched-chain
CC       fatty acids (BCFAs). Catalyzes the initial and rate-limiting
CC       dehydrogenation step that removes two hydrogen molecules and introduces
CC       a trans double bond between the alpha and beta carbons of the acyl CoA
CC       molecules while generating hydrogen peroxide as a by-product
CC       (PubMed:29287774). Metabolizes multi-methyl-branched fatty acyl-CoA
CC       esters such as (2S)-pristanoyl-CoA, displaying redundancy with ACOX3
CC       (PubMed:29287774). Can oxidize straight-chain mono- and dicarboxylyl
CC       fatty acyl-CoAs with lower efficiency (By similarity)
CC       (PubMed:29287774). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q63448,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:29287774}.
