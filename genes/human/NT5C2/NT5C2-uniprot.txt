CC   -!- FUNCTION: Broad specificity cytosolic 5'-nucleotidase that catalyzes
CC       the dephosphorylation of 6-hydroxypurine nucleoside 5'-monophosphates
CC       (PubMed:10092873, PubMed:12907246, PubMed:1659319, PubMed:9371705). In
CC       addition, possesses a phosphotransferase activity by which it can
CC       transfer a phosphate from a donor nucleoside monophosphate to an
CC       acceptor nucleoside, preferably inosine, deoxyinosine and guanosine
CC       (PubMed:1659319, PubMed:9371705). Has the highest activities for IMP
CC       and GMP followed by dIMP, dGMP and XMP (PubMed:10092873,
CC       PubMed:12907246, PubMed:1659319, PubMed:9371705). Could also catalyze
CC       the transfer of phosphates from pyrimidine monophosphates but with
CC       lower efficiency (PubMed:1659319, PubMed:9371705). Through these
CC       activities regulates the purine nucleoside/nucleotide pools within the
CC       cell (PubMed:10092873, PubMed:12907246, PubMed:1659319,
CC       PubMed:9371705). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10092873,
