CC   -!- FUNCTION: Pyruvate kinase that catalyzes the conversion of
CC       phosphoenolpyruvate to pyruvate with the synthesis of ATP, and which
CC       plays a key role in glycolysis (PubMed:11960989). Also produces the
CC       side product 2-phospholactate which can inhibit fructose-2,6-
CC       bisphosphate production (PubMed:27294321). 2-phospholactate can be
CC       dephosphorylated by PGP which prevents the inhibition of fructose-2,6-
CC       bisphosphate production and allows glycolysis to occur
CC       (PubMed:27294321). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11960989,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:27294321}.
