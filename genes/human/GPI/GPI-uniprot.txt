CC       interconverting D-glucose-6-phosphate (G6P) and D-mannose-6-phosphate
CC       (M6P) (By similarity). Also displays anomerase activity,

CC       phosphate and M6P (By similarity). In addition to its metabolic role,

CC       motility factor (AMF), a secreted angiogenic factor that enhances
CC       endothelial cell motility (PubMed:11437381). Functions as neuroleukin,
