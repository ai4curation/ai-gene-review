CC   -!- FUNCTION: Small GTPase involved in protein trafficking between
CC       different compartments (PubMed:8253837). Modulates vesicle budding and
CC       uncoating within the Golgi complex (PubMed:8253837). In its GTP-bound
CC       form, triggers the recruitment of coatomer proteins to the Golgi
CC       membrane (PubMed:8253837). The hydrolysis of ARF1-bound GTP, which is
CC       mediated by ARFGAPs proteins, is required for dissociation of coat
CC       proteins from Golgi membranes and vesicles (PubMed:8253837). The GTP-
CC       bound form interacts with PICK1 to limit PICK1-mediated inhibition of
CC       Arp2/3 complex activity; the function is linked to AMPA receptor
CC       (AMPAR) trafficking, regulation of synaptic plasticity of excitatory
CC       synapses and spine shrinkage during long-term depression (LTD) (By
CC       similarity). Plays a key role in the regulation of intestinal stem
CC       cells and gut microbiota, and is essential for maintaining intestinal
CC       homeostasis (By similarity). Also plays a critical role in mast cell
CC       expansion but not in mast cell maturation by facilitating optimal
CC       mTORC1 activation (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P84079,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8253837}.
