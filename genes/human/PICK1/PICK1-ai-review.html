GO_REF:0000024 		REMOVE
Summary: MISANNOTATION: PICK1 (Protein interacting with C kinase 1) is NOT a kinase. It is a PDZ domain scaffold/adapter protein that BINDS protein kinase C (has GO:0005080 "protein kinase C binding"). UniProt describes it as a "Probable adapter protein that bind to and organize" signaling complexes. PICK1 is also a phosphorylation SUBSTRATE - "Phosphorylation at Thr-82 appears to inhibit the interaction" with GluR2. The ISS evidence is invalid as PICK1 has no kinase domain and doesn't catalyze phosphorylation.
