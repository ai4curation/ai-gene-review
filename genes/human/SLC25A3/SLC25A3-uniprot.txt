CC   -!- FUNCTION: Inorganic ion transporter that transports phosphate or copper
CC       ions across the mitochondrial inner membrane into the matrix
CC       compartment (By similarity) (PubMed:17273968, PubMed:29237729).
CC       Mediates proton-coupled symport of phosphate ions necessary for
CC       mitochondrial oxidative phosphorylation of ADP to ATP (By similarity)
CC       (PubMed:17273968). Transports copper ions probably in the form of
CC       anionic copper(I) complexes to maintain mitochondrial matrix copper
CC       pool and to supply copper for cytochrome C oxidase complex assembly
CC       (PubMed:29237729). May also play a role in regulation of the
CC       mitochondrial permeability transition pore (mPTP) (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P12234, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P16036,
