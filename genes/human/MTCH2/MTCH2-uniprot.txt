CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protein insertase that mediates insertion of transmembrane
CC       proteins into the mitochondrial outer membrane (PubMed:36264797).
CC       Catalyzes insertion of proteins with alpha-helical transmembrane
CC       regions, such as signal-anchored, tail-anchored and multi-pass membrane
CC       proteins (PubMed:36264797). Does not mediate insertion of beta-barrel
CC       transmembrane proteins (PubMed:36264797). Also acts as a receptor for
CC       the truncated form of pro-apoptotic BH3-interacting domain death
CC       agonist (p15 BID) and has therefore a critical function in apoptosis
CC       (By similarity). Regulates the quiescence/cycling of hematopoietic stem
CC       cells (HSCs) (By similarity). Acts as a regulator of mitochondrial
CC       fusion, essential for the naive-to-primed interconversion of embryonic
CC       stem cells (ESCs) (By similarity). Acts as a regulator of lipid
CC       homeostasis and has a regulatory role in adipocyte differentiation and
CC       biology (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q791V5,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:36264797}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with p15BID (By similarity). Interacts with

CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:40105103}.

CC       {ECO:0000255}.
