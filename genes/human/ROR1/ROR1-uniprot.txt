CC   -!- FUNCTION: Has very low kinase activity in vitro and is unlikely to
CC       function as a tyrosine kinase in vivo (PubMed:25029443). Receptor for
CC       ligand WNT5A which activates downstream NFkB signaling pathway and may
CC       result in the inhibition of WNT3A-mediated signaling (PubMed:25029443,
CC       PubMed:27162350). In the inner ear, crucial for spiral ganglion neurons
CC       to innervate auditory hair cells (PubMed:27162350). Via IGFBP5 ligand,
CC       forms a complex with ERBB2 to enhance CREB oncogenic signaling
CC       (PubMed:36949068). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25029443,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:27162350, ECO:0000269|PubMed:36949068}.
