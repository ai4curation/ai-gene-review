CC   -!- FUNCTION: Central receptor component of the translocase of the outer
CC       membrane of mitochondria (TOM) complex essential for the recognition
CC       and translocation of cytosolically synthesized mitochondrial
CC       preproteins (PubMed:40080546). Together with TOMM22 functions as the
CC       transit peptide receptor at the surface of the mitochondrion outer
CC       membrane and facilitates the movement of preproteins into the TOM40
CC       translocation pore (PubMed:18331822). The TOM complex associates with
CC       the ion channel VDAC2 and PINK1 kinase at depolarized mitochondria,
CC       this interaction stabilizes PINK1 at the outer mitochondrial membrane
CC       and triggers downstream mitophagy by the recruitment of the E3
CC       ubiquitin ligase PRKN (PubMed:40080546). Required for the translocation
CC       across the mitochondrial outer membrane of cytochrome P450
CC       monooxygenases (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q62760,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18331822, ECO:0000269|PubMed:40080546}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the translocase of the outer mitochondrial membrane
CC       (TOM complex) consisting of at least TOMM5, TOMM6, TOMM7, TOMM20,
CC       TOMM22 and TOMM40 (PubMed:18331822, PubMed:40080546). TOMM70 may also
CC       be found in the TOM complex (PubMed:18331822). The TOM complex
CC       interacts with the VDAC2 homodimer (PubMed:40080546). Upon
CC       mitochondrial depolarization, the TOM-VDAC assembly interacts with
CC       PINK1; the interaction stabilizes PINK1 at the outer mitochondrial
CC       membrane and triggers downstream mitophagy (PubMed:38416681,
CC       PubMed:38848361, PubMed:40080546). Interacts with APEX1
CC       (PubMed:20231292). Interacts with TBC1D21 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9DCC8, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18331822,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:20231292, ECO:0000269|PubMed:38416681,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:38848361, ECO:0000269|PubMed:40080546}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       Q15388; Q9P0U1: TOMM7; NbExp=2; IntAct=EBI-711636, EBI-1180558;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Mitochondrion outer membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20628368, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25764979,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:25997101, ECO:0000269|PubMed:31644573}; Single-pass
CC       membrane protein {ECO:0000269|PubMed:40080546}.
CC   -!- PTM: Ubiquitinated by PRKN during mitophagy, leading to its degradation
CC       and enhancement of mitophagy. Deubiquitinated by USP30.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24896179, ECO:0000269|PubMed:25621951}.
