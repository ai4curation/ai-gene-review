CC       transformylase that incorporates a formyl group to the AMP analog AICAR
CC       (5-amino-1-(5-phospho-beta-D-ribosyl)imidazole-4-carboxamide) to
CC       produce the intermediate formyl-AICAR (FAICAR) (PubMed:10985775,
CC       PubMed:11948179, PubMed:9378707). Can use both 10-formyldihydrofolate
CC       and 10-formyltetrahydrofolate as the formyl donor in this reaction
CC       (PubMed:10985775). Also catalyzes the cyclization of FAICAR to inosine
CC       monophosphate (IMP) (PubMed:11948179, PubMed:14756554). Is able to
CC       convert thio-AICAR to 6-mercaptopurine ribonucleotide, an inhibitor of
CC       purine biosynthesis used in the treatment of human leukemias
CC       (PubMed:10985775). Promotes insulin receptor/INSR autophosphorylation
