CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Calcitonin]: Calcitonin is a peptide hormone that causes a
CC       rapid but short-lived drop in the level of calcium and phosphate in
CC       blood by promoting the incorporation of those ions in the bones.
CC       Calcitonin function is mediated by the calcitonin receptor/CALCR and
CC       the CALCR-RAMP2 (AMYR2) receptor complex (PubMed:35324283).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:35324283}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: [Katacalcin]: Katacalcin is a potent plasma calcium-lowering
CC       peptide. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:6132180}.
