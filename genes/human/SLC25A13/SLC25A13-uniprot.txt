CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mitochondrial electrogenic aspartate/glutamate antiporter
CC       that favors efflux of aspartate and entry of glutamate and proton
CC       within the mitochondria as part of the malate-aspartate shuttle
CC       (PubMed:11566871, PubMed:38937634, PubMed:38945283, PubMed:39419476).
CC       Substrate exchange across the membrane occurs consecutively with one
CC       substrate being transported first, then dissociating from the substrate
CC       binding site before the second substrate binds for transport in the
CC       opposite direction (PubMed:38937634). Also mediates the uptake of L-
CC       cysteinesulfinate (3-sulfino-L-alanine) by mitochondria in exchange for
CC       L-glutamate and proton (PubMed:11566871). Can also exchange L-
CC       cysteinesulfinate with aspartate in their anionic form without any
CC       proton translocation (PubMed:11566871). Lacks transport activity
CC       towards gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) (PubMed:38945283).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11566871, ECO:0000269|PubMed:38937634,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:38945283, ECO:0000269|PubMed:39419476}.
