CC   -!- FUNCTION: Histone methyltransferase that specifically trimethylates
CC       'Lys-9' of histone H3 using mono- and dimethylated H3 'Lys-9' as
CC       substrate (PubMed:22939621, PubMed:28428426). Acts redundantly with the
CC       methyltransferase met-2 to position chromosome arms at the nuclear
CC       lamina (PubMed:22939621). Required for small-RNA-induced H3K9
CC       methylation (PubMed:26365259). Together with met-2, protects and
CC       stabilizes repeat-rich genomic regions by suppressing transcription-
CC       induced replication stress through methylation of H3K9
CC       (PubMed:27668659). Suppresses transcription from various repetitive
CC       genomic elements through methylation of H3K9; exposure to moderately
CC       raised temperature reduces methylation by set-25 leading to de-
CC       repression of transcription of repetitive elements lasting at least 3
CC       subsequent generations, despite culture at lower temperatures
CC       (PubMed:28428426). May play a role in transgenerational transmission of
CC       environmental information (PubMed:28428426).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22939621, ECO:0000269|PubMed:26365259,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:27668659}.
