CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions in mitochondrial division (PubMed:10619028,
CC       PubMed:15716954, PubMed:18722182, PubMed:19327994). Functions in
CC       peroxisomal division (By similarity). Mediates membrane fission,
CC       perhaps mainly of the mitochondrial outer membrane (PubMed:10619028).
CC       Mitochondrial fission may be promoted by recruitment to mitochondrial
CC       membranes via the egl-1/ced-9 complex (PubMed:21949250). Involved in
CC       the coordination of mitochondrial division with autophagy in response
CC       to acute heat stress during larval development (PubMed:33734301). Plays
CC       a role in apoptosis by promoting mitochondrial elimination and cell-
CC       death execution, acting downstream of caspase ced-3, and perhaps
CC       independently of FIS1-related protein fis-2, caspase ced-9 and
CC       apoptosis-inducing factor AIFM/wah-1 (PubMed:15716954,
CC       PubMed:18722182). Role in promoting apoptosis dependent upon cleavage
CC       of drp-1 by ced-3 (PubMed:18722182). Involved in negatively modulating
CC       longevity in concert with the Insulin/IGF-1-like signaling (IIS)
CC       mediated pathway (PubMed:21463460). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O00429,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10619028, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15716954,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18722182, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19327994,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21463460, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21949250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:33734301}.
