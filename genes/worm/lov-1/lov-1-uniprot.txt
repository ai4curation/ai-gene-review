CC       targeting of pkd-2. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10517638,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11553327, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12411744,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15563610, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15753033,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16943275}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via PLAT domain) with atp-2 (via N-terminus) and
CC       with kin-10 (via C-terminus). Interacts (via C-terminus) with isoform a
CC       of stam-1/pqn-19 (via C-terminus). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15563610,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16481400, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17581863}.

CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15563610, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17581863}.
