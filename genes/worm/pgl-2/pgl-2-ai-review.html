Gene Description

P granule abnormality protein 2 (PGL-2) is a temporally-restricted P granule component expressed only during postembryonic development in C. elegans germline. Unlike constitutive PGL-1/PGL-3 which have ribonuclease activity, PGL-2 functions primarily as an RNA-binding structural component without enzymatic activity. It participates in P granule assembly, post-transcriptional regulation, and germline development through protein-protein interactions with PGL-1 and PGL-3, but provides limited functional redundancy compared to the strong PGL-1/PGL-3 backup system.
