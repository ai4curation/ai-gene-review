Gene Description

BtuE (XCC4213) is a predicted glutathione peroxidase-family protein in Xanthomonas campestris pv. campestris. The protein contains a conserved glutathione peroxidase domain (IPR000889, PF00255) and a thioredoxin-like fold (IPR036249), with a predicted active-site residue at position 37. As a member of the glutathione peroxidase family, BtuE is expected to catalyze the reduction of hydrogen peroxide and organic hydroperoxides using glutathione or a thiol-based reductant, thereby protecting the cell from oxidative damage. No direct experimental characterization of this protein in X. campestris has been reported; functional inference is based entirely on conserved domain architecture and family membership.
