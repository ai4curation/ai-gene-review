CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as a scaffold protein and likely plays a role in N-
CC       methyl-D-aspartic acid receptor (NMDAR)-mediated synaptic excitatory
CC       transmission (PubMed:31196628). May be involved in synapse formation in
CC       cone photoreceptor cells (PubMed:29590622). May play a role in the
CC       regulation of tight junction formation (By similarity). Binds
CC       phosphatidylinositol 3,4-bisphosphate (PtdIns(3,4)P2) (By similarity).
CC       May pNF-kappa-Blay a role in the regulation of NOD2-mediated NF-kappa-B
CC       activation in immune response (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3, ECO:0000269|PubMed:29590622,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31196628}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via the second PDZ domain) with CTNND2 (via the
CC       extreme C-terminus) (By similarity). Interacts (via the second PDZ
CC       domain) with PKP4 (via the extreme C-terminus); the interaction directs
CC       FRMPD2 to the basolateral membranes (By similarity). Interacts (via the
CC       second PDZ domain) with ARVCF (via the extreme C-terminus) (By
CC       similarity). Interacts (via the second PDZ domain) with NMDAR subunits
CC       GRIN2A/GLUN2A and GRIN2B/GLUN2B (via the extreme C-terminus); the
CC       interaction is direct and is likely to promote NMDAR-mediated neural
CC       signal transmission (PubMed:31196628). Binds GRIN2A with lower affinity
CC       than GRIN2B (PubMed:31196628). Interacts (via the third PDZ domain)
CC       with LRIT1 (via the extreme C-terminus); the interaction leads to their
CC       colocalization in photoreceptor synapses (PubMed:29590622). Interacts
CC       with NOD2; the interaction is likely to trigger NOD2-mediated nuclear
CC       factor kappaB activation (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3, ECO:0000269|PubMed:29590622,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:31196628}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3}.
CC       Postsynaptic density {ECO:0000269|PubMed:31196628}. Basolateral cell
CC       membrane {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3}. Cell junction, tight junction
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3}. Note=Colocalizes with CTNNB1, CDH1 and
CC       PKP4 at the basolateral membrane. Colocalizes with TJP1 at tight
CC       junctions. Its recruitment to cell-cell contacts requires CDH1.
CC       Colocalizes with NOD2 to the basolacteral membrane in intestinal
CC       epithelial cells. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The FERM and PDZ 2 domains are necessary for localization to
CC       the basolateral cell membrane. The FERM domain binds to
CC       phosphatidylinositol 3,4-bisphosphate (PtdIns(3,4)P2) and is sufficient
CC       for membrane localization. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q68DX3}.
