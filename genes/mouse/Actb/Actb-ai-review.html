Gene Description

Actb (Beta-actin) is a highly conserved cytoplasmic actin isoform that polymerizes to form actin filaments (F-actin), a major component of the cytoskeleton. Functions in cell motility, cell division, cytokinesis, and maintenance of cell shape. Also participates in nuclear functions including chromatin remodeling as part of BAF/SWI-SNF complexes. Has intrinsic ATP hydrolysis activity essential for actin dynamics.
