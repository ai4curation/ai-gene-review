Gene Description

Breast cancer type 1 susceptibility protein homolog (BRCA1). E3 ubiquitin-protein ligase that forms a heterodimer with BARD1 to mediate K6-linked polyubiquitination. Plays a central role in DNA repair by facilitating cellular responses to DNA damage, particularly double-strand break repair via homologous recombination. Component of the BRCA1-A complex and BASC complex. Also functions as a transcriptional regulator. Required for appropriate cell cycle checkpoint responses after ionizing irradiation.
