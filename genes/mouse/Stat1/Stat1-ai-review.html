Gene Description

Signal transducer and activator of transcription 1 (STAT1). Transcription factor that mediates cellular responses to interferons (IFN-alpha, IFN-beta, IFN-gamma) and other cytokines. Activated by phosphorylation through JAK kinases, forms homodimers (GAF) or heterodimers with STAT2 (ISGF3) to regulate transcription of interferon-stimulated genes. Critical for antiviral responses and immune function.
