Gene Description

Tert (Telomerase reverse transcriptase) is the catalytic protein subunit of telomerase, essential for telomere maintenance. Catalyzes RNA-directed DNA synthesis to add TTAGGG repeats to chromosome ends using the telomerase RNA component (Terc) as template. Critical for cellular immortalization and tissue regeneration. Mouse Tert has higher telomerase activity than human, and mice have longer telomeres.
