Gene Description

Jak1 (Janus kinase 1) is a non-receptor tyrosine kinase essential for cytokine signaling. Contains FERM, SH2-like, pseudokinase, and kinase domains. Required for Type I/II interferon signaling, IL-6 family cytokines, and common gamma-chain dependent cytokines. Phosphorylates and activates STAT transcription factors. Essential for immune cell development and antiviral responses in mouse.
