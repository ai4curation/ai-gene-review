Gene Description

Phosphatidylinositol 3,4,5-trisphosphate 3-phosphatase and dual-specificity protein phosphatase (PTEN). Major tumor suppressor that dephosphorylates phosphatidylinositol-3,4,5-trisphosphate (PIP3) to negatively regulate the PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. Also acts as a protein phosphatase on serine/threonine and tyrosine residues. Critical for cell cycle control, apoptosis regulation, and cell migration.
