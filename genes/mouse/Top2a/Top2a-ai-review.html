Gene Description

Top2a (DNA topoisomerase 2-alpha) is an essential nuclear enzyme that catalyzes ATP-dependent double-strand DNA passage to resolve DNA supercoiling, decatenate sister chromatids, and unknot DNA. Required for DNA replication, chromosome condensation, and mitotic chromosome segregation. Highly expressed in proliferating cells. Target of anticancer drugs like doxorubicin and etoposide.
