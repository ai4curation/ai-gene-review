Summary: OVER-ANNOTATION: Cyclin B1 does NOT catalyze phosphorylation itself - it activates CDK1 (Cdc2) which performs the catalytic phosphorylation. PMID:12124778 studies the "Cdk1/CyclinB1 kinase complex" where the kinase activity belongs to CDK1. UniProt confirms: "Interacts with the CDC2 protein kinase to form a serine/threonine kinase holoenzyme complex...The cyclin subunit imparts substrate specificity to the complex." The correct annotation should be to kinase activator activity (GO:0061575, already annotated via IEA) rather than the phosphorylation process.
Proposed replacements: regulation of protein kinase activity
