Identity verification and core definitions
- Gene/protein identity and organism: The mouse Uox gene encodes urate oxidase (uricase; EC 1.7.3.3), a tetrameric enzyme in terrestrial vertebrates that oxidizes uric acid in purine catabolism; apes/humans have lost uricase activity by pseudogenization, but rodents, including mouse (Mus musculus), retain functional Uox (PNAS, 2014; https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1320393111).
