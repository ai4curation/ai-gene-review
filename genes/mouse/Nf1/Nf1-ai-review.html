Gene Description

Nf1 (Neurofibromin 1) is a large tumor suppressor protein that functions as a GTPase-activating protein (GAP) for Ras. The GAP-related domain (GRD) stimulates intrinsic GTPase activity of Ras proteins, accelerating conversion from active GTP-bound to inactive GDP-bound state. Also contains SEC14/CRAL-TRIO lipid-binding domain. Loss-of-function causes neurofibromatosis type 1-like phenotypes in mouse models. Essential for neural development and tumor suppression.
