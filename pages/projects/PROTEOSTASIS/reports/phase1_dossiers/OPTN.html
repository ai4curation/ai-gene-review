  • UniProt: Q96CV9
  • In branches: ALP, UPS
  • Notes: Receptor for selective autophagy. Binds to ATG8 and ubiquitinated or degron-contaning substrates. Active in aggrephagy, Ub-dependent mitophagy, xenophagy.
