Bacterial protein export

Boundary module for KEGG ppu03060 protein export in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The strict bacterial protein-export story combines Sec-dependent post-translational translocation through SecA/SecYEG with SecDF-YajC support, SRP/FtsY co-translational membrane-protein targeting, YidC membrane-protein insertion, LepB/LspA signal peptide processing, and two TatABC-like loci for twin-arginine export of folded substrates. Type I secretion ABC exporters, LolCDE lipoprotein outer-membrane localization, and broad secretion-system maps are neighboring modules rather than core ppu03060 protein export here.
