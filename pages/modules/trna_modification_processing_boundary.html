tRNA modification, editing, and processing boundary

Boundary module for PSEPK tRNA modification and processing genes from the translation/RNA-processing umbrella. It covers wobble-uridine side-chain chemistry, t6A37 formation, tRNA methylation, pseudouridylation, deamination, lysidine formation, thiolation, dihydrouridine synthesis, aminocarboxypropylation, prenylation, and CCA-end processing.
