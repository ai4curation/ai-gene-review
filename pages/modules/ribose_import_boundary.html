Ribose import boundary

Boundary module for two ribose-like ABC importer loci in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. PP_2454-PP_2456 encodes a predicted Rbs D-ribose ABC importer with the nearby PP_2459 RbsD D-ribose pyranase accessory enzyme, while PP_2758-PP_2761 encodes a reviewed-UniProt-supported RbsABC D-ribose importer. PP_2757 is an adjacent cell-envelope sugar-binding protein whose ligand and relationship to the PP_2758-PP_2761 transporter remain unresolved.

This boundary module was seeded from C33 and extended with C39 of the ppu02010 partition. It should remain separate from glucose_import_boundary because the PP_2454-PP_2456 and PP_2758-PP_2761 transporters are ribose-specific or ribose-like, whereas PP_1015-PP_1018 is the Gts glucose uptake system. Current first-pass evidence supports PP_2758/rbsA/PP_2760/PP_2761 as a second D-ribose ABC importer, but PP_2757 remains an adjacent unresolved sugar-binding protein whose ligand and chemotaxis relationship require experimental testing.
