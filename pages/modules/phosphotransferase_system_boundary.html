Phosphotransferase system boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu02060 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains the fructose PTS branch fruB-fruK-fruA plus PTS/Ntr phosphorelay components ptsH, ptsN, and ptsP. The satisfiable biology is a fructose-specific PEP-dependent PTS uptake/catabolic entry route and a distinct HPr/PtsP/PtsN regulatory phosphorelay context, not a generic all-sugar PTS transport system.

This module records a boundary around the broad KEGG PTS map. FruBKA is the concrete fructose-specific PTS transport and catabolic-entry branch. PtsH, PtsP, and PtsN are retained as regulatory/general phosphorelay context; no additional substrate-specific PTS transport annotation is proposed from this bucket.

Part 1: Fructose-specific PTS uptake and catabolic entry
FruBKA fructose PTS branchBiological Processfructose_pts_branch

FruB supplies the cytoplasmic PEP-dependent phosphorelay, FruA imports and phosphorylates fructose, and FruK converts fructose 1-phosphate to fructose 1,6-bisphosphate for central carbohydrate metabolism.
