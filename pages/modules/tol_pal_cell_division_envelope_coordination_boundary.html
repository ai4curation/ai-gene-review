Tol-Pal envelope and cell-division coordination boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 Tol-Pal-mediated envelope coordination during cell division. The module captures TolQ, TolR, TolA, TolB, and Pal as the envelope-spanning Tol-Pal machinery supporting outer-membrane invagination and envelope integrity, with CpoB as an adjacent periplasmic coordinator of peptidoglycan synthesis and outer-membrane constriction.
