Bacterial flagellar assembly boundary

Umbrella boundary module for KEGG ppu02040 flagellar assembly in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The PSEPK primary set is a coherent flagellar biology map, but it is too broad for one gene-review batch because it combines flagellar regulation, export and assembly factors, basal-body/hook/filament structural proteins, motor/switch/stator components, and nonflagellar transport/envelope spillovers. Curate it through those sub-buckets rather than as one undifferentiated flagellar pathway.

This parent module is an index over completed first-pass submodules. The submodule reviews carry the gene-level flagellar or spillover claims; this umbrella documents ppu02040 pathway satisfiability and keeps the structural apparatus, export machinery, motor machinery, regulation, and nonflagellar side entries separated.
