Pili, adhesion, and surface-structure boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02020 pili/surface-adhesion partition and the motility/chemotaxis/biofilm fimbrial extension in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. It captures the existing PilA type IV pilin review, a FimC/FimD-like chaperone-usher pair extended with PP_1888 and fimI structural-subunit candidates, a PP_2356 phytochrome-like sensor near a Csu-like chaperone-usher cluster, and a PP_3126 polysaccharide-export side node. This is surface-structure context from broad signaling/motility umbrella maps rather than one canonical signaling pathway.

Several Csu-like subunits currently have no fetched GOA rows. They are represented conservatively as structural/pilus-assembly candidates from local gene context, product names, and FanG-like domains. PP_1888 and fimI extend the FimD/PP_1890 chaperone-usher context as predicted fimbrial structural subunits; PP_0655 is excluded from this module because its Hcp/T6SS domains support secretion-system context rather than a fimbrial-subunit role.
