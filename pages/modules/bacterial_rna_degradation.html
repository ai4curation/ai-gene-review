Bacterial RNA degradation

Boundary module for KEGG ppu03018 RNA degradation in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The strict RNA-turnover core includes RppH-mediated 5'-end activation, RNase E endonucleolytic cleavage, PcnB polyadenylation, PNPase and RNase R exonucleolytic decay, and DEAD-box RNA helicases that remodel RNA or ribonucleoprotein substrates. The KEGG bucket also contains Hfq and Rho as RNA-stability/transcription-termination context, enolase and chaperones as degradosome/proteostasis side nodes, polyphosphate kinases as nucleotide/polyP side context, and RecQ as a likely DNA-repair spillover rather than an RNA degradation factor.
