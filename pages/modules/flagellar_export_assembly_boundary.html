Flagellar export and assembly boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02040 flagellar export/assembly subset in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The first-pass module covers the FlhA/FlhB/FliPQR membrane export gate, FliO accessory context, the FliI/FliH/FliJ cytosolic-side export-energy module, hook-length control by FliK, and assembly/chaperone support from FliT, FliS, FlgA, and PP_4396/FlgN-like. The motility/chemotaxis/biofilm functional-bucket extension adds PP_4328, PP_4329, PP_4342, flhF, and PP_4377 as flagellar hook-length, export-gate, localization/number-control, and FlaG accessory spillovers. It separates export and assembly support from the motor/switch/stator, basal-body/hook/ filament structural, flagellar-regulation, and nonflagellar envelope buckets.

First-pass interpretation: the membrane export gate is represented by FlhA/FlhB and FliP/FliQ/FliR with FliO as accessory context. FliI is the ATPase, while FliH and FliJ are cytosolic-side export-energy/accessory components. FliK controls hook length. FliS, FliT, FlgA, and PP_4396/FlgN-like provide assembly-chaperone or late-assembly support. Broad protein secretion, protein targeting, chemotaxis, motility, and ATP-synthase-like automated terms are retained only where appropriate or marked as over-broad in the individual gene reviews. The motility functional-bucket extension adds a short PP_4329 FlhB-family exporter next to the canonical flhB/Q88EV1 component, a PP_4328 FliK-like hook-control candidate, FlhF/PP_4342 placement or number-control GTPase/ATPase context, and PP_4377 FlaG accessory context.
