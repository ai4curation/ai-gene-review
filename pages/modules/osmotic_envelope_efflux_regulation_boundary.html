Osmotic envelope and efflux regulation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02020 osmotic/envelope/efflux partition in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. It captures EnvZ/OmpR and EvgS/EvgA-like phosphorelay regulators plus the adjacent MdtABC RND multidrug efflux pump context. This is a partition of the broad KEGG two-component-system map, not a single linear pathway.

The partition table groups ompR, envZ, PP_2100, evgA, mdtC, mdtB, and mdtA into the osmotic/envelope/efflux regulation bucket.

The MdtABC efflux pump is transport context for the regulatory bucket, not a His-Asp phosphorelay component. Its subunits are kept in the same boundary module because the KEGG ppu02020 umbrella groups local regulatory and efflux context together.
