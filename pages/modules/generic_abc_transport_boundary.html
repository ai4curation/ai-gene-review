Generic and residual ABC/transporter component boundary

Boundary module for the PSEPK transport/membrane/efflux generic ABC residual split. The batch is not a single substrate pathway: it contains MacB/ABC-3-like efflux components, fused ABCB/type-I-exporter-like proteins, binding-protein-dependent importer pieces, named sulfonate, amino-acid, cobalamin, and heme/hemin spillover components, plus non-ABC outliers.

PSEPK transport/membrane/efflux generic ABC transporter batch
The partition groups 33 residual ABC-family or ABC-like transport-bucket genes for first-pass curation.

First-pass interpretation: keep functions component-level. Several genes are spillovers from better biological modules (c-di-GMP signaling, MlaA phospholipid transfer, thiamine biosynthesis, amino-acid/sulfonate/cobalamin/hemin import) but are represented here to close the residual transport split without asserting one generic ABC pathway.
