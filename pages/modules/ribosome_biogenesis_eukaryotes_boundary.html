Ribosome biogenesis in eukaryotes boundary

Boundary/absence module for KEGG ppu03008 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains rnc/PP_1433, PP_2912, and orn/PP_4902. These are native bacterial RNA-processing or kinase-like side nodes: RNase III rRNA processing, oligoribonuclease RNA-fragment turnover, and an inferred RIO-type serine/threonine kinase-like protein. This should not be interpreted as evidence for a eukaryotic ribosome-biogenesis pathway in a bacterium.
