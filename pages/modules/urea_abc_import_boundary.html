Urea ABC import boundary

Boundary module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 PP_4841-PP_4845 UrtABCDE urea ABC importer pulled from the KEGG ppu02010 ABC-transporter umbrella map. UrtA is the signal-peptide-bearing periplasmic substrate-binding component, UrtB and UrtC are the inner-membrane permeases, and UrtD and UrtE are the ATP-binding energy-coupling subunits. The module records urea import as the core first-pass function and treats amino-acid or branched-chain amino-acid annotations on the locus as likely family-transfer spillover.
