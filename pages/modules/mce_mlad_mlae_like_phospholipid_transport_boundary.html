PP_0140-PP_0142 Mce/MlaD-MlaE-like phospholipid transport candidate boundary

Boundary module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 ppu02010 C03 PP_0140-PP_0142 locus. PP_0140 is an Mce/MlaD-domain protein, PP_0141 is an ABC ATP-binding protein, and PP_0142 is an inner-membrane MlaE-family lipid ABC transporter permease. The module records a putative envelope phospholipid ABC transporter candidate, distinct from the better supported PP_0958-PP_0962 Mla/Ttg2 system.
