tRNA m7G46 methylation

A Pseudomonas putida KT2440 UniPathway UPA00989 module for SAM-dependent formation of N7-methylguanosine at position 46 of tRNA. The module is centered on TrmB, the tRNA (guanine(46)-N7)-methyltransferase encoded by PP_5103. Broader RNA methylation and methyltransferase-complex annotations are retained only as context unless supported by more specific evidence.

OpenScientist PSEPK UPA00989 pathway satisfiability review
The PSEPK-specific OpenScientist report finds UPA00989 covered by the sole trmB/PP_5103/Q88CS7 candidate, with no paralog ambiguity and no expected bacterial Trm82/WDR4-like second subunit; it reinforces the GO:0043527 tRNA methyltransferase complex annotation as likely over-propagated.

First-pass interpretation: UPA00989 is satisfiable as a single-enzyme tRNA modification module in KT2440. The pathway boundary should not imply a broader RNA methylation module or a defined tRNA methyltransferase complex without additional biochemical or interaction evidence. OpenScientist's PSEPK-specific review supports this boundary: Q88CS7 is the unique KT2440 TrmB/EC 2.1.1.33/PF02390 candidate, bacterial TrmB is treated as a stand-alone enzyme, and a eukaryotic Trm82/WDR4-like accessory subunit is not expected in this taxon.
