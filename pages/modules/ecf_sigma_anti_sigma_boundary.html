ECF sigma and anti-sigma regulation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02020 ECF sigma/anti-sigma partition in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. It captures predicted sigma-70 ECF sigma factors and adjacent or orphan FecR-like transmembrane anti-sigma/sensor proteins. This is a curated regulatory boundary from the broad KEGG two-component-system map rather than a canonical His-Asp phosphorelay.
