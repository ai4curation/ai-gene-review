C5-branched dibasic acid metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00660 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific map is not one dedicated C5-branched dibasic acid pathway: it combines acetolactate/acetohydroxy-acid synthase enzymes and leucine biosynthesis branch enzymes with a GalC gallate-catabolism aldolase and the central-carbon SucCD succinate-CoA ligase complex. It is curated here as an overlap between branched-chain amino acid biosynthesis, gallate degradation, and TCA-cycle succinyl-CoA turnover.
