Osmoprotectant compatible-solute transport boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 choline, glycine betaine, and carnitine compatible-solute uptake systems pulled from the KEGG ppu02010 ABC transporter umbrella map. The PP_0294-PP_0296 CbcV/CbcW/CbcX locus forms a coherent predicted ABC importer for choline/betaine/carnitine-family compatible solutes. The PP_0868-PP_0871 Yeh-like locus forms a second predicted quaternary-amine/glycine-betaine ABC importer. The opuA-PP_2775 block forms a predicted glycine-betaine/L-proline compatible-solute importer with an ATPase/permease fusion plus periplasmic binding component. The PP_3558-PP_3559 pair is a partial predicted glycine-betaine compatible-solute ABC importer with binding and permease components, but the ATPase partner is unresolved. PP_0304 CaiX, PP_0076, and BetX are related periplasmic substrate-binding components with compatible-solute binding-family domains; they are represented as related binding components until cognate permease/ATPase pairings or substrate preferences are verified. The adjacent PP_0873 PotF-I putrescine-binding component is split into the polyamine transport boundary rather than folded into this compatible-solute module.
