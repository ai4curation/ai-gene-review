Aminobenzoate degradation boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00627 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific aminobenzoate-degradation map mixes gallate, ferulate, vanillin/vanillate, nitroaromatic reductase, Baeyer-Villiger monooxygenase, UbiX/prenyl-FMN, enoyl-CoA hydratase, and unresolved amidase side nodes. It does not represent one complete aminobenzoate catabolic pathway in KT2440.
