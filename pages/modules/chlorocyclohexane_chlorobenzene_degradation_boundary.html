Chlorocyclohexane/chlorobenzene degradation boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00361 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The PSEPK organism-specific map contains only catechol ortho-cleavage/beta-ketoadipate lower-pathway enzymes, not a complete chlorocyclohexane or chlorobenzene upper degradation route. The module therefore records the supported catechol ring-cleavage and muconate-cycloisomerase chemistry while explicitly treating chlorinated-aromatic degradation as pathway-map context rather than a satisfiable KT2440 module.
