Folate biosynthesis

A bacterial folate biosynthesis module covering formation of the pterin precursor from GTP, formation of p-aminobenzoate from chorismate, condensation to dihydropteroate, conversion to dihydrofolate, reduction to tetrahydrofolate, and folylpolyglutamate formation. In Pseudomonas putida KT2440, KEGG ppu00790 is broader than strict folate synthesis: it also pulls in queuosine, molybdenum cofactor, tetrahydrobiopterin/phenylalanine hydroxylase, riboflavin, and generic MobA-like side nodes. Those are tracked as boundary context unless they directly form folate or tetrahydrofolate.
