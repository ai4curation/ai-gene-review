Chromosome dimer resolution and DNA translocation boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 chromosome dimer resolution and division-coupled DNA translocation. The module captures XerC and XerD as the cytoplasmic tyrosine recombinases that resolve chromosome dimers, FtsK as the septal membrane DNA translocase that coordinates chromosome segregation with cell division and activates Xer recombination, and PP_2841 as a related integrase-family recombinase candidate outside the core XerCD-FtsK system.
