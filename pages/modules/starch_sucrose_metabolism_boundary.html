Starch and sucrose metabolism boundary

Broad boundary module for KEGG ppu00500 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The PSEPK membership centers on bacterial glycogen/alpha-glucan metabolism, trehalose formation from maltooligosaccharides, UDP-glucose-dependent cellulose synthesis, and shared sugar-phosphate or cell-envelope precursor nodes. The module separates those contexts rather than treating the KEGG map as a plant-like starch/sucrose pathway.
