Flagellar regulation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02040 flagellar regulatory subset in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The first-pass module covers the housekeeping sigma factor RpoD, sigma-54/RpoN, the flagellar sigma factor FliA, the FleQ master regulator, the AtoC/FleR-like sigma-54-associated response regulator, and the anti-sigma factor FlgM. It separates transcriptional control of the flagellar program from the export apparatus, structural apparatus, motor/switch/stator, and nonflagellar spillover buckets.

First-pass interpretation: RpoD is included because KEGG ppu02040 carries the housekeeping sigma factor, but it is not flagellum-specific. RpoN/sigma-54 and FleQ provide the main early flagellar regulatory layer, with AtoC retained as a nearby NtrC/FleR-like response-regulator transcriptional activator whose exact KT2440 promoter targets remain unresolved. FliA/sigma-28 drives late flagellar gene transcription, while FlgM gates FliA activity as the anti-sigma checkpoint factor. This module does not treat biofilm, c-di-GMP, or global housekeeping transcription as part of the structural flagellar apparatus.
