QseBC-like two-component regulation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02024 QseBC-like two-component regulation bucket in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The bucket contains two predicted membrane sensor kinase/DNA-binding response-regulator pairs (PP_2714-PP_2713 and PP_4224-qseB) plus the KdpE-like DNA-binding response activator kdpE. This is the most regulatory component of the ppu02024 quorum-sensing umbrella, but the first-pass evidence supports generic two-component phosphorelay transcription regulation rather than a resolved LuxI/LuxR quorum-sensing route.
