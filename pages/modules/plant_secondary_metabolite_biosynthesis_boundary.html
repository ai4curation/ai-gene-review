Plant secondary metabolite biosynthesis boundary

Boundary/absence module for KEGG ppu00999 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains three shikimate-dehydrogenase-like proteins, one periplasmic beta-glucosidase, and S-adenosylmethionine synthase. These are real central precursor, glycoside-hydrolysis, and methyl donor side nodes, but they do not constitute a complete plant secondary metabolite biosynthesis pathway in this bacterium.
