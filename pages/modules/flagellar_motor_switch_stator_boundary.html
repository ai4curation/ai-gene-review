Flagellar motor, switch, and stator boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02040 flagellar motor/switch/stator subset in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The first-pass module covers the MotCD stator candidate pair encoded in the main flagellar cluster (PP_4336/PP_4335), the second MotAB stator pair (motA/motB), the FliG/FliM/FliN C-ring switch/rotor, two FliL-family motor-associated proteins, and the YcgR c-di-GMP-dependent flagellar brake from the motility/chemotaxis/biofilm functional-bucket extension. It separates motor operation from the broader ppu02040 export/assembly, basal-body/hook/filament, flagellar-regulation, and c-di-GMP turnover buckets.

First-pass interpretation: PP_4336/PP_4335 are treated as the MotCD stator pair (MotC-like A subunit and MotD-like B subunit) on the basis of the P. putida stator study and UniProt/domain evidence. motA/motB are the second MotAB stator. FliG/FliM/FliN form the C-ring switch/rotor. fliL and PP_5209 are retained as FliL-family basal-body/motor-associated support proteins, but their exact stator partner and load-dependent requirements are not resolved in this pass. ycgR is added as a c-di-GMP-binding flagellar brake that acts on motor output rather than c-di-GMP turnover.
