Basic-amino-acid ABC import boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 basic-amino-acid ABC-transporter loci pulled from the KEGG ppu02010 ABC-transporter umbrella map. PP_0280-PP_0283 encodes an Art/Aot system: PP_0280 is an ArtM-family permease, PP_0281 is an ArtQ-like permease, artJ is a signal-peptide-bearing L-arginine periplasmic binding component, and aotP is the ATP-binding energy-coupling subunit named for arginine/ornithine transport. PP_4483-PP_4486 encodes a related His/ArgT-family system: HisP is the ATP-binding subunit, HisM and HisQ are permeases, and ArgT is a lysine/arginine/ornithine periplasmic substrate-binding protein. The module records coherent predicted basic-amino-acid uptake systems while leaving exact substrate preference among histidine, lysine, arginine, and ornithine unresolved.

PP_0280-PP_0283 Art/Aot arginine/ornithine ABC importTransport Stepart_aot_arginine_ornithine_abc_import

The PP_0280-PP_0283 locus encodes a predicted amino-acid ABC importer. ArtM and the ArtQ-like PP_0281 protein are the membrane permeases, ArtJ is the L-arginine periplasmic binding component, and AotP is the ATP-binding energy-coupling component. AotP's product name supports an arginine/ornithine transporter assignment, but the ligand range and relative arginine versus ornithine specificity remain open.
