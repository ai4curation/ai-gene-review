Folate one-carbon interconversion (MTHFD1 cytosolic, MTHFD2 + MTHFD1L mitochondrial)

One-carbon (folate) metabolism supplies activated one-carbon units for de novo purine and thymidylate synthesis and for the methionine/methylation cycle. The one-carbon units are carried on tetrahydrofolate (THF) at three oxidation states — 5,10-methylene-THF, 5,10-methenyl-THF and 10-formyl-THF — and are interconverted by the MTHFD family of enzymes, which are compartmentalised between cytosol and mitochondria and run the cycle in opposite directions. In the cytosol the trifunctional MTHFD1 (C-1-THF synthase) reversibly interconverts all three states: an NADP-linked methylenetetrahydrofolate dehydrogenase, a methenyltetrahydrofolate cyclohydrolase and a formate-tetrahydrofolate ligase (10-formyl-THF synthetase), predominantly assembling one-carbon units for cytosolic nucleotide synthesis. In mitochondria, serine (via SHMT2) generates 5,10-methylene-THF, which the bifunctional NAD-dependent MTHFD2 (dehydrogenase/cyclohydrolase, requiring Mg2+ and phosphate) oxidises to 10-formyl-THF; the monofunctional MTHFD1L (10-formyl-THF synthetase) then releases formate, exporting the one-carbon unit to the cytosol where MTHFD1 re-incorporates it onto THF. This mitochondrion-to-cytosol formate shuttle is the dominant source of one-carbon units in proliferating cells, making MTHFD2 in particular a prominent cancer-metabolism target. Inherited MTHFD1 deficiency causes a combined immunodeficiency with megaloblastic anemia; MTHFD1 and MTHFD1L variants are neural-tube-defect risk factors.

Folate one-carbon interconversion (GO:0035999 / GO:0006730) by the MTHFD family, grounded to the completed human gene reviews: cytosolic trifunctional MTHFD1 (P11586, GO:0004488 NADP+ dehydrogenase + GO:0004477 cyclohydrolase + GO:0004329 formate-THF ligase) and the mitochondrial arm MTHFD2 (P13995, GO:0004487 NAD+ dehydrogenase + GO:0004477 cyclohydrolase, Mg2+/phosphate- dependent) and MTHFD1L (Q6UB35, GO:0004329 formate-THF ligase; its N-terminal dehydrogenase/ cyclohydrolase domain is catalytically dead — the InterPro-predicted NADP+-dehydrogenase MF was REMOVEd in the gene review as a wrong IEA). All three are paralogues in PANTHER PTHR48099; the NAD+ (GO:0004487) vs NADP+ (GO:0004488) dehydrogenase distinction is the key MTHFD2-vs-MTHFD1 difference. This module sits directly downstream of serine_biosynthesis_one_carbon (SHMT generates the methylene-THF) and feeds de novo purine/thymidylate synthesis and the methionine/ methylation cycle; MTHFR (methylene-THF reductase, on main) diverts methylene-THF to 5-methyl-THF for methionine synthesis. GO term ids/labels verified against the local go.db. Disorders: MTHFD1 deficiency — combined immunodeficiency with megaloblastic anemia; MTHFD1/ MTHFD1L — neural-tube-defect susceptibility.
