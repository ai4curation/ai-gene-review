Taurine and hypotaurine metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00430 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific map contains TauD/AtsK, a directly supported alpha-ketoglutarate-dependent taurine dioxygenase, plus acetate, glutamate, and glutathione metabolism side nodes. This module records the supported taurine/sulfonate catabolic step while keeping phosphate acetyltransferase, glutamate dehydrogenase, and glutathione hydrolases as boundary context.
