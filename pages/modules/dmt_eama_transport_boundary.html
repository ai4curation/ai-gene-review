DMT/EamA small drug and metabolite transport boundary

Boundary module for the PSEPK transport/membrane/efflux split containing DMT, EamA, RarD, YedA/RhtA, and SMR/SugE/EmrE-like small drug or metabolite transporter candidates. The module keeps substrate, direction, and coupling assignments conservative unless product or family evidence is specific.
