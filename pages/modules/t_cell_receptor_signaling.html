T cell receptor signaling pathway module

A compact T cell receptor (TCR) signaling module. Antigen-bound TCR/CD3 complexes are phosphorylated by Src-family kinases such as LCK, recruit and activate ZAP70, phosphorylate LAT/SLP76 adaptor scaffolds, and split into PLC gamma/calcium/NFAT, Ras-MAPK/AP-1, and PKC/NF-kappaB outputs. The module is grounded in GO:0050852 and uses PAINT PTN anchors for the LCK/ZAP70 non-receptor tyrosine kinase tier where the local cache supports them.
