OpenScientist species-aware pathway research for PSEPK UPA00637
OpenScientist species-aware research supports UPA00637 as a covered KT2440 backbone module with adjacent opgH and opgG, while keeping accessory OPG tailoring genes outside this module's scope.

First-pass interpretation: UPA00637 is satisfiable as a compact two-gene OPG biosynthesis module in KT2440. OpgH is the multi-pass inner-membrane glucosyltransferase, while OpgG is a Sec-exported periplasmic OpgD/OpgG-family beta-1,2-glucanase inferred from characterized orthologs. Accessory OPG decoration or chain-length-control genes are outside this backbone module. Direct KT2440 biochemical evidence and the exact division of labor between OpgH and OpgG remain follow-up questions.
