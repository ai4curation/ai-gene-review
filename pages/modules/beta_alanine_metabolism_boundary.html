Beta-alanine metabolism boundary

Broad boundary module for KEGG ppu00410 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific map combines reductive pyrimidine catabolism toward beta-alanine, beta-alanine transamination, pantothenate ligation, and several aldehyde or acyl-CoA metabolism side nodes. The module records those supported activities while keeping fatty-acid beta-oxidation, phenylacetate, valine, and polyamine aldehyde chemistry separate from a strict beta-alanine route.
