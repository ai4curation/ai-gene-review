Oxidative stress peroxide detoxification boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 peroxide and organic-hydroperoxide detoxification enzymes from the stress/detoxification functional bucket. The membership combines OsmC/Ohr-family proteins, peroxiredoxin and thioredoxin peroxidase proteins, cytochrome/DyP peroxidases, and a non-heme chloroperoxidase. It is curated as bacterial cellular oxidant detoxification, not as peroxisome organelle biology or a single linear metabolic pathway.
