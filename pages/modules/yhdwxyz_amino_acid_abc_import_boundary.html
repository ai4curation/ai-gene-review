YhdWXYZ amino-acid ABC import boundary

Boundary module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 PP_1297-PP_1300 YhdWXYZ amino-acid ABC importer pulled from the KEGG ppu02010 ABC-transporter umbrella map. YhdW is the predicted solute-binding component, YhdX and YhdY are inner-membrane permeases, and YhdZ is the ABC ATP-binding energy-coupling subunit. The module records a coherent predicted amino-acid uptake system while leaving the exact amino-acid substrate unresolved.
