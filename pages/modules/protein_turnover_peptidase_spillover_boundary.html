Protein turnover peptidase spillover boundary

Boundary module for low-information Pseudomonas putida KT2440 peptidase-family genes from the protein folding/targeting/turnover umbrella bucket. The module records only domain/GOA-supported generic or class-specific peptidase activities and keeps product-name-only or weak domain records unresolved.
