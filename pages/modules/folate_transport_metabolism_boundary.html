Folate transport and metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu04981 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains folate-dependent or folate-maintenance enzymes: glyA1/glyA2 serine hydroxymethyltransferases, metH cobalamin-dependent methionine synthase, folA dihydrofolate reductase, thyA thymidylate synthase, and fau 5-formyltetrahydrofolate cyclo-ligase. No folate transporter is present in the extracted KT2440 membership, so the satisfiable biology is folate cofactor maintenance and folate-linked one-carbon metabolism overlapping existing folate biosynthesis, one-carbon-by-folate, cobalamin, methionine, and nucleotide contexts.
