rRNA modification and ribosome maturation boundary

Boundary module for PSEPK rRNA methyltransferases and pseudouridine synthases from the translation/RNA-processing umbrella. The module keeps site-specific 16S/23S rRNA methylation and pseudouridylation reactions distinct from generic RNA binding, broad methyltransferase, and unresolved methyltransferase-domain side nodes.
