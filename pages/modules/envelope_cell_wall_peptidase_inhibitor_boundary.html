First-pass interpretation: this module is an envelope boundary rather than a single pathway. M23/M37, NlpC/P60, S41A, M14, and S66 peptidases are separated from alpha-2-macroglobulin, chagasin/I42, and ecotin inhibitor proteins. PP_2108 and PP_5729 are retained as unresolved no-core candidates. Broad signal-transduction and generic endopeptidase parent terms were not promoted to core functions when more specific activities were available.
