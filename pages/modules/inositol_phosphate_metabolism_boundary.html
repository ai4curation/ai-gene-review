Inositol phosphate metabolism boundary

Boundary/absence module for KEGG ppu00562 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains SuhB, an inositol-1-monophosphatase family protein with bacterial rRNA transcription-antitermination context, plus TpiA and two MmsA paralogs that belong primarily to central-carbon, valine/thymine, beta-alanine, and propanoate metabolism. This module should not be read as a complete eukaryote-like phosphatidylinositol signalling pathway in KT2440.
