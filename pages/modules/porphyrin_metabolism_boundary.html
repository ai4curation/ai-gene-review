Porphyrin metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00860 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The bucket contains a coherent tetrapyrrole/heme biosynthesis spine from glutamyl-tRNA to protoheme, heme O/heme A maturation for terminal oxidases, a large aerobic cobalamin/corrinoid biosynthesis branch, a siroheme branch, and heme/iron utilization or storage side nodes. These branches share uroporphyrinogen/precorrin chemistry but should not be collapsed into one satisfiable linear pathway.
