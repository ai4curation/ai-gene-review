Exopolysaccharide biosynthesis boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00543 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific map is centered on alginate/exopolysaccharide biosynthesis and modification, but it also includes several serine O-acetyltransferase enzymes that belong primarily to L-cysteine biosynthesis. This module keeps alginate polymerization, alginate O-acetylation/regulation, unresolved glycosyltransferase side candidates, and cysteine-biosynthesis spillovers separate.
