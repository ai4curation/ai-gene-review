Aminoacyl-tRNA biosynthesis

Species-neutral module seeded from KEGG ppu00970 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The core module is protein-translation tRNA charging by aminoacyl-tRNA synthetases, with GatCAB indirect Asn/Gln-tRNA formation, initiator methionyl-tRNA formylation, and selenocysteine-tRNA side chemistry tracked as related translation-boundary steps. PP_0613 is retained as an unresolved amidase-family side node rather than a satisfiable aminoacyl-tRNA step.
