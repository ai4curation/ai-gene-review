Polyketide sugar unit biosynthesis boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00523 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The PSEPK membership is a compact dTDP-deoxy-sugar/dTDP-L-rhamnose enzyme set rather than evidence for a complete polyketide sugar-unit biosynthesis or natural-product glycosylation pathway. The module records the shared nucleotide-sugar chemistry and keeps the polyketide pathway label as KEGG context.
