GPCR-cAMP-PKA signaling pathway module

A compact Gs-coupled GPCR signaling module. An agonist-occupied GPCR activates heterotrimeric Gs, G alpha s stimulates adenylyl cyclase, cAMP accumulates and activates PKA, and PKA phosphorylates cytosolic and nuclear substrates including CREB. The module is grounded in GO:0007188. ADRB2 is anchored to PAINT GPCR nodes; downstream GNAS, ADCY5, PRKACA, and CREB1 are curated UniProt exemplars without PTN ancestry claims in this first pass.
