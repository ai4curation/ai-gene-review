The ppu02010 curation strategy is to maintain this umbrella boundary module as an index and put actionable annotations into coherent substrate or envelope submodules. Do not use this module to justify broad substrate claims for individual ABC components.

Part 1: Umbrella-map boundary
ABC transporter umbrella-map boundaryTransport Stepabc_transporter_umbrella_boundary

ppu02010 is an umbrella transport map. It should be curated by substrate family, locus block, and component role rather than as a single pathway with one shared biological objective.
