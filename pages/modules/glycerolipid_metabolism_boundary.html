Glycerolipid metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00561 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map combines bacterial glycerolipid and glycerophospholipid precursor synthesis, diacylglycerol recycling, glycerol entry into central metabolism, and several side nodes from alcohol, glycerate/hydroxypyruvate, and generic lipase metabolism. The module treats PlsX/PlsY/PlsB/PlsC/DgkA as the cell-envelope lipid core and keeps other genes as boundary context unless reviewed evidence supports a tighter glycerolipid role.
