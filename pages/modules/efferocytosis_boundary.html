Efferocytosis boundary

Boundary/absence module for KEGG ppu04148 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains only speC/PP_0864, an ornithine decarboxylase in putrescine/polyamine biosynthesis, and petA/PP_1317, the Rieske iron-sulfur subunit of respiratory complex III. This should not be interpreted as evidence for eukaryotic apoptotic-cell clearance or efferocytosis in a bacterium.
