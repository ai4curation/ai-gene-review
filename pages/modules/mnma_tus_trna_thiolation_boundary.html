MnmA/Tus tRNA thiolation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu04122 Tus/MnmA sulfur-relay subset in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The satisfiable first-pass pathway is sulfur relay from TusA-family carriers through the TusBCD complex and TusE to MnmA, which catalyzes ATP-dependent 2-thiolation of wobble-position uridine in tRNA. tusA-I is retained as a TusA-family sulfur-carrier paralog whose exact client remains unresolved, while PP_3994 is promoted as the TusC-like member of the TusBCD complex despite lacking fetched GOA rows.
