Arginine and proline metabolism

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 arginine/proline metabolism as represented by KEGG ppu00330. The pathway combines several biologically distinct routes: proline biosynthesis and interconversion, succinylated arginine catabolism, agmatine/putrescine/polyamine metabolism, 5-oxoproline turnover, and neighboring oxidoreductase or aminotransferase reactions. This module is intentionally broader than the strict arginine biosynthesis module.
