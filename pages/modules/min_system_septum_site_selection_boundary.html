MinCDE septum-site selection boundary

Boundary module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 MinCDE cell-division site-selection system. The module captures MinD as the membrane-associated ATPase, MinC as the FtsZ/septum inhibitor, and MinE as the topological specificity factor that permits division at the correct internal site while retaining polar inhibition.
