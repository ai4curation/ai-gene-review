Selenocompound metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00450 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific map contains a selenium-specific Sec-tRNA biosynthesis spine, SelD selenophosphate synthase followed by SelA L-seryl-tRNA(Sec) selenium transferase, plus methionine, cysteine, sulfate-assimilation, and aminoacyl-tRNA side nodes included by broad KEGG selenocompound chemistry.

This module is intentionally a boundary module. Only SelD and SelA are selenium-specific Sec-tRNA biosynthesis enzymes in the ppu00450 membership set. MetG, CysD/CysNC, MetB, MdeA, MetH, MetE, PP_4348, PP_4594, and PP_4637 should remain sulfur, methionine, sulfate-assimilation, translation, or candidate side-context nodes unless gene-specific selenium substrate evidence is found.
