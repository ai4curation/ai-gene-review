Monobactam biosynthesis boundary module

A boundary module for curating monobactam and related beta-lactam antibiotic biosynthesis calls. In Pseudomonas putida KT2440, KEGG ppu00261 is not evidence by itself for a complete monobactam pathway: the bucket contains aspartate-family amino-acid precursor enzymes, sulfate-activation enzymes, and an MbtH-like protein that is more plausibly nonribosomal-peptide or siderophore context. These roles should be curated as precursor or side-node chemistry unless gene-level evidence supports a committed beta-lactam antibiotic biosynthetic step.
