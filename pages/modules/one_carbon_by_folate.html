One-carbon pool by folate

A bacterial folate-mediated one-carbon metabolism module covering interconversion of tetrahydrofolate one-carbon carriers and their use in purine, thymidylate, and methyl-cycle reactions. In Pseudomonas putida KT2440, KEGG ppu00670 also pulls in glycine-cleavage enzymes, methionine-cycle enzymes, betaine/choline oxidation, and lipoamide dehydrogenases. Those are tracked as boundary or source/sink context unless they directly interconvert folate one-carbon carriers or consume a folate-bound one-carbon unit.
