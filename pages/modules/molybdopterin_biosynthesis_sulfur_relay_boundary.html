Molybdopterin biosynthesis sulfur relay boundary

Boundary module for the ppu04122 molybdopterin/MoCo sulfur-relay subset in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The satisfiable first-pass pathway covers MoaA and MoaC formation of cyclic pyranopterin monophosphate, MoeB activation of the MoaD sulfur-carrier subunit, MoaE/MoaD molybdopterin synthase chemistry, and MoaB/Mog-family support for Mo-molybdopterin cofactor biosynthesis. The PP_1969 and PP_2482 MoaA-like paralogs and the moaB-I/moaB-II paralog pair remain boundary candidates with unresolved paralog-specific roles.
