Iron uptake regulation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02020 iron-uptake regulation partition in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. It captures PfeS/PfeR-like two-component regulators, TonB-dependent ferric siderophore receptor activity, and FecI/FecR-like ECF sigma/anti-sigma signaling. This is a partition of the broad KEGG two-component-system map, not a single linear pathway.

The bucket mixes canonical two-component regulators with FecI/FecR ECF sigma signaling and TonB-dependent outer-membrane receptor context. The module therefore records a boundary around iron uptake regulation rather than treating all members as His-Asp phosphorelay proteins.
