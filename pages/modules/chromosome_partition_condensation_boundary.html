Chromosome partition and condensation boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 chromosome partition, chromosome organization, and condensation. The module captures ParB with promoted PP_0002 as the origin-proximal ParB/Soj-like candidate pair, PP_0693/PP_0694 as MksF/MksE-like condensin-associated candidates, several ParA-family P-loop ATPase candidates whose chromosomal versus mobile-element context remains unresolved, and the strongest Smc-ScpA-ScpB condensation/segregation machinery.

The module intentionally avoids treating every ParA-family protein as a core chromosome-partition member; PP_2412, PP_3700, PP_4334, and PP_5070 remain unresolved candidate partition ATPases, while PP_2161 is retained only as a weak DUF4404/coiled-coil candidate. This boundary completes the chromosome partition/condensation portion of the larger chromosome/cell-cycle functional bucket but leaves broad keyword spillovers PP_1105 and tig outside the module.
