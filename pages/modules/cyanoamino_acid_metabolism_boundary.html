Cyanoamino acid metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu00460 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The mapped set contains asparaginase-family enzymes plus serine hydroxymethyltransferase, a periplasmic beta-glucosidase, and glutathione hydrolases. This module keeps those supported activities visible while avoiding inference of a complete cyanogenic-glycoside or cyanoamino-acid detoxification pathway without substrate-specific evidence.
