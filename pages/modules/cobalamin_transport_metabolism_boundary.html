Cobalamin transport and metabolism boundary

Boundary module for KEGG ppu04980 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains pduO/PP_1349, a corrinoid adenosyltransferase in cobalamin/cobamide metabolism, and metH/PP_2375, a cobalamin-dependent methionine synthase. This bucket supports cobalamin metabolism as enzyme/cofactor context, but should not be interpreted as evidence for a complete cobalamin transport pathway in KT2440.
