Boundary module for PSEPK TonB-dependent transport. It covers TonB/ExbB/ExbD energy-transduction proteins plus the first-pass TonB-dependent outer-membrane receptor panel, with substrate specificity represented gene by gene.

Involvement of the TonB system in tolerance to solvents and drugs in Pseudomonas putida DOT-T1E
The existing exbB and tonB reviews cite this Pseudomonas TonB-system study for exbB-exbD-tonB linkage and iron-limitation phenotypes in a close P. putida strain.

PSEPK transport/membrane/efflux TonB-dependent receptor partition batch
The receptor split covers 30 TonB-dependent outer-membrane receptor genes, including ferric siderophore, CntO, heme, B12-family, copper-receptor, and generic receptor candidates.

This boundary now separates inner-membrane energy transduction from the TonB-dependent outer-membrane receptor panel. Orphan TonB paralog/receptor pairing and the ligand specificity of generic receptor candidates remain open.
