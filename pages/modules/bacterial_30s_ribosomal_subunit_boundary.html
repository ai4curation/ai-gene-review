Bacterial 30S ribosomal subunit boundary

Boundary module for the KEGG ppu03010 30S ribosomal-protein subset in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. This module covers the 21 protein-coding small-subunit participants selected from the organism-specific ribosome map; large-subunit proteins are tracked separately in the planned 50S module.

file:projects/P_PUTIDA/curate_ppu03010_30s_ribosomal_subunit_first_pass.py
ppu03010 30S first-pass curation script
The script applies the light first-pass annotation decisions to all 21 30S ribosomal-protein reviews and preserves term-specific GOA evidence.

This is a protein-participant boundary module, not a full biochemical model of bacterial translation. The 16S rRNA molecule and initiation/elongation/release factors are outside this ppu03010 protein sub-batch unless they appear in a later curation bucket.
