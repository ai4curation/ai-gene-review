Bacterial non-homologous end joining

Compact DNA double-strand-break repair module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 KEGG ppu03450. The organism-specific pathway contains the bacterial Ku DNA-end-binding protein and multifunctional LigD enzyme. Ku binds linear double-stranded DNA ends and recruits/stimulates LigD, while LigD supplies ATP-dependent DNA ligase activity and NHEJ-associated polymerase and exonuclease/phosphoesterase end-processing activities.
