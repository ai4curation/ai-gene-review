Caprolactam degradation boundary

Boundary/absence module for KEGG ppu00930 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific KEGG map contains only shared CoA-thioester hydration and fatty-acid/phenylacetate beta-oxidation side enzymes (`fadB`, PP_2217, and `paaF`). It lacks route-defining caprolactam lactamase, 6-aminohexanoate conversion, or adipate/semialdehyde upper-pathway enzymes, so this bucket should not be interpreted as a complete caprolactam degradation pathway.
