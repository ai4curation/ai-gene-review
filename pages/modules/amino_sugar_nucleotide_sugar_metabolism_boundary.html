Amino sugar and nucleotide sugar metabolism boundary

Broad boundary module for KEGG ppu00520 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The PSEPK membership combines UDP-N-acetylglucosamine biosynthesis, de novo UDP-N-acetylmuramate formation for peptidoglycan, MurNAc recycling/salvage, nucleotide-sugar interconversion for envelope glycans, and shared sugar-phosphate precursor reactions. The module is scoped to keep those contexts separate rather than treating the KEGG map as one linear pathway.
