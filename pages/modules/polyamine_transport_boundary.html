Polyamine transport boundary

Boundary module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 polyamine transport components encountered during ppu02010 and ppu02024 ABC-transporter/quorum-sensing-map partitioning. The module separates complete or near-complete Pot/Spu/Ydc-like polyamine ABC import loci from singleton PotF-like or binding-protein boundary components whose cognate partners remain unresolved.
