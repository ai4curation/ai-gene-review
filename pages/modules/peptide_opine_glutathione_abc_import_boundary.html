Peptide, opine, and glutathione ABC import boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02024-derived PP_3220-PP_3223 and PP_4453-PP_4458 ABC import loci in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. PP_3220-PP_3223 form a predicted dipeptide-like ABC importer. The PP_4453-PP_4458 locus contains GsiA/GsiC glutathione-import subunits plus opine-named permease and periplasmic binding components, with adjacent opine oxidase genes outside this ppu02024 bucket. The module records transport context and substrate ambiguity; it is not evidence for direct quorum sensing.

This module should be treated as a transport boundary extracted from the ppu02024 umbrella, not as quorum-sensing evidence. PP_3220-PP_3223 are the cleaner dipeptide-like importer. The PP_4453-PP_4458 locus has mixed Gsi and opine product names and needs substrate-specific genetics or uptake assays before propagation of glutathione, peptide, or opine labels across every subunit.

Part 1: Dipeptide-like ABC import
PP_3220-PP_3223 dipeptide-like ABC importTransport Steppp3220_pp3223_dipeptide_abc_import

PP_3220 is an ABC-type dipeptide transporter ATP-binding subunit, PP_3221 and PP_3222 are multi-pass permeases, and PP_3223 is an SBP_5-family periplasmic binding component. The exact ligand range is unresolved, but dipeptide import is the most specific current product assignment.
