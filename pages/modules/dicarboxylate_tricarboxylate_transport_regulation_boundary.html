Dicarboxylate/tricarboxylate transport regulation boundary

Boundary module for the ppu02020 Dct/Tct transport-regulation partition in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. It captures DctD/DctB-like regulatory pairs, a DctPQM TRAP C4-dicarboxylate importer, multiple DctA-family C4-dicarboxylate symporters, a TctABC/TctD-like tricarboxylate transport locus, and a PP_3124 short-chain fatty-acid transporter side node. This is a transport-regulatory partition of the broad KEGG two-component-system map, not a single linear two-component pathway.
