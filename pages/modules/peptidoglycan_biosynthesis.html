Module KB / Peptidoglycan biosynthesis

Peptidoglycan biosynthesis

Species-neutral first-pass module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 KEGG ppu00550 peptidoglycan biosynthesis batch. The module separates cytosolic UDP-MurNAc-peptide precursor assembly, MurNAc/muropeptide recycling and salvage, lipid-linked precursor assembly, lipid II flipping, glycan polymerization, D,D-transpeptidation, and carboxypeptidase/remodeling side nodes, plus additional peptidoglycan turnover and cell-wall remodeling candidates from the cell-envelope/division functional partition. It also records that MurJ is an expected lipid II flippase candidate even though it is not in the KEGG ppu00550 membership table.
