CcmABCD heme export and cytochrome c maturation boundary

Boundary module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 PP_4324-PP_4327 CcmD-C-B-A heme-handling system from the KEGG ppu02010 ABC-transporter umbrella map, plus the separately curated PP_2150 ApbE-family flavinylation context from the cell-envelope/division partition. CcmB and CcmC are multi-pass inner-membrane heme-handling components, CcmD is a small inner-membrane CcmD-family protein, and CcmA is the ATP-binding energy-coupling subunit of CcmAB. PP_2150 is represented as an envelope flavoprotein maturation side context, not as a CcmABCD heme-export component.

This boundary module was created from the ppu02010 ABC-transporter partition work and extended with the PP_2150 ApbE/FMNylylation singleton from the cell-envelope/division partition. It should remain distinct from porphyrin/heme biosynthesis modules and from terminal oxidase assembly modules; PP_4324-PP_4327 represent the Ccm heme-handling/export step needed for c-type cytochrome maturation, while PP_2150 is retained only as related envelope flavoprotein maturation context.
