Bacterial DNA-directed RNA polymerase core

Compact bacterial RNA polymerase core module for Pseudomonas putida KT2440 KEGG ppu03020. The organism-specific pathway contains rpoA, rpoB, rpoC, and rpoZ, corresponding to the alpha, beta, beta-prime, and omega subunits of the alpha2-beta-beta-prime-omega DNA-directed RNA polymerase core. The core catalyzes DNA-template-directed RNA synthesis; sigma factors and transcription regulators are intentionally outside this compact core-complex boundary.
