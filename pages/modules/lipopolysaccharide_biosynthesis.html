Lipopolysaccharide biosynthesis

Species-neutral first-pass module for the Pseudomonas putida KT2440 KEGG ppu00540 lipopolysaccharide biosynthesis batch. The module separates lipid IVA/lipid A biosynthesis, Kdo2-lipid A formation, LPS core oligosaccharide assembly, and lipid A/LPS remodeling side nodes. Broad membrane, cofactor, and generic transferase/hydrolase annotations should not be treated as separate core functions when more specific enzyme terms are present.

The module intentionally keeps LPS biosynthesis and lipid A remodeling together because KEGG ppu00540 includes both de novo assembly enzymes and modification candidates. Substrate-specific claims for PP_0024, PP_4592, yijP, lpxOA, PP_4570, PP_0063, PP_3016, and PP_3134 should remain conservative until stronger experimental or curated evidence is available. The cell-envelope/division bucket also surfaced maa, but its first-pass review supports maltose O-acetyltransferase activity rather than lipid A biosynthesis, so it is intentionally not included as a module part.
