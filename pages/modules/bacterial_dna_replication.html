Bacterial DNA replication

Boundary module for KEGG ppu03030 DNA replication in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The strict chromosome-replication core includes DnaA origin recognition/initiation, DnaB/DnaG primosome function, SSB-coated single-stranded DNA, DNA polymerase III synthesis with beta-clamp and clamp-loader subunits, DnaQ proofreading, RNA-primer removal by RNase H and PolA, and NAD-dependent nick sealing by LigA. The KEGG map also includes LigB and DnaQ-like exonuclease or DnaB-like helicase candidates that are retained as satisfiable boundary members pending module-level evidence.
