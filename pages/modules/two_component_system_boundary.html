Two-component-system umbrella boundary

Umbrella boundary module for KEGG ppu02020 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The organism-specific map contains 99 primary genes and mixes canonical sensor-kinase/response-regulator pairs with ECF sigma/anti-sigma systems, transport-regulation neighborhoods, metal and nutrient homeostasis, pili and adhesion context, efflux machinery, and non-TCS spillovers. This parent module records the partition and should not be used as evidence that every member is a direct participant in one satisfiable two-component pathway.

This parent module records pathway satisfiability for the ppu02020 KEGG umbrella. The submodule reviews carry the gene-level biological claims. Use this module to navigate the partition, not to assign broad two-component signaling to transporters, pili, efflux components, sigma factors, dnaA, or etp.

Part 1: Umbrella-map boundary
ppu02020 umbrella boundarySignaling Pathwayppu02020_umbrella_boundary

ppu02020 is an umbrella KEGG map. Curate it through the partitioned submodules and side-context spillovers rather than as one linear phosphorelay pathway.

Part 2: Canonical reusable TCS motif
Reusable two-component relay motif indexSignaling Pathwayreusable_two_component_relay_index

`two_component_relay` captures the generic sensor histidine kinase, optional Hpt, and response-regulator phosphorelay motif. Simple bacterial pairs generally conform with the Hpt step collapsed.
