Quorum-sensing umbrella boundary

Umbrella boundary module for KEGG ppu02024 in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. The PSEPK primary set is not a single LuxI/LuxR quorum-sensing pathway; it is a mixed map dominated by ABC importers, transport and efflux components, QseBC-like two-component regulators, iron regulation, surface adhesion, and other side contexts. This parent module records the partition and prevents broad quorum-sensing claims from being propagated to all 60 primary members.
