Peroxisome boundary in bacteria

Boundary/absence module for KEGG ppu04146 Peroxisome in Pseudomonas putida KT2440. PSEPK is a bacterium and has no eukaryotic peroxisome organelle, peroxin import system, or peroxisomal matrix compartment. The organism-specific KEGG map instead pulls in soluble oxidative-stress enzymes, fatty-acid activation enzymes, NADP-dependent isocitrate dehydrogenases, HMG-CoA lyases, and NudC NAD-capped RNA/NADH hydrolase context that are satisfiable as cytoplasmic or membrane-associated bacterial functions but not as peroxisome localization or peroxisome-biogenesis annotations.
