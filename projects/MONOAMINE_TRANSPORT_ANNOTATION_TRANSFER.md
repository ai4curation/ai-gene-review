# Monoamine Transport Annotation Transfer

## Overview

This document records AI-assisted review of GO annotations to the following **obsolete** GO terms:
- GO:0015844 monoamine transport
- GO:0051937 catecholamine transport
- GO:0006837 serotonin transport
- GO:7770032 tyramine transport
- GO:0015872 dopamine transport
- GO:0048241 epinephrine transport
- GO:0015874 norepinephrine transport

Each annotation is transferred to a surviving descendant term or removed.

### Evidence Discipline (Applied to all suggestions)

- **Primary evidence only**: Each row's suggested GO action must be justified exclusively by the PMID cited in that row.
- **No implicit cross-paper inference**: A row must not use mechanistic detail from other PMIDs to choose a more specific GO term.
- **If extra context is mentioned**: It must be labeled explicitly as auxiliary context and must not drive the proposed transfer.
- **Specificity rule**: If the cited PMID supports only broad aminergic/monoamine effects (e.g., KO neurotransmitter turnover phenotype) and does not directly establish substrate-specific uptake, do not assign transmitter-specific uptake terms from that PMID alone.
