# PSEPK protein folding/turnover: Chaperone, holdase, and foldase systems

Core and accessory bacterial protein-folding factors: Hsp33, SurA, FKBP/PPiases, HscAB, GroES, ClpB-like disaggregase, DnaJ-like and GrpE/CbpA/CbpM co-chaperone context, plus cold-shock nucleic-acid chaperone side context. Curate as bacterial proteostasis/folding, not as a eukaryotic ER-processing or integrated-stress-response module.
