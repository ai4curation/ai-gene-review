# AHR:TCDD binds ARNT
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8937177](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8937177)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Ligand-bound aryl hydrocarbon receptor (AHR:TCDD) disassociates from the chaperone subunits to then dimerise with the aryl hydrocarbon receptor nuclear translocator (ARNT) (Lees & Whitelaw 1999).
