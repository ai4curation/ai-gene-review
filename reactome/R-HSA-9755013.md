# NME1,2 hexamers phosphorylate RBV-DP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9755013](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9755013)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Both nucleoside diphosphate kinases A and B (NME1, NME2) hexamers phosphorylate ribavirin diphosphate (RBV-DP) to the triphosphate (RBV-TP) (Bourdais et al, 1996; Gallois-Montbrun et al, 2003).
