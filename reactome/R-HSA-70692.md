# ornithine => putrescine + CO2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70692](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70692)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

L-ornithine is converted into putrescine by cytosolic ornithine decarboxylase (ODC). The enzyme, conjugated with pyridoxal phosphate, is active as a dimer (Almrud et al. 2000; Dufe et al. 2007).
