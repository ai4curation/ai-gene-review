# miR-92b binds 3'UTR of NLK mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4518575](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4518575)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NLK mRNA is a direct target of miR-92b which binds the 3' UTR and promotes degradation (Wang et al, 2013).
