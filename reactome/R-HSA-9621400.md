# EGR2 and SREBF2 dimer bind SCD5 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9621400](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9621400)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SCD5 expression is upregulated during Schwann cell myelination in an EGR2- and SREBF2 dependent manner (LeBlanc et all, 2005; Jang et al, 2010). SCD5 encodes a stearyl-CoA desaturase involved in long chain fatty acid biosynthesis (Wang et al, 2005; Zhang et al, 2005).
