# Phosphorylation of GAB2 by SFKs
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1433454](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1433454)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

GAB2 bound to GRB2 on KIT is then phosphorylated on a number of tyrosine residues through the action of Src family kinases (SFKs).
