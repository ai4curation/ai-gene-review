# tRNA ligase complex ligates tRNA exons
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5696816](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5696816)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The RNA ligase complex ligates tRNA exons yielding intact, spliced tRNAs (Popow et al. 2011, Popow et al. 2014). The reaction apparently occurs in one step with no residual 2' phosphate that requires removal, as in yeast.
