# Squalene is oxidized to its epoxide
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191299](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191299)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Squalene monooxygenase (squalene epoxidase, SE) is located on the endoplamic reticulum. It catalyzes the oxidation of squalene (SQNE) to squalene 2,3-epoxide (SQNX) (Laden et al. 2000).
