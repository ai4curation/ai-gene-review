# Pink1 is recruited from the cytoplasm to the mitochondria
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5205661](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5205661)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PINK1 is constitutively synthesized and imported into all mitochondria. In healthy mitochondria PINK1 is cleaved by voltage-sensitive proteolysis.
