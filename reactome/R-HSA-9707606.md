# PGRMC2:Hemes translocate to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9707606](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9707606)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PGRMC2 binds heme reversibly. Also, PGRMC2 can bind to AAAS, a subunit of the nuclear pore complex, suggesting nuclear import of the PGRMC2:heme complex through the NPC takes place (Jühlen et al, 2016; Parker et al, 2017; Galmozzi et al 2020).
