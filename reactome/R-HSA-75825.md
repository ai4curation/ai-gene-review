# Proteasome mediated degradation of Cyclin D1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-75825](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-75825)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylated Cyclin D1 is degraded during S phase by the 26S proteasome allowing for efficient DNA synthesis.
