# Polyubiquitinated RUNX3 translocates to the cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8952399](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8952399)

## Summary

Polyubiquitination of RUNX3 by MDM2 promotes translocation of RUNX3 from the nucleus to the cytosol (Chi et al. 2009).
