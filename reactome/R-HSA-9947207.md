# MSMO1 oxidizes 4a-me-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol to 4a-carboxy-5a-cholest-8-ene-3b-ol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9947207](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9947207)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MSMO1 (Methylsterol monooxygenase 1), associated with the endoplasmic reticulum membrane, oxidizes 4a-me-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol to 4a-carboxy-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol. Evidence for this function of human MSMO1 protein is indirect, coming from studies of steroid synthesis intermediates accumulated in patients with defects in the enzyme (He et al. 2011).
