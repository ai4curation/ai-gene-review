# Epo:p-8Y-Epor:p-12Y-Jak2:Lyn:Irs2 binds Pi3k
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9027268](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9027268)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Phosphatidylinositol 4,5-bisphosphate 3-kinase (Pi3k) directly binds phosphotyrosine-479 in the cytosolic domain of Epor (Damen et al. 1993, He et al. 1993, Miura et al. 1994, Damen et al. 1995, Klingmuller et al. 1997).
