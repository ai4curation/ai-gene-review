# NOSTRIN mediated translocation of eNOS from plasma membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-203625](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-203625)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NOSTRIN translocates eNOS from the plasma membrane to intracellular vesicular structures.  NOSTRIN internalization of eNOS is proposed to occur via vesicle fission and caveolar transport through cooperation with dynamin and N-WASP.
