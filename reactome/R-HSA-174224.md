# Dissociation of Cdc20 from APC/C complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-174224](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-174224)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In late mitosis, Cdc20 dissociates from the APC/C and is replaced by the activator Cdh1 (Ballabeni et al. 2011).
