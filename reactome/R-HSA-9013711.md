# HES1 gene expression is stimulated by NOTCH4
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9013711](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9013711)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NOTCH4 expression correlates with increased HES1 gene transcription in different cell types (Raafat et al. 2004, Lai et al. 2013, Simoes et al. 2015). A recombinant human NICD4 stimulates transcription from the recombinant human HES1-luciferase reporter construct (Raafat et al. 2004).
