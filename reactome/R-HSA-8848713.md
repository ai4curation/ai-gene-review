# PTK6 binds BCAR1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8848713](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8848713)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated PTK6 (BRK) binds BCAR1 (p130CAS), and this interaction involves the SH2 domain of PTK6 (Zheng et al. 2012).
