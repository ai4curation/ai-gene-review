# HYAL1 hydrolyses (HA)50
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2160874](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2160874)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In the acidic environment of the lysosome, hyaluronidase 1 (HYAL1) is able to hydrolyse large 20kDa HA fragments (approximately 50 disaccharide units) to 800 Da fragments (2 disaccharide units).
