# PGK1 phosphorylates AKT1S1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9975250](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9975250)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphoglycerate kinase 1 (PGK1) can act as a protein serine/threonine kinase that, under normoxia conditions, phosphorylates AKT1S1 (PRAS40) on threonine residue T246, which suppresses autophagy-mediated cell death and promotes cellular proliferation (Zhang et al. 2022).
