# CYP8B1 12-hydroxylates 4CHOL7aOLONE
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-192157](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-192157)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Sterol 12alpha hydroxylase (CYP8B1), an enzyme associated with the endoplasmic reticulum membrane, catalyses the 12-alpha-hydroxylation of 7-Alpha-hydroxycholest-4-en-3-one (4CHOL7aOLONE) to 7-alpha,12-alpha-dihydroxycholest-4-en-3-one (4CHOL7a,12aDONE). The human gene has been cloned (Gafvels et al. 1999) and encodes an enzyme with CYP8B1 activity when overexpressed in cultured cells, in agreement with the enzymatic properties of its well-characterised rabbit homolog (Ishida et al. 1992).
