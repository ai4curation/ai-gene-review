# p-S215 FOXO1 binds RAG gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5692785](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5692785)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After phosphorylation by MAPKAPK5, p-S218 FOXO1 binds to the RAG gene promoter to promote transcription (Lin et al, 2010; Ochiai et al, 2012; Chow et al, 2013).
