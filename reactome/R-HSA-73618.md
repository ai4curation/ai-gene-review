# conversion of 5,6-Dihydrothymine to 3-Ureidoisobutyrate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-73618](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-73618)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic dihydropyrimidinase tetramer catalyzes the reaction of 5,6-dihydrothymine and water to yield 3-ureidoisobutyrate (Hamajima et al. 1998).
