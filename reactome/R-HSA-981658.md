# Complement factor I binds C4BP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-981658](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-981658)

## Summary

C4b-binding protein is a cofactor for Complement Factor I (CFI), allowing it to bind and thereby mediating C4b proteolysis.
