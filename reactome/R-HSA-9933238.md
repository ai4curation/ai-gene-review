# SS18-containing ATPase complex binds SMARCC1 dimer:SMARCD1:SMARCE1:SMARCB1:ARID1:DPF1,2,3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9933238](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9933238)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Isolation of SMARC4A-containing complexes suggests that the ATPase module of cBAF, pBAF and ncBAF complexes exist as discrete, isolatable complexes. The ATPase modules of cBAF and ncBAF are the same, and consist of SMARCA2/4, a dimer of ACTB and ACTL6A/B (which together form the actin-related protein or ARP module linking the catalytic core of the SWI/SNF complex to the core/base module), the BCL7 proteins and SS18 (Kadoch et al, 2013; Mashtalir et al, 2018; Mashtalir et al, 2020; He et al, 2020; reviewed in Eustermann et al, 2024).
