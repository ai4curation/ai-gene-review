# Addition of galactose to Core 6 glycoprotein
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1964501](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1964501)

## Summary

Beta-1,4-galactosyltransferase 5 (B4GALT5) mediates the transfer of galactose from UDP-galactose to Core 6 glycoproteins (Sato et al. 1998).
