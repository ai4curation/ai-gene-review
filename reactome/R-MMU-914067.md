# Shp2 can recruit Grb2
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-914067](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-914067)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

SHP2 can associate with GRB2 (Stein-Gerlach et al. 1995). IL-3 induces the phosphorylation of SHP2 and its association with GRB2 (Welham et al. 1994).
SHP2 may act as a scaffold protein to recruit other signaling molecules, e.g. SHP2 was reported to link GRB2 to the receptor tyrosine kinase c-kit (Tauchi et al. 1994).
