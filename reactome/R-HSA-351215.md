# Putrescine + dc-Adenosyl methionine  => Spermidine + 5'-methylthioadenosine
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-351215](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-351215)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Spermidine synthase (SRM) carries out the final step of spermidine biosynthesis, transferring an aminopropyl group from decarboxylated S-adenosylmethionine (dc-AdoMet) to putrescine to form spermidine (SPM) (Myohanen et al. 1991; Wu et al. 2007).
