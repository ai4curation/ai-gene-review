# Mevalonate-5-phosphate is further phosphorylated
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191422](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191422)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic phosphomevalonate kinase (PMK) catalyzes the reversible, ATP-dependent phosphorylation of mevalonate-5-phosphate (MVA5P), producing mevalonate-5-pyrophosphate (MVA5PP) (Herdendorf & Miziorko 2006; Hogenboom et al. 2004; ).
