# ERBB2 binds pertuzumab
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9665726](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9665726)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Pertuzumab is an ERBB2-directed therapeutic antibody that binds to ERBB2 and sterically blocks receptor dimerization and signaling (Franklin et al. 2004).
