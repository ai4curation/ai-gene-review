# p-Y-Stat1,3,5 dimerize
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9676912](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9676912)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

After being phosphorylated, Stat1, Stat3, and Stat5 (Stat5a and Stat5b) form homodimers (de Koning et al. 1996, Gits et al. 2006). Phospho-Stat1 and phospho-Stat3 can also form heterodimers (de Koning et al. 1996, Gits et al. 2006).
