# Cdk5r1 gene expression is stimulated by Npas4
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9634937](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9634937)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

In mouse neurons, Npas4 directly positively regulates expression of the Cdk5r1 gene, encoding Cdk5 activator p35 (Yun et al. 2013).
