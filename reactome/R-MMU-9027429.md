# Lyn phosphorylates Plcg1,2 in Epo:phospho-Epor:phospho-Jak2:Lyn:Irs2:Plcg1,2
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9027429](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9027429)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Plcg1 (Phospholipase C gamma1) or Plcg2 bound to the activated Epor is phosphorylated on tyrosine residues (Mason-Garcia et al. 1992, Boudot et al. 1999, Boudot et al. 2002, Tong et al. 2004, Halupa et al. 2005, Schnoder et al. 2015) by the kinase LYN (Boudot et al. 2003).
