# beta-hydroxyisobutyryl-CoA + H2O => beta-hydroxyisobutyrate + CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70881](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70881)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mitochondrial 3-hydroxyisobutyryl-CoA hydrolase (HIBCH) catalyzes the hydrolysis of beta-hydroxyisobutyryl-CoA to form beta-hydroxyisobutyrate (3-hydroxy-2-methylpropanoate) and CoA (Hawes et al. 1996).
