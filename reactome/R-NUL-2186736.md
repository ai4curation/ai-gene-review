# Rnf111 binds SKI/SKIL in complex with SMAD2/3:SMAD4 upon TGF-beta stimulation
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-2186736](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-2186736)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recombinant mouse Rnf111 (Arkadia) binds to the complex of endogenous human Smad2/3 and recombinant human SKI/SKIL in response to TGF-beta or activin stimulation (Levy et al. 2007).
