# Disassembly of the Rev-importin beta-B23:Ran-GTP complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-180736](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-180736)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The association of importin-beta with Ran-GTP causes the disassembly of the Rev-importin ß-B23 complex releasing the Rev in the nucleus.
