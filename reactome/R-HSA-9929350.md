# Ubiquitination of CD274 by BTRC-SCF E3-ligase complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9929350](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9929350)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

β-TrCP-SCF complex catalyses PD-L1(CD274) polyubiquitination in the presence of GSK3β. Deletion of the destruction box domain or mutation of the GSK3B phosphorylation motif abrogates GSK3β-mediated PD-L1 ubiquitination and proteasomal degradation, reflecting the involvement of β-TrCP ubiquitin-E3 ligase activity in PD-L1 stability (Li et al., 2016)
