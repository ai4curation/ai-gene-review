# miR-17 microRNA binds CNOT6L mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8948583](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8948583)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MicroRNA miR-17 binds the CNOT6L mRNA. CNOT6L mRNA acts as a competing endogenous RNA (ceRNA) for PTEN, preventing binding of miR-17 microRNA to PTEN mRNA and PTEN downregulation (Tay et al. 2011).
