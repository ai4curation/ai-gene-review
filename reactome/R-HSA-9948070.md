# POMGNT1 catalyzes FKRP:FKTN:RXYLT1 formation
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9948070](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9948070)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Binding of FKTN to POMGNT1 (which itself binds to the trisaccharide linker) is a prerequisite for the formation of the FKRP:FKTN:RXYLT1 complex (Xiong et al., 2006; Kuwabara et al., 2016).
