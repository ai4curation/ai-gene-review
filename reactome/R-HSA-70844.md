# alpha-methylacetoacetyl-CoA + CoA => propionyl-CoA + acetyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70844](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70844)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mitochondrial acetyl-CoA acetyltransferase (ACAT1) catalyzes the reaction of alpha-methyl-acetoacetyl-CoA and CoA to form propionyl-CoA and acetyl-CoA. Structural studies have shown the active enzyme to be a tetramer of ACAT1 polypeptides whose amino-terminal 34 residues, a mitochondrial targeting sequence, have been removed (Haapalainen et al. 2007).
