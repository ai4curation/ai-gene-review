# (S)-Hydroxyoctanoyl-CoA+NAD => 3-Oxooctanoyl-CoA+NADH+H
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77331](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77331)

## Summary

HADH in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates (S)-3-hydroxyoctanoyl-CoA to form 3-oxooctanoyl-CoA (Vredendaal et al. 1996).
