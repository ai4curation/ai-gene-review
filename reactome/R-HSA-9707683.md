# PGRMC2 binds Hemes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9707683](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9707683)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mitochondria-bound PGRMC1 transfers heme to ER-bound PGRMC2, which delivers heme to proteins in the ER and nucleus, including heme-responsive transcription factors such as Rev-Erbα (Parker et al, 2017; Galmozzi et al, 2020).
