# 5-PP-IP5 transports from the nucleus to the cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855203](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855203)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol 5-diphospho-1,2,3,4,6-pentakisphosphate (5-PP-IP5) translocates from the nucleus to the cytosol via the nuclear pore (Fridy et al. 2007).
