# Autophosphorylation of PTK2 at Y397
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-354073](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-354073)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The co-localization of PTK2/FAK with integrins in focal adhesions and the actin cytoskeleton is essential for the activation and phosphorylation of PTK2/FAK. <br>PTK2/FAK has six tyrosine phosphorylation sites and tyrosine 397 is the main auto-phosphorylation site present upstream of the kinase domain.
