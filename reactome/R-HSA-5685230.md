# CHEK1 phosphorylates RAD51
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5685230](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5685230)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated CHEK1 phosphorylates RAD51 on threonine residue T309, which is necessary for RAD51 association with chromatin (Sorensen et al. 2005).
