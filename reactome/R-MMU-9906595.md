# Activation of Akt1 by shear stress in osteocytes
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9906595](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9906595)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

The integrin alphaV:beta3 (Itgav:Itgb3) complex located in the plasma membrane of dendrites of osteocytes positively regulates activation (phosphorylation) of Akt1 via phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase (Pi3k) and, presumably, its product phosphatidylinositol-3,4,5-trisphosphate in response to mechanical loading (Batra et al. 2014, Riquelme et al. 2021). Akt1 is phosphorylated on both tyrosine-308 and serine-473 (Xia et al. 2010). Akt1 is the dominant isoform of Akt in osteocytes (Batra et al. 2014).
