# Phosphorylation of USP8 by P-AKT
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1358791](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1358791)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated AKT phosphorylates USP8, thereby stabilizing it and allowing it to deubiquitinate NRDP1, which results in increased NRDP1 level and downregulation of ERBB3. This represents a negative feedback loop in ERBB3-mediated signaling.
