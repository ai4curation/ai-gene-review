# MTRFR:MTRES1 binds 39S ribosomal subunit:mRNA:peptidyl-tRNA:tRNA:MALSU1:MIEF1:NDUFAB1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9937403](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9937403)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The 39S subunit of a translationally stalled mitochondrial ribosome is separated from the 28S subunit and then binds the MTRFR:MTRES1 complex (Desai et al. 2020). The tRNA that is initially present at the E site of the stalled ribosome is not present in the 39S subunit:MTRFR:MTRES1 complex, therefore the tRNA is presumed to be ejected by the MTRFR:MTRES1 complex (Desai et al 2020). MTRFR occupies the empty A site of the 39S subunit while MTRES1 binds the anticodon stem-loop of the peptidyl tRNA (Desai et al. 2020).
