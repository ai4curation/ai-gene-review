# NOTCH1 PEST domain mutants bind promoters of HEY genes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4396402](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4396402)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NICD1 PEST domain mutant coactivator complexes are expected to bind promoters of HEY genes (HEY1, HEY2 and HEYL) in a similar manner to the wild-type NOTCH1 Coactivator Complex (Maier and Gessler 2000, Arnett et al. 2010).
