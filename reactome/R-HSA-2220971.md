# CDK8 phosphorylates NICD1 PEST domain mutants 
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2220971](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2220971)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

While CDK8 can probably phosphorylate the TAD domain of NICD1 PEST domain mutants, it cannot phosphorylate the PEST domain of these mutants as it is partially or completely truncated (Fryer et al. 2004).
