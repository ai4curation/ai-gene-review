# ATF5 activates expression of DEFA5
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9856669](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9856669)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

During the mitochondrial response to unfolded protein (mtUPR), a reduction of mitochondrial protein import causes ATF5 to accumulate in the nucleus and activate expression of the extracellular defensin DEFA5 (HD-5) (Fiorese et al. 2016).
