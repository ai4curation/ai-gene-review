# p-Y-Cbl autoubiquitinates and multiubiquitinates p-Y-559-Csf12
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9763892](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9763892)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

After Cbl is phosphorylated and binds Csf1r, Cbl autopolyubiquitinates and polyubiquitinates Csf1r (Wang et al. 1996, Lee et al. 1999, Wang et al. 1999, Xiong et al. 2011). The multiubiquitination of Csf1r results in a subsequent wave of phosphorylation by Csf1r and the internalization and proteolysis of Csf1r (Lee et al. 1999, Xiong et al. 2011). Thus multiubiquitination of Csf1r is required for full activity of Csf1r and for termination of signaling by Csf1r.
