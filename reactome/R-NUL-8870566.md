# Cdk5:p25 phosphorylates FOXO3
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-8870566](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-8870566)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recombinant mouse Cdk5 in complex with recombinant mouse p25 phosphorylates recombinant human FOXO3 on serine residues S43, S173, S294 and S325 (Shi et al. 2016).
