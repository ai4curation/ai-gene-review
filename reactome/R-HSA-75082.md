# ATAC spliceosome mediated Lariat formation,5' splice site cleavage
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-75082](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-75082)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ATAC C Complex' is present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ATAC C Complex with lariat containing 5'-end cleaved mRNA' is present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'nucleus' (Valadkhan and Manley 2001, Valadkhan et al. 2007).<br>
