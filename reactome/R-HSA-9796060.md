# NFE2L2 dependent NFE2L2 expression
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9796060](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9796060)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NFE2L2 regulates its own expression thus forming a self-regulatory loop that helps to build up the NFE2L2 response in stress conditions and provides protective support (Kwak et al, 2002)
