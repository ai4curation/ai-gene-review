# trans-2,3-dehydropristanoyl-CoA + H2O => 3-hydroxypristanoyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-389986](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-389986)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Peroxisomal HSD17B4 dimer catalyzes the reaction of trans-2,3-dehydropristanoyl-CoA and H2O to form 3-hydroxypristanoyl-CoA. The enzyme is bifunctional - an aminoterminal domain catalyzes the dehydrogenation of a variety of 3-hydroxyacyl-CoA's and a carboxyterminal domain catalyzes the hydration of a variety of trans-2,3-dehydroacyl-CoA's, the reaction annotated here (Ferdinandusse et al. 2004; Jiang et al. 1996, 1997). Defects in the enzyme are associated with a severe disorder of peroxisomal fatty acid metabolism in humans (Ferdinandusse et al. 2006).
