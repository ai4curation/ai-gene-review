# oxaloacetate + glutamate <=> aspartate + alpha-ketoglutarate [GOT1]
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70581](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70581)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic aspartate aminotransferase (glutamate oxaloacetate transaminase 1 - GOT1) catalyzes the reversible reaction of oxaloacetate (OA) and glutamate (L-Glu) to form aspartate (L-Asp) and 2-oxoglutarate (2OG, alpha-ketoglutarate) (Doyle et al. 1990). Unpublished crystallographic data (PBD 3IIO) suggest the enzyme is a homodimer.
