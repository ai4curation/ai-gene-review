# PIAS2,3,4 SUMOylate RORA with SUMO2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4719413](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4719413)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PIAS2-2 (PIASx-alpha, PIAS3, and PIAS4 SUMOylate RORA with SUMO2 at lysine-240 (Hwang et al. 2009). SUMOylation increases the transcriptional activity of RORA.
