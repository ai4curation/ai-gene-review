# NFE2L2 dependent ATF4 expression
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9796061](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9796061)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Stress induction of ATF4 is significantly regulated transcriptionally through an NFE2L2-dependent mechanism. This helps in regulating the oxidative stress impact (Miyamoto et al, 2011, DeNicola et al, 2015)
