# METTL21A transfers 3xCH3 from 3xAdoMet to HSPA8
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8932221](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8932221)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Protein N-lysine methyltransferase METTL21A trimethylates lysine-561 of Heat shock cognate 71 kDa protein (HSPA8) and corresponding lysine residues in other Hsp70 isoforms (Jakobsson et al. 2013, Cloutier et al. 2013).
