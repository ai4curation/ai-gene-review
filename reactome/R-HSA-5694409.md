# Nucleotide exchange on RAB1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5694409](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5694409)

## Summary

The TRAPPC complex acts as a guanine-nucleotide exchange factor for RAB1, activating it (Yamasaki et al, 2009; reviewed in Brunet and Sacher, 2014).
