# (S)-Hydroxyhexanoyl-CoA+NAD => 3-Oxohexanoyl-CoA+NADH+H
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77323](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77323)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

HADH in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates (S)-3-hydroxyhexanoyl-CoA to form 3-oxohexanoyl-CoA (Vredendaal et al. 1996).
