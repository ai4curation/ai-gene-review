# Elongation of docosatetraenoyl-CoA to tetracosatetraenoyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2046095](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2046095)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Docosatetraenoyl-CoA is elongated to tetracosatetraenoyl-CoA (TTA-CoA, 9,12,15,18-tetracosatetraenoic acid, 9,12,15,18-24:4(n-6)) by the elongase enzyme ELOVL2.
