# ANKLE2 binds PP2A
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9667965](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9667965)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

ANKLE2 binds the PP2A complex that contains the B55-alpha regulatory subunit and facilitates BANF1 dephosphorylation (Asencio et al. 2012).
