# NMNAT3 transfers an adenylyl group from ATP to NAMN to yield NAAD
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-200474](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-200474)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NMNAT3 catalyzes the reaction of nicotinate D-ribonucleotide and ATP to form deamino-NAD+ (nicotinate adenine dinucleotide) and pyrophosphate (Sorci et al. 2007). The active form of the enzyme is a tetramer in vitro (Zhang et al. 2003). Recombinant FLAG-tagged protein expressed in HeLa cells localizes both to the cytosol and to mitochondria (Berger et al. 2005). The mitochondrial protein is annotated here.
