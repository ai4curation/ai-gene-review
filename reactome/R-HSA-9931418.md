# CK2 phosphorylates CD274
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9931418](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9931418)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Casein Kinase 2 (CK2) phosphorylates and stabilizes PD-L1 (CD274) in dendritic and cancer cells. CK2 phosphorylates PD-L1 at Thr285 (T285) and Thr290 (T290), thus disrupting PD-L1 binding with the speckle-type POZ protein, an adaptor protein of the cullin-3 (CUL3) ubiquitin E3 ubiquitin ligase complex SPOP:CUL3:RBX1, protecting PD-L1 from SPOP:CUL3:RBX1-mediated proteasomal degradation (Zhao et al., 2022).
