# RNF41 ubiquitinates activated ERBB3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1358792](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1358792)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RNF41 ubiquitinates activated ERBB3, thereby downregulating ERBB3-mediated signaling. This reaction is part of a negative feedback loop in ERBB2:ERBB3 signaling.
