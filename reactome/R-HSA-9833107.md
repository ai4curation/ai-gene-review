# Association of NS1 (1C) with Cul5 and SCF
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9833107](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9833107)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

hRSV nonstructural protein 1C (NS1) binds to the elongin C (ELOC) and cullin-5 (CUL5) components of the Cul5-SCF complex to direct the ubiquitination and proteasome-dependent degradation of STAT2 (Elliott et al, 2007).
