# Complement factor I binds to MCP, CR1:C4b, C3b
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-977602](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-977602)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Membrane cofactor protein (MCP, CD46) and Complement Receptor 1 (CR1) act as cofactors for the protease activity of complement factor I (CFI) which binds MCP or CR1 complexes with C3b or C4b, inactivating  C3b/C4b.
