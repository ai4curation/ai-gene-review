# NLRX1 inhibits MAVS-DDX58 interaction
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-936564](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-936564)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NLRX1 is a member of nucleotide-binding domain and leucine-rich repeat containing (NLR) protein family. NLRX1 competes with RIG-I (DDX58) for MAVS (IPS-1) interaction and has been identified as a negative regulator of RLR signaling. NLRX1 resides at the outer mitochondrial membrane where MAVS is located and this interaction is mediated by the CARD region of MAVS (IPS-1) and a putative nucleotide-binding domain (NBD) of NLRX1. This interaction between NLRX1 and MAVS prevents the association of DDX58 (RIG-1) and/or IFIH1 (MDA5) with MAVS.
