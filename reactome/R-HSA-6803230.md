# Dissociation of moderate activity BRAF complexes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6803230](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6803230)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After phosphorylation by MAP2Ks, the scaffolded kinase complex assembled by moderately active BRAF mutants dissociates, as is the case for WT complexes (reviewed in Lavoie and Therrien et al, 2015).
