# SPI1 (PU.1), PML isoform 4, and EP300 bind the promoter of the CEBPE gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9617064](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9617064)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SPI1 (PU.1) binds the promoter of the CEBPE gene. PML (isoform 4) interacts with SPI1 and recruits the coactivator  EP300 (p300) to SPI1 (Yoshida et al. 2007). The PML-RARA leukemogenic fusion protein dissociates the SPI1:PML:EP300 complex and inhibits transcription of CEBPE, thereby interfering with granulocyte differentiation (Yoshida et al. 2007).
