# Cytoplasmic phosphorylated Cdc6 is ubiquitinated by the anaphase-promoting complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-69015](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-69015)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'phosphorylated Cdc6', 1 molecule of 'ubiquitin', and 1 molecule of 'ATP' are present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ubiquitinated Cdc6' is present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'cytosol' and  is mediated by the 'endopeptidase activity' of 'anaphase-promoting complex (APC)'.<br>
