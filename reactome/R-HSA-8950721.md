# Expression of Programmed cell death protein 4
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8950721](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8950721)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Experiments using human cord blood CD4(+) T cells show 22 protein spots and 20 protein spots, upregulated and downregulated proteins respectively, following Interleukin-12 stimulation (Rosengren et.al, 2005). Among the up-regulated proteins is :Programmed cell death protein 4(PDCD4).
