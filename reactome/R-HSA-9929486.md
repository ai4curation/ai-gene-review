# SPOP-mediated degradation of CD274 by 26S Proteosome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9929486](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9929486)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PD-L1 (CD274) polyubiquitinated by the SPOP:CUL3:RBX1 E3 ubiquitin ligase complex gets degraded by the 26S proteasome complex (Zhang et al., 2018, Dong et al, 2022).
