# AKT1 E17K mutant phosphorylates caspase-9
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2399985](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2399985)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

AKT1 E17K gain-of-function mutant is expected to phosphorylate caspase-9 (CASP9), like the wild-type AKT (Cardone et al. 1998), but this has not been experimentally tested.
