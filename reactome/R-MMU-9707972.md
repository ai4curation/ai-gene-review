# Socs1,3:p-4Y-Csf3r:Csf3 dimer:Lyn:p-Y-Jak1:p-Jak2:p-Syk:p-Hck:p-Tyk2 binds Cul5, EloB, EloC, and Rnf7
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9707972](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9707972)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Socs1 and Socs3 recruit Elongin B (Elob), Elongin C (Eloc), Cullin5 (Cul5), and Rnf7 to form a ubiquitin E3 ligase complex (Babon et al. 2008, Babon et al. 2009, Kershaw et al. 2014). Socs3 has 100-fold higher affinity for Cul5 than Socs1 has (Babon et al. 2009).
