# I(1,3,4,5,6)P5 is phosphorylated to 5-PP-IP4 by IP6K1/3 in the cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855223](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855223)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol hexakisphosphate kinase 1 (IP6K1) and 3 (IP6K3) phosphorylate inositol 1,3,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate (I(1,3,4,5,6)P5) to  inositol 5-diphospho-(1,3,4,6)-tetrakisphosphate (5-PP-IP4) (Saiardi et al. 2001, Saiardi et al. 2000, Draskovic et al. 2008).
