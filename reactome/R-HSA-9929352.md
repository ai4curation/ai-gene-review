# Ubiquitinated CD274 is degraded by the 26S proteasome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9929352](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9929352)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PD-L1 (CD274) is targeted for 26S proteasomal degradation through phosphorylation by GSK3B and the subsequent ubiquitination by the beta-TrCP-SCF E3 ubiquitin ligase complex (Li et al., 2016).
