# NS4A binds AUP1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9921605](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9921605)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

DENV NS4A protein associates with unmodified Lipid droplet-regulating VLDL assembly factor AUP1 (but not with its ubiquitin-modified variant). This interaction apparently increases AUP1 acyltransferase activity, enhancing the induction of lipophagy which facilitates production of virus progeny particles (Zhang et al., 2018).
