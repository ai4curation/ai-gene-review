# SOS-mediated nucleotide exchange on RAS (PDGF receptor:GRB2:SOS)
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-186834](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-186834)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SOS is the guanine nucleotide exchange factor (GEF) for RAS. SOS activates RAS nucleotide exchange from the inactive form (bound to GDP) to an active form (bound to GTP).<br>
