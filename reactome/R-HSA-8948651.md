# miR-106a,(miR106b) microRNA binds CNOT6L mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8948651](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8948651)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MicroRNA miR-106a (possibly also miR-106b) binds the CNOT5L mRNA. CNOT6L mRNA acts as a competing endogenous RNA (ceRNA) for PTEN, preventing binding of miR-106a microRNA to PTEN mRNA and PTEN downregulation (Tay et al. 2011).
