# Epo:p-8Y-Epor:p-12Y-Jak2:Lyn:Irs2 binds Plcg1,2
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9027436](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9027436)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Plcg1 (Phospholipase C gamma1) or Plcg2 binds phosphotyrosine-432 and phosphotyrosine-479 of Epor (Boudot et al. 1999, Boudot et al. 2002, Tong et al. 2004, Halupa et al. 2005, Schnoder et al. 2015). After binding, Plcg1,2 is phosphorylated (Boudot et al. 1999).
