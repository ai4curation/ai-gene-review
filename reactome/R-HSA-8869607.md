# NMRK2 phosphorylates NAR to yield NAMN
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8869607](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8869607)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic NMRK2 (nicotinamide riboside kinase 2), localized predominantly in myocytes (Li et al. 1999), catalyzes the reaction of NAR (N-ribosylnicotinate) and ATP to yield NAMN (beta-nicotinate D-ribonucleotide), ADP, and H+ (Tempel et al. 2007).
