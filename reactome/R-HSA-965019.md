# 2xPNPO:2xFMN oxidizes PDXP to PXLP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-965019](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-965019)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Pyridoxine-5'-phosphate oxidase (PNPO) is able to oxidize pyridoxine phosphate (PDXP) to pyridoxal 5'-phosphate (PXLP) (Kang et al. 2004).
