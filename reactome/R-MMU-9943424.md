# Stat1 and Nfatc1 bind the Tbx21 (T-bet) gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9943424](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9943424)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Stat1 and Nfatc1 bind the conserved noncoding sequence 12 (CNS-12) of the mouse Tbx21 (T-bet) gene (Zhong et al. 2025). Tbx21 expression is initiated by Ifng signaling via Stat1  (Lighvani et al. 2001, Afkarian et al. 2002) and by TCR signaling via Stim1, calcium signaling, and Nfatc1 (Zhong et al. 2025). Tbx21 then activates expression of the Il21 receptor subunit Il21rb2, allowing maintenance of Tbx21 expression by Il21 acting via Stat4 (Schulz et al. 2009).
