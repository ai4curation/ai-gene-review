# Addition of GlcNAc to the Tn antigen via a beta-1,6 linkage forms a Core 6 glycoprotein
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-914008](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-914008)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

An unknown N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase mediates the transfer of GlcNAc to Tn antigens via an beta-1,6 linkage to create Core 6 mucins (Brockhausen et al. 2009).
