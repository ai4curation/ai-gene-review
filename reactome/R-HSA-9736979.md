# p-2S-SMAD2,3:SMAD4:SP1 binds CDKN2B gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9736979](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9736979)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SMAD2/3:SMAD4:SP1 complex binds SP1 and SMAD promoter elements of CDKN2B (p15-INK4B) gene and stimulates transcription of CDKN2B (Feng et al. 2000).
