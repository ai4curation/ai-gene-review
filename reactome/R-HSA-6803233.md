# Dissociation of oncogenic RAS:RAF complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6803233](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6803233)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After phosphorylation by MAP2Ks, the scaffolded kinase complex assembled by oncogenic RAS presumably dissociates, as is the case for WT complexes (reviewed in Lavoie and Therrien et al, 2015; Stephen et al, 2014).
