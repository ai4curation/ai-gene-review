# ORC6 translocates to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9734713](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9734713)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylation at threonine residue T195 facilitates translocation of ORC6 to the nucleus (Ghosh et al. 2011). In the nucleus, ORC6 is thought to dissociate from the importin complex.
