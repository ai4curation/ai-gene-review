# Glucagon:GCGR mediates GTP-GDP exchange
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-825631](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-825631)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The G(s)alpha G-beta G-gamma complex bound to glucagon, in the plasma membrane, releases a molecule of bound GDP, binds a molecule of GTP, and dissociates to yield a G(s)alpha:GTP complex and a G-beta:G-gamma dimer (Siu et al. 2013).
