# FBXW7 promotes ubiquitination of mouse p-NICD4
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9604628](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9604628)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The human E3 ubiquitin ligase FBXW7, a component of the SCF ubiquitin ligase complex, binds to and ubiquitinates phosphorylated mouse NICD4 (Notch4 intracellular domain fragment), targeting it for proteasome-mediated degradation (Wu et al. 2001).
