# Activation of CDC42 by intersectin-1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-3928643](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-3928643)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Activated intersectin 1 (ITSN1) activates CDC42 by exchanging GDP with GTP, resulting in high levels of CDC42:GTP in spines.
