# ERBB2 TMD/JMD mutants bind TKIs
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9665702](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9665702)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The following ERBB2 TMD/JMD mutants are sensitive to lapatinib:<br>ERBB2 S653C (de Martino et al. 2014);<br>ERBB2 R678Q (Bose et al. 2013);<br><br>The following ERBB2 TMD/JMD mutants are sensitive to neratinib:<br>ERBB2 V659E (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 G660D (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 G660R (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 R678Q (Bose et al. 2013, Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 Q709L (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br><br>The following ERBB2 TMD/JMD mutants are sensitive to afatinib:<br>ERBB2 G660D (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 G660R (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 S653C (de Martino et al. 2014);<br>ERBB2 R678Q (Pahuja et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 Q709L (Pahuja et al. 2018).
