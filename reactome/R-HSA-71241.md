# TMLHE dimer dioxygenates TMLYS and 2OG to form HTMLYS and SUCCA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-71241](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-71241)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Trimethyllysine dioxygenase (TMLHE) dimer in the mitochondrial matrix catalyzes the reaction of oxygen, 2-oxoglutarate (2OG), and N6,N6,N6-trimethyl-L-lysine (TMLYS) to form CO2, 3-hydroxy-N6,N6,N6-trimethyl-L-lysine (HTMLYS), and succinate (SUCCA) (Monfregola et al. 2005; Vaz et al. 2001).
