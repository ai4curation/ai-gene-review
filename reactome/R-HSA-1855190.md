# I(1,4,5)P3 transports from the cytosol to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855190](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855190)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate (I(1,4,5)P3) translocates from the cytosol to the nucleus via the nuclear pore (Dewaste et al. 2003, Nalaskowski et al. 2002).
