# Isopentenyl pyrophosphate rearranges to dimethylallyl pyrophosphate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191382](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191382)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic  Isopentenyl-diphosphate delta-isomerase isoforms 1 and 2 (IDI1 or 2)) catalyze an essential activation step in the isoprenoid biosynthetic pathway, rearranging isopentenyl pyrophosphate (IPPP) into its highly electrophilic isomer, dimethylallyl pyrophosphate (DMAPP) (Hahn et al. 1996). IPP isomerase may also be located in human peroxisomes but its function there is not clear.
