# TCN2:RCbl is degraded to release RCbl
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-3000263](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-3000263)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

A lysosomal protease that is sensitive to treatment with chloroquine but otherwise uncharacterized hydrolyzes transcobalamin 2 (TCN2) bound to cobalamins (RCbl), releasing the latter (Youngdahl Turner et al. 1979).
