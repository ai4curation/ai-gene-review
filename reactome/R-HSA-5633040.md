# CSNK1A1 and ADRBK1 dissociate from p-SMO dimer
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5633040](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5633040)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After phosphorylating the SMO dimer, CSNK1A1 and ADRBK1 presumably dissociate, although this has not been demonstrated explicitly (Chen et al, 2011).
