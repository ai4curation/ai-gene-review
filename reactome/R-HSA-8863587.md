# CDK5:p25 translocates to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8863587](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8863587)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The complex of CDK5 and p25 can translocate to the nucleus (Patrick et al. 1999). Nuclear envelope fragmentation, initiated by CDK5:p25-mediate phosphorylation of lamin A and B1, increases access of the CDK5:p25 complex to nuclear proteins (Chang et al. 2011).
