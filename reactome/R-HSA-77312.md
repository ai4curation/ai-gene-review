# (S)-Hydroxybutanoyl-CoA+NAD => Acetoacetyl-CoA+NADH+H
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77312](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77312)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

HADH in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates (S)-3-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA to form acetoacetyl-CoA (Vredendaal et al. 1996; Barycki et al. 1999).
