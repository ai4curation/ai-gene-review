# Resistant ERBB2 KD mutants do not bind trastuzumab
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9665232](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9665232)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The following ERBB2 KD mutants are resistant to the therapeutic antibody trastuzumab (herceptin):<br><br>ERBB2 L755P (Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 L755S (Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 I767M (Bose et al. 2013);<br>ERBB2 D769Y (Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 V777L (Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 G778_P780dup (Bose et al. 2013, Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 T798M (Rexer et al. 2013);<br>ERBB2 V842I (Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 T862A (Nagano et al. 2018);<br>ERBB2 L869R (Hanker et al. 2017);<br><br>For ERBB2 R896C, both resistance (Bose et al. 2013) and sensitivity (Nagano et al. 2018) to trastuzumab have been reported.
