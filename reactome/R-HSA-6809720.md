# PI3P is dephosphorylated to PI by MTM1:MTMR12
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6809720](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6809720)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Binding of MTMR12 to MTM1 enhances phosphatidylinositol-3-phosphatase activity of MTM1 at cytosolic vesicular structures, presumably early or late endosomes (Caldwell et al. 1991, Nandurkar et al. 2003, Gupta et al. 2013).
