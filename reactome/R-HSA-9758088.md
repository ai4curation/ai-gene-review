# UBXN7 binds VCP hexamer:UBF1:NPLOC4
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9758088](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9758088)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

UBXN7 interacts with a complex of VCP, UBF1 and NPLOC4 to promote the CRL3- and 26 S proteasome-mediated degradation of factors such as NFE2L2 (Alexandru et al, 2008; Tao et al, 2017).
