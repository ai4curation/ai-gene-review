# MVD decarboxylates MVA5PP to IPPP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191414](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191414)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mevalonate pyrophosphate decarboxylase (MPD) decarboxylates mevalonate-5-pyrophosphate (MVA5PP) into isopentenyl pyrophosphate (IPPP) while hydrolysing ATP to ADP and orthophosphate (Toth & Huwyler 1996).
