# Caspase-mediated cleavage of gelsolin
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-201622](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-201622)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Gelsolin is cleaved by caspase-3  generating a constitutively <br>active fragment that can depolymerize F-actin  contributing to actin cytoskeletal collapse (Kothakota et al., 1997)
