# 3-hydroxykynurenine + H2O => 3-hydroxyanthranilate + alanine + H+
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-71217](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-71217)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic kynureninase catalyzes the hydrolysis of 3-hydroxy-L-kynurenine to form L-alanine and 3-hydroxyanthranilate.
