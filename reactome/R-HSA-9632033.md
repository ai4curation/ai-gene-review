# CSGALNACTs transfer GalNAc to CS chain
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9632033](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9632033)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Chondroitin sulfate N-acetylgalactosaminyltransferases 1 and 2 (CSGALNACT1 and 2) (Uyama et al. 2002, Gotoh et al. 2002) transfer N-acetylgalactosamine (GalNAc) from UDP-GalNAc to the terminal glucuronate (GlcA) residue of the CS chain. CSGALNACTs may also regulate CS chain length (reviewed in Mikami & Kitagawa, 2013). Mutations in CSGALNACT1 can lead to mild skeletal dysplasia (SDJLABA, MIM:618870; Vodopiutz et al., 2017).
