# B3GNT6 transfers GlcNAc to Tn antigen
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-914010](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-914010)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Acetylgalactosaminyl-O-glycosyl-glycoprotein beta-1,3-N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase (B3GNT6) catalyzes the addition of N-acetylglucosamine to the T antigen to form the Core 3 glycoprotein (Vavasseur et al., 2006). The reaction occurs in the Golgi.

The UDP-GlcNAc:betaGal beta-1,3-N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase family (B3GNTs) consists of 9 members in humans (Kolbinger et al, 1998; Shiraishi et al, 2001; Togayachi et al, 2001; Iwai et al, 2002; Huang et al, 2004; Ishida et al, 2005; Zheng et al, 2004).
