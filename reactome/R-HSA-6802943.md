# RAS:GTP:inactive p-RAF complexes phosphorylate MAP2Ks
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802943](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802943)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

As in the WT pathway, activation of ERK signaling downstream of kinase-dead or inhibited forms of BRAF depends on phosphorylation of two serine residues in the activation loop of the MAP2K proteins (Wan et al, 2004; Garnett et al, 2005; Heidorn et al, 2010;  Poulikakos et al, 2010; Hatzivassiliou et al, 2010; reviewed in Samatar and Poulikakos, 2014; Lavoie and Therrien, 2015).
