# LIPT1 transfers lipoyl group from lipoyl-GCSH to DLAT
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9858752](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9858752)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mitochondrial lipoyltransferase 1 (LIPT1) catalyses the transfer of a lipoyl group from lipoyl-K107-GCSH to two lysine residues of the PDH complex subunit E2 (DLAT) (Fujiwara et al. 1999; Cao et al., 2018). Defects in LIPT1 reduce lipoylation of pyruvate dehydrogenase and alpha-ketoglutarate dehydrogenase, causing a cofactor disorder of mitochondrial energy metabolism (Soreze et al. 2013, Tort et al. 2014; Ni et al., 2019).
