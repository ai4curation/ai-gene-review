# RUNX3 binds EP300
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8951951](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8951951)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The histone acetyltransferase EP300 (p300) forms a complex with RUNX3, presumably bound to CBFB (Jin et al. 2004, Lee et al. 2013). EP300 can also form a complex with other RUNX family members, RUNX1 and RUNX2 (Jin et al. 2004).
