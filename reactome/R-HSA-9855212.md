# SDHA binds to SDHB
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9855212](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9855212)

## Summary

The matured SDH subunits A and B (SDHA, SDHB) bind, releasing the chaperone SDHAF4 (Na et al., 2014; reviewed in Van Vranken et al., 2014).
