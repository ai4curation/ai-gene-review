# NICD3 binds RBPJ and MAML in the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9013647](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9013647)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mouse Notch3 intracellular domain (NICD3) forms a complex with human RBPJ (CSL) and any of the human mastermind proteins MAML1, MAML2 or MAML3 (Lin et al. 2002).
