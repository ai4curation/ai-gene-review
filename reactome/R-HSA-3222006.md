**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-3222006](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-3222006)

## Summary

STK11 (LKB1) phosphorylates NUAK1 at threonine residue T211, resulting in NUAK1 activation (Hou et al. 2011).
