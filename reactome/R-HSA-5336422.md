# Clathrin-mediated endocytosis of DT:HBEGF:CD9
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5336422](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5336422)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The complex of diphtheria toxin (DT A:B) and target cell surface proteins HBEGF and CD9 is taken up by endocytosis into a clathrin-coated vesicle (Moya et al. 1985; Murphy 2011).
