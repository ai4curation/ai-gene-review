# depalmitoylated eNOS translocates from plasma membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-202132](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-202132)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Depalmitoylated, eNOS is thought to be displaced from the plasma membrane and redistributed to other intracellular membranes, including the Golgi, where re-palmitoylation occurs. The mechanism of transport from the plasma membrane is unknown.
