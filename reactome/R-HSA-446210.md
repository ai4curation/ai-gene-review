# Synthesis of UDP-N-acetyl-glucosamine
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-446210](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-446210)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

UDP-acetylglucosamine acts as a donor for the first two steps of the N-glycan precursor biosynthesis pathway, and is later used as a substrate for further modifications after the precursor has been attached to the protein. It is synthesized from fructose 6-phosphate, glutamine, acetyl-CoA, and UTP in four steps.
