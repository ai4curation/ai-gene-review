# Activated MAP2Ks phosphorylate MAPKs downstream of high kinase activity BRAF mutants
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802910](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802910)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Highly active BRAF mutants show constitutive phosphorylation of MAPKs that does not depend on RAS activation or RAF dimerization (Davies et al, 2002; Wan et al, 2004; Garnett et al, 2005; reveiwed in Lavoie and Therrien, 2015).
