# GSK3 phosphorylates p-GLI3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5610732](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5610732)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

GSK3-mediated phosphorylation of GLI3 is primed by earlier phosphorylations by PKA and CK1 and is required for the subsequent recruitment of beta-TrCP (Tempe et al, 2006; Wang and Li, 2006).
