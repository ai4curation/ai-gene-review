# Carboxypeptidase E (rat Carboxypeptidase H) cleaves Insulin(25-56) to yield Insulin(25-54)
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9023166](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9023166)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Carboxypeptidase E (also called Carboxypeptidase H) purified from rat beta cells cleaves the C-terminal two amino acid residues (ariginine-56 and arginine-55) from the B chain derived from human proinsulin (Davidson and Hutton 1987).
