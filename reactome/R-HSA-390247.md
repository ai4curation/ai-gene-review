# Beta-oxidation of very long chain fatty acids
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-390247](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-390247)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Linear fatty acids containing more than 18 carbons are broken down by beta-oxidation in peroxisomes to yield acetyl-CoA and medium chain-length fatty acyl CoA's such as octanoyl-CoA (Wanders and Waterham 2006).
