**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77327](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77327)

## Summary

ACADS in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates hexanoyl-CoA to form trans-hex-2-enoyl-CoA. (Krane & Beinert 1956; Finocchiaro et al. 1987).
