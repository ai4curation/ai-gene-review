# Arginine<=>Agmatine+CO2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-350598](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-350598)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The polyamine agmatine is formed by decarboxylation of L-arginine by arginine decarboxylase (ADC). Human ADC is a 460-amino acid protein that shows about 48% identity to mammalian ornithine decarboxylase (ODC) but has no ODC activity (Zhu et al. 2004).
