# CR2:C3d,C3dg,iC3b binds CD19:CD81
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8853252](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8853252)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Complement receptor CR2 (CD21), having bound to C3d, Cdg or 1C3b, forms the B cell co-receptor complex with CD19 and CD81 (Matsumoto et al. 1993).
