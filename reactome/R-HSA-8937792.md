# RUNX3 binds SRC
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8937792](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8937792)

## Summary

Activated SRC binds to RUNX3 in the cytosol. The interaction involves the Runt domain of RUNX3 (Goh et al. 2010).
