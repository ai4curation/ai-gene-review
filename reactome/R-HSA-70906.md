# 4-imidazolone-5-propanoate + H2O => N-formiminoglutamate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70906](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70906)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic imidazolonepropionase catalyzes the hydrolysis of 4-imidazolone-5-propanoate to form N-Formimino-L-glutamate. While evidence from many experimental systems indicates that this reaction occurs, existence of the human enzyme is inferred only from high-throughput screening studies (e.g., Yamada et al. 2004).
