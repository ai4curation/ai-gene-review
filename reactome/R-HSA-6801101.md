# RABGGTA binds RABGGTB
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6801101](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6801101)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RABGGTA associates with RABGGTB to form RAB geranylgeranyl transferase (RGGT or RAB GGTase). This was initially shown using proteins purified from rat brain (Seabra et al. 1992), but the complex is evolutionarily conserved in human cells (Baron and Seabra 2008).
