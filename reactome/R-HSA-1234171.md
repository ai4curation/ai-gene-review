# HIF-alpha binds ARNT (HIF1-beta) forming HIF-alpha:ARNT
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1234171](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1234171)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

HIF-alpha (HIF1A, HIF2A (EPAS1, MOP2), HIF3A isoform 1) forms a heterodimer with ARNT (HIF1-beta) (Wang et al. 1995, Jiang et al. 1996, Hogenesch et al. 1997, Tian et al. 1997, Gu et al. 1998, Erbel et al. 2003, Heikkilä et al. 2011, Zhang et al. 2014, Kim et al. 2015).
