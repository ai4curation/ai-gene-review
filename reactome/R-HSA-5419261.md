# 55S ribosome with peptidyl-tRNA in A site binds GFM1:GTP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5419261](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5419261)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

GFMT1:GTP (EF-G1mt:GTP) binds ribosomes possessing a peptidyl-tRNA at the A site and an empty P site (Bhargava et al. 2004, Tsuboi et al. 2009, inferred from bovine homologs in Chung and Spremulli 1990).
