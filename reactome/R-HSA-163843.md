# Furin cleaves pro-GAS6 to GAS6
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-163843](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-163843)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Furin, associated with the Golgi membrane, cleaves pro-GAS6 to form mature, gamma-carboxylated GAS6 (Wasley et al. 1993).
