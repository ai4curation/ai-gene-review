# PMM1,2 isomerise Man6P to Man1P
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-446201](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-446201)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic phosphomannomutases 1 and 2 (PMM1 and PMM2) catalyse the isomerisation of mannose 6-phosphate (Man6P) to mannose 1-phosphate (Man1P) (Silvaggi et al. 2006, Wada & Sakamoto 1997, Matthijs et al. 1997). Mutations in the PMM2 gene are one of the causes of Jaeken syndrome, a disease of glycosylation, type CDGIa. (Matthijs et al. 1997b).
