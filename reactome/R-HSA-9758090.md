# Ubiquitinated NFE2L2 is extracted from CRL3 complex for degradation
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9758090](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9758090)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The complex of VCP/p97 with cofactors UFD1, NPLOC4 and UBXN7 extract ubiquitinated NFE2L2 from the KEAP1-CUL3 ubiquitin ligase complex prior to its 26S proteasome-mediated degradation (Tao et al, 2017; Di Gregorio et al, 2021; reviewed in van den Boom and Meyer, 2020).
