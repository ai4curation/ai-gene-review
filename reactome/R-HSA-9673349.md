# WNT5 binds FZD7
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9673349](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9673349)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Non-canonical WNT5A pathways (the ones that do not induce the activation of β-catenin) are usually initiated by the binding of WNT5A to FZD receptors, including FZD7 (Nishita et al. 2010 & Anastas et al. 2014).
