**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77333](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77333)

## Summary

ECHS1 in the mitochondrial matrix hydrates trans-oct-2-enoyl-CoA to form (S)-3-hydroxyoctanoyl-CoA (Stern & Del Campillo 1956).
