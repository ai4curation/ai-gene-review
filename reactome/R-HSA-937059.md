# Phosphorylation of IRAK2 bound to the activated IRAK4:MyD88 oligomer:TIRAP:activated TLR complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-937059](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-937059)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

IRAK4 deficient macrophages fail to induce IRAK2 phosphorylation (Kawagoe et al. 2008), suggesting that activated IRAK4 phosphorylates IRAK2 as it does IRAK1.<p>Phosphorylation sites of IRAK2 remain to be characterized.
