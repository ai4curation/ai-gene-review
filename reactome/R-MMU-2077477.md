# Numb recruits Itch to Notch1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-2077477](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-2077477)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

By expressing exogenous recombinant mouse Notch1, Numb and Itch in human embryonic kidney cell line HEK293 and mouse myogenic cell line C2C12, it was shown that Numb over-expression results in a reduction of the internalized pool of biotinylated Notch1.This effect depends on the presence of an Itch-interacting domain of Numb.
