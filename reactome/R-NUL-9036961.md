# Ntrk3 binds Grb2 through Shc1
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9036961](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9036961)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mouse Shc1, phosphorylated by recombinant rat Ntrk3 (TrkC), activated by recombinant human NTF3 (NT-3), binds mouse Grb2 (Gunn-Moore et al. 1997).
