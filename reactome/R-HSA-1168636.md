# p-RasGRP1,3:DAG cause RAS to exchange GDP for GTP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1168636](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1168636)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RasGRP1 (Roose et al. 2007) and RasGRP3 (Ohba et al. 2000, Yamashita et al. 2000, Rebhun et al. 2000, Lorenzo et al. 2001) catalyze the exchange of GDP for GTP bound by RAS.
