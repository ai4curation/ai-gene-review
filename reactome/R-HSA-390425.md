# FAR1 reduces PalmCoA to HXOL
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-390425](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-390425)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Peroxisomal fatty acyl-CoA reductase 1 (FAR1) catalyzes the reaction of palmitoyl-CoA (PalmCoA) and 2 NADPH + 2 H+ to form hexadecanol (HXOL), CoASH, and 2 NADP+. As judged from mRNA levels, FAR1 is widely expressed in the body (Cheng and Russell 2004).
