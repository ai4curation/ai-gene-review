# ERBB2 TMD/JMD mutants do not bind pertuzumab
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9665708](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9665708)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

ERBB2 V659E, ERBB2 G660D, ERBB2 G660R and probably ERBB2 Q709L are resistant to therapeutic antibody pertuzumab (Pahuja et al. 2018).
