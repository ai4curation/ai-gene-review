# miR-19b microRNA binds CNOT6L mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8948612](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8948612)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MicroRNA miR-19b, encoded by the MIR19B1 and MIR19B2 genes, binds the CNOT6L mRNA. CNOT6L mRNA acts as a competing endogenous RNA (ceRNA) for PTEN, preventing binding of miR-19b microRNA to PTEN mRNA and PTEN downregulation (Tay et al. 2011).
