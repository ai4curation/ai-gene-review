# ST6GALNAC2 transfers sialic acid to Core 1 mucins
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-981814](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-981814)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The human gene ST6GALNAC2 encodes GalNAc alpha-2,6-sialyltransferase II which mediates the transfer of sialic acid (Neu5Ac) onto the T antigen (Samyn-Petit et al. 2000).
