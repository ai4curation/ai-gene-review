# MIR23B microRNA binds 3'UTR of TGFBR3 mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9860168](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9860168)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

miR‐23b‐3p reduced the luciferase activity by binding to the site of 285‐292 of TGFBR3 3ʹ‐UTR. This suggests that TGFBR3 is a target gene of miR‐23b‐3p (Yang et al, 2019)
