# FOXO1 and p-2S-Smad2/3:Smad4 bind Mstn gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9625492](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9625492)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recombinant human FOXO1 and the endogenous mouse p-2S-Smad2/3:Smad4 complex bind the promoter of the mouse Mstn gene, encoding myostatin (Allen and Unterman 2007).
