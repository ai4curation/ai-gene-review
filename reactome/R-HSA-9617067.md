# All-trans retinoic acid binds RARA:RXRA at the promoter of the CEBPE gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9617067](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9617067)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The RARA:RXR heterodimeric retinoic acid receptor binds a retinoic acid receptor element (RARE) in the promoter of the CEBPE gene (Park et al. 1999). Retinoic acid binds RARA:RXR at the CEBPE gene and activates transcription of CEBPE (Park et al. 1999).
