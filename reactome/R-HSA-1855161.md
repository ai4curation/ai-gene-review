# I(1,3,4,5,6)P5 transports from the nucleus to the cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855161](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855161)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol 1,3,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate I(1,3,4,5,6)P5 translocates from the nucleus to the cytosol via the nuclear pore (Nalaskowski et al. 2002; Ho et al. 2002, Brehm et al. 2007).
