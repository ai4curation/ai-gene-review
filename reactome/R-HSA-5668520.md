# 26Sproteasome degrades K48polyUb-NIK
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5668520](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5668520)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Polyubiquitinated NIK is targeted for proteasomal degradation and as a consequence the NIK levels are invisible and thus prevent non-canonical NF-kB activation.
