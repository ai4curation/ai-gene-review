# HMGCS1 condenses Ac-CoA and ACA-CoA to form bHMG-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191323](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191323)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The cytosolic form of 3-hydroxy-3-methylglutaryl Coenzyme A synthase (HMGCS1) catalyzes the condensation of acetyl CoA with acetoacetyl CoA to produce HMG-CoA. HMGCS1 is ubiquitously expressed in the body and is involved in synthesis of isoprenoids and cholesterol biosynthesis (Rokosz et al. 1994).
