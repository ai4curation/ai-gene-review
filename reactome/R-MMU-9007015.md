# Mecp2 is phosphorylated at S421
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9007015](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9007015)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Mouse calmodulin-activated kinase CaMK IV, and possibly CaMK II, phosphorylates mouse Mecp2 on serine residue S421, increasing the affinity of Mecp2 for chromatin (Zhou et al. 2006, Tao et al. 2009.
