# GPI dimer isomerizes Fru(6)P to G6P
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70475](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70475)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The reversible isomerization of Fru(6)P (fructose-6-phosphate) to form G6P (glucose-6-phosphate) is catalyzed by cytosolic GPI (phosphoglucose isomerase) dimer (Noltman 1972; Tsuboi et al. 1958; Xu and Beutler 1994).
