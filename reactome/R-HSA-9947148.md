# CYP51A1 14-demethylates 24,25-dhLAN to 4,4-diMeCholesta-8,14-dien-3-ol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9947148](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9947148)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

CYP51A1 (Lanosterol 14-alpha demethylase), inferred to be located in the endoplasmic reticulum membrane (Aoyama et al. 1996), demethylates 24,25-dhLAN (24,25-dihydrolanosterol) to 4,4-diMeCholesta-8,14-dien-3-ol (Sonoda et al. 1993; Stromstedt et al. 1996), in a reaction coupled to POR-mediated reduction of oxidized heme iron atoms.
