# CLDN5 (Claudin-5) gene expression is stimulated by RUNX1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8936007](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8936007)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Binding of the RUNX1:CBFB complex to the promoter of the CLDN5 gene stimulates transcription of the tight junction component Claudin-5 (Miao et al. 2015).
