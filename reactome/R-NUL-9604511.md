# Notch4 binds FLT4 gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9604511](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9604511)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Recombinant mouse Notch4 intracellular domain fragment (NICD4), in complex with human RBPJ (CSL) and MAML, binds to RBPJ (CSL) response elements in the promoter of the human FLT4 (VEGFR) gene (Shawber et al. 2007).
