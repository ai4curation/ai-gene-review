# NS5 interacts with Spliceosome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9921507](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9921507)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Nuclear DENV NS5 protein binds to the components CD2BP2 and DDX23 of the Spliceosomal Intermediate C (Bact) Complex, interfering with cellular splicing, and apparently producing a less restrictive environment for viral replication (De Maio et al., 2016).
