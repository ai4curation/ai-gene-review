# 2xPNPO:2xFMN oxidizes PXAP to PXLP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-965079](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-965079)

## Summary

Pyridoxine-5'-phosphate oxidase (PNPO) is able to oxidize pyridoxamine phosphate (PXAP) to pyridoxal 5'-phosphate (PXLP) (Kang et al. 2004).
