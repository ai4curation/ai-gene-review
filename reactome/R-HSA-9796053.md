# PRKCI phosphorylates NFE2L2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9796053](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9796053)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylation of NFE2L2 Ser40 by PRKCI (PKC iota, an atypical PKC) is involved in the nuclear translocation and ARE transactivation of NFE2L2 by oxidative stress (Numazawa et al, 2003)
