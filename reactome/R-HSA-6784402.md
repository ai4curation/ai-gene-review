# ALDH4A1 converts 1PYR-3OH-5COOH to 4-OH-L-glutamate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6784402](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6784402)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mitochondrial delta-1-pyrroline-5-carboxylate dehydrogenase (ALDH4A1) catalyzes the reaction sequence, here represented as a single event, of 1PYR-3OH-5COOH and NAD+ to form 4-OH-L-glutamate and NADH + H+. ALDH4A1 also catalyzes the corresponding reaction of proline catabolism, as shown in biochemical and structural studies (Adams & Goldstone 1960; Srivastava et al. 2012), and mutations in ALDH4A1 disrupt both catabolic processes in human patients (Valle et al. 1979).
