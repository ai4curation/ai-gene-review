# Il15ra:Il15:p-Y-Il2rb:p-Y-Jak1:p-Y-Il2rg:p-Y-Jak3 phosphorylates Shc1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-8983294](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-8983294)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

 Interleukin-15 (Il15) stimulates the phosphorylation of Shc1 bound to Interleukin-2 receptor subunit beta (Il2rb) (Zambricki et al 2005), which is a component of the Il15 and Il2 receptor complexes (Bennet et al. 1994, Li et al. 1994).
