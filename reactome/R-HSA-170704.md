# Phosphorylation of DSIF by the P-TEFb(Cyclin T1:Cdk9) complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-170704](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-170704)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylation of the Spt5 subunit of DSIF by P-TEFb(Cyclin T1:Cdk9) results in the conversion of DSIF to an elongation factor (Ivanov al. 2000).
