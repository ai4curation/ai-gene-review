# SfaS binds the fibronectin matrix
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9638552](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9638552)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

S fimbriae on pathogenic E. coli bind the terminal alpha-sialic acid-2-3-beta-Gal group on fibronectin (fibronectin matrix) through the SfaS adhesin (Sarén et al. 1999).
