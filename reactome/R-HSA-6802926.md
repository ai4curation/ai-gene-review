# Mutant RAS:p-RAF complexes phosphorylate MAP2Ks
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802926](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802926)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MAP2K proteins are phosphorylated downstream of oncogenic RAS mutants; somewhat surprisingly, however, steady state levels of phospho-MAP2K and phospho-MAPKs are not always elevated in cancer cells (reviewed in Ramos, 2008; Neuzillet et al, 2014; Stephen et al, 2014).
