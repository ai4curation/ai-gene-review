# Rbpj:Nicd and Msgn1 bind the Lfng gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9824231](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9824231)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Mesogenin1 (Msgn1) binds the upstream regulatory region of the Lunatic fringe (Lfng) gene (Chalamalasetty et al. 2011). Msgn1 binds both the block A conserved promoter element that regulates expression in the posterior presomitic mesoderm and the block B conserved element that regulates expression in the anterior presomitic mesoderm (Chalamalasetty et al. 2011). Rbpj and the Notch intracellular domain (Nicd) fragment produced by Notch1 signaling bind the block A conserved element (Morales et al. 2002).
