# FLT3 mutants:GRB2:SOS1-mediated nucleotide exchange on RAS
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9695853](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9695853)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SOS1-mediated nucleotide exchange on RAS downstream of activated FLT3 mutants leads to stimulation of the MAP kinase signaling cascade (Mizuki et al, 2000; Hayakawa et al, 2000; Masson et al, 2009; reviewed in Kazi and Ronnstrand, 2019).
