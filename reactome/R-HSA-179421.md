# Degradation of multiubiquitinated Nek2A
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-179421](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-179421)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Nek2A is degraded by the 26S proteasome following ubiquitylation by the E3 ubiquitin ligase APC/C: Cdc20.
