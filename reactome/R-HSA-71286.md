# guanidinoacetate + S-adenosylmethionine => creatine + S-adenosylhomocysteine
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-71286](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-71286)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic guanidinoacetate methyltransferase catalyzes the reaction of S-adenosylmethionine and guanidinoacetate to form S-adenosylhomocysteine and creatine (Stockler et al. 1996).
