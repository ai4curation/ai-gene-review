# MTRFR:MTRES1:39S ribosomal subunit:mRNA:peptidyl-tRNA:MALSU1:MIEF1:NDUFAB1 dissociates
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9937374](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9937374)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After the MTRFR:MTRES1 complex binds the dissociated 39S ribosomal subunit. MTRFR appears to bind the empty A site, MTRES1 binds and repositions the peptidyl-tRNA, ejecting the E-site tRNA. The MTRFR:MTRES1 heterodimer then hydrolyzes the peptidyl-tRNA and ejects the nascent polypeptide chain and the P-site tRNA, regenerating the free 39S ribosomal subunit (Desai et al. 2020). Like other translation termination factors, MTRFR has a conserved GGQ motif which participates in hydrolyzing the peptidyl-tRNA bond (Desai et al. 2020)
