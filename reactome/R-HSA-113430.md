# Extrusion of 5'-end of 30 nt long transcript through the pore in Pol II complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-113430](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-113430)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'Pol II transcription complex containing transcript to +30' is present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'Pol II transcription complex containing extruded transcript to +30' is present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'nucleus' (Buratowski 2009).
