**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77321](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77321)

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of '3-Oxohexanoyl-CoA', and 1 molecule of 'CoA' are present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'Acetyl-CoA', and 1 molecule of 'Butanoyl-CoA' are present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'mitochondrial matrix' and  is mediated by the 'transferase activity' of 'Trifunctional Protein'.<br>
