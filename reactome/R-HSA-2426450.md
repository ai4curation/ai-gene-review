# Laminins:Nidogens binds collagen type IV networks
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2426450](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2426450)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Laminin-bound nidogens can bind to type IV collagen (Aumailey et al. 1989, 1993, Fox et al. 1991, Reinhardt et al. 1993, Ries et al. 2001, Bechtel et al. 2012).<br><br>Basement membrane formation involves self-assembly of laminin and of collagen IV into two independent networks (Yurchenco & Schittny 1990, Timpl & Brown 1996) that are connected by nidogen (Fox et al. 1991, Aumailley & Smyth 1998, Aumailey et al. 2000) and the heparan sulfate chains of both perlecan and agrin (Hohenester & Yurchenko 2013).
