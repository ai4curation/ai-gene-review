# EGR2 and SOX10 bind the MAG gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9616116](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9616116)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

EGR2 and SOX10 bind to elements in the MAG gene to promote expression during myelination in the peripheral and central nervous system (Jones et al, 2007; Jang et al, 2006; LeBlanc et al, 2007).
