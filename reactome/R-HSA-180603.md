# Proteosome-mediated degradation of APOBEC3G
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-180603](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-180603)

## Summary

Following multi-ubiquitination by the Vif-Cul5-SCF complex, APOBEC3G is degraded by the 26S proteasome.
