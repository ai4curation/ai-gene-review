# SHP1 and SHP2 dephosphorylate Y628 of IL3RB
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-914036](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-914036)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Synthetic phosphopeptides based on Bc were dephosphorylated by SHP1 and SHP2, peptides phosphorylated at Y628 were the best substrate followed by those phosphorylated at Y766.
