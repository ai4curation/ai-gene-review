# Isomerization of 25(R) DHCA-CoA to 25(S) DHCA-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-193452](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-193452)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The isomerization of 25(R) DHCA-CoA to 25(S) DHCA-CoA, catalyzed by 2-methylacyl-CoA racemase, occurs in the peroxisomal matrix.
