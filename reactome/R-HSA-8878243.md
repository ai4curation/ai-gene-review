# HES1 gene transcription is inhibited by RUNX3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8878243](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8878243)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Binding of RUNX3 to the NOTCH1 coactivator complex at the HES1 gene promoter results in repression of HES1 transcription downstream of NOTCH1 signaling (Gao et al. 2010).
