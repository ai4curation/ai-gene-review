# Transcriptional activation of ACACA by ChREBP:MLX
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-163743](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-163743)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The ACACA (Acetyl-CoA carboxylase 1) gene, activated by binding of MLXIPL:MLX (ChREBP:MLX) to its promoter region, is transcribed and the resulting mRNA is translated to yield ACACA protein (Ma et al. 2005, 2006, 2007).
