# I(1,3,4,5)P4 transports from the cytosol to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855168](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855168)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol 1,3,4,5-tetrakisphosphate (I(1,3,4,5)P4) translocates from the cytosol to the nucleus via the nuclear pore (Dewaste et al. 2003, Nalaskowski et al. 2002).
