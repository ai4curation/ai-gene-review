# Lef1:Ctnnb1, Nicd (Notch), and Tbx6 bind the Hes7 gene in presomitic mesoderm
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9824206](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9824206)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Tbx6 binds two sites approximately 1.3 kb upstream of the transcription start site of Hes7 (González et al. 2013). Lef1:Ctnnb1 and the Notch intracellular domain (Nicd) complexed with Rbpj also bind within the upstream 2.6 kb of the Hes7 promoter and activate transcription (González et al. 2013).
