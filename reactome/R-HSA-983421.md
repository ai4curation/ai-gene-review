# Journey of cargo through Golgi complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-983421](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-983421)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cargo must pass through the various compartments of the Golgi complex to reach the plasma membrane. This may occur through either of the two well known models, cisternal maturation or vesicular transport (Refer to review articles Glick et al. 2009, Jackson 2009, Nakano et al. 2010).
