# (24R, 25R) 3alpha,7alpha,12alpha,24-tetrahydroxy-5beta-cholestanoyl-CoA is oxidized to 3alpha,7alpha,12alpha-trihydroxy-5beta-cholest-24-one-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-193455](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-193455)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

(24R, 25R) 3alpha,7alpha,12alpha,24-tetrahydroxy-5beta-cholestanoyl-CoA and NAD+ react to form 3alpha,7alpha,12alpha-trihydroxy-5beta-cholest-24-one-CoA,  NADH, and H+. This oxidation reaction, catalyzed by the peroxisomal D-bifunctional enzyme (Huyghe et al. 2006), occurs in the peroxisomal matrix (Ferdinandusse et al. 2000).
