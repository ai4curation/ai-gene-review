# histidine => urocanate + NH4+
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70899](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70899)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic histidine ammonia lyase (HAL) catalyzes the reaction of histidine to form urocanate and NH4+ (Kawai et al. 2005).
