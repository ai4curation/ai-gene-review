# conversion of dUMP to dTMP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-73605](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-73605)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic thymidylate synthase catalyzes the reaction of dUMP and N5,N10-methylene tetrahydrofolate to form TMP and dihydrofolate (Davisson et al. 1989). The enzyme is a homodimer (Phan et al. 2001).
