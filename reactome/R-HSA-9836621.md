# p-S225-SPHK1 localizes to cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9836621](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9836621)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated sphingosin kinase 1 (SPHK1) gets exported from nucleus. This process is part of a feedback loop between PKR and SPHK1, activating pro-survival pathways in the cellular response to external stimuli (Qiao et al, 2020).
