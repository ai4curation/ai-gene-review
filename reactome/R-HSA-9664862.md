# ATM dimer:PEX5 phosphorylates PEX5
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9664862](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9664862)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

When serine/threonine kinase Ataxia telangiectasia mutated protein (ATM) is activated it can phosphorylate Peroxisomal targeting signal 1 receptor protein (PEX5) at Ser141 (Zhang J et al. 2015).
