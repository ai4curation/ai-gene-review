# NAPRT1 dimer transfers PRIB to NCA to form NAMN
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-197186](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-197186)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic nicotinate phosphoribosyltransferase (NAPRT1) catalyzes the Mg++-dependent reaction of nicotinate and phosphoribosyl pyrophosphate to form nicotinate mononucleotide (NAMN, nicotinate D-ribonucleotide) and pyrophosphate. The active form of the enzyme is a homodimer (Preiss and Handler 1958; Niedel and Dietrich 1973; Hara et al. 2007).
