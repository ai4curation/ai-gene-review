# HIBCH mutants don't synthesize beta-hydroxyisobutyrate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9916727](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9916727)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The following mutations have been identified in patients presenting with Leigh-like disease, are annotated as pathogenic or likely pathogenic by ClinVar, and show decreased protein levels and/or activity in fibroblasts in vitro:<br><br>
Y122C: Brown et al, 1982; Loutpatty et al, 2007;<br><br>
C163F: D'Gama et al, 2020;<br><br>
G317F: Ferdinandusse et al, 2014;<br><br>
K377*: Reuter et al, 2014; <br><br>
G345S: Peters et al, 2015.<br><br>
