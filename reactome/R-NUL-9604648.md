# Proteasome degrades ubiquitinated mouse NICD4
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9604648](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9604648)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Ubiquitination of mouse NICD4 (Notch4 intracellular domain fragment), expressed in human cell line BOSC23, mediated by human FBXW7, targets NICD4 for proteasome-mediated degradation (Wu et al. 2001).
