# SPAST (spastin) binds the IST1 subunit of ESCRT-III at the sites of microtubule attachment to chromatin
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9668405](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9668405)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Spastin (SPAST), an AAA+ ATPase that severs microtubules attached to the chromosome surfaces, binds the ESCRT-III subunit IST1 (Vietri et al. 2015).
