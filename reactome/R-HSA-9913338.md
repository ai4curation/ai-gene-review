# Sarcoglycan complex (SGC) translocates to the Golgi membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9913338](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9913338)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After binding together in the ER, the tetrameric sarcoglycan complex translocates to the Golgi, where it interacts with the forming dystroglycan complex (reviewed in Tarakci and Bergman, 2016).
