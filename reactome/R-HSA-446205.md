# Synthesis of GDP-mannose
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-446205](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-446205)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

GDP-mannose is the mannose donor for the first 5 mannose addition reactions in the N-glycan precursor synthesis, and also for the synthesis of Dolichyl-phosphate-mannose involved in other mannose transfer reactions. It is synthesized from fructose 6-phosphate and GTP in three steps.
