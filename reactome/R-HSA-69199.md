# Dephosphorylation of Cyclin E:Cdk2 complexes by Cdc25A
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-69199](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-69199)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cdc25A dephosphorylates Cdk2 and activates cyclin E-Cdk2 and cyclin A-Cdk2 kinases (Blomberg  and Hoffmann, 1999).
