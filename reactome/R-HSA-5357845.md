# K63polyUb-RIPK1 is deubiquitinated
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5357845](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5357845)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

K63-deubiquitination of RIP1 abolishes its ability to activate NF-kappa-B upon TNF-alpha stimulation and leads to the formation of the cytosolic caspase-8 containing complex II  and subsequent apoptosis.
