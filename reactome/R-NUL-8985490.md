# Runx2:RB1 binds the BGLAP gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-8985490](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-8985490)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The complex of recombinant mouse Runx2 and recombinant human RB1 binds the human BGLAP promoter (Thomas et al. 2001). The complex of endogenous mouse Runx2 and endogenous mouse Rb1 was also demonstrated to bind the mouse Bglap gene promoter (Thomas et al. 2001, Calo et al. 2010).
