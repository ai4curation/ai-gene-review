# AMPK-negatively regulates binding of MAPT to microtubules
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9619539](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9619539)
**Species:** Bos taurus

## Summary

Phosphorylation of recombinant human MAPT (Tau) by recombinant rat AMPK complex negatively regulates binding of recombinant human MAPT to bovine microtubules (Thornton et al. 2011).
