# proHPSE binds SDC1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9792569](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9792569)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

proHPSE, heparanase secreted from the cell in an inactive form, binds heparan sulfate-conjugated syndecan 1 (HS(6)-SDC1) associated with the plasma membrane (Levy-Adam et al. 2010; Shteingauz et al. 2014).
