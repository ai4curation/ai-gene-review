# ODC dimerizes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9955574](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9955574)

## Summary

Cytosolic pyridoxal phosphate-conjugated ornithine decarboxylase 1 (PXLP-K69-ODC1) monomers dimerize to form catalytically active ODC (Almrud et al. 2000).
