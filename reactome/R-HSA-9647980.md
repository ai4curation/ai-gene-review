# mature RAS proteins translocate to plasma membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9647980](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9647980)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After farnesylation, C-terminal proteolysis, carboxymethylation and palmitoylation, RAS proteins translocate to the plasma membrane (reviewed in Gysin et al, 2011).
