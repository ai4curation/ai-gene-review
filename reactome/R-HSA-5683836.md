# SFTPC gene produces pro-SFTPC protein
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5683836](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5683836)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The human gene SFTPC produces surfactant protein C (SFTPC) (Whitsett & Glasser 1998, Kelly et al. 1996). The transcription complex transcription termination factor 1 and thyroid transcription factor 1-associated protein 26 (TTF1:CCDC59), enhances TTF1-transactivated SFTPC promoter activity.
