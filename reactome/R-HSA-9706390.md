# RHOBTB3 binds interacting proteins at trans-Golgi network
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9706390](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9706390)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RHOBTB3, like other RHOBTB family members, RHOBTB1 and RHOBTB2, binds CUL3 (cullin 3) a core component of BTB:CUL3:RBX1 E3 ubiquitin ligase complexes (Berthold et al. 2008). The following proteins are substrates of RHOBTB3-dependent ubiquitin ligase complexes:<br>RAB9A, RAB9B (Espinosa et al. 2009)<br>Cyclin E1 (Lu and Pfeffer 2013)<br>LRRC41 (MUF1) (Schenková et al. 2012).<br><br>The following proteins bind directly to RHOBTB3 but there is no evidence that they are substrates of RHOBTB3-dependent ubiquitin ligase complexes:<br>HGS (HRS) (Pridgeon et al. 2009)<br>PLIN3 (TIP47) (Espinosa et al. 2009)<br>5-HT7a serotonin receptor (Matthys et al. 2012)<br>VHL (Zhang et al. 2015).
