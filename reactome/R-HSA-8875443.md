**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8875443](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8875443)

## Summary

The ubiquitin hydrolase USP8 can deubiquitinate LRIG1, thus interfering with LRIG1-mediated MET downregulation (Oh et al. 2014).
