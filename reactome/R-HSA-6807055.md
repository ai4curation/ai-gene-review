# DHCR7 reduces 7-dehydroCHOL to CHOL
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6807055](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6807055)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

DHCR7 (7-dehydrocholesterol reductase) associated with the endoplasmic reticulum membrane reduces 7-dehydroCHOL (7-dehydrocholesterol) to CHOL (cholesterol) (Kandutsch & Russell1960; Mitsche et al. 2015; Moebius et al. 1998).
