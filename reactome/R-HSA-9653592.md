# pS181-S-Farn-Me KRAS4B translocates to the outer mitochondrial membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9653592](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9653592)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PKC- or -PRKG2-dependent phosphorylation of KRAS4B at serine 181 promotes its trafficking to intracellular membranes, including the mitochondrial outer membrane where it interacts with BCL2L1 (also known as BCL-XL) to promote apoptosis (Bivona et al, 2006; Barcelo et al, 2013; Jang et al, 2015; reviewed in Ahearn et al, 2018).
