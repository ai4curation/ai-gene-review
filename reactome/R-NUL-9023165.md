# Pcsk1 (rat) cleaves human proinsulin to yield Insulin(25-56) and Insulin(57-110)
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9023165](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9023165)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Rat Pcsk1 cleaves C-terminal to arginine-56 in human proinsulin to yield the B chain (plus two C-terminal arginine residues) and a peptide containing the C-peptide and the A chain (Davidson et al. 1988, Rhodes et al. 1992).
