# TP53 stimulates RABGGTA expression
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6801089](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6801089)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Binding of TP53 (p53) to the p53 response element in the promoter of the RABGGTA gene promotes RABGGTA transcription (Jen and Cheung 2005).
