# ATM:PEX5 translocates from cytosol to peroxisomal membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9664883](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9664883)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After the binding of Peroxisomal targeting signal 1 receptor (PEX5) and Ataxia telangiectasia mutated protein (ATM), PEX5 recruits the complex to the peroxisomal membrane for the next steps of the degradation process (Zhang J et al. 2015).
