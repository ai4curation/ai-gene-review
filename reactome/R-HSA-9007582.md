# RAD51 gene expression is stimulated by E2F1 and inhibited by E2F6
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9007582](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9007582)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Transcription of the RAD51 gene is directly stimulated by E2F1 (Wu et al. 2014) and inhibited by E2F6 (Bertoli et al. 2013).
