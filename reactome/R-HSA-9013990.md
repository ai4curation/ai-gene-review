# TANK is ubiquitinated within TANK:K63polyUb-TRAF3:TICAM1:TLR3:viral dsRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9013990](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9013990)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Upon stimulation by pathogen-associated inflammatory signals TANK associates with TRAF3 which may result in K63-linked ubiquitination of TANK (Gatot JC et al. 2007). How the ubiquitination of TANK contributes to the activation of TBK1 and/or IKKepsilon remains unclear.
