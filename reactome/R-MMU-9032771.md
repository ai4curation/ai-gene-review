# Dock3 binds Fyn associated with Ntrk2
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9032771](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9032771)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Activation of mouse Ntrk2 (TrkB) with recombinant mouse Bdnf results in recruitment of recombinant mouse Dock3 to activated recombinant mouse Fyn (Namekata et al. 2010).
