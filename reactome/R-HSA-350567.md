# Antizyme OAZ binds to Ornithine decarboxylase
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-350567](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-350567)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Members of the OAZ (Ornithine decarboxylase antizyme) protein family bind ODC (ornithine decarboxylase) monomers (Kanerva et al. 2008), preventing the latter from dimerizing to form catalytically active ODC (Pegg 2006; Zhang et al. 2003). AZIN1 (Antizyme inhibitor 1) negatively regulates OAZ - ODC interaction by itself binding OAZ (Wu et al. 2015).
