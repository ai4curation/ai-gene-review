# Formation of Elongin BC complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-112435](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-112435)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'Elongin B protein', and 1 molecule of 'Elongin C protein' are present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'Elongin B:C complex' is present. This reaction takes place in the 'nucleus' (Aso et al., 1995; Duan et al., 1995b; Kibel et al., 1995).
