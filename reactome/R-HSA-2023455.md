# Autocatalytic phosphorylation of FGFR1c P252X mutant dimers
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2023455](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2023455)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

FGFR1 gain-of-function mutations at P252 that result in increased binding affinity to ligand are presumed to be phosphorylated on the same sites as the wild-type receptor, although this has not been demonstrated (Ibrahimi, 2004a).<br><br>
