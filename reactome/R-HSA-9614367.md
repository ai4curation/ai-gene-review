# HCMV Nuclear Pore Docking
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9614367](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9614367)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Virion capsids eventually dissociate from microtubules, dock at nuclear pores, and release their DNA into the nucleus. The role of tegument proteins in this process is implicated but has not been described in detail.
