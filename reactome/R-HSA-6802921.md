# Activated MAP2Ks phosphorylate MAPKs downstream of moderate kinase activity BRAF mutants
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802921](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802921)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MAPKs are phosphorylated downstream of moderately activated BRAF mutants in a MAP2K-dependent manner (Wan et al, 2004; Roring et al, 2012; Freeman et al, 2013; reviewed in Lavoie and Therrien, 2015).
