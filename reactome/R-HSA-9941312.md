# CHST15 transfers sulfate to (6-)GalNAc-4-sulfate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9941312](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9941312)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Carbohydrate sulfotransferase 15 (CHST15) catalyses the transfer of one or more sulfate ions from PAPS to the C-6 hydroxyl group of the GalNAc 4-sulfate residue of dermatan 4-sulfate (C4S) (Ohtake et al. 2003).
