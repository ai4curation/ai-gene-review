# Complement factor I inactivates C4BP-bound C4b
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-981637](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-981637)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

C4b-binding protein is a cofactor in Factor I mediated C4b proteolysis. C4b is cleaved, releasing C4c, leaving C4d bound to the cell surface.
