# IDS hydrolyses Heparan sulfate chain(5)
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1678650](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1678650)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Iduronate-2-sulfatase (IDS) hydrolyses 2-sulfate groups from L-iduronate 2-sulfate units of heparan sulfate. Defects in IDS are the cause of mucopolysaccharidosis type II (MPSII, MIM:309900), also called Hunter syndrome (Wilson et al. 1990).
