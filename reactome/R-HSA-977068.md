# Termination of O-glycan biosynthesis
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-977068](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-977068)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

O-glycan biosynthesis can be terminated (or modified) by the addition of sialic acid residues on Core 1 and 2 glycoproteins by sialyltransferases (Varki et al. 2009).
