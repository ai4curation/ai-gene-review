# FOXO1,FOXO3 binds Rbl2 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9620806](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9620806)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recombinant human FOXO1 and FOXO3 directly bind FOXO elements in the first intron of the mouse Rbl2 (p130 gene) (Chen et al. 2006).
