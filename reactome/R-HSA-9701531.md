# p-Y705,S727 STAT3 is acetylated
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9701531](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9701531)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylated STAT3 is acetylated by p300 at lysine 685, stabilizing dimerization and promoter binding (Yuan et al, 2005; reviewed in Yu et al, 2014; Guanizo et al, 2018).
