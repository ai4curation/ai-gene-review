# pS181-S-Farn-Me KRAS4B binds BCL2L1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9653595](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9653595)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the mitochondrial outer membrane, phosphorylated KRAS4B binds BCL2L1 (also known as BCL-XL) to promote apoptosis (Bivona et al, 2006; reviewed in Ahearn et al, 2018).
