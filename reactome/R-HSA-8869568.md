# NPM1 binds TFAP2A homodimer
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8869568](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8869568)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In response to retinoic acid treatment, NPM1 (nucleophosmin) forms a complex with the TFAP2A (AP-2 alpha) homodimer (Liu et al. 2007).
