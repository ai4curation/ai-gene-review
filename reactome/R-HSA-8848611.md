# Phosphorylated PXN recruits the CRK complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8848611](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8848611)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PXN (paxillin), phosphorylated by PTK6 (BRK) on tyrosine residues Y31 and Y118, recruits CRK (CrkII) complex which acts as a guanine exchange factor (GEF) for RAC1 (Chen et al. 2004).
