# Pex5l binds Acox1 or Uox
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9033925](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9033925)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Pex5l binds monomeric Acox1 and monomeric Uox in preference to dimeric Acox1 and tetrameric Uox (Freitas et al. 2015). UOX is not expressed in humans.
