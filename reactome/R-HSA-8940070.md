# NT5E:Zn2+ hydrolyses NAD+
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8940070](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8940070)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

5'-nucleotidase (NT5E, CD73) hydrolyzes NAD+ to yield beta-nicotinamide D-ribonucleotide (NMN) and AMP, annotated here. NT5E also catalyzes the hydrolysis of a variety of nucleotide monophosphates (Garavaglia et al. 2012). NT5E is a glycolipid-anchored plasma membrane enzyme (Misumi et al. 1990) that is active in dimeric form and requires one zinc ion per subunit (Zimmermann 1992).
