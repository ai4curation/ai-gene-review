# ABL1 binds PPARG2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9842028](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9842028)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated ABL1 binds to PPARG isoform PPARG2 through an evolutionarily conserved PxxP motif located between amino acids 9-12 in the AF1 domain portion present only in the PPARG2 isoform (Keshet et al. 2014).
