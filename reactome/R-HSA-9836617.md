# p-PKR dimer phosphorylates SPHK1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9836617](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9836617)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated PKR phosphorylates sphingosin kinase 1 (SPHK1) at S225 in the nucleus, priming it for nuclear export. This process is part of a feedback loop between PKR and SPHK1, activating pro-survival pathways in the cellular response to external stimuli (Qiao et al, 2020).
