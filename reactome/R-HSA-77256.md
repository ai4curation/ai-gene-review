**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77256](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77256)

ECHS1 in the mitochondrial matrix hydrates trans-dodec-2-enoyl-CoA to form (S)-3-hydroxylauroyl-CoA (Stern & Del Campillo 1956).
