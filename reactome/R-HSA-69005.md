# Cdc6 protein is phosphorylated by CDK
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-69005](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-69005)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'CDC6', and 1 molecule of 'ATP' are present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ADP', and 1 molecule of 'phosphorylated Cdc6' are present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'nucleus' and  is mediated by the 'kinase activity' of 'CDK'.<br>
