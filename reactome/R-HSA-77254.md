# (S)-3-Hydroxydodecanoyl-CoA+NAD => 3-Oxododecanoyl-CoA+NADH+H
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77254](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77254)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

HADH in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates (S)-3-hydroxylauroyl-CoA to form 3-oxolauroyl-CoA (Vredendaal et al. 1996).
