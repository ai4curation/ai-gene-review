# Formation of the CE:GMP intermediate complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77081](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77081)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

A highly conserved lysine within the guanylyltransferase (GT) site of the mRNA capping enzyme attacks the alpha-phosphate of GTP. An enzyme-GMP covalent intermediate is formed.<BR>
