# NEDD8-K699-CUL2:ELOB:ELOC:RBX1:KLHDC10 K48 polyubiquitinates alanine-tailed nascent peptide
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9954721](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9954721)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The CRL-KLHDC10 E3 ubiquitin ligase complex (NEDD8-K699-CUL2:ELOB:ELOC:RBX1:KLHDC10) polymerizes ubiquitin monomers with K48 linkages onto exposed lysine residues of an alanine-tailed nascent peptide that has been extracted from a 60S ribosomal subunit (Thrun et al. 2021). The K48-polyubiquitin targets the nascent peptide for degradation by the 26S proteasome (Thrun et al. 2021).
