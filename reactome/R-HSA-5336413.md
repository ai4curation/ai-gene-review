# DT:HBEGF:CD9 is transported from clathrin-coated vesicle to endosome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5336413](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5336413)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The target cell clathrin-coated vesicle containing diphtheria toxin (DT A:B) in a complex with target cell proteins HBEGF and CD9 is transformed into an endocytic vesicle (Murphy 2011).
