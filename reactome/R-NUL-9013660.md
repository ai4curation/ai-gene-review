# Notch3 coactivator complex binds Hes1 gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9013660](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9013660)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mouse Notch3 intracellular domain (NICD3), in complex with human RBPJ and MAML proteins (MAML1, MAML2 or MAML3; possibly MAMLD1) binds to the promoter of the mouse Hes1 gene (Lin et al. 2002).
