# RXYLT1 transfers initial Xyl to matriglycan chain
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9940805](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9940805)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The ribitol-5-phosphate xylosyltransferase 1 (RXYLT1) subunit of the FKRP:FKTN:RXYLT1 complex transfers xylose (Xyl) to the terminal RboP moiety of growing matriglycan chains present as O-mannosyl modifications in alpha-dystroglycan (DAG1) (Manya et al., 2016; Nishihara et al., 2018). Mutations in RXYLT1 can cause muscular dystrophy-dystroglycanopathies (MDDGA10, MIM:615041, Vuillaumier-Barrot et al., 2012).
