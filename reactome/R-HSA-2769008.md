# NOTCH1 PEST domain mutants bind DLL1 
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2769008](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2769008)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NOTCH1 PEST domain mutants are expected to bind to DLL1 ligand in an identical fashion to wild-type NOTCH1 (Jarriault et al. 1998, Yang et al. 2005, Cordle et al. 2008).
