# NFE2L2 dependent ABCF2 expression
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9796042](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9796042)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

ABCF2 is an NFE2L2 target gene that contains a functional ARE sequence in the promoter which is confirmed through ChIP assay in Human Ovarian cancer cell lines. It has role in Cisplatin resistance (Bao et al, 2017)
