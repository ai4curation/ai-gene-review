# Gamma-secretase cleaves Notch4
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9604300](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9604300)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Enzymatic activity of the human gamma-secretase complex is need for the release of the cytoplasmic fragment (NICD4) from the membrane-bound recombinant mouse Notch4-derived int-3 oncogene expressed in human cells (Das et al. 2004).
