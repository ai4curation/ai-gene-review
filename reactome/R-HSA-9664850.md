# ATM binds PEX5
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9664850](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9664850)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The first step in pexophagy is the initiation by a signal that triggers downstream events to eventually degrade the peroxisome. Ataxia-telangiectasia mutated protein (ATM) is a serine/threonine protein kinase that is involve in DNA damage response. ATM is known to be involved in pexophagy (Katarzyna ZR et al. 2016). ATM binds with Peroxisomal targeting signal 1 receptor (PEX5) with the help of a SRL binding sequence in ATM (Zhang J et al. 2015).
