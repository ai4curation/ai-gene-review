# RAD51B binds RAD51C
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-983285](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-983285)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The Rad51-like proteins Rad51B and Rad51C form a highly stable complex. This complex assists Rad51 in the early stages of homologous recombination.
