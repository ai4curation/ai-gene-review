# Addition of sialic acids on some Spike glycosyl sidechains
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9697018](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9697018)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Glycosyl sidechains at Asn-1158 and Asn-1194 are sialylated, presumably by the cell's sialyltransferases (Watanabe et al, 2020).
