# PLK1 phosphorylates OPTN
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2562526](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2562526)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activated PLK1 phosphorylates OPTN (optineurin) on serine residue S177. Phosphorylation at S177 disrupts OPTN binding to Golgi-membrane localized RAB8A (Kachaner et al. 2012).
