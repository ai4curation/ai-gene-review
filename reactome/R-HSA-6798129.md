**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6798129](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6798129)

## Summary

TP53 (p53) binds the p53 response element located in the third intron of the caspase-6 (CASP6) gene (MacLachlan and El-Deiry 2002).
