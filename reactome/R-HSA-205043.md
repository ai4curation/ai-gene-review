# NRIF signals cell death from the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-205043](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-205043)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NRIF (nuclear receptor-interacting factor) is a DNA binding protein that is essential for p75-mediated apoptosis in retina and sympathetic neurons. Neurotrophin or proneurotrophin binding to p75TR induces nuclear translocation of NRIF, which involves gamma-secretase cleavage of p75NTR ICD (Intra Cellular Domain). Once in the nucleus, NRIF mediates apoptosis, probably by acting as transcription factor.
