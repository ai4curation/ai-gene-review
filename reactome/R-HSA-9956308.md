# STARD7 transports PC from ER membrane to mitochondrial outer membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9956308](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9956308)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

STARD7 (StAR-related lipid transfer protein 7, mitochondrial), associated with the mitochondrial outer membrane, mediates the transport of PC (phosphatidyl choline) from the endoplasmic reticulum membrane to the mitochondrial outer membrane (Horibata et al. 2017).
