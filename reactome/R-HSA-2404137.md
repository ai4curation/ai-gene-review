# LRAT esterifies RBP1:atROL and FACYLs to atREs
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2404137](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2404137)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

All-trans-retinyl esters (atREs) are the main storage form of retinol in hepatic stellate cells. Lecithin retinol acyltransferase (LRAT) mediates the esterification of all-trans-retinol (atROL) with a variety of fatty acyl groups extracted from phosphocholines (PC) to form REs, which are stored in lipid droplets in the cytosol, and 2-acyl-phosphocholines. Cellular retinol-binding protein 1 (RBP1) is an effective donor of atROL for LRAT (Ruiz et al. 1999).
