# KEAP1:NEDD8-CUL3:RBX1 complex ubiquitinates NFE2L2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9755505](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9755505)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The KEAP1:CUL3:RBX1 E3-ubiquitin ligase complex is a negative regulator of Nuclear factor erythroid 2-related (NFE2L2). In the absence of oxidative alterations this E3-ubiquitin ligase complex is bound to NFE2L2 and targets it for ubiquitination (Cullinan et al, 2004; Kobayashi et al, 2004; reviewed in Baird and Yamamoto, 2020).
