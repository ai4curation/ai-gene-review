# Notch1 binds Jag1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-2053963](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-2053963)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Cultured mouse endothelial cells expressing Notch1 are activated by immobilized mouse Jag1, as demonstrated by expression of Notch target genes Hes1 and Hey1. The activation of Notch1 by Jag1 is confirmed by a co-culture assay in which Jag1-expressing cells are mixed with Notch1-expressing cells transfected with the Notch reporter construct.
