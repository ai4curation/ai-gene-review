# FDPS dimer transfers IPPP to DMAPP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191322](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191322)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The prenyltransferase enzyme family is involved in the biosynthesis of isoprenoids. Two members of this family are known to catalyse the sequential condensation of isopentenyl pyrophosphate (IPPP) to dimethylallyl pyrophosphate (DMAPP): farnesyl pyrophosphate synthase dimer (FPPS dimer) and geranylgeranyl pyrophosphate synthetase hexamer (GGPPS hexamer) (Kavanaugh et al, 2006). Both enzymes utlise Mg2+ as cofactor (Chang et al. 2021).
