# FANCD2 deubiquitination by USP1:WDR48
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6786171](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6786171)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The FA pathway is negatively regulated through the USP1:WDR48-mediated deubiquitination of FANCD2 (Nijman et al. 2005). WDR48 (UAF1) forms a complex with and activates USP1 (Cohn et al. 2007).
