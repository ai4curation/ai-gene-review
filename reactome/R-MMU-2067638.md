# Gamma secretase cleaves Dner:Notch1 complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-2067638](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-2067638)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

In the mouse myogenic cell line C2C12, transfected with a full-length mouse Notch1 expressing vector, NICD1 produced by S3 cleavage is detectable after cells are treated with a soluble recombinant mouse Dner. Also, the effect of soluble Dner on morphology of Notch1 expressing primary glial cells isolated from mouse cerebellum is dependent on the activity of gamma-secretase.
