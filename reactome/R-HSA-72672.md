# The 60S subunit joins the translation initiation complex 
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-72672](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-72672)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Joining of the 60S subunit to form the 80S ribosome is catalyzed by the presence of GTP-bound eIF5B.
