**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1912368](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1912368)

## Summary

MicroRNA miR-302A, upregulated in melanoma, binds the 3'UTR of NOTCH4, resulting in inhibition of NOTCH4 mRNA translation.
