# TANK is ubiquitinated within TANK:K63polyUb-TRAF3:TRIF:activated TLR4
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5362500](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5362500)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Upon stimulation by pathogen-associated inflammatory signals TANK associates with TRAF3 which results in K63-linked ubiquitination of TANK (Gatot JC et al. 2007). How the ubiquitination of TANK contributes to the activation of TBK1 and/or IKKepsilon remains unclear.
