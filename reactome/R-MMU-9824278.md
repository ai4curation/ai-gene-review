# Hes7 binds the Hes7 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9824278](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9824278)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Hairy and enhancer of split 7 (Hes7) protein binds an N-box in the promoter of its own gene (Bessho et al. 2003, Chen et al. 2005) and represses activation of Hes7 expression by Notch signaling (Bessho et al. 2003). This autorepression provides a mechanism for cyclic expression of Hes7 during somite formation.
