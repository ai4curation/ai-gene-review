# Formation of neural BAF (nBaf)
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9934111](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9934111)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

As neuronal progenitors differentiate and exit the mitotic cell cycle, the components of the npBAF complex alter. npBAF-specific subunits ACTL6A, SS18 and DPF2/PHF10 are replaced by neuron-specific subunits ACTL6B, SS18L1 and DPF1 or DPF3 (Lessard et al, 2007; Staahl et al, 2013; reviewed in Stahl and Crabtree, 2013; Kadoch and Crabtree, 2015; Innis and Cabot, 2020; Cenik and Shilatifard, 2021).
