# RSF complex binds the centromere
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-606287](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-606287)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The RSF complex binds the centromere in mid-G1 phase after deposition of new CENPA-containing nucleosomes has occurred. The RSF complex is required for stable incorporation of the previously deposited CENPA-containing nucleosomes.
