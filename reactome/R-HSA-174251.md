# Phosphorylation of Cdh1 by Cyclin B1:Cdc2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-174251](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-174251)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the onset of mitosis, Cdh1 is phosphorylated by Cyclin B-Cdc2 resulting  in a conformational change that prevents Cdh1 from activating the APC/C.
