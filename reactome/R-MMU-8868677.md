# Cdk5:p25 phosphorylates Jun
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-8868677](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-8868677)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

In mouse neurons, Cdk5 in complex with p25 phosphorylates Jun directly at serine residues S63 and S73, which are also target sites for JNKs (Sun et al. 2009).
