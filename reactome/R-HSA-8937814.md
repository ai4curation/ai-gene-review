# RUNX3 translocates to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8937814](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8937814)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Translocation of RUNX3 from the cytosol to the nucleus is stimulated by TGF-beta (TGFB1) treatment (Ito et al. 2005) and inhibited by SRC-mediated phosphorylation of RUNX3 on multiple tyrosine residues (Goh et al. 2010).
