# TPI1 dimer isomerizes GA3P to DHAP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-70481](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-70481)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The reversible conversion of GA3P (glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate) to DHAP (dihydroxyacetone phosphate) is catalyzed by cytosolic TPI1 (triose phosphate isomerase) (Watanabe et al. 1996; Lu et al. 1984).
