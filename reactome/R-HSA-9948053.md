# NS3 binds FASN dimer
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9948053](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9948053)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

DENV NS3 binds Fatty acid synthase (FASN) and recruits it to the DENV replication complex, allowing fatty acid biosynthesis to support ER membrane remodeling. In vitro, NS3 also enhances catalytic activity of FASN (Heaton et al., 2010). In mosquito cells cellular membranes showed a FASN‐dependent enrichment in lipids and lipid signaling (Perera et al., 2012; reviewed in Fernández-Oliva et al., 2019).
