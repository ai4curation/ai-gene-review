# SLC35A1,4 import CDP-ribitol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9940804](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9940804)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Both CMP-sialic acid transporter SLC35A1 and UDP-sugar transporter protein SLC35A4 transport CDP-ribitol from cytosol into the Golgi, in exchange for one molecule of CDP (Ury et al., 2021).
