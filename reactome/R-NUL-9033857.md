# Pex14 binds PEX5L (in PEX5L:PEX7:Acaa1a)
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9033857](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9033857)
**Species:** Cricetulus griseus

## Summary

Hamster PEX5L bound to human PEX7:rat Acaa1a interacts with rat Pex14 thus bringing PEX7 and its cargo to dock at the peroxisomal membrane (Otera et al. 2000).
