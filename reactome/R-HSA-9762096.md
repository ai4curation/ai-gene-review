# Ub,pS335,S338,T NFE2L2 is degraded
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9762096](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9762096)

## Summary

After BTRC:CUL1-mediated ubiquitination, NFE2L2 is degraded by the proteasome (Rada et al, 2011; Rada et al, 2012; reviewed in Baird and Yamamoto, 2020; Yamamoto et al, 2018).
