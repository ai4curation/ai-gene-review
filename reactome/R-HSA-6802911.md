# High kinase activity BRAF complexes phosphorylate MAP2Ks
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802911](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802911)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Highly active BRAF mutants such as V600E are constitutively active and phosphorylate MAP2Ks in a RAS- and dimer interface-independent manner (Davies et al, 2002; Wan et al, 2004; Garnett et al, 2005; Roring et al, 2012; Poulikakos et al, 2010; reviewed in Lavoie and Therrien, 2015).
