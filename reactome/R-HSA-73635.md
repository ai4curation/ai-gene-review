# dUMP or TMP + ATP <=> dUDP or TDP + ADP [DTYMK]
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-73635](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-73635)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic deoxythymidylate kinase (DTYMK) catalyzes the reversible reaction of either dUMP or TMP with ATP to form dUDP or TDP. The active form of the enzyme is a homodimer (Lee and Cheng 1977).
