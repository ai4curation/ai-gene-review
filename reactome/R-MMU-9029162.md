# Jak2 phosphorylates Vav1 in Epo:p-8Y-Epor:p-12Y-Jak2:Lyn:Irs2:p-Crkl:Rapgef1:p-Y-Shc1:Grb2-1:Vav1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9029162](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9029162)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Phosphorylated Jak2 phosphorylates Vav1 bound to Grb2 in a large complex assembled on the phosphorylated Epor (Miura et al. 1994). Phosphorylation of Vav1 activates its guanine nucleotide exchange activity (Crespo et al. 1997).
