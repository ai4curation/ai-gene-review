# RANBP2 SUMOylates HDAC4 with SUMO1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4615872](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4615872)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RANBP2 SUMOylates HDAC4 at lysine-559 with SUMO1 (Kirsh et al. 2002, Knipscheer et al. 2008). SUMOylation increases transcription repression by HDAC4.
