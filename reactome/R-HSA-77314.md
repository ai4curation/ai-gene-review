# Crotonoyl-CoA+H2O => (S)-3-Hydroxybutanoyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77314](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77314)

## Summary

ECHS1 in the mitochondrial matrix hydrates (2E)-butenoyl-CoA (crotonoyl-CoA) to form (S)-3-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA (Stern & Del Campillo 1956).
