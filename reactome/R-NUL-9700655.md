# Active ALK phosphorylates Src
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9700655](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9700655)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Endogenous Src is phosphorylated when co-expressed in Ba/F3 cells with the human NPM-ALK fusion in a manner that depends on ALK kinase activity (Cussac et al, 2004).
