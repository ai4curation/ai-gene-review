# Cdc25A mediated dephosphorylation of Cyclin A:phospho-Cdk2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-174110](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-174110)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cdc25A, and probably Cdc25B, regulate the entry into S phase cell cycle by
removing inhibitory phosphates from the Cdk2 subunit of Cyclin A:Cdk2.
