# PEX2:PEX10:PEX12 binds PEX5L (in PEX5L:PEX7:PEX13:PEX14:PEX2:PEX10:PEX12) and Ub:UBE2D1,2,3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9033527](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9033527)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

A RING E3 ubiquitin ligase sub-complex containing PEX10, PEX12, and PEX2 ubiquitinates PEX5L. PEX10:PEX12:PEX2 is believed to bind an activated E2-ubiquitin conjugate (one of Ub:UBE2D1, Ub:UBE2D2, Ub:UBE2D3) and PEX5L in a complex that also contains PEX13 and PEX14 (Chang et al. 1999, Carvalho et al. 2007, Grou et al. 2008, Grou et al. 2009, Okumoto et al. 2011).
