# Pregn-5-ene-3,20-dione isomerizes to progesterone
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-193052](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-193052)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Pregn-5-ene-3,20-dione isomerizes to progesterone. This reaction is catalyzed by the isomerase activity of 3 beta-HSD, associated with the endoplasmic reticulum membrane. The active form of the enzyme is a homodimer. The enzyme occurs in two isoforms that are similar in their biochemical properties but differ in their tissue expression: type I (HSD3B1) is found in placenta and skin, while type II (HSD3B2) is found in the adrenal glands and gonads.<br>The hormone lutropin (LH) triggers ovulation and development of the corpus luteum, that in turn increases production of progesterone.
