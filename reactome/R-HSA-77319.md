# Butanoyl-CoA+FAD => Crotonoyl-CoA+FADH2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77319](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77319)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

ACADS in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates butanoyl-CoA to form (2E)-butenoyl-CoA (crotonoyl-CoA). (Krane & Beinert 1956; Finocchiaro et al. 1987).
