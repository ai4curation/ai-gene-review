# Src phosphorylates Robo3.1 in response to Ntn1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9011252](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9011252)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

In mouse neurons, Ntn1 (netrin-1)-mediated activation of Dcc, induces Src-mediated phosphorylation of Robo3.1 on a conserved tyrosine Y1019 (Zelina et al. 2014).
