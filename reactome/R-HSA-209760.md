# Endocytic translocation of CUBN:GC:25(OH)D to lysosomal lumen
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-209760](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-209760)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The internalized CUBN:GC:25(OH)D complex enters the lysosome where it can be acted upon the protease legumain (Halfon et al. 1998, Chen et al. 2000).
