# p25-bound CDK5 phosphorylates CDC25C
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8863011](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8863011)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

CDK5 activated by binding to p25 phosphorylates CDC25C protein tyrosine phosphatase at serine and threonine residues T48, T67, S122, T130, S168, and S214. CDK5-mediated phosphorylation of CDC25C interferes with 14-3-3-epsilon (YWHAE) mediated retention of CDC25C in the cytosol. Once activated by CDK5, CDC25C promotes activation of CDK1 (Chang et al. 2012).
