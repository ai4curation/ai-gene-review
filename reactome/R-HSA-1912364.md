# MIR181C microRNA binds 3'UTR of NOTCH4 mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1912364](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1912364)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

miR-181C microRNA inhibits translation of NOTCH4 mRNA by binding to its 3'UTR. miR181c is a candidate tumor suppressor in gastric cancer.
