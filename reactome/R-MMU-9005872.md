# Active Prkaca, CAMK IV does not phosphorylate Mecp2_e2 mutant R306C at T308
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9005872](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9005872)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Recombinant mouse mutant Mecp2 protein Mecp2_e2 R306C cannot be phosphorylated by recombinant mouse PKA C alpha (Prkaca) or recombinant active mouse CaMK IV (Ebert et al. 2013).
