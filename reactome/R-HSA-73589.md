# conversion of 5,6-dihydrouracil to 3-ureidopropionate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-73589](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-73589)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic dihydropyrimidinase tetramer catalyzes the reaction of 5,6-dihydrouracil and water to form 3-ureidopropionate (Hamajima et al. 1998).
