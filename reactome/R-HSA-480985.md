# Synthesis of dolichyl-phosphate-glucose
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-480985](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-480985)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Dolichyl-phosphate-glucose functions as a donor of glucose groups in reactions including three steps of N-glycan precursor biosynthesis. Dolichyl-phosphate-glucose itself is synthesized from UDP-glucose and dolichol phosphate on the cytosolic face of the endoplasmic reticulum membrane, then flipped to the luminal surface of that membrane.
