# PtpA binds ATP6V1H
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9636397](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9636397)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The H subunit of human cytosolic ATPase (ATP6V1H) binds to the Mtb probable low molecular weight protein-tyrosine phosphatase (ptpA), directly impairing ATPase trafficking to the phagosome (Wong et al. 2011).
