# NT5C2 tetramer dephosphorylates RBV-MP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9755078](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9755078)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Nucleate cell types can hydrolyze ribavirin monophosphate (RBV-MP) to ribavirin (RBV). The enzymes possibly responsible for the reaction are cytosolic purine 5'-nucleotidase (NT5C2) and alkaline phosphatases (Page and Connor, 1990).
