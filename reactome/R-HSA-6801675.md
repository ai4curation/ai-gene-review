# PLK2 phosphorylates NPM1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6801675](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6801675)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

PLK2 phosphorylates NPM1 (nucleophosmin) on serine residue S4, which may be necessary to trigger centrosome duplication (Krause and Hoffmann 2010).
