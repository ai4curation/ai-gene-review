# MIR34 microRNAs bind 3'UTR of NOTCH2 mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1912367](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1912367)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Translation of NOTCH2 mRNA is inhibited by MIR34 microRNAs (MIR34A, MIR34B and MIR34C), which bind to the 3'UTR of NOTCH2 mRNA.
