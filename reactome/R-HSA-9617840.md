# FOXO1,FOXO3,FOXO4 bind CDKN1A gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9617840](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9617840)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

FOXO1, FOXO3 and FOXO4 can all bind to forkhead box elements in the promoter region of the CDKN1A gene, encoding CDK inhibitor p21Cip1 (Seoane et al. 2004, Tinkum et al. 2013).
