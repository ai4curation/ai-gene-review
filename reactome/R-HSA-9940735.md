# FKTN transfers RboP to GalNAc-GlcNAc-ManP-DAG1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9940735](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9940735)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The ribitol-5-phosphate transferase FKTN subunit of the FKRP:FKTN:RXYLT1 complex transfers ribitol-5-phosphate (RboP) from CDP-ribitol to the terminal N-acetylgalactosamine (GalNAc) moiety of the core M3 trisaccharide linker glycan present as O-mannosyl modifications in alpha-dystroglycan (DAG1) (Kanagawa et al., 2016; Nishihara et al., 2018). Mutations in FKTN can cause muscular dystrophy-dystroglycanopathies of varying severity (MDDGA4, MIM:253800, Kondo-Iida et al., 1999; MDDGB4, MIM:613152, Beltran-Valero de Bernabe et al., 2003; MDDGC4, MIM:611588, Godfrey et al., 2006).<br><br>Binding of FKTN to POMGNT1 (which itself binds to the trisaccharide linker) is a prerequisite for the formation of the FKRP:FKTN:RXYLT1 complex (Xiong et al., 2006; Kuwabara et al., 2016). Mutations in POMGNT1 can cause muscular dystrophy-dystroglycanopathies of varying severity (MDDGA3, MIM:253280, Yoshida et al., 2001; MDDGB3, MIM:613151, Biancheri et al., 2006; MDDGC3, MIM:613157, Clement et al., 2008).
