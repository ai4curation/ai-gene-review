# v-ATPase:Ragulator:RagA,B:GDP:RagC,D:GDP binds SLC38A9:Arginine
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9640175](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9640175)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The N-terminal domain of SLC38A9 bound to L-arginine interacts indirectly with the Ragulator complex via the Rag GTPases RRAGA and RRAGC (Wang et al. 2015, Rebsamen et al. 2015, Wyant et al. 2017, Shen and Sabatini 2018).
