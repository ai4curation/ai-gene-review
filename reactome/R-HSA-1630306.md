# GLB1 hydrolyses a glycosaminoglycan
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1630306](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1630306)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Beta-galactosidase (GLB1) can cleave terminal galactose residues from glycosaminoglycans such as keratan sulfate (KS) (Asp et al. 1969). Defects in GLB1 cause the lysosomal storage diseases GM1 gangliosidosis (Yoshida et al. 1991) and Morquio syndrome type B (Oshima et al. 1991).
