# ATFS-1 translocates from the cytosol to the mitochondrial matrix
**Reactome ID:** [R-CEL-9856612](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-CEL-9856612)
**Species:** Caenorhabditis elegans

## Summary

In unstressed cells, ATFS-1 is translocated from the cytosol to the mitochondrial matrix where it is constitutively degraded by the protease Lon-P (Nargund et al. 2012).
