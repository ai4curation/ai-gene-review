# POLG:POLG2 and TWNK (PEO1) bind mitochondrial DNA:RNA primer to form H strand replisome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9913634](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9913634)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The DNA helicase TWINKLE (TWNK, PEO1) and the DNA polymerase POLgamma (POLG:POLG2) bind OriH located near the 5' end of the D loop region of mitochondrial DNA (Garrido et al. 2003, Jemt et al. 2011, Jemt et al. 2015) to form a minimal mitochondrial replisome for replication of the H strand (Korhonen et al. 2004). TWNK loads onto DNA as an open ringed hexamer (Kaur et al. 2020, Kaur et al. 2021). TWNK is required for mitochondrial DNA replication, indicating that it is the sole replicative helicase in mitochondria (Milenkovic et al. 2013).
