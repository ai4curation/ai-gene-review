# Sos-mediated nucleotide exchange of Ras (Tie2 receptor:Grb2:Sos)
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-210977](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-210977)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Sos-1 bound to Grb2:Tie2 complex promotes the exchange of inactive Ras-GDP to active Ras-GTP (Boriack-Sjodin et al.1998, Khan et al. 2004).
