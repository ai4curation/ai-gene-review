# VPS4 mediates disassembly of ESCRTIII subunits to promote sealing of holes in the nuclear envelope
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9668415](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9668415)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The AAA+ ATPase action of VPS4 causes reorganization/disassembly of ESCRT-III filaments, which leads to membrane fusion and sealing of nuclear envelope holes (Vietri et al. 2015).
