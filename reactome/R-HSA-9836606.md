# SPHK1 binds PKR
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9836606](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9836606)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Sphingosine kinase 1 (SPHK1) that has been phosphorylated at S225 binds to inactive protein kinase R (PKR, EIF2AK2), preventing its activation, antagonizing the apoptotic pathways (Qiao et al, 2020).
