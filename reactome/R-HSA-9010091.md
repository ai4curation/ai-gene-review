# APP translocates from plasma membrane to endosome lumen
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9010091](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9010091)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

APP that does not get processed by alpha-secretase in the non-amyloidogenic pathway is internalised to endosomes for further processing (Baranello et al. 2015).
