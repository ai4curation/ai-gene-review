# TRMT61B methylates adenosine-58 in tRNA yielding 1-methyladenosine-58
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6787525](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6787525)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

A TRMT61B oligomer, probably a tetramer, transfers a methyl group from S-adenosylmethionine to the (N)1 position of adenosine-58 in 3 mitochondrial tRNAs (tRNA(Leu)(UUR), tRNA(Lys), tRNA(Ser(UCN)) (Chujo and Suzuki 2012).
