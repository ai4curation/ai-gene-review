# NPC transports GCK1:GCKR from cytosol to nucleoplasm
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-170796](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-170796)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The complex of glucokinase (GCK1) and glucokinase regulatory protein (GCKR) is translocated to the nucleus via the nuclear pore (Shiota et al. 1999).
