# Maturation of nucleoprotein
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9683610](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9683610)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Nucleoprotein, the most abundant viral protein expressed during infection, is found in the cytosol and plasma membrane. After phosphorylation and sumoylation it trimerizes and is moved to the Golgi, the virion budding site (Li et al, 2005; Surjit et al, 2005).
