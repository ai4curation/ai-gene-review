# PCSK9:LDLR:Clathrin-coated vesicle transport from plasma membrane to endolysosome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6784729](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6784729)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The PCSK9:LDLR:Clathrin-coated vesicle is internalized to endosomes and, after that, to lysosomes where PCSK9 and LDLR are degraded (Wang et al. 2012).
