# I(1,3,4,6)P4 transports from the cytosol to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855201](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855201)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol 1,3,4,6-tetrakisphosphate (I(1,3,4,6)P4) translocates from the cytosol to the nucleus via the nuclear pore (Ho et al. 2002, Nalaskowski et al. 2002).
