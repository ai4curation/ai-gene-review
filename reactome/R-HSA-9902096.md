# COX18 inserts nascent MT-CO2 in COX20:TMEM77
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9902096](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9902096)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Nascent MT-CO2 localizes to the inner mitochondrial membrane, helped by the insertase COX18 (Bourens & Barrientos, 2017). Folding of MT-CO2 requires the chaperone complex COX20:TMEM177 which stabilizes the newly synthesized protein and presents it to metallochaperones (Szklarczyk et al., 2013; Bourens et al., 2014; Lorenzi et al., 2018). One Mg2+ ion is inserted, probably during folding.
