# FAM20B phosphorylates Xyl moiety
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9941039](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9941039)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Glycosaminoglycan xylosylkinase FAM20B phosphorylates the xylose (Xyl) moiety of the GAG linker glycan chain of proteoglycans, making the chain unavailable for the biosynthesis of heparan/chondoitin/dermatan (Koike et al., 2009; reviewed in  Nowicka & Gręda, 2019).
