# FOXO1,FOXO3 binds Ccng2 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9620857](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9620857)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recombinant human FOXO1 (Chen et al. 2006) and FOXO3 (Martinez-Gac et al. 2004, Chen et al. 2006) bind evolutionarily conserved forkhead box elements in the promoter region of the mouse Ccng2 gene, encoding Cyclin-G2.
