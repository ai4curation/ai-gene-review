# p-Y-Stat1,3,5 dimer binds Socs1 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9705472](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9705472)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Stat1 dimers (Saito et al. 2000, Chan et al. 2014), Stat3 dimers (Naka et al. 1997), or Stat5 dimers (inferred from Stat1 and Stat3) are believed to bind the promoter of the Socs1 gene.
