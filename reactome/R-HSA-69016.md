# Ubiquitinated Cdc6 is degraded by the proteasome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-69016](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-69016)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ubiquitinated Cdc6' is present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ubiquitin' is present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'cytosol' and  is mediated by the 'endopeptidase activity' of '26S proteasome'.<br>
