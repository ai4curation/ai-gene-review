# NS1 binds MBL
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9921544](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9921544)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Secreted DENV NS1 binds to mannose-binding lectin (MBL), protecting DENV from MBL-mediated neutralization. This suggests a mechanism of viral immune evasion at the very earliest phase of infection (Thiemecca et al., 2016).
