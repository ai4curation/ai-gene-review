# IL4:IL4R:JAK2 binds IL2RG:JAK3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6786092](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6786092)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Interleukin 2 receptor gamma subunit (IL2RG), with constitutively-associated Janus kinase 3 (JAK3), binds the IL4:IL4R:JAK2 complex, interacting with IL4 and IL4R to form the ligand-bound type I IL4 receptor complex, IL4R1 (LaPorte et al. 2008).
