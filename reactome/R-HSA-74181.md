# acetoacetyl-CoA + CoA <=> 2 acetyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-74181](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-74181)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Acetyl-CoA acetyltransferase tetramer (ACAT1) in the mitochondrial matrix catalyzes the reversible reaction of acetoacetyl-CoA and CoA to form two molecules of acetyl-CoA (Middleton et al. 1986).
