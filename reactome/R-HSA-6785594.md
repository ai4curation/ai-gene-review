# FANCD2 binds FANCI
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6785594](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6785594)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

FANCD2 binds FANCI, forming the ID2 complex (Yuan et al. 2009, Joo et al. 2011). The ID2 complex plays an important role in the repair of DNA interstrand crosslinks (the Fanconi anemia pathway).
