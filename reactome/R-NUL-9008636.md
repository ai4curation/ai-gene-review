# GSK3B phosphorylates Runx2
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9008636](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9008636)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recombinant human GSK3B phosphorylates recombinant mouse Runx2 on three serine residues in an evolutionarily conserved motif SPPWSYDQS (Kugimiya et al. 2007).
