# RANBP2 SUMOylates HDAC4 with SUMO2,3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4615987](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4615987)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RANBP2 SUMOylates HDAC4 at lysine-559 with SUMO2,3 (Kirsh et al. 2002). SUMOylation increases transcription repression by HDAC4.
