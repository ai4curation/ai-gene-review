# Dissociation of Phospho-Nlp from the centrosome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-380303](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-380303)

## Summary

Mitotic activation of Plk1 is required for efficient displacement of Nlp from the centrosome (Casenghi et al., 2003).
