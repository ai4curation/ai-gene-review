# Akt Phosphorylates As160 (Tbc1d4)
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-1445150](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-1445150)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Akt2 and, to a lesser extent, Akt1 phosphorylate As160 (Tbc1d4) in response to insulin signaling (Hill et al. 1999, Kane et al. 2002, Bae et al. 2003, Jiang et al. 2003, Katome et al. 2003, Green et al. 2008, Ng et al. 2008, Gonzalez and McGraw 2009, Ng et al. 2010).
