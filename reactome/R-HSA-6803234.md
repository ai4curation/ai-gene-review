# Dissociation of paradoxically activated RAS:BRAF complexes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6803234](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6803234)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After phosphorylation by MAP2Ks, the scaffolded kinase complex assembled by kinase dead or inhibited BRAF mutants dissociates, as is the case for WT complexes (reviewed in Lavoie and Therrien et al, 2015).
