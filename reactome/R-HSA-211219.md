# Translocation of caspase-3 to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-211219](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-211219)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Active caspase-3 is translocated from the cytoplasm to the nucleus during progression through apoptosis (Kamada et al., 2005).
