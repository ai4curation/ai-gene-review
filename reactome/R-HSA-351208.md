# Spermidine  => N-acetylated spermidine
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-351208](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-351208)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Spermidine/spermine N1-acetyltransferase (SAT1, also known as diamine acetyltransferase 1) acetylates spermidine (SPM) to yield N-acetylspermidine (NASPM). This is the rate-limiting step in polyamine catabolism (Casero et al. 1991; Fogel-Petrovic et al. 1993).
