# 5-PP-IP4 transports from the nucleus to the cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855173](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855173)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol 5-diphospho-1,3,4,6-tetrakisphosphate (5-PP-IP4) translocates from the nucleus to the cytosol via the nuclear pore (Saiardi et al. 2001, Saiardi et al. 2000).
