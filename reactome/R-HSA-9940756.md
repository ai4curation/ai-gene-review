# FKRP dimer transfers RboP to matriglycan chain
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9940756](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9940756)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The ribitol-5-phosphate transferase FKRP:Mg2+ dimer subunit of the FKRP:FKTN:RXYLT1 complex transfers ribitol-5-phosphate (RboP) from CDP-ribitol to the terminal RboP moiety of growing matriglycan chains present as O-mannosyl modifications in alpha-dystroglycan (DAG1) (Kanagawa et al., 2016; Nishihara et al., 2018; Kuwabara et al., 2020). Mutations in FKRP can cause muscular dystrophy-dystroglycanopathies of varying severity (MDDGA5, MIM:613153, Beltran-Valero de Bernabe et al., 2004; MDDGB5, MIM:606612, Brockington et al., 2001a; MDDGC5, MIM:607155, Brockington et al., 2001b).
