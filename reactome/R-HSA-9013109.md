# RHOC GEFs activate RHOC
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9013109](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9013109)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The following guanine nucleotide exchange factors (GEFs) were shown to bind RHOC and catalyze GDP to GTP exchange on RHOC, resulting in formation of the active RHOC:GTP complex:<br>AKAP13 (Zheng et al. 1995)<br>ARHGEF1 (Jaiswal et al. 2011; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>ARHGEF5 (Wang et al. 2009)<br>ARHGEF10 (Mohl et al. 2006)<br>ARHGEF10L (Winkler et al. 2005)<br>ARHGEF11 (Jaiswal et al. 2011; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>ARHGEF12 (Jaiswal et al. 2011; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>ARHGEF17 (Mitin et al. 2012)<br>ARHGEF25 (Sloan et al. 2012)<br>ARHGEF28 (Jaiswal et al. 2011; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>ARHGEF40 (Curtis et al. 2004)<br>MCF2 (Sloan et al. 2012; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>MCF2L (Sloan et al. 2012; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>PREX1 (Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>VAV2 (Sloan et al. 2012; Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br><br>The following GEFs do not act on RHOC:<br>ARHGEF3 (Arthur et al. 2002; Sloan et al. 2012)<br>ARHGEF4 (Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>ARHGEF9 (Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>DNMBP (Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>ITSN1 (Jaiswal et al. 2013)<br>TIAM1 (Jaiswal et al. 2013)
