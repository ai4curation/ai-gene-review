# SH binds to BCAP31
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9837136](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9837136)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

HRSV small hydrophobic protein binds B-cell receptor-associated protein 31 (BCAP31, BAP31) in the endoplasmic reticulum membrane, which may lead to sequestering of BAP31 from contacts with other cellular proteins (Li et al, 2015).
