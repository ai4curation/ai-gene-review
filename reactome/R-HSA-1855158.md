# 5-PP-IP5 is phosphorylated to 5-PPP-IP5 by IP6K1/3 in the cytosol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855158](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855158)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Inositol hexakisphosphate kinase 1 (IP6K1) and 3 (IP6K3) phosphorylate inositol 5-diphospho-1,2,3,4,6-pentakisphosphate (5-PP-IP5) to inositol 5-triphospho- 1,2,3,4,6-pentakisphosphate (5-PPP-IP5).<br><br>The following lists the above proteins with their corresponding literature references: IP6K1 (Saiardi et al. 2001, Draskovic et al. 2008) and IP6K3 (Saiardi et al. 2001, Draskovic et al. 2008).
