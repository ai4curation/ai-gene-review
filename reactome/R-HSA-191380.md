# Mevalonate is phosphorylated to mevalonate-5-phosphate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191380](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191380)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic mevalonate kinase (MK) catalyzes the phosphorylation of mevalonate (MVA) to mevalonate-5-phosphate (MVA5P) (Hogenboom et al. 2004; Houten & Waterham 2000: Schafer et al. 1992).
