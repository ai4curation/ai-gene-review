# NPAS4 binds ARNT
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9634850](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9634850)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NPAS4 is a basic-helix-loop-helix (bHLH) transcription factor that needs to dimerize with another bHLH protein in order to be able to bind to target DNA. ARNT (ARNT1) is a bHLH transcription factor that can form a transcriptionally active heterodimer with NPAS4 (Ooe et al. 2004). In vivo, endogenous Arnt and Npas4 can be co-immunoprecipitated from the mouse brain (Ooe et al. 2009).
