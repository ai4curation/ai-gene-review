# PDK1 activates PKC zeta
**Reactome ID:** [R-RNO-437185](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-RNO-437185)
**Species:** Rattus norvegicus

## Summary

3-phosphoinositide dependent protein kinase-1 (Pdpk1, also known as Pdk1 and PKB kinase because of its activity at Protein kinase B) phosphorylates T410 of protein kinase C zeta type (Prkcz, also known as PKC zeta), leading to activation. The motif surrounding T410 is highly conserved in other PKC family members suggesting that Pdpk1 might activate other PKCs.
