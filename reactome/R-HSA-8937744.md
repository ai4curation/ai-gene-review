# PTPN11 binds p-7Y-RUNX1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8937744](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8937744)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Based on mouse studies, tyrosine phosphorylated RUNX1 forms a complex with PTPN11 (SHP2) protein tyrosine phosphatase in the nucleus (Huang et al. 2012).
