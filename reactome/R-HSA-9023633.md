# DPP4(1-766) hydrolyzes Glucagon-like Peptide-1 (GLP-1)
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9023633](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9023633)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Dipeptidyl Peptidase IV (DPP4) cleaves 2 amino acids from the N-terminus of GLP-1, inactivating it. DPP4 determines the half life of GLP-1 in the bloodstream. It is unknown if the soluble form of DPP4, the membrane-bound form, or both catalyze the cleavage of GLP-1.
