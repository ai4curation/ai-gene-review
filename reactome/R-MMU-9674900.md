# Csf3:Csf3r induces phosphorylation of Jak1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9674900](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9674900)

## Summary

Jak1 is phosphorylated in response to Csf3 binding Csf3r and phosphorylated Jak1 is required for subsequent phosporylation of Csf3r (Shimoda et al 1997).
