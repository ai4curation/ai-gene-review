# p-4Y-SIRPA:CD47:SKAP2 binds FYB
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-391151](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-391151)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The Fyn binding protein FYB (SLAP130, ADAP) has been found to associate with SIRP alpha (SIRPA). Recruitment of FYB to SIRPA requires SKAP2.
