# miR-106a binds RUNX1 mRNA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8938507](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8938507)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MicroRNA miR-106a binds the 3' UTR of the RUNX1 mRNA. As miR-106a does not affect RUNX1 mRNA levels, it presumably function as part of the nonendonucleolytic RISC (Fontana et al. 2007).
