# Socs1,3:p-4Y-Csf3r:Csf3 dimer:Lyn:p-Y-Jak1:p-Jak2:p-Syk:p-Hck:p-Tyk2 is endocytosed
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9707979](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9707979)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Binding of Csf3 to Csf3r results in the endocytosis of the Csf3:Csf3r complex (Hermans et al. 1999). Endocytosis depends on a dileucine motif in the C-terminal region of Csf3r.
