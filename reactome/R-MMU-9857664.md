# p-T308,S473-Akt1 phosphorylates Itga5 (Integrin alpha5) in Itga5:Itgb1 (Integrin alpha5:Integrin beta1)
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9857664](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9857664)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Akt1 phosphorylated on threonine-308 and serine-475 phosphorylates integrin alpha5 (Itga5) on an unknown residue (Batra et al. 2014). Itga5 is located in a complex with integrin beta1 (Itgb1) in the plasma membrane of osteocytes (Batra et al. 2012).
