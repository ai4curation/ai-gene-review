# Myosin Binds ATP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-445700](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-445700)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

With the expulsion of ADP from the nucleotide binding pocket, ATP, if available will immediately bind myosin. When bound to ATP, the myosin head is in its low-energy configuration and the cross-bridge with the actin filament is disrupted.
