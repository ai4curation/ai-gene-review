# Activated FGFR3 point, translocation and fusion mutant-associated PI3K phosphorylates PIP2 to PIP3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5655289](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5655289)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Once recruited to the activated receptor, PI3K phosphorylates PIP2 to PIP3, leading to activation of AKT signaling. PI3K signaling has been demonstrated downstream of a number of other FGFR mutants (Chen, 2004; Demiroglu, 2001; Guasch, 2001; Byron, 2008; Kunii, 2008; Agazie, 2003; Takeda, 2007; reviewed in Parker et al, 2014; Carter et al, 2015).
