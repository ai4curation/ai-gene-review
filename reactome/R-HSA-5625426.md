# Retrograde IFT
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5625426](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5625426)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Retrograde trains are shorter than anterograde particles and travel along the cilliary axoneme at an estimated rate of 3 micrometers per second (reviewed in Cole and Snell, 2009).
