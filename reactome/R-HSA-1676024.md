# PI is phosphorylated to PI3P by PIK3C2A/3 at the late endosome membrane
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1676024](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1676024)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the late endosome membrane, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase catalytic subunit type 3 (PIK3C3) aka VPS34 binds to phosphoinositide 3-kinase regulatory subunit 4 (PIK3R4).  The PIK3C3:PIK3R4 complex and phosphatidylinositol-4-phosphate 3-kinase C2 domain-containing subunit alpha (PIK3C2A) phosphorylate phosphatidylinositol (PI) to phosphatidylinositol 3-phosphate (PI3P).<br><br>The following lists the above proteins with their corresponding literature references: PIK3C3:PIK3R4 (Panaretou et al. 1997, Volinia et al. 1995, Cao et al. 2007) and PIK3C2A (Arcaro et al. 2000, Domin et al. 2000).
