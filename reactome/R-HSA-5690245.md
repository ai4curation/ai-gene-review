# p-T182 MAPKAPK5 binds DNAJB1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5690245](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5690245)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic MAPKAPK5 forms a complex with DNAJB1 through an interaction mediated by the C-terminal tails of both proteins (Kostenko et al, 2014).
