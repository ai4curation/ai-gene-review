# MITF-M-dependent CDH1 gene expression
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9926527](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9926527)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Expression of E-cadherin (CDH1) is upregulated in an MITF-M dependent manner during melanocyte lineage differentiation and in melanoma cells (Mansky et al, 2002; Dilshat et al, 2021; Laurette et al, 2015).<br>Changes in relative levels of E- and N-cadherin contribute to migration of neural crest cells during melanocyte differentiation, and also play a role during the development of melanoma (Li et al, 2001; Gupta et al, 2005; LaBonne et al, 2000; reviewed in Mort et al, 2015).
