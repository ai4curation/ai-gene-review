# Spike protein gets N-glycosylated
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9694793](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9694793)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Glycosyltransferases in the endoplasmatic reticulum are responsible for the attachment of numerous high-mannose N-glycans on the SARS-CoV-2 spike protein. After virion assembly and release these glycosidations are required for fusion with host cells (Watanabe et al, 2020, Breuer et al, 2001).<br><br>O-glycosylations do occur on the Spike protein with an average overall frequency per site of less than 50 percent (Sanda et al, 2020).
