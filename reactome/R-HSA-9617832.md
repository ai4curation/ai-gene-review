# FOXO4:ATXN3 binds SOD2 gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9617832](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9617832)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Endogenous human FOXO4 and ATXN3 form a complex that binds to adjacent FOXO- and ATXN3- binding sites in the promoter of the SOD2 gene, encoding mitochondrial  manganese-dependent superoxide dismutase (Araujo et al. 2011).
