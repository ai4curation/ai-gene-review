# GUSB tetramer hydrolyses (HA)2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2162227](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2162227)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The tetrameric lysosomal enzyme beta-glucuronidase hydrolyses glucuronate from the HA tetrasaccharide (Oshima et al. 1987) resulting in the single sugars glucuronic acid and N-acetylglucosamine. These single sugars then exit the lysosome. L-aspartic acid is an inhibitor of enzyme activity (Kreamer et al. 2001).
