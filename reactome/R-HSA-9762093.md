# BTRC binds p-S344, 347 NFE2L2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9762093](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9762093)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In response to GSK3B-dependent phosphorylation at, minimally, serine residues 344 and 347, the CUL1:BTRC complex binds to NFE2L2 through the Neh6 domain (Salazar et al, 2006; Rada et al, 2011; Rada et al, 2012; Rojo et al, 2012; reviewed in Baird and Yamamoto, 2020; Yamamoto et al, 2018).
