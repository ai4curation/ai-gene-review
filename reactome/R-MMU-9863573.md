# Akt1 binds phosphoinositol-3,4,5-trisphosphate
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9863573](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9863573)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

The Pleckstrin Homology (PH) domain of Akt1 (Pkb) binds phosphatidylinositol-3,4,5-trisphosphate at the plasma membrane (Frech et al. 1997).
