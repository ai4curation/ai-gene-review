# nucleoplasmic GCK1:GCKR complex => glucokinase (GCK1) + glucokinase regulatory protein (GCKR)
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-170810](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-170810)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In the presence of high glucose concentrations, the complex of nucleoplasmic glucokinase (GCK1) and glucokinase regulatory protein (GCKR) dissociates (Shiota et al. 1999).
