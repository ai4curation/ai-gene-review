# p-4Y-SIRPA:CD47 binds SKAP2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-391157](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-391157)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SRC-family-associated phosphoprotein 2 (SCAP2) has been shown to bind to SIRP alpha. Evidence from immunoprecipitation experiments performed in COS-7 lysates suggests that the SH3 domain of SCAP2 is involved in the interaction.
