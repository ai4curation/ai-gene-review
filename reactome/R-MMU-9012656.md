# phospho-Epor:phospho-Jak2:Lyn:Irs2 binds Stat5
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9012656](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9012656)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Stat5 (Stat5a or Stat5b) binds the phosphorylated cytoplasmic domain of Epor via phosphotyrosine-343 and phosphotyrosine-479 of Epor (Chin et al. 1996, Gobert et al. 1996, Sawyer and Penta 1996, Damen et al. 1997, Iwatsuki et al. 1997, Barber et al. 2001, Bittorf et al. 2000).
