# NBR1 binds MAP1LC3B
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9664867](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9664867)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Once recruited to the peroxisomal membrane, Next to BRCA1 gene 1 protein (NBR1) can bind with Microtubule associated proteins 1A/1B light chain 3B (MAP1LC3B)/LC3 with the help of its LC3 interacting region (LIR) domain (Kirkin V et al. 2009). This targets the peroxisomes to the autophagosomes and the degradation process starts.
