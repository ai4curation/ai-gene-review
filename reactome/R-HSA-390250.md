# 3-ketohexacosanoyl-CoA + CoASH => tetracosanoyl-CoA + acetyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-390250](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-390250)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Peroxisomal ACAA1 catalyzes the reaction of 3-ketohexacosanoyl-CoA and CoASH to form tetracosanoyl-CoA + acetyl-CoA (Bout et al. 1991).
