# pre-M is N-glycosylated
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9918988](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9918988)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Dengue virus pre-prM is N-glycosylated at Asn-7, Asn-31, Asn-52, and Asn-69 (Asn-121, Asn-145, Asn-166 and Asn-183 of the polyprotein), requiring, among others, the oligosaccharyl transferase activity of the OST complex (Labeau et al., 2020). The exact structure of the glycan modification is unknown. Results come from experiments with strain FGA/89 of Dengue virus (serotype 1) (Courageot et al., 2000; reviewed in Idris et al., 2016; Yap et al., 2017; Feng et al., 2022). The dependence of prM and E folding and glycosylation on the dolichyl-phosphate mannosyltransferase complex (DPM1:DPM2:DPM3) has been shown directly (Labeau et al., 2020).
