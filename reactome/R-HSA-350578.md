# NQO1 interaction with ODC
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-350578](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-350578)

## Summary

Asher et al. (2015) have described a process in which ODC is stabilized and protected from proteasomal degradation by interaction with NQO1 (NAD(P)H quinone oxidoreductase).
