# TNF:TNFR1:TRADD:TRAF2:K63polyUb-RIPK1:BIRC2/3:LUBAC recruits TAK1 complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5357860](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5357860)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Polyubiquitinated RIPK1 binds to TAB2 resulting in the recruitment of the TAB2:TAK1 complex. The K63-linked ubiqitination on Lys377 of RIPK1 was shown to mediate the association of RIPK1 with the TAB2:TAK1 complex (Li H et al. 2006)
