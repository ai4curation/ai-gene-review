# AHR:TCDD:2xHSP90AB1:AIP:PTGES3 dissociates
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8937191](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8937191)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After ligand binding and activation, the AHR complex translocates to the nucleus where it disassociates from the chaperone subunits to then dimerise with the aryl hydrocarbon receptor nuclear translocator (ARNT) (Lees & Whitelaw 1999).
