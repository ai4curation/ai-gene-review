# Reduction of presqualene diphosphate to form squalene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-191402](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-191402)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In the second step of squalene formation from farnesyl diphosphate (FAPP), FDFT (Farnesyl-diphosphate farnesyltransferase) catalyzes the reduction of presqualene diphosphate (PSQPP) to squalene (SQNE) (Pandit et al. 2000).
