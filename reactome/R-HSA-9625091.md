# FOXO1,FOXO3 bind ABCA6 gene promoter
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9625091](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9625091)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

FOXO1 and FOXO3 bind forkhead box response elements in the promoter of the ABCA6 gene, encoding ATP-binding cassette sub-family A member 6, a putative transporter protein involved in lipid homeostasis (Gai et al. 2013).
