# NSDHL decarboxylates 4a-carboxy-4b-methyl-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol to 4a-methyl-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9945822](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9945822)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NSDHL (Sterol-4-alpha-carboxylate 3-dehydrogenase, decarboxylating), in the endoplasmic reticulum membrane, decarboxylates 4a-carboxy-4b-methyl-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol to 4a-methyl-5a-cholest-8-en-3b-ol (Liu et al. 1999).
