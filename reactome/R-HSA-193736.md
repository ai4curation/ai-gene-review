# Isomerization of 3,7,24THCA-CoA to (24R, 25R) 3alpha,7alpha,24-trihydroxy-5beta-cholestanoyl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-193736](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-193736)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The isomerization of 3,7,24THCA-CoA to  (24R, 25R) 3alpha,7alpha,24-trihydroxy-5beta-cholestanoyl-CoA, catalyzed by 2-methylacyl-CoA racemase, occurs in the peroxisomal matrix.
