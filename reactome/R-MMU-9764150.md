# Csf1 dimer:p-Y-559-Csf1r dimer:SFK:Grb2:Sos1 dissociates yielding Csf1 dimer:p-Y-559-Csf1r dimer:SFK and Grb2:Sos1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9764150](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9764150)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Grb2:Sos1 transiently associates with phosphorylated Csf1r after Csf1r undergoes an initial wave of autophosphorylation (Wang et al. 1999). Grb2:Sos1 then dissociates from Csf1r.
