# eNOS binds NOSIP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-203553](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-203553)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

NOSIP (eNOS interacting protein) binds to the carboxyl-terminal region of the eNOS oxygenase domain.  Note that the eNOS binding sites for caveolin and NOSIP overlap.
