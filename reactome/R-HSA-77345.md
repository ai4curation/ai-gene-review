# Decanoyl-CoA+FAD => trans-Dec-2-enoyl-CoA+FADH2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-77345](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-77345)

## Summary

ACADM in the mitochondrial matrix dehydrogenates decanoyl-CoA to form trans-dec-2-enoyl-CoA. (Krane & Beinert 1956; Finocchiaro et al. 1987).
