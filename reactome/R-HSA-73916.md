# 2 acetyl-CoA <=> acetoacetyl-CoA + CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-73916](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-73916)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Acetyl-CoA acetyltransferase tetramer (ACAT1) in the mitochondrial matrix catalyzes the reversible reaction of two molecules of acetyl-CoA to form acetoacetyl-CoA and CoA (Middleton et al. 1986).
