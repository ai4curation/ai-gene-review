# Interaction of integrin alphaIIb beta 3 with THBS1 (Thrombospondin-1)
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-349603](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-349603)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Thrombospondin-1 (TSP-1) secreted from platelet alpha granules on thrombin activation associates with actin cytoskeleton. This association of TSP-1 with actin cytoskeleton is mediated by the membrane receptor alphaIIbbeta3.
