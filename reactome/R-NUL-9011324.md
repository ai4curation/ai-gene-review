# Proteasome degrades SAX-3 ubiquitinated by EBAX-1
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9011324](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9011324)
**Species:** Caenorhabditis elegans

## Summary

Human proteasome degrades recombinant C. elegans SAX-3 (orthologue of ROBO3.1) ubiquitinated by recombinant C. elegans EBAX-1 (orthologue of ZSWIM8), expressed in human embryonic kidney cell line 293T (Wang et al. 2013).
