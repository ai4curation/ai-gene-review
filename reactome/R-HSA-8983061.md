# TSLP binds CRLF2:IL7R
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8983061](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8983061)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The functional receptor for human Thymic stromal lymphopoietin (TSLP)  consists of two subunits, Cytokine receptor-like factor 2 (TSLPR) and Interleukin-7 receptor subunit alpha (IL7R) (Reche et al.2001).<br>This is a black box event since it is not clear whether this binding event occurs in association with other IL7 receptor complexes or independently.
