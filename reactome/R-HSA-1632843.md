# AMBRA1 binds DYNLL1,DYNLL2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1632843](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1632843)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Activating molecule in BECN1-regulated autophagy protein 1 (AMBRA1) is anchored to microtubules through an interaction with dynein light chains 1 and 2, components of the dynein motor complex (Di Bartolomeo et al. 2010, Fimia et al. 2011).
