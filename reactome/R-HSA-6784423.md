# HOGA1 tetramer aldol-cleaves 4-OH-2-oxoglutarate (HOG) to glyoxylate and pyruvate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6784423](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6784423)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Mitochondrial HOGA1 aldol-cleaves 4-OH-2-oxoglutarate (HOG) to glyoxylate and pyruvate. The mature protein lacks a 25-residue mitochondrial targeting sequence and forms a homotetramer (Riedel et al. 2011).
