# p-Y-Stat1,3,5 dimer binds Socs3 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9705459](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9705459)

## Summary

Stat1 (Auernhammer et al. 1999, Gatto et al. 2004), Stat3 (Auernhammer et al. 1999, Hou et al. 2008), and Stat5 (Emanuelli et al. 2000) dimers bind the 5' region of the Socs3 gene.
