# TGN-lysosome vesicle uncoating and release of nonameric complex to lysosome
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2213236](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2213236)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

On reaching the late endosomes/lysosomes the TGN-lysosome vesicle uncoats and fuses with the lysosome to releases the MHC II nonameric complex.
