# 2-oxoglutarate [mitochondrial matrix] + 2-oxoadipate [cytosol] <=> 2-oxoglutarate [cytosol] + 2-oxoadipate [mitochondrial matrix]
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-372480](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-372480)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

SLC25A21, the mitochondrial 2-oxodicarboxylate carrier, mediates the exchange of 2-oxoadipate and 2-oxoglutarate across the inner mitochondrial membrane. While the exchange is reversible, under physiological conditions it proceeds in the direction of 2-oxoadipate import into the mitochondrial matrix and 2-oxoglutarate export (Fiermonte et al. 2001).
