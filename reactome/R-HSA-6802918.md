# Activated MAP2Ks phosphorylate MAPKs downstream of inactive BRAF mutants
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802918](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802918)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Downstream of kinase-dead or inhibited forms of BRAF, activated MAP2K phosphorylates MAPK on threonine and tyrosine residues in the activation loop (Wan et al, 2004; Garnett et al, 2005; Heidorn et al, 2010; Poulikakos et al, 2010; Hatzivassilliou et al, 2010; reviewed in Samatar and Poulikakos, 2014; Lavoie and Therrien, 2015).
