# Interaction of integrin alphaIIbbeta3 with Fibronectin
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-349593](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-349593)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Fibronectin plays a role in cell adhesion and cell migration. It mediates platelet adhesion in the vasculature by interacting with integrin alphaIIbbeta3.
