# Formation of AT-AC C complex
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-75079](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-75079)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'ATAC B Complex' is present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of 'U4 ATAC snRNP', and 1 molecule of 'ATAC C Complex' are present.<br><br> This reaction takes place in the 'nucleus' (Pikielny et al.1989, Kreivi et al. 1996).<br>
