# HDACs deacetylate p-STAT3 dimers
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9701565](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9701565)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

HDAC1, 2, and 3 deacetylate lysine 685 of STAT3. Deacetylation reduces the transcriptional activity of STAT3 dimers as assessed by reporter gene assays in HEK293 cells (Yuan et al, 2005).
