# Spermine  => N-acetylated spermine
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-351207](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-351207)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Spermidine/spermine N1-acetyltransferase (SAT1, also known as diamine acetyltransferase 1) acetylates spermine (SPN) to yield N-acetylspermine (NASPN). This is the rate-limiting step in polyamine catabolism (Hegde et al. 2007).
