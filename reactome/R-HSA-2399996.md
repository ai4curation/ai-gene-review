# AKT1 E17K mutant phosphorylates CREB1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2399996](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2399996)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

AKT1 E17K gain-of-function mutant is expected to phosphorylate CREB1, like the wild-type AKT (Du et al. 1998), but this has not been experimentally tested.
