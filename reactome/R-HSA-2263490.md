# Defective GALNS does not hydrolyse sulfate from Gal6S in keratan sulfate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2263490](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2263490)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

From a recent review of mutations for MPSIVA (MIM:253000) (Tomatsu et al. 2005), almost 80% of mutations in N-acetylgalactosamine 6-sulfatase (GALNS; MIM:612222) were missense mutations and of these, the most common ones are R386C, G301C and I113F.
