# p-Y705-STAT3 dimer, p-Y641-STAT6 dimer translocate to nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6786293](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6786293)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylated, dimerized Signal transducer and activator of transcription 6 (STAT6) translocates to the nucleus where it regulates the expression of  multiple genes (Mikita et al. 1996, Daines et al. 2003, Hebenstreit et al. 2006, Goenka & Kaplan 2011).
