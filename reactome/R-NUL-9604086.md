# PEX19:Pex3 binds PEX16
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9604086](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9604086)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The cytosolic PEX19:Pex3 complex binds PEX16 located in the peroxisomal membrane (human PEX19, human PEX16, and rat Pex3, Matsuzaki and Fujiki 2008).
