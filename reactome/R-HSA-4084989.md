# NANP dephosphorylates Neu5Ac-9-P to Neu5Ac
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4084989](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4084989)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

N-acylneuraminate 9-phosphatase (NANP) dephosphorylates N-acetylneuraminate -9-phosphate (Neu5Ac-9-P) to produce N-acetylneuraminate (Neu5Ac). NANP requires Mg<sup>2+</sup> as a cofactor (Maliekal et al. 2006).
