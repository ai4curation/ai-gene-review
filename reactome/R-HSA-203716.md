# eNOS:Caveolin-1 complex binds to Nostrin
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-203716](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-203716)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

eNOS interacts with the SH3 domain of NOSTRIN (positions 434-506).  Caveolin-1 also binds directly to NOSTRIN (residues 323-434), thus allowing formation of a ternary complex.
