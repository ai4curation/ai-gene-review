# Transport of HCMV DNA Into the Nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9614369](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9614369)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After the Human Cytomegalovirus (HCMV) capsid docks at the nuclear pore complex the HCMV genome containing capsid is transported into the nucleus.
