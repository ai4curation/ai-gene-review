# hPSMF1 binds bovine 20S core particle
**Reactome ID:** [R-NUL-9908837](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-NUL-9908837)
**Species:** Bos taurus

## Summary

Human PSMF1 (PI31) binds to the bovine 20S core particle (20S CP) (McCutchen-Maloney et al. 2000: human PSMF1 and bovine 20S CP were used; Hsu et al. 2023: human PSMF1 and bovine 20S CP were used; Wang et al. 2024: human PSMF1 and bovine 20S CP were used).
