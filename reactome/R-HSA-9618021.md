# FOXG1 binds FOXO:SMAD complexes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9618021](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9618021)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

FOXG1 can bind to complexes of FOXO transcription factors and activated SMAD2/3:SMAD4 via direct interaction with FOXO3, and probably also FOXO1 or FOXO4 (Seoane et al. 2004).
