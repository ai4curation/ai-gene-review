# HK1 phosphorylates Mannose to Man6P
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9909159](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9909159)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic hexokinase 1 (HK1) catalyzes the phosphorylation of mannose (Man) to form D-mannose 6-phosphate (Man6P). The properties of the human enzyme are inferred from those of its better-studied rat ortholog (Abraham et al. 1961, 1964).
