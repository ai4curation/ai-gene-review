# dUDP or TDP + ADP <=> dUMP or TMP + ATP [DTYMK]
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-75126](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-75126)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic deoxythymidylate kinase (DTYMK) catalyzes the reversible reaction of either dUDP or TDP with ADP to form dUMP or TMP. The active form of the enzyme is a homodimer (Lee and Cheng 1977).
