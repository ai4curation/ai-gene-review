# LGMN hydrolyzes GC, releasing CUBN and 25(OH)D
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-350158](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-350158)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

In the acidic environment of the lysosome, LGMN (legumain asparagine-specific endoprotease) hydrolyzes the GC (vitamin D-binding protein) component of the CUBN:GC:25(OH)D complex, releasing cubilin (CUBN) and 25(OH)D (25-hydroxyvitamin D, calcidiol) (Nykjaer et al. 1999).
