# MDM2 polyubiquitinates RUNX3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8952382](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8952382)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

MDM2 polyubiquitinates RUNX3 on lysine residues K94 and K148. MDM-mediated ubiquitination of RUNX3 is inhibited by p14-ARF (Chi et al. 2009).
