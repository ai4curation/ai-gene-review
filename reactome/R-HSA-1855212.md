# 1/3-PP-IP5 transports from the cytosol to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-1855212](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-1855212)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

1-diphospho-2,3,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate (1-PP-IP5) and 3-diphospho-1,2,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate (3-PP-IP5) translocate from the cytosol to the nucleus via the nuclear pore (Saiardi et al. 2001, Mulugu et al. 2007).
