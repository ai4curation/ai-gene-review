# Ubiquitination of DLL/JAG ligands upon binding to NOTCH1 PEST domain mutants
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2769007](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2769007)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Ubiquitination of DLL/JAG ligands upon binding to NOTCH1 PEST domain mutants has not been investigated but is assumed to occur based on the behavior of the wild-type NOTCH1 (Lai et al. 2001, Itoh et al. 2003, Koo et al. 2005, Lai et al. 2005, Le Borgne et al. 2005, Pistouli et al. 2005, Song et al. 2006, Koo et al. 2007, Koutelou et al. 2008).
