# UST transfers sulfate to (2-)GlcA-GalNAc-6-sulfate
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9941305](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9941305)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Uronyl 2-sulfotransferase (UST) occasionally sulfates GlcA in the C-2 position (bound to 6‑O‑sulfated GalNAc moieties) in chondroitin sulfate (CS) chains of CS proteoglycans. The main activity of UST is toward iduronyl residues in dermatan sulfate (CSPG, Kobayashi et al., 1999; Xu et al., 2007).
