# Prkaa2 dissociate from p-Plins:Hspa8
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9613562](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9613562)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Once phosphorylated, Plins are believed to dissociate from Prkaa2 and translocate to the cytosol (S Kaushik et al. 2015, S Kaushik et al. 2016). The precise dissociation mechanism of Plins is unclear.
