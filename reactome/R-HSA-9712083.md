# ALK mutants bind PI3KCA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9712083](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9712083)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The catalytic subunit of PI3K is recruited downstream ALK fusions and point mutants signal through the PI3K pathway as assessed by both binding and activity assays (Bai et al, 2000; Slupianek and Tomasz, 2001; Slupianek et al, 2004; Koivunen et al, 2008; George et al, 2008; Wang et al, 2011; reviewed in Roskoski, 2013; Hallberg and Palmer, 2013; Della Corte et al, 2018; Ducray et al, 2019).
