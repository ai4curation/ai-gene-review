# Isomerization of 25(R) THCA-CoA to 25(S) THCA-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-192056](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-192056)

## Summary

The isomerization of 25(R) THCA-CoA to 25(S) THCA-CoA, catalyzed by 2-methylacyl-CoA racemase, occurs in the peroxisomal matrix.
