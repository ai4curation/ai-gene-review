# 26S proteasome degrades Ub-NFE2L2
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-8932355](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-8932355)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Ubiquitinated Nuclear factor erythroid 2-related (NFE2L2) undergoes proteasomal degradation and this maintains the protein level and activity at low levels. (Kobayashi et al, 2004; McMahon et al, 2003)<br>
