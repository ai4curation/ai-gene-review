# NADSYN1 hexamer amidates NAAD to NAD+
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-197271](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-197271)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Cytosolic hexameric NAD synthase 1 (NADSYN1) catalyses the final step in the biosynthesis of NAD+, both in de novo synthesis and in the salvage pathway. The enzymes makes use of an amide donor in the reaction (Hara et al. 2003).
