# ATM:PEX5 binds ATM
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9664879](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9664879)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

After Peroxisomal targeting signal 1 receptor (PEX5) recruits Ataxia telangiectasia mutated protein (ATM) to the peroxisomal membrane, ATM is activated by the reactive oxygen species (ROS). ROS can oxidize and activate ATM by forming a disulphide cross-linked dimer at the Csy2991 residue (Guo Z et al. 2010).
