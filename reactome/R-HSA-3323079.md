# GSR catalyzes glutathione (oxidized) + NADPH + H+ => 2 glutathione (reduced) + NADP+
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-3323079](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-3323079)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Glutathione reductase (GSR) in the mitochondrial matrix regenerates reduced glutathione from oxidized glutathione and NADPH (Berkholz et al. 2008).
