# GGPS1 hexamer transfers IPPP to GPP
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9717830](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9717830)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Further condensation of an isopentenyl pyrophosphate (IPPP) with geranyl pyrophosphate (GPP) to form farnesyl pyrophosphate (FAPP) is catalyzed by the prenyltransferases FPP synthase and GGPP synthase (Ericsson et al. 1998; Kavanagh et al, 2006; Kuzuguchi et al. 1999).
