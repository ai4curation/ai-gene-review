# RAS:GTP:moderate kinase activity p-RAF complexes phosphorylate MAP2Ks
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-6802919](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-6802919)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Recruitment of MAP2Ks and MAPKs downstream of moderately activated BRAF mutants leads to phosphorylation and activation of MAP2Ks, as in the WT pathway (Wan et al, 2004; Freeman et al, 2013; Roring et al, 2012; reviewed in Lavoie and Therrien, 2015).
