# Epo:p-8Y-Epor:p-12Y-Jak2:Lyn:Irs2 binds Crkl:Rapgef1
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9030346](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9030346)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Crkl, in a constitutive complex with Rapgef1 (C3G, a nucleotide exchange factor for Rap1), binds the cytoplasmic domain of phosphorylated Epor (Chin et al. 1997).
