# SFTPB dimer,C translocate from multivesicle body to lamellar body
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-5684865](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-5684865)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

The mature surfactant proteins B dimer and C (SFTPB dimer and C) are translocated to lamellar bodies (LBs), ready for secretion (Whitsett et al. 2010). The mechanism of transport is unknown.
