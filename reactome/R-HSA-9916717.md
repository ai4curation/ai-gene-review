# ECHS1 mutants don't synthesize beta-hydroxyisobutyryl-CoA
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9916717](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9916717)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

ECHS1 catalyzes the fourth step in valine catabolism, the conversion of methylacrylyl-CoA to 3-hydroxyisobutyryl-CoA, also known as beta-hydroxyisobutyryl-CoA (Yamada et al, 2015). ECHS1 also plays a role in the beta-oxidation of short-chain fatty acids, contributing to cellular energetics through the production of acetyl-CoA (Kanazawa et al, 1993; Pougovkina et al, 2014; Yamada et al, 2015). Mutations in ECHS1 cause a Leigh-like syndrome whose pathology is likely the result of defects in both valine and fatty acid catabolism (Haack et al, 2015; Yamada et al, 2105; reviewed in Rahman, 2023; Schubert-Baldo and Vilarinho, 2020).<br>The following ECHS1 mutations have been identified in patients with Leigh-like syndrome, show decreased or abolished ECHS1 activity and/or protein levels in vitro, and are classified as pathogenic or likely pathogenic in ClinGen, and are categorized as ECHS1 disease set members in Reactome:<br><br><br>F33S: Haack et al, 2015; Kohda et al, 2016;<br><br><br>R54H: Haack et al, 2015;<br><br><br>N59S: Haack et al, 2015; Yamada et al, 2015; Kohda et al, 2016;<br><br><br>I66T: Haack et al, 2015;<br><br><br>E77Q: Haack et al, 2015;<br><br><br>A138V: Yamada et al, 2015;<br><br><br>D150G: Haack et al, 2105;<br><br><br>A158D: Peters et al, 2014;<br><br><br>Q159R: Haack et al, 2015; Ferdinandusse et al, 2015; Fitzsimons et al, 2018; Kohda et al, 2016;<br><br><br>C225R: Haack et al, 2015; Ferdinandusse et al, 2015;<br><br><br><br>These mutations have been identified in patients with Leigh-like syndrome and are classified as pathogenic or likely pathogenic by ClinGen, but have not been biochemically characterized in vitro and are therefore annotated as candidates of the ECHS1 disease set:<br><br><br>A2V: Sakai et al, 2015;<br><br><br>A132T: Haack et al, 2015;<br><br><br>T180A: Tetreault et al, 2015; Fitzsimons et al, 2018;<br><br><br>G195S: Haack et al, 2015; Tetreault et al, 2015;<br><br><br>A238V: Tetreault et al, 2015;<br><br><br>K273E: Haack et al, 2105; Ferdinandusse et al, 2015.
