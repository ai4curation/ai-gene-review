# NBR1 binds ATM:Ub-p-PEX5:SQSTM1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9664881](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9664881)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

As autophagy receptors, Next to BRCA1 gene 1 protein (NBR1) and Sequestosome 1 (SQSTM1) can cooperate to achieve pexophagy. SQSTM1 supports NBR1 to bind ubiquitinated peroxisomes through its UBA domain and enhances the efficiency of the degradation process (Kirkin V et al. 2009).
