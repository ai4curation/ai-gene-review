# Phosphorylated OPTN translocates to the nucleus
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-2562594](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-2562594)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Phosphorylation of OPTN (optineurin) on serine S177 by PLK1 promotes translocation of OPTN to the nucleus (Kachaner et al. 2012).
