# SUMOylation of RAD52 with SUMO1
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-4568863](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-4568863)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

RAD52 is SUMOylated at lysine-411, lysine-412, and lysine-414 with SUMO1. SUMOylation is important for localization of RAD52 to the nucleus.
