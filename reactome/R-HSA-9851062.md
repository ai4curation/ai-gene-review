# AcK685 pY705,S727 STAT3 binds the MIR21 gene
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9851062](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9851062)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

STAT3 binds to the MIR21 gene as assessed by chromatin immunoprecipitation (ChIP). STAT3 binding represses expression of miR-21 (Zhang et al, 2011).
