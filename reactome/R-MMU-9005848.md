# Active Prkaca and CaMK IV phosphorylate Mecp2 at T308
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-9005848](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-9005848)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

Recombinant mouse Prkaca (PKA C-alpha), as well as the activated recombinant mouse Camk4 complex (CaMK IV), phosphorylate recombinant mouse Mecp2 on threonine residue T308 (Ebert et al. 2013).
