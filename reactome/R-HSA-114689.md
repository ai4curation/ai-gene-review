# PLC gamma 2-mediated PIP2 hydrolysis
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-114689](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-114689)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

At the beginning of this reaction, 1 molecule of '1-Phosphatidyl-D-myo-inositol 4,5-bisphosphate' is present. At the end of this reaction, 1 molecule of '1D-myo-Inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate', and 1 molecule of '1,2-Diacylglycerol' are present.<br><br> This reaction is mediated by the 'phospholipase C activity' of 'Phosphorylated phospholipase C gamma 2'.<br>
