# Relaxin receptor 1 binds relaxin 2 and 3
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-444838](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-444838)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Relaxin receptor 1 (RXFP1 or LGR7) was identified as a receptor for porcine relaxin and subsequently shown to bind human relaxin-2 and relaxin-3.
