# Activation of WASP and N-WASP by CDC42
**Reactome ID:** [R-MMU-442572](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-MMU-442572)
**Species:** Mus musculus

## Summary

The activation of WASP and N-WASP (WASL) by CDC42:GTP was demonstrated by mouse system-based experiments (Symons et al. 1996, Kolluri et al. 1996, Rohatgi et al. 1999, Park and Cox 2009)
