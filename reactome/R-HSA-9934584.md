# The complex of MDM2 and CDH1 translocates to early endosomes
**Reactome ID:** [R-HSA-9934584](https://reactome.org/content/detail/R-HSA-9934584)
**Species:** Homo sapiens

## Summary

Ubiquitinated CDH1 (E-cadherin) and MDM2 co-localize in the early endosome, co-transported from the plasma membrane by endocytosis (Yang et al. 2006). As shown by expression of a dominant negative mutant of dynamin-2 (DNM2), a component of endocytic machinery, and a dominant negative mutant of EPS15, a ubiquitin-interacting part of the endocytic machinery, endocytosis is necessary for MDM2-mediated degradation of CDH1, with ubiquitin likely serving as a sorting signal for CDH1 endocytosis (Yang et al. 2006).
