| Cdc42 | Small Rho-family GTPase | Polarity-associated Rho-family GTPase engaged by Eph receptor networks; contributes to filopodial dynamics and polarized cytoskeletal responses (pqac-00000003, pqac-00000004) | Supports cell polarity, protrusion control, and migration/guidance decisions downstream of Eph activation, especially in neural and motility-associated contexts (pqac-00000003, pqac-00000004) | Functions within broader Eph-induced Rho GTPase circuitry together with Rac1 and RhoA; regulated indirectly by adaptor/GEF assemblies recruited to activated receptors (pqac-00000003, pqac-00000004) |
