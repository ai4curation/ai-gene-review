| 2 | Nodal receptor activation | Assembly of the receptor complex allows the constitutively active type II receptor kinase to phosphorylate the GS domain of the type I receptor, generating an active activin-class receptor complex, typically conceptualized as a heterotetrameric signaling assembly.
