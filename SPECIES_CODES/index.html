    Domain Distribution Summary

    • Bacteria: ~100+ genes across 23+ species
    • Archaea: 3 genes across 2-3 species
    • Fungi: ~30 genes across 10 species
    • Plants: ~29 genes across 3 species
    • Animals: ~210 genes across 9 species
    • Viruses/Phages: ~12 genes across 8 species

    Notes

    1. Species codes are typically 5-6 uppercase characters derived from the organism name
    2. Numeric codes (188937, 559292) are NCBI Taxonomy IDs
    3. Some codes like "human", "mouse", "yeast", "worm", "fly", "pombe" are common names
    4. The "9" prefix typically indicates unclassified/environmental sequences
    5. "BP" prefix typically indicates bacteriophages
