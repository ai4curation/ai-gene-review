Review Summary

This rule contains a critical functional mismatch, assigning (S)-2-hydroxyglutarate dehydrogenase activity to proteins with D-3-phosphoglycerate dehydrogenase domains. These are biochemically distinct enzymatic activities with different substrates, products, and metabolic roles. The rule creates false positive annotations and should be removed.

Action Rationale

Clear functional mismatch between condition (D-3-phosphoglycerate dehydrogenase) and annotation ((S)-2-hydroxyglutarate dehydrogenase). These enzymes have distinct substrates, products, cofactors, and metabolic pathways. The rule produces false positive annotations and should be deprecated to prevent misleading functional genomics analyses.
